
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -
Tags: [[Su]], [[Teleportation]]

Gain the ability to perform a 10 minute ritual that allows you to bond with 2 specific weapons.
- You gain [[DR]] 10 against [[disarm]] combat maneuvers made against these weapons
- When [[Thrown]], they automatically return to your hand after the attack is resolved if desired
- If these weapons somehow manage to leave your possession otherwise, you can spend a swift action to have them return to your hand(s)
