
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Wis]] +1 or a Deific Boon from [[Frogg Saran]]
Tags: [[Healing]]

You are gifted a pouch from your deity. At the end of each [[rest]], if the pouch is empty, it is filled with food from your god. The ambrosia can be eaten using the same action as [[Use Potion]] on yourself or [[administer potion]] on another creature.

It heals (7+2*[[LvL]]) and nourishes as a full meal.
