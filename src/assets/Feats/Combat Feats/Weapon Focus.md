<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

Choose one: slash, pierce or crush

You can use the default special attacks of these weapon types ([[Base Special Attacks|cleave]]/[[Base Special Attacks|lacerate]], [[Base Special Attacks|lunge]]/[[Base Special Attacks|pierce defense]], [[Base Special Attacks|backbreaker]]/[[Base Special Attacks|concussive strike]], [[Base Special Attacks|snipe]]/[[Base Special Attacks|maim]]) at-will.

You also gain a new one depending on the type you chose, usable 1/combat.

Slash: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Great Cleave</span> - Attack all creatures currently within [[reach]].

Pierce: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Impale</span> - Creature struck takes your base weapon damage if they move by the start of your next turn.

Crush: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Tenderize</span> - [[Staggered|stagger]] [[On-hit]] (fort negates)

The default and newly acquired special attacks also add your [[LvL]] to damage.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span>
If you have the [[unarmed combatant]] feat, then you can apply the effects of weapon focus (crush) to the special attacks granted by it instead of the standard crush attacks. If you have [[Shield Focus]], you can choose to apply the effects of weapon focus (crush) to [[Base Special Attacks|endure]]/[[Base Special Attacks|shield bash]].
