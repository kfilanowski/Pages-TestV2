Trade rate of fire/movement for increased accuracy and range.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

Carefully line up a [[ranged attack]] and increase the action required to make it to a [[FRA]]. Ignore the [[Attack]] penalty for shooting in the second [[Ranged Attack|range increment]].

If your target is in the first increment, instead gain +2 attack.

For effects that don't have range increments, such as spells, you can increase the action to cast to a FRA to gain the +2 bonus to attack.
