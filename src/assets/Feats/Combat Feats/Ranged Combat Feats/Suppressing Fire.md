As a standard action, you can [[prepare]] to cover an ally's movement using a ranged weapon you can make [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]] with. They are treated as though they took the [[disengage]] action on their next turn.

When your ally leaves a [[Threaten|threatened]] space, you can make a single [[Simple Attack]] against the threatening creature.
