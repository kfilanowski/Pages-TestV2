
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

* You can [[Dual Wielding|dual wield]] with any 1h weapon, not just light weapons.
* When dual wielding, you can sacrifice your "off hand" attack to gain [[Advantage]] with your "main hand" attack.
* You can also sacrifice your "off hand" attack to [[Feint]] a creature. You must use your "main hand" to attack the creature you feinted.
