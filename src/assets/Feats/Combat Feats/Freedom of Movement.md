
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -
* You take no penalty to speed while [[staggered]] or [[frosty]]. This can reasonably extend to other minor temporary speed [[Status]] that aren't these exact status conditions (such as the speed penalty from the [[icicle]] cantrip).
* If [[knocked down]], your speed only decreases by 15.
* If you would be unable to move at all, such as being [[stunned]] or [[entangled]], your speed is instead 5. You bring any grapplers with you.
