When you defeat an enemy, gain a +4 bonus to [[+Intimidation]] checks for the rest of combat. Whenever you defeat a creature, you can [[Demoralize]] another creature as a free action. If they were already shaken, they instead are [[Frightened]] for 1 round

This free demoralize attempt can only occur once each round.
