
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

Even while unarmed you [[threaten]] with punches, kicks and elbows. Your unarmed strikes deal d8 dmg, or 2d6 if your hands are empty and intact.

Gain 2 special attacks usable with unarmed strikes.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Blunt Force Trauma</span> - [[On-hit]] [[Staggered|stagger]] your target for one round (fort negates). If this attack [[Defeated|defeats]] the target, make a [[thrown]] weapon attack using the defeated creature as ammunition.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Power Fist</span> - On hit stagger your target for one round (fort negates). If this attack defeats the target, it flies 1,000 ft into the air and gently lands on the ground one minute later. With a low roof, the creature is launched less high, but for just as long.
