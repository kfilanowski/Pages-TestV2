
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

- Whenever you make a [[thrown]] weapon attack, the weapon you throw returns back to your hand immediately, allowing you to continuously make attacks with the same weapon(s) in the same turn.
- You also ignore the attack penalty from using thrown weapons in their second [[Ranged Attack|range increment]].
If a weapon's special effect requires being on the opponent to function (such as a [[Net]]), then you cannot ricochet them.
