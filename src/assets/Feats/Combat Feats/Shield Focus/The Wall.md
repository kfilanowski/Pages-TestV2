
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - Shield Focus

When you wield 2 shields
* You can use the shield special attacks ([[Base Special Attacks|shield bash]]/[[Base Special Attacks|endure]]) any number of times per battle.
* Gain a +2 bonus to [[AC]] against any enemy you attack with a shield for 1 round.
* [[Tank Up]] does not cost any movement to activate and it does not reduce your speed, effectively allowing you to always tank up, but still only ever in 1 direction.
