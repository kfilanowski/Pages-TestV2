
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements </span>- 

* When you [[disarm]] a creature, you can immediately wield the weapon you steal. If you don’t have a free hand, you can drop equipment to hold the disarmed gear.
* You get a +2 bonus to [[Attack]] with weapons you have disarmed from your opponents this combat.
* You get a +2 bonus to [[Caster Level|CL]] with a spell focus you have disarmed from your opponents this combat.
