
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -  

* Whenever you land an [[AoO]] against a creature that attempts to move out of your [[Threaten|threatened]] spaces, their movement is halted and their speed is reduced to zero for their turn
* The first time a turn a creature you threaten damages an ally within your [[Reach]] (that isn't you), make an AoO against it
	* <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Sentinel SHOULD proc on things like</span> - Any [[Attack Roll]] that deals damage, [[AoE]] damaging effects like [[Fire Blast]], single target damaging effects like [[Magic Missile]], high impact [[Persistent]] effects like [[Wall of Fire]]
	* <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Sentinel SHOULDn't proc on things like</span> - Damage over time effects like [[Ablaze]] or [[Bleeding|Bleed]], damage reflecting effects like [[Elemental Shield]], low impact persistent effects like [[Self Immolation]]

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
Because the trigger to [[Sentinel]] is the same as some reactive features (like [[Avenging Wrath]]), you cannot use this feat and such abilities to attack a creature twice. Though nothing is stopping you if your ally is harmed more than once.
