<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Rapid Reload]]

Your hands are a blur while reloading, cranking back crossbows and loading bullets with such incredible dexterity it borders superhuman.

<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Crossbows </span>- You can spend 1 mana as an instant action. Reloading with your crossbow no longer requires any actions for 1 round.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Guns </span>- You can reload your [[gun]] as an instant action by spending 1 mana for each shot.
