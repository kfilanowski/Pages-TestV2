
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - +2 [[INT]]

By spending a [[memory]] slot as a free action, you can memorize everything you are currently looking at, as though taking a photo. When looking back at this memory, you can comb it for further details, such as reading text or inspecting faces in a crowd.

You can only memorize one such scene at a time.

If you already have your memory slots full, you can forget something to use this feat.
