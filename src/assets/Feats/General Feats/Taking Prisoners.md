Whenever your actions [[Defeat]] a creature, you instead inflict [[Time Out]]

As a standard action against a defeated creature, you can perform a series of nonlethal blows in key places so they are put in time out, and to make it so they cannot lose the condition until they [[rest]] or someone performs a [[+Medicine]] check against them.
