*Your efficient packing and planning pays dividends.* 

Tags: [[Recharge]]
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements </span>- 
- You gain +5 [[Inventory|inventory slots]].

By spending 1 hour in a settlement, 1,000 gold and any number of inventory slots, you can prepare nebulous "goods" for later retrieval. As a standard action you can root through your stuff to pull out ANY item that you could fit in the slots within the gold limit.

You only "spend" money equal to the item's actual gold cost, meaning if you pull out a 2 gold slice of special goblin bread for a hungry child, you only need to spend 2 gold to replenish your *brilliant plan* next time you recharge this ability.

As stated previously, it requires an hour of shopping in a settlement to recharge this ability. Particularly small settlements with low [[populations]] may limit the types of goods you can obtain (ie a banana in a remote northern fishing village).
