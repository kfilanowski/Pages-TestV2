Tags: [[Su]]

As a standard action, you can touch your companion creature to turn them into a tiny action figure version of themselves, provided they are willing. If left in figurine form for too long, your companion will free itself when it gets hungry, thirsty or lonely.

As a standard action, you can toss this action figure up to 30 ft away to transform it back. If you are currently in combat, the companion can spring to life using its own standard action.
