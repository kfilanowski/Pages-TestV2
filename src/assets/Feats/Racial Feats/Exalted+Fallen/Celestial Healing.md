<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Exalted - Fallen]]

Your blood pouring over your body has the helpful side effect of healing your other wounds.

Whenever you take damage from [[Bleeding|bleed]], you get an additional save against an ongoing negative [[Status]] effect . You can cause yourself to start bleeding for 1 damage as a swift action.

You can withhold this healing effect and give it to another within reach by letting your blood wash over them. This is a standard action.
