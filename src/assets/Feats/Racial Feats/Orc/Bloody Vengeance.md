<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Orc]], Vengeful

When a non-[[Minion]] ally is [[defeated]] while in combat, fly into a rage as per [[Barbarian]] as an instant action. If you are already a barbarian, your bonus to strength increases by 1. You immediately mark the creature with your vengeful trait and become [[Taunted]] by them.

This rage ends when the creature is defeated.
