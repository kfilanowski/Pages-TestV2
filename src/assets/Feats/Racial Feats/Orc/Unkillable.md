
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Orc]]

You are no longer [[drained]] when you are [[Defeated]]. When you would otherwise die, you instead are drained 20, and in a coma until you recover this drain. It requires a creature with [[+Medicine]] proficiency to [[Assess the Wounded]] to realize you are still alive.

Particularly destructive deaths, like guillotine or [[Disintegration]] do not function against you, leaving your head still attached and your atoms still...not a fine dust.

If you are attacked further while in this coma, you die as normal, but any [[Healing]] effect used on you within a few minutes can bring you back to life.
