
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Amazon]], [[STR]] +3, [[LvL]] 5

Multiply your [[Carry Weight]] by 16.

The following [[special attack]] can be used at will.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Heavy Artillery</span> - You can [[Thrown|throw]] an object weighing at least 1 ton to deal 3d12+STR (16+STR) dmg, defaulting to crush dmg. This counts as a [[+Siege Weapon]].

As far as [[handedness]] is concerned, it requires 2 hands to toss a rock like this. You can lower the base damage of this attack to 3d8+STR (13+STR) to do it one-handed.
