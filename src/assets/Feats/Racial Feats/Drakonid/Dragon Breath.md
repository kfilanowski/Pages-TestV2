
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[Drakonid]], Breath weapon racial feature, Level 7

The damage of your breath weapon increases to 5d8+[[LvL]] (reflex halves). You can fire it in either a 40 ft cone or an 80 ft line.

In addition, it gains a bonus effect depending on what elemental damage type you chosen (reflex negates).
- [[Necrotic]] - Inflicts [[bone chilled]] for 2 rounds
- [[Fire]] - Foes are set [[ablaze]]
- [[Frost]] - The area of the breath weapon becomes a [[Slippery Surfaces|slippery surface]]. You can pass over it without risk of getting [[knocked down]]
- [[Zap]] - Inflicts [[staggered]] for 1 round
- [[Poison]] - Inflicts [[sickened]] 2
- [[Force]] - Also has the [[teleportation]] tag. You can [[Teleportation|teleport]] to any point in the [[AoE]] of your breath weapon.
- [[Holy]] - Does not damage allies. Grants all allies in the AoE a [[divine shield]]
- [[Psychic]] - Foes are [[confused]] for 1 round
- [[Sonic]] - Also gains the [[fear]] tag. Inflicts [[frightened]] for 1 round
