<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Magic</span> - Feats that specifically apply to spells.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">MM</span> - Short for meta magic. A subsection of magic feats that allow you to apply general augments to spells by increasing the mana cost by 1. Most require intelligence.

You can only apply 1 metamagic feat to a spell at once.
If you apply this to an [[Innate Technique]], then it requires a swift action in addition to its usual cast time (unless it is a swift, immediate or free action).
