
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[INT]] +3

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
- Only a spell that targets a single creature can be a bouncing spell. 
- Uniquely, this feat can be applied as an immediate action and it costs 3 mana.

Whenever a creature passes a save against your spell, or when you miss/[[Graze]] a [[Spell Attack]], you can choose to target a new creature within 15 ft of the original target to be the new target.

When this happens, the original target suffers no ill effects, such as half damage from a passed save or graze.
