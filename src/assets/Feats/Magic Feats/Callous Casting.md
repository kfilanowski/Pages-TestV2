
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

As a swift action, increase the DC of an instantaneous spell you cast this turn by 2 if you include any non-[[minion]] allies (including yourself) in its AoE.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span>
If you have an ability to effectively not include allies in an AoE, then you do not get the bonus of this feat.
If the ally is [[Immunity|immune]] or has [[Resistance]] to the effect, then you do not get the bonus of this feat.
