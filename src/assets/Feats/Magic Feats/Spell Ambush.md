
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[+Stealth]] Proficiency

Your spell [[DC]] is 2 higher against creatures you successfully [[hide]] from using stealth (aka if they would normally be [[DD]] from hiding). This only applies to the spell's initial save.

This does not apply to other ways to cause a target to be DD, such as if you [[blinded]] them or [[feint]] them.

Within reason, some abilities that similarly can only be used on the opening turn of combat to deceive or fight dirty against an enemy can also use this ability (like the [[Succubus]]' Sudden Betrayal ability).
