
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[INT]] +1

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
- Only an [[AoE]] spell without a duration can be a lingering spell. 

A lingering spell takes a spell like [[Fire Blast]] and lets the explosion last until the start of your next turn. In the case of fire blast, any creature that enters the explosion radius must immediately make a save against the effect.

This adds the [[Concentration]] and [[Persistent]] tag to the spell.
