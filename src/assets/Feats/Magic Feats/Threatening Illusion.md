When you create a [[figment]] effect that creates a creature (such as [[Imagination]]), it also [[Threaten|threatens]] and provides [[Flanking]] as long as creatures have not saved to disbelieve.

As a standard action, or as an [[AoO]], you can have the figment attack a creature. The creature takes d8 per spell level psychic damage (will negates and creature disbelieves illusion).
