
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - [[INT]] +1

- When you cast a spell that deals damage and targets multiple creatures, choose a "primary target." The [[DC]] of the spell for that creature is 2 higher. 
- If the spell allows attacks against multiple creatures (such as [[Gloom Bolt]]), you also get a +2 bonus to your [[Spell Attack]] against this creature.
