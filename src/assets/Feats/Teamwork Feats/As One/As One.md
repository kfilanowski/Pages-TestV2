<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> -

You can spend your movement during your allies' turns.

You subtract this movement from the movement on your next turn. If you have already moved (such as when you go first in initiative), then your movement is limited by whatever remained at the end of that turn.

Turn based games make movement awkward, but the idea is you are moving together, so other positional effects, such as [[escape route]] or [[+Shield Actions]], can treat moving allies as within reach for the duration of the movement in circumstances where allies converge on a location or start in positions that are close to each other.
