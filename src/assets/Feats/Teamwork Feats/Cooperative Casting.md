
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Requirements</span> - The ability to cast spells

You can use the [[Aid]] action on an ally within 30 ft to increase the [[Caster Level|CL]] ally by 2 for 1 round. When you do this, you can also spend 1 [[Mana]] to decrease the mana cost of their next spell by 1.

If your level is higher than the creature you are assisting, the CL boost increases to 3.
