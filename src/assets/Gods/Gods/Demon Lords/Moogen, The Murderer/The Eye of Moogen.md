The iconography that Moogen and her followers use is centered around her eyes. They are white with many black circles encircling each other, growing smaller until they become a pupil. It is said that Moogen herself can gaze through any Eye of Moogen, such as those printed onto banners or that are featured in the [[Moogen]] spells. As such, great care is taken by any non-worshippers to remove any and all Moogen iconography from places they don't want any prying eyes.
