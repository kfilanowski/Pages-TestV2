The training never stops. You are constantly under the effects of High [[Gravity]] and it does not affect your speed or carrying capacity, no matter the tier.

In gravity neutral scenarios (ie gravity is normal), you can temporarily turn off this gravity training as a free action, providing the following benefits.
- It no longer requires a swift action in order to take the [[Jump]] action and creatures can automatically jump as far and as high as a creature's [[Land Speed]]. When you jump, you ignore [[Fall Damage]].
- You can [[Dash]] as part of activating this ability, and as a swift action on each subsequent turn.
- Multiply your [[Carry Weight]] by 10x

You can typically only benefit from this surge of agility for a few minutes at a time, and only a few times a day, lest the gravity training be ineffective.
