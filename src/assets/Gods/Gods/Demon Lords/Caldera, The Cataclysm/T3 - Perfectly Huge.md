When you flex, you exert a weak gravitational force. Whenever a creature with [[Line of Effect]] is affected by forced movement, you can choose to pull them 10 ft closer to you as an instant action after the effect is resolved.

You can cause any unattended loose object you can carry with 1% of your [[Carry Weight]] to fly into a free hand if it is within 30 ft.
