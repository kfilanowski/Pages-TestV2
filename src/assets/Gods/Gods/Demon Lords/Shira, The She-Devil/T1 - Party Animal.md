While at a social gathering, such as a party, festival, ball etc, you can exist in two places at once. This otherwise functions as a [[Ninja]] shadow clone, but the clone disappears once the event ends or you begin combat.

So long as both clones aren't in someone's line of sight at once, people do not question how you pop up so frequently.
