You regularly suffer from vivid nightmares where you experience the hardship that you have caused. You experience the financial troubles of those you have swindled, the relationship troubles your infidelity has cause etc. Only through rectifying your actions, seeking forgiveness from those you have wronged can you assuage the dreams.

Against a willing or [[Helpless]] creature, you can also cause them to experience this same condition, manifesting as a unique [[Affliction]]. You must be somewhat aware of a specific sin the creature has committed, and the nightmares they experience are related to this event. Not used to this condition, the creature is unable to properly benefit from [[Rest]]. Once they repent for this sin, the nightmares stop and they can rest easy, hopefully with them having learned their lesson.
