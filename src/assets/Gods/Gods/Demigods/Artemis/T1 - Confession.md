You cannot use [[+deception]] to [[convince truth]]. You cannot tell half truths. If you do not want to speak the truth, your only option is to remain silent. 

You gain a marking, usually on your hand, that showcases your belief in Artemis. Any creature trained in [[+Religion]] can automatically tell its significance. If you place your mark against a creature, it also cannot deceive as you can't.

You also cannot reveal any secrets told to you in confidence by other creatures. There is no phrase that needs to preface these statements, only sentiment. You can inherently tell when a creature tells you something they believe is a secret that needs to be kept. Even methods like [[detect thoughts]] or [[Intrusive Thoughts (SS)|intrusive thoughts]] cannot reveal these secrets.

While your mark is in contact with another creature, they can intuit that their secrets are safe with you. Any secrets you tell them while in contact, they also cannot share, even after you separate from them.
