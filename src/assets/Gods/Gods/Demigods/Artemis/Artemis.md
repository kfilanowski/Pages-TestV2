Artemis is the goddess of the moon, redemption and secrets. Artemis has a deep respect for community. "Love thy neighbor" is a nice-sounding statement, but the followers of Artemis live by this mantra. It is something that has always restricted a wide-reaching adoption of Artemis, but she insists that communities do not grow too large to where you no longer know everybody in your neighborhood. This even informs the city structures where Artemis is popular: Districts are limited to populations of about 200 in order to keep people integrated into their communities.

The clergy and most devout followers of Artemis are sworn to absolute secrecy and cannot act on any information people tell them in confidence. Confession is a common method for people to deal with their problems, and a common method followers of Artemis use to solve problems around the world. The greatest failings that people keep close to them often cause the greatest problems. Without a proper diagnosis for a problem, healing cannot begin. As such, [[Eternal]] are often the most devout followers of Artemis, despite most of their communities existing in the [[Feywilds]].

Artemis once again walks amongst mortals. Her physical form is that of a air [[Geniekin]]. She is nearly 10 feet tall, has light blue skin and silver eyes. Her hair and her numerous face and body tattoos forming patterns across her skin are a milky golden yellow. She wears a simple robe that matches this golden yellow aesthetic. Her medium length hair always seems to flow in the wind away from her face, no matter the direction she faces.

Artemis once had a domain within [[Elysium]], but has since fallen out of favor with the rise of the [[Brazzeria]]. Maybe one day she can reclaim her spot among the other [[Heavenly Deities]].
