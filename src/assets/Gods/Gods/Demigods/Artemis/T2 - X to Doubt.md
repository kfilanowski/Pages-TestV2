The greatest lies are those we tell ourselves.
As an instant action, you can gaze upon a creature to see if they have confidence in what they stand for and what they say. You can only do this in response to statements with moral weight to them like "all hellion are the same" or "I'm concerned for the safety of the children." You reveal any doubt they have about themselves, their past actions or their beliefs (will negates).

When this ability is successful, you learn one of the following things about the creature, depending on what is most relevant in this particular scenario.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1</span> - The creature is possessed, [[Dominated]] or otherwise magically influenced by some outside force that forces them to think or act a certain way.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 </span>- The creature is actively deceiving others for social capital or other nefarious schemes
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3 </span>- The creature is ashamed of themselves, and doesn't believe their own words or actions, or only does so superficially
