Tags: [[Su]] 
You can use this ability by making a wish and dropping a sum of money into a well, or water sources that are or are soon to be moving underground.

Your wish along with your money will work its way back into the world in a way that will aid the creature you wish for at some point in the near future. This could be a special delivery, a discount on a purchase they planned to make or an item they uncover in the ground. [[Ulule]] decides how best to use these funds to assist in your goal, and pays more attention to the intent behind the wish than the exact wording when deciding what to do.
