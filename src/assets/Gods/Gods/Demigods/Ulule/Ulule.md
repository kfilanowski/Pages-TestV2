Ulule is a relatively niche revered figure of earthkin [[Geniekin]]. She fell in love with a mortal and wished to make a home for her new family. She found a giant beautiful tree and infused it with her own life. It turned the tree into a mix of wood, metal and stone. It later became known as the Iron Pine. This enormous tree and its roots served as the center point for these people in the heart of the [[Flame Empire]]. Her followers are generally upstanding people, albeit they keep themselves secluded away from others. Many that worship Ulule also worship the [[Earth Mother]].

The empire did not take too kindly to what they viewed as squatters and plotted to kill her. They succeeded, but the geniekin still remain to this day, cloistered in their Iron Pine, protected by its seemingly indestructible umbrella of protection that branches deep underground. The Iron Pine uniquely has a mind of its own, and its said that Ulule's spirit still resides within it. She selectively allows creatures like [[Earthbender]]s to manipulate its shape to better suit their needs.

Ulule is a genie composed mostly of marble and gemstones that adorn her face. Great care is taken towards statues of her, which are particularly lifelike given she herself was made of stone.

The motto often repeated by her followers is "What goes around comes around."
