You can manipulate the heavens such that clouds form in a shape of your choosing. If you choose to shape them into text, you can write up to two simple sentences. If you choose something that isn't text, it is usually limited to a single entity, symbol or object.

This message can also show up in a more dramatic fashion, with flashing lightning illuminating your cloud canvas. This guarantees all creatures see it. You can also try to hide it from onlookers, weather provided. Other creatures with Ao Shin GBP always notice it. Depending on the weather conditions, creatures may see it. If you are in a desert for instance, clouds will stand out. But in cloudier environs, you can roll a [[luck check]] to only deliver your message to desirable creatures.
