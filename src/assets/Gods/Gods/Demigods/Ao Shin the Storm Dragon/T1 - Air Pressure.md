You can call upon Ao Shin's destructive power for the purpose of the [[Breaking Objects]] and [[Breakout]] functions of athletics. You can use your [[+Religion]] or [[Arcana]] bonus in place of [[+Athletics]] for these purposes at a range of 30 ft. No matter the skill, you get a +4 bonus.

During particularly stormy, rainy or windy weather, you instead use [[Prayer]] get a +8 bonus to this check, and can destroy wide areas like fields and small structures like houses.
