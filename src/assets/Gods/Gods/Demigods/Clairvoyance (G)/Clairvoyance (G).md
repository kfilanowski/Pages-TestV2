Clairvoyance is the goddess of greed and [[Giants]]. More specifically, [[cloud giant]]s. She is one of the last living first generation of [[The Titans]], and as such, has lived for over 1000 years. She resides in the tallest mountain in the treacherous north of [[The Land of Frost]], Cumulus Mountain. There she hoards wealth from the surrounding humanoid civilizations that she has accumulated over the centuries. She is extremely ambitious and antagonistic, commanding her daughters to harass travelers all throughout the region. Clairvoyance is a greedy deity, primarily concerned with the enrichment of herself and her daughters. They hunt for sport, and do not discriminate in what they hunt, many even preferring the taste of humanoids over other prey. 

Because of how isolationist this clique of cloud giants is, not much is known about them. Mysteriously, they are all females, which are similar to [[Amazon]], but they lack the necessary males to aid in the creation of offspring. If the stories are to be believed, then the Clairvoyance's mother, the titan matriarch [[Thea]], was able to create Amazon after the loss of her husband through her tears. Clairvoyance and her daughters' unique ability to see long distances through clouds makes intelligence gather operations almost guaranteed to fail. Only a rare few have ever spoken to her and lived to tell the tale, even fewer outside of battle.

Clairvoyance herself stands several times taller than her daughters, who already stretched up to 20 ft tall. Her skin is a light blue, with completely white hair, eyes and tattoos covering her body in intricate patterns. She chooses to wear a tight toga, adorned with all kinds of trinkets she has created. She takes great pride in the jewelry she has stolen from others and melted down into a shape more befitting of her stature. Her favorite is a bejeweled staff with a crystalline ball in it that contains a raging storm inside of it. 

The motto often repeated by her followers is "Anything worthwhile in life must be fought for."

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note - </span>
Unlike other gods, Clairvoyance is a very simple god when it comes to earning her favor: You can simply buy Clairvoyance GBP, with her requiring more money each point. Without the [[T1 - Blade of Avarice|Blade of Avarice]] boon, you must manually deliver it to her or her daughters in [[The Land of Frost]].

For every 20k gold you offer her, you gain 1 GBP. This can also be given in the form of [[Gear Upgrades]] and other valuable materials.
