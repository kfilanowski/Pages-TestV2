You see people in black in white; the haves and have nots. You can tell just by looking at a creature that they are more or less wealthy than you based on this black (less wealthy) or white (more wealthy) aura. 

By ritually sacrificing people with wealth attached to them, you can transfer that wealth directly to [[Clairvoyance (G)]], teleporting their body (and any valuables sacrificed) directly to her. You must transfer wealth in increments of at least 1,000g, with any value in between rounded down to the nearest 1,000.

Every 20k you "donate," you get 1 GBP.
