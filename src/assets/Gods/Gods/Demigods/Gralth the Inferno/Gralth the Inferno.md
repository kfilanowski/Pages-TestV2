Gralth is the god of dragons. He is over 1000 years old, and having lived even before the beginning of [[The Age of Dragons]], he still remembers a time where dragons once roamed Nack freely. He makes his home hundreds of miles underground inside of the planet's mantle underneath a colossal volcano in the [[Land of Fire]]. The land surrounding this volcano is his [[domain]]. It is called the Domain of Dragons, which is a place where most other types of creatures are not allowed to roam. How most people know him is as the progenitor of the [[Drakonid]] race; his attempt at creating a bridge between his kind and humanoids, or via his children that share his name and seek to earn his approval.

Gralth the Inferno is largely a deity only concerned with his own kind. Dragon slaying is often seen as a sport, a necessity or even a rite of passage that can earn a creature accolades. This disgusts Gralth, and he does everything in his power to curb humanoids and the like of the ability to do so. His boons allow dragons to exist more safely in the world. When he makes an appearance on the surface, it is usually in efforts to protect dragons on their journey to retreat to his domain. As such, he is reviled by many governments and organizations in the Land of Fire and has an enormous bounty on his head.

Gralth is roughly humanoid in shape and nearly a 100 feet tall. His scales are a reddish orange color. He has head of flaming hair flowing from between his horns. Those that have witnessed him fight and lived to tell the tale say he fights like a [[Martial Artist]], preferring to throw hands over chomping and clawing at people. Many do not know it, but he is the one who initially created Dragon Style (see [[Fighting Styles]]).

The motto often repeated by his followers is "Live and let live, until we must fight to survive."
