This boon functions differently depending on if the creature is a dragon, or if they are not a dragon. Creatures kind of in between (like [[Drakonid]]) choose whatever they are farther from to transform into.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">If you are not a dragon...</span>
This boon gives you the ability to transform into a specific draconic form, chosen when you gain this boon. You cannot use any abilities dependent on your original form (ie a long frog tongue). 

- You gain a [[fly]] speed equal to your [[land speed]] 
- You gain the dragon's Overland Flight feature.
- Your size is (up to) large in draconic form. 
- While in draconic form, you can use a [[breath weapon]], dealing 2d8+(2*[[LvL]]) damage of a specific elemental type (chosen when you gain this boon) in either a 30 ft cone or a 60 ft line. This breath weapon has the [[recharge]] tag, and the tag of the elemental damage type you chose.

You can then choose between one of the following draconic subtypes, chosen when you gain this ability.
- <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">True Dragon</span> [[Polymorph|poly, true]] - You are quadrupedal. You gain a bite and claw [[Natural Attack]] which do 2d8 pierce and slash damage respectively. You lose access to your equipment, but still gain any passive bonuses from it (see [[Polymorph|poly, true]]) for more details.
- <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Protodragon</span> [[Polymorph|poly, chimeric]] - You are bipedal. You do not lose access to your equipment. 

You can swap forms as a standard action. You can enter draconic form as part of the same action as using your breath weapon.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">If you are a dragon...</span> 
This boon gives you the ability to transform into a specific humanoid form, chosen when you gain this boon. While in this form, you lose any abilities dependent on your physical form (like Tail Swipe). You are constantly under the effect of [[Tongues]]. 

You can swap forms as a free action 1/round.
