Tags: [[Su]] [[Language-Dependent]]
You learn draconic, the [[Languages|language]] of dragons. If you already knew it, learn a new language.

As a free action when you speak, you can increase the volume of your voice so it is amplified several times. This mainly increases the distance it can be heard and doesn't cause any immediate damage or negative effects to those nearby.

As a [[FRA]], you can let out a draconic roar that echoes for miles. This roar contains a message, as per the limitations of the [[Sending]] technique, but its range is limited and it can only be understood by creatures that have this boon or are [[Dragons]].
This part of the boon has the [[recharge]] tag.
