You have [[advantage]] on [[navigation]] checks.

When you are travelling long distances, you and your allies' speed are never slowed by environmental conditions (including [[Rocks]]). This does not give you any specific adaptation to the region you travel to. You must still have the ability to survive deep oceans and high mountain peaks if you want to visit such places.

To a lesser extent, Tourists are known to travel to weird locations without much reason other than to explore. In neutral scenarios, you can convince others to let you pass or tolerate your presence temporarily. This usually does not work for people that have a specific agenda, like guards in places of interest.
