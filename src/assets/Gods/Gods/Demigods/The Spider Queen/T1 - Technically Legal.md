The spider queen constantly whispers to you, teaching you ways to break rule systems and get away with it. By considering a particular act, you can think of ways to construe ways to legally justify said act, obfuscate the legality by pleading to the emotions of others or convincing others that the law is of little importance.

When doing these things you are treated as though you have [[Skill Proficiency]] in [[+Linguistics]] and [[+Deception]], each with a +4 bonus.

This also functions for social rules, not just written law.
