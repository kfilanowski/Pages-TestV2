The Spider Queen is an entity that is worshipped almost exclusively by dark elves and other intelligent spider creatures. The original Spider Queen blessed a particularly brave [[Elf]] that delved into her lair. The original now passed, the title of Spider Queen is passed down through the dark elf Shi'Sara royal family. 

The Spider Queens have always encouraged competition. Even at a young age, children are encouraged to race their siblings when very young, and surpass their siblings in martial or magical prowess as they age. The only thing that matters to the subjects of the Queen is to make the most money, draw in the most followers, have the most powerful armies and make the most ground-breaking discoveries. Being talents is one way to achieve these goals, but eliminating the competition entirely isn't something that is discouraged.

All spider queens are adept at [[Cryomancy]], and this manifests in their skin turning from a dull gray to a light blue. This blessing granted by the original Spider Queen comes in the form a spider body from the waist down. The Spider Queen will often have other unusual traits for an elf, like a mouth that can open particularly wide, incredibly large size and multiple sets of eyes.
