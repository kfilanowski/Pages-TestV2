The spider queens blessings bring you closer to her.

- You gain a [[Climb]] speed equal to your [[Land Speed]]. It can be used along any surface. 
- You can cling to walls efficiently enough to allow full use of both your hands while climbing.
- You ignore web-based [[Difficult Terrain]] and cannot be [[Entangled]] by webs.
