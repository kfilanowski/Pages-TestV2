So if gods come in tiers, what determines the tier of a deity? I'll do my best to describe how I conceptualize the ideas in my head to justify my choices. A god's tier is not set in stone and can change over time.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Tier 1</span>
These are level 12 characters with the [[Demigod]] [[Ascendance]]. They are incredibly powerful and show promise, but are very much still mortal. If they can capture the hearts and minds of enough people, they can continue to exist. Few demigods ever make a lasting impact. 

Other high level creatures with great spiritual or cultural significance can also fall into this category, with local legends and reverence fueling their strength. Something like the [painted lady](https://avatar.fandom.com/wiki/Painted_Lady) from TLA. Kings and emperors of the past have been ascribed deific power/significance through concepts like [the divine right of kings](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Divine_right_of_kings) in the west or [the mandate of heaven](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mandate_of_Heaven) in the east.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Tier 2</span>
These demigods are creatures that are still mortal, but are higher than level 12. They have managed to solidify a niche on the world with a group of dedicated followers that extends past that of a single generation from a single town.

The [[Inevitable]] are considered T2 deities. They represent concepts such as death or time, and destroy those who seek to defy their authority and bend their laws. Their active role in the mortal realm prevents them from ever becoming more powerful deities.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Tier 3</span> 
This is where gods become untouchable to mortals. They do no have stat blocks and can no longer directly intervene in the lives of mortals. See the [[power of god]] page for more details.

Tier 3 gods are well known in many regions of the world. Entire races and countries subscribe to their way of life. [[Demon Lords]] are commonly T3 gods, as their followers are forced to constantly butt heads with creatures around the world, keeping their population and influence in check.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Tier 4</span>
The most powerful of all the deities represent forces so fundamental to existence that they cannot help but be relevant. Basically every intelligent creatures knows of, and must respect these deities, lest they feel their wrath.

Other gods bend their knees to these deities and allow their followers to abide by certain practices, such as respect for the dead or reverence for the natural world, to avoid having their followers wiped out.
