Whenever you hold a written work, you can concentrate on it for 1 minute in order to animate the physical object, causing it to speak as though it were the author as they were when the work was completed, published etc. The author is willing to discuss the contents of their work, but only to those they deem worthy of elucidation.

By showing your commitment to their field through your an appropriate [[+Knowledge]] proficiency, or through a DC 20 [[+Persuasion]] check, you can convince them to speak on the subject. Other things can potentially convince the author of your worthiness as well, such as your knowledge of [[Necromancy]], or having an item of significance.

This conversation can cause you to learn more about the context the work was written in, the intended purpose of the book, where research contained in the book was performed and other metacontextual elements of the work.

Because the entity that you speak with is a snippet of the author frozen in time, you cannot teach it anything, but you can potentially ask their opinion on something they do not know about (like current events). This discussion is something that lasts up to 1 hour, after which, the written work goes back to being whatever it was prior to its creation.
