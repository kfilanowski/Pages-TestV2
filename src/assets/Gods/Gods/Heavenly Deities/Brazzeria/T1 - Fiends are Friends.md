You get advantage on [[+Persuasion]] checks made against [[Fiends]] and those tainted by fiends. You can concentrate for 1 minute to get a general sense of distance and direction of the highest concentration of fiends within 100 miles. 

By succeeding on a [[+Planes]] check, or touching a fiend, you can tell if its soul/psyche is too shattered to be redeemed. This is most common in monstrous fiends, or amalgamations of souls.
