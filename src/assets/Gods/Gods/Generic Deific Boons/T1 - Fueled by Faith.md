The gods provide. You no longer need to eat, drink or sleep. You can also stretch other physical limitations to greater extremes, allowing you to hold you breath for several times longer or keep your eyes open for extended periods without discomfort.

Your appearance is always as though you were thoroughly bathed and groomed.

In order to benefit from [[Rest]] you must simply perform light activity (meditation, reading, keeping watch) for 8 hours instead of sleep.
