Tags: [[Su]], [[Polymorph|poly, true]]
You can transform into a specific small or smaller terrestrial or aquatic beast that must be chosen when you gain this boon. This grants you [[Scent]] (5 ft) and either a swim speed, climb speed or 40 land speed. You gain no other additional abilities a creature possesses.
You can only transform into this beast. The transformation lasts until you end it. You can exit this form as a swift action.
