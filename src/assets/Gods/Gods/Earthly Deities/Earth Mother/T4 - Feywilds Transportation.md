The [[Feywilds]] is a place that mirrors the mortal plane, and if you know where to look, you can temporarily slip into the feywilds and back again to speed up travel. By slipping under fallen logs, climbing into tree branches and passing through waterfalls, you can always find a portal to the feywilds in a setting unmarred by civilization within 1 hour. While traveling through such areas, you can use this popping in and out of [[Planes]] to speed up overland travel and make tracking you almost impossible.

You and any you bring along can travel 1,000 miles each day through this method. Any creature that lacks this boon cannot follow you unless they roll a DC 40 [[+Survival]] check to [[Tracking (P)|track]] you. In a [[+Chase]], you can win 1 round using this feature.
