You can change into a tiny frog. While in frog shape, you gain the following qualities from the [[Polymorph List]].

| Mad Hops | Increase the distance the [[jump]] action brings you by 5 ft. You can jump vertically as high as you can jump horizontally. |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Tongue   | You can make [[Drag]] attacks as [[Melee Attack\|melee attacks]] at range of 30 ft during your turn.                        |
| Smol     | Decrease to size tiny.  Decrease [[Reach]] to 0.                                                                            |
| Speed    | Gain a [[Swim]] speed equal to [[Land Speed]]                                                                               |
You can transform into or back from frog form as a free action 1/turn.
