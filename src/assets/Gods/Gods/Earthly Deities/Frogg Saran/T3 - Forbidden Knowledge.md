Tags: [[Sp]], [[Recharge]]

You can expend a use of [[prayer]] in order to find out forbidden lore concerning a creature you can see. Choose a random creature within 30 ft of you and then roll [[serendipity]]. The higher the roll, the more pertinent the information. This knowledge is always presented in a numerical format such as "2 kings killed" for important/secret high roll info or "0 dogs pet" for low impact knowledge.
