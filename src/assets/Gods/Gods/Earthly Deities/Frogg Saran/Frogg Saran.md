Frogg Saran is chaos incarnate. He is known to give blessings and curses in equal measure. Frogg Saran is a very active force in the world of Nack, worming his way into many people's lives whether they like it or not. All this god seeks is to increase the amount of entropy in the universe. His followers start riots and revolutions merely to change the status quo. They reincarnate people just to see the reaction to their new body.

Frogg Saran's odd fixation on the rubbery amphibious creature causes frogs to be revered by his followers. Special care should be taken when interacting with any frog or frog-adjacent creature, or risk unwittingly getting dragged into the machinations of this mad god. You never know when what looks like an ordinary frog could be a [[Giant Frog]], a [[Lunatic]], or worse yet, Frogg himself in disguise.

The true form of Frogg Saran is unknown, though many suspect his true form is that of a smug frog, save for its eyes are replaced with gnashing maws. It is a common way he appears before mortals, but with other common occurrences being cheese and tentacles, some depict him as an otherworldly aberration, or a rodent..
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Frogg and Prayer</span>
Because it is possible to have faith in Frogg with negative [[WIS]], the traditional scaling for [[Prayer]] doesn't work. You instead get a point of prayer for each point of [[LUK]] you have, and it doesn't always work. When he does answer prayers, they are typically in unexpected ways, and tend to be useful in ways other than providing advice.

This could mean the place you are looking for suddenly explodes, the handsome fellow you were interested in talking to reveals they are a homosexual, or a frog will lead the way.

In any such case, the advice/assistance comes in a much more dramatic and roundabout way.
