Ice, oil, wet floors and the lot. 

Slippery surface can cause a problem for clumsy individuals. By treating such surfaces as difficult terrain, any further penalties can be avoided. Attempting to move at full speed requires a DC 10 [[+Acrobatics]] check to [[Balance]]. Failure inflicts [[Knocked Down|Knockdown]]. Attempting to [[Jump]] into or off of a slippery surface also requires a check (but no more than one check is needed per round).

Some abilities create slippery surfaces. These use the creature's DC instead of the base 10 listed here.

Any [[forced movement]] against a creature standing on a slippery surface is increased by 5 ft.
