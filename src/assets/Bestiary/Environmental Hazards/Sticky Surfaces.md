Tar, quicksand, webs, sap and the like.

Sticky surface can cause problems for everybody, but particularly for those that are untrained in [[+Athletics]]. Moving into a sticky surface inflicts the [[Entangled]] condition unless the creature succeeds on a DC 10 check athletics check to [[breakout]]. This can only happen once a turn. The entangled condition can be removed with breakout as a swift action.

Some abilities create sticky surfaces. These use the creature's DC instead of the base 10 listed here. They also may require a different action to escape. In any case, some things can reasonably free stuck creatures like slashing someone free from a web.
