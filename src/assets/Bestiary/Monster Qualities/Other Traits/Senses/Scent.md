Uses a creature sense of smell. Aquatic creatures can only use this underwater. [[Amphibious]] creatures can use it in water or land. If you have an item with a creatures scent on it, you get a +2 bonus on survival checks to [[Tracking (P)|track]] them. 

Scents can be remembered similarly to how you can recognize someone's voice. Creatures with scent automatically get a [[+Perception]] check (vs [[+Disguise]]) against someone impersonating a creature they are very close to.

A creature that is [[sickened]] cannot use scent.
