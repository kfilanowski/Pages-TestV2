Tags: [[Healing]]

The creature regains a specified amount of [[+HP]] at the start of their turn and ends all [[Bleeding|bleed]] effects before taking damage. This healing is capable of restoring lost limbs over a few days, or instantly if the limb is reattached. If at the start of this creature's turn it's regeneration is not disabled....
- It loses the [[defeated]] condition
- Restores HP equal to its regeneration value
- Is [[staggered]] instead of [[dazed]]
- Does not accumulate [[Drained|drain]]/[[Afflictions]]

All forms of regeneration have some means to stop it (typically for 1 round), usually taking a specific type of damage.

If a creature is defeated while its regeneration is disabled, it is unable to regenerate for 10 minutes (see [[Prevent Recovery (P)|prevent recovery]] for other means to keep those pesky healers down).
