Tags: [[Healing]]
The creature gains specified amount of hp the start of their turn if they are not defeated. When the creature is defeated, this ability is disabled for 10 minutes (see [[Prevent Recovery (P)|prevent recovery]] for means to keep those pesky healers down).

If a creature with FH is [[Bleeding]], roll a [[luck check]] (DC 10) to see if the bleeding stops at the end of their turns.
