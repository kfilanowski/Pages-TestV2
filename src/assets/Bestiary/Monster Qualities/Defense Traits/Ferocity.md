When the creature is reduced to zero hit points, they are not [[defeated]] until the end of their next turn. On said turn they are [[staggered]]. If a creature with ferocity is healed before their next turn, the creature is not defeated at the end of its next turn, and this ability is not consumed, however they are still staggered.

This ability can only activate once per minute.

[[Fast Healing|FH]] does not function while in ferocity.

Many effects in the game have some type of bonus effect on defeat, such as the bonus [[Special Attack|special attacks]] granted by the [[Unarmed Combatant]] feat, [[Aran]] or the [[Feast]] technique. These effects trigger when you would have defeated an enemy, but they have ferocity.

Effects that trigger when you are defeated still activate after the ferocity ends, like [[Diehard]]'s retaliatory attack, or [[Caustic Surprise]]'s after defeat effect.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note - </span>
The wording is key. If a creature uses its actions on its ferocity turn to heal itself, the ability is still consumed. They do still stave off defeat however.
