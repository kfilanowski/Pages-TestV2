Packs of beasts, platoons of soldiers and swarms of insects are all groups of weaker enemies that have slightly different representation than a typical monster. Group type enemies have a few quirks
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Universal Traits</span>

| AoE Vulnerability        | Takes 50% more dmg from any [[AoE]] effect.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Single Target Resistance | [[Resistance]] to any damage that only affects a single creature, most common with [[attack roll]]s. Certain attacks like [[Base Special Attacks\|cleave]] can ignore this resistance, but you can only attack the swarm with such moves.<br><br>Single target effects that deal no damage have no effect. |
| Poise Immunity           | Group enemies do not have [[poise]] and cannot be poise broken.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Other Traits</span>

| Distraction  | When a creature takes damage from certain group enemies, they can be overwhelmed by the sheer number of creatures. They are [[staggered]] for one round (fort negates)                                                                                                                          |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Swarm Attack | Common with insects. A swarm does not [[threaten]]. These creatures cannot make attacks and must instead enter a creatures space to deal their damage automatically at the end of their turn. This allows them to damage multiple creatures if they are close together with a big enough swarm. |
| Auto Attack  | Does [[threaten]]. Any attacks they make automatically hit. If they choose to combat maneuver, they deal 20+[[LvL]] poise damage.                                                                                                                                                               |
