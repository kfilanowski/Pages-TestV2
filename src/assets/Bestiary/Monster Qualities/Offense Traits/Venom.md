A status effect typically inflicted with one of a creature's [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]]. Functions as follows...

Venom comes in 3 stages. In order to advance from one to the next, the afflicted creature must fail a [[Fortitude]] save. The venom continues until they creature passes a fortitude save. Some creatures may have different effects for their venom, but this is the assumed default if no other details are listed.

| Stage                                                 | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Stage 1</span> | Creature is [[sickened]] 1, which cannot be removed until the creature is no longer envenomed. 1 minute after the creature fails its first save, it must roll again or hit stage 2.                                                                       |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Stage 2</span> | Creature is sickened 2, which cannot be removed until the creature is no longer envenomed. The creature also has [[Advantage\|Disadvantage]] on [[Skill Check]]s. 10 minutes after the creature fails its second save, it must roll again or hit stage 3. |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Stage 3</span> | Creature is sickened 3, which cannot be removed until the creature is no longer envenomed. The creature is [[Drained]] 10<br><br>In stage 3, a creature must roll a save every 10 minutes. Each failure inflicts drained 5.                               |
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
If a creature is exposed to venom again while already suffering from such an effect, they get [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on their next save against envenomed progressing.
