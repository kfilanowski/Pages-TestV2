Tags: [[Su]]
The creature has the ability to use a supernatural area of effect attack. Common forms include lines and cones of elemental damage.

Most breath weapons are [[recharge]] effects that have some condition to use them again.

If a creature with a breath weapon is currently [[Grapple|grappling]] a creature, they can choose to use their breath weapon instead of maintaining the grapple. The grapple ends and the newly freed creature does not receive a saving throw on the breath weapon.
