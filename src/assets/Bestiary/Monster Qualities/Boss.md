These creatures are distinct in that they command a certain degree of authority or power. Bosses are not always unique enemies, but there is a overlap. Bosses have significant boosts to their action economy. They get a handful of other bonuses as well.

[[+Monster Lore]] is particularly helpful against bosses. As part of a successful monster lore check, a character also learns any special mechanics of a boss creature, something more involved to defeat it. Think like a video game boss fight where you need to break its armor before you can get to the weak spot.

## Boss Qualities

| Recovery     | Bosses almost always have the [[Recovery]] defensive trait.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Boss Actions | The boss can act out of turn in response to specific events, usually at the end of a player's (or their allies') turns. These actions should typically be low impact compared to its main turn, doing things like creating disruptive terrain, summoning minions etc.<br>The intention is to make the boss feel a little bit more dynamic, like it's responding to the player's actions and acting accordingly. Try to avoid adding too much mobility or risk having this be an easy escape. |
| Health       | Bosses have massively increased health compared to creatures of their level. I typically multiply their health by 2.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
