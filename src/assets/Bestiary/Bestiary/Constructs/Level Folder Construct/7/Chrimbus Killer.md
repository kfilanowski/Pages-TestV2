<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Gift Grenade</span> - As a swift action, a Chrimbus Killer can drop off a gift in an adjacent space. <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Boss Actions</span> - Can do 2 of the following actions each turn.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Fire the Missiles</span> [[Recharge]] - When another creature ends their turn, fire a single missile at a creature within 60 ft. It deals 7d8 (28) fire damage in a 10 ft radius (reflex halves) and leaves flames on the ground that set creatures [[Ablaze]] if they end their turn inside of them. Chrimbus Killers come loaded with 5 missiles.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Unload</span> - When another creature ends their turn, the Chrimbus killer revs up its gun and sprays a 30 ft cone of bullets, dealing 7d8 (28) pierce damage (reflex halves). If a creature ends its turn in the radius, it takes this damage again. It cannot use its arm cannon attack on its next turn.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Lock On </span>- When a creature damages the Chrimbus Killer, it can choose to laser focus on it. It gets [[Advantage]] on attack rolls against it and the creature has disadvantage on saving throws against the Chrimbus Killer's offensive abilities for 1 round.
