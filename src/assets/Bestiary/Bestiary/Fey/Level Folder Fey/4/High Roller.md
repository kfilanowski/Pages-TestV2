<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Lucky Break</span> -  A high roller gets its name from its ability to pull wins out of its ass from seemingly nowhere. Whenever it rolls a d6 and rolls a 6, it rolls an additional d6. This can happen infinitely.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Loaded Dice</span> [[recharge]] - By blowing on a standard dice (a d6) and throwing it up to 30 feet, a high roller can cause it to explode. Deals a number of d6s of force damage equal to the end result of the loaded dice (reflex halves). This explosion is a 10 ft radius, with its center point on the ground where the dice lands.

For example, i roll a 4 on the d6. This ability does 4d6 damage.

Because of _lucky break_, this can potentially do infinite damage. If the high roller initially rolls a 6 when determining how many damage dice there are, this ability immediately recharges.
## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">"Nature Spirit"</span> - A high roller has the ability to transform into a particular butterfly. They are typically exceptionally colorful and sparkly. They can speak while transformed. They use this form to tempt unsuspecting creatures into playing their silly games of chance.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">99% of Gamblers</span> - A high roller is always enticed by games of chance, the higher the risk the better. They respect the results of such gambles, even to their own detriment. As such, high rollers are typically in crippling debt. Only 1% of them are filthy rich.

Rich high rollers typically have expensive [[gear upgrades]] at their disposal.
