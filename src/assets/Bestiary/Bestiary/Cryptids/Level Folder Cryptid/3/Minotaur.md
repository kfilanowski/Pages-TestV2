## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">A-MAZE-ing</span> - Minotaurs are exceptionally good at not getting lost. Once upon a time. they built elaborate mazes between their settlements and the outside world. Most lie in ruin. A minotaur always knows how to get to any point in their maze quickly, and can even physically change the maze with earth bending to create shortcuts. Their tremorsense increases to include any area that can be considered a "maze."
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Can't Look Away</span> - Minotaurs have [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on saves against effects that [[Taunted|taunt]] originating from creatures who are significantly red or bleeding.
