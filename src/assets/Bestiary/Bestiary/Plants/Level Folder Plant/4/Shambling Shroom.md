<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Spores Abound</span> [[Necrotic]] [[Persistent]] - A shambling shroom's greatest calling is to plant mushrooms wherever it goes. To do so, it is always on the move, leaving behind spores that linger in spaces it moves through for 1 minute before settling. As a swift action, a shambling shroom can shake its body to cause them to erupt around it, leaving behind a cloud within its [[Reach|natural reach]].
Whenever a creature moves into a space occupied by these spores, they take root and start quickly growing. [[Undead]] creatures are unharmed by this process. Any other creature takes 2d6 (7) necrotic damage and is [[Sickened]] 1 (fort halves dmg and negates sicken). The sickened condition is considered [[Permanent Status]] until the creature washes, burns, or uses [[First Aid (P)|first aid]] to get them off or steps in sunlight.
