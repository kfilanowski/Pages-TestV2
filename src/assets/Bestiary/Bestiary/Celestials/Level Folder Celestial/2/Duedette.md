<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Shine</span> - Duedettes have an unconventional [[Natural Attack]] that they can make as long as they have an empty hand. It is either made as a [[Ranged Attack]] with a range of 30 ft or a [[Melee Attack]] against a foe within their [[Reach|natural reach]]. It inflicts [[Blinded]] [[On-hit]], or [[Blurred Vision]] on a [[Graze]]. Each condition only lasts 1 round.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Repayment </span>- When a creature harms the duedette, they can spend an immediate action to make a Shine attack against the creature.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Debtor's Bane</span> - Whenever a duedette does damage with a [[Melee Attack]], it automatically reclaims money directly from the creature. The creature does not need to carry the money in order for the duedette to reclaim it, taking it from secret stashes, banks etc. They take up to 100g each time, and the money is transported to an intermediary point in [[Elysium]] before being redistributed to the creature owed the debt.
They can only use this against a creature that has made some type of contract or agreement that requires monetary repayment. 
## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Memo</span> - When a duedette is [[Defeated]], a bird erupts from their body that flies off to deliver a message to its superior of its demise. Can be easily intercepted with most forms of ranged attacks within 3 rounds. Past that it usually flies to high and too far for most creatures to catch it.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Just Business</span> - A duedette is forbidden from attacking creatures unprovoked, as they they were under the effects of [[Sanctuary]]. They are often permitted to use force when collecting debts, but will typically use it as a last resort. They can still fight in self-defense, however.
