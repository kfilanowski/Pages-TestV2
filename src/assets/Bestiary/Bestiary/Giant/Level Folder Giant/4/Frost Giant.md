## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Team Spirit</span> - Frost giants live in tight enclaves and are wildly fascinated with their home team. They take great pride in their abilities and readily accept challengers. They are even willing to accept challenges from people who do not play hockey, with the goal of the challengers usually to score a single goal on the team of giants. 

Teams are always played with an equal number of players, usually with at least 4, and players aren't allowed to deal any [[+HP]] damage to others. Other than that, there are no hard fast rules. The goal of the players is to pass a wide stretch with a puck. Because the frost giants are exceedingly good at bullying players, they will likely need to think of clever ways to disarm, distract or otherwise get the giants out of the picture. They will also need to maneuver across ice (see [[Frosty Terrain]]).

Giants are pretty big, so their rinks are over 400 ft wide from goal to goal. A start from the center requires 200 ft for the puck to travel.
