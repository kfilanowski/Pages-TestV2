## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Rise from Ashes</span> - It is a sudokus eternal torment that they must die again and again. After a sudoku is defeated it turns into a pile of smoldering ash. The ash stays warm for 24 hours, then the sudoku rises again, immediately exploding in a fireblast. If it is inside of a container too small for it to fit, it bursts out.

Only by dousing the ashes in [[holy water]] can it be permanently slain.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Wet Blanket</span> - While [[wet]], a sudoku's fireblast DC is decreased by 2 and its regeneration is disabled. While mostly submerged in water, it cannot use fireblast or fire bending.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">KYS NOW</span> - With a DC 25 [[+Persuasion]] check as a standard action, a creature can convince a sudoku to end it all immediately. Sudoku prefer to perform some kind of dramatic and explosive finish, but if no such option is available, they jump off of cliffs, snap their necks etc, immediately killing them.
[[Weakness]] - 3 holy, 3 frost
