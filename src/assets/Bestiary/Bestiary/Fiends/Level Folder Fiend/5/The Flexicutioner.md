[[Breath Weapon]] [[fire]] [[recharge]] -  Fang unlocks her jaw and unleashes a torrent of concentrated flames in a 60 ft line. Affected creatures take 6d6 (21) fire damage and are set [[ablaze]] (reflex halves dmg and negates ablaze).
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Rift Slasher</span> [[concentration]] [[persistent]] [[recharge]] [[necrotic]] -  Fang slices the ground with her axe and temporarily opens up a rift to [[The Underworld]]. A 15 ft line in front of her cracks open and becomes inaccessible, shunting all creatures in the [[AoE]] to the side. Damned souls reach for any creatures within 15 feet to pull them into the rift. They roll a +13 [[drag]] attempt immediately against all creatures within 15 feet, and passively when a creature walks into range. All poise broken creatures are partially dragged into the rift before getting ejected, taking 6d6 (21) necrotic damage.

The rift persists for 1 minute and has a 1 minute cooldown.
