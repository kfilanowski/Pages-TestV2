<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Master</span> - A pamper grows attached to creatures, preferring fiends or fiendish creatures, particularly the charismatic ones. They can give themselves the [[companion]] tag, receiving all the benefits. They get a +8 bonus to [[+Perception]] and [[+Survival]] checks to locate or track their master.

## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

[[Weakness]] - Holy 3
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Pet Pal</span> - Pampers tend to act more like animals than they do intelligent creatures. While they might not be the brightest, they still possess the ability to reason and speak. They only speak unless they absolutely have to, preferring nonverbal vocalizations. They will also speak if they believe they are alone with their master.

They can roll [[+Disguise]] to appear as an unusual animal, though any creature with [[+Planes]] proficiency automatically sees through this ruse.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Vengeance</span> - If their master is [[defeated]] or slain in the presence of the pamper, the pamper is [[taunted]] by the creature that killed/defeated their master. This taunt does not end until the pamper is slain, the target is slain, their master is healed or the pamper is otherwise unable to detect/attack the target.

If they evade vengeance, the pamper gains the +8 bonus to perception/survival usually reserved for hunting down their master against this killer for 1 week.
