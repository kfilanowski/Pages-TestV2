## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Infernal Seal</span> - The Chained Oblivion are a set of fiends that were too powerful to control and too numerous to rule without endless fighting between factions. As such, the [[Demon Lords]] decided to seal them away for later use. Powerful creatures that serve the interests of demon lords can summon these creatures in their dormant state, where they appear in some type of magical seal. This sealed form varies from case to case, but is usually large and unwieldy, weighing several tons and is big enough to contained the chained oblivion.

This seal can be broken away with a specific password (which can gleaned through a DC 40 [[+Planes]] check), the destruction of a particular object used as part of the seal, or a DC 40 [[Brute Force]] check.

Even when the seal is broken, it still takes 2 full rounds (about 12 seconds) for the creature to awaken, during which it is in [[Stasis]]. At the end of the second round, they unleash a devastating burst, activating their Awaken ability and ending their turn.

With a [[+Planes]] check, you can slow or halt this process as a standard action while adjacent to the seal. A result of 30 or higher slows the process by 1 round and a result of 40 stops it completely and prevents its reactivation for 1 minute.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Defeat </span>- The chained oblivion cannot die from being [[Defeated]]. While defeated, they are [[Invincible]]. After 1 minute of being defeated, they return back to being in stasis, as per the Infernal Seal feature. During this minute, creatures can determine what the methods are for unsealing the creature again.
