## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Boss Mechanic: Wooden Scales</span> - A wildfire dragon has surprisingly soft and vulnerable scales, so it has developed a unique form of protection. It uses its magic to form a wooden scale exoskeleton on the outside of its body. As the wildfire dragon fights, it gives off heat which burns away these scales, leaving itself vulnerable, but giving it greater mobility.

When a wildfire dragon reaches 210 hp, and every time it loses 50 more (160, 110, 60, 10), a layer of the scales explodes violently, activating Scale Shatter, decreasing the dragon's AC by 1, but increasing its speeds by 10. Healing past this threshold does not allow them to regain this AC or use scale shatter again.
<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">From the Ashes </span>- Wildfire dragons must consume the ashen remains of creatures in order to replenish the protection from their wooden scales. They need a steady supply of ashes with their usual meals over the course of a week to regain 1 layer of scales. They typically bury themselves somewhere near their supply.
