The generals of the [[Shadowling]] armies. They seek to bring down powerful foes so that way they may take control of them. They use them to inhabit the brighter parts of the world so that they may spread their corrupting influence.

When it fails or ends, willing or not, they appear within 10 ft of the creature they targeted.

When it ends, willing or not, they appear within 10 ft of the creature they targeted.
 
 <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)"> Corruption (Su) (Recharge)</span> [[Necrotic]] - As a swift action, a shadowling can invade a creatures body without taking control of it, eroding it from the inside. The shadowling exits and appears within 30 ft of the target at the start of its next turn. The target creature takes 8d6 (28) necrotic damage and is [[staggered]] for 1 round (fort halves dmg and negates stagger).
