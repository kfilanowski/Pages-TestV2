<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Innate Techniques (CL 15)</span> - [[Dispel Magic]]

[[Gaze]] [[Su]] - A beholder has many eyes, but one particularly large one it uses to cancel out magical effects. At the start of the beholder's turn, it decides to focus it's gaze on a single creature it can see (free action). The creature is [[siphoned]] for 1 round (will negates). It cannot use its eye stalks ability against this target for 1 round.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Eyestalks (Su)</span> - The beholder's extra eyes have the ability to unleash devastating laser beams at creatures within 100 ft. As a standard action it can shoot three of these laser beams at three different creatures. The effect is rolled randomly (reroll duplicates), more details below.

Outside of combat, the beholders are able to exert more fine control over its eye stalks, allowing it to telekinetically move objects and disintegrate holes into things with ease. Feel free to change up a few eye beams to make your beholders extra special.

## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Boss Mechanics</span> - Whenever a beholder is [[staggered]], instead of its usual effects, it can can only shoot 2 eyestalks on its turn. When it is [[dazed]], instead it can only shoot 1 eyestalk. When it is [[stunned]], its eyestalk ability is disabled, but it can otherwise act normally.

When a beholder is [[siphoned]], instead of its usual effects, its primary eye closes and it cannot use its gaze.

Whenever a beholder is [[blinded]], instead of gaining the blinded condition, its eyestalk DC decreases by 2. [[Blurred vision]] is instead converted into -1 to its eyestalk DC.
