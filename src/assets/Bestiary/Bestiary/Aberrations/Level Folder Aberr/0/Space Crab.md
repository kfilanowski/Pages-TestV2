This alien scavenger with no eyes skitters along the ground on all fours, raising its forelegs in response to threats. Only when it is pouncing midair does its target realize the enormous mouth on its underside.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">New Home</span> - Against a defeated creature, a space crab can attach to the creature's head and gain control over the actions of the soon to be corpse. After 1 hour, it kills the creature and reanimates it as a zombie as though casting [[animate dead]], but the creature retains its full level. It can then spend all of its actions each turn to command this undead. Only through the space crab's or it's host's death will it detach. When the space crab is killed, the host automatically dies. The space crab must use this on a creature that is initially alive. It cannot use this ability on a creature that was already dead. Use the [[zombie]] stat block for generic humanoid host. Hosts can be anything; the space crab just has to get lucky.
