<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Innate Techniques</span> - [[Roar]], [[Boasting Taunt]]

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Mimicry</span> [[Su]] [[recharge]] - Whenever a macaw sees an <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">enemy</span> use a [[Technique Rules|technique]] or [[special attack]], it can use it on its next turn. All techniques function as though they were innate techniques and use the macaws statistics.

If the macaw uses a [[mana burn]] or [[recharge]] ability, it cannot use mimicry again until it rests. It is limited to level 2 or lower techniques. The macaw cannot use this ability while [[silenced]].

Mimicry cannot be used in consecutive rounds.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Shanty</span> - A macaw can use a standard action to start singing a song. All allied pirates, seamen and sea creatures within 60 ft get a +2 bonus to [[attack]]. Continuing to sing on subsequent turns is a free action.
The macaw cannot use this ability while [[silenced]].
## <span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</span>

<span style="color:rgb(247, 138, 255)">Tongue Twister</span> - Fond of repetition, the macaw will often mimic interesting words and phrases. A creature trained in [[+Linguistics]] can roll an opposed linguistics check to as a swift action to distract the macaw if they are within 30 ft, or farther if they can increase the volume of their voice significantly. Success stops the macaw from singing any shanties and prevents it from taking any actions it on its next turn other than movement.
This can only function once per combat.
