Having weakness means you take more damage from a specific [[Damage]] type. It is expressed in numerical values like "Holy 3." Denoting that if an attack does holy damage, after all damage calculation, it deals 3 more.

Creatures can also have a weakness to something that isn't damage. They have a -4 penalty to [[+Saving Throw|Saves]] against effects of a certain [[Tag]].

Just for clarity, weakness functions like [[DR]] in that it is applied after everything else. An example...
1. I cast [[fire blast]] on ice cube man with weakness 5 fire.
2. Fire blast damage roll is 23
3. Ice cube man passes his save.
4. Half of 23 is 11.
5. 11+5 = 16 final damage
