How much hp a player has is largely determined by their class. Creatures have a base hp value of 20, and then a value that they add each [[LvL]], <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">including level one</span>. Classes and creatures all fall under one of the following categories based on their play style.

| Class Type | HP/lv | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| ---------- | ----- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Specialist | 6     | Specialist creatures rely almost solely on spellcasting and supernatural abilities. They have a great many skills in their repertoire. They tend to have unusual defensive qualities, but a few solid hits are usually enough to take them down.                     |
| Mixed      | 8     | Mixed creatures tend to be somewhere in between martials and specialists. They usually combine [[Su]]pernatural abilities and spells with weapon strikes or are capable of doing either, but not quite as well as a martial or specialist.                           |
| Martial    | 10    | Martials creatures have limited supernatural abilities that aren't directly tied to weapon attacks, however they tend to have a bit more nuance to their strategy than brutes. They usually have high [[Attack]] and [[AC]].                                         |
| Brute      | 12    | Brute creatures rely almost solely on hitting things very hard to solve all their problems. On top of high hp, they usually have other extraordinary defensive qualities like [[DR]], [[Resistance]] and [[Healing]]. They usually require heavy focus to take down. |
