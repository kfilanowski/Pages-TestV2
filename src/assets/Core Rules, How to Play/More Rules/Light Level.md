Light levels have 3 distinct categories.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Bright Light</span> - A sunny day or a torch lit room. Bright light let's creatures see things clearly. Most creatures can see in bright light without any penalty. Sources of bright light taper off into dim light before turning into darkness. For instance a [[Torch]] gives 20 ft of bright light, so it would give an equal amount of distance of dim light.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Dim Light</span> - Moonlight or at the edge of your torch light. Many humanoids cannot see well in dim light. Features in dim light appear only as a silhouette with dull, if any, color. Without special senses, creatures have [[Partial Concealment]] in dim light.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Darkness</span> - The depths of a cave or deep underwater. Few creatures can see clearly in a complete darkness. Some, such as [[Human]]s, can't see at all. Those that can (such as with [[Nightvision]] or [[Darkvision]]) still only see in grayscale. Without special senses, creatures have [[Total Concealment]] in darkness.
