When you stack [[DR]] and [[Resistance]], add them together in the way that reduces damage the most.

When you have multiple sources of increasing damage by a percentage, add the percentages together, do not multiply (ie 1.5x dmg and 1.5x dmg adds together to 2x dmg).

This can happen in rare circumstances where you [[Crit]] a [[Groups|swarm]] with [[Base Special Attacks|cleave]] for instance.

When you have multiple sources of halving damage, such as through a passed [[+Saving Throw|Save]] and [[Resistance]], then you take 50% of the halved damage (20 damage fireblast halved to 10, then halved again to 5).

When you have [[Resistance]] against an effect that has a multiplier to damage, such as a [[Crit]], apply the resistance to the final damage of the crit (10 damage gunshot, 15 crit, 7 with resistance).
