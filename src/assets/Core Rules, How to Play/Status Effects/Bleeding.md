Bleeding creatures take a specified amount of [[True Damage]] at the start of each of their turns for a specified duration. If no duration is specified, it lasts indefinitely. The [[DC]] to stop the bleeding (with the [[+Medicine]] skill, or magic healing with a [[CL]] check) is the attacker's [[LvL]]+12.

Instead of taking damage, bleed prevents a creature from losing the [[Defeated]] condition when healed (unless the healing removes the bleed).

Bleed effects do not stack. Only use the most severe effect.
