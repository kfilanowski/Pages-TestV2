A creature who is possessed has another soul inside their body that can exert control over the body. The amount of control can differ depending on the effect from just using their eyes, to taking control of the entire body.

In any case, the possessing creature usually leaves their body behind [[Stunned]] and vulnerable to attack, unless stated otherwise. The intruder can willingly stop possessing the creature and return to their own body as a free action.
