Lowers your current and maximum [[+HP]] by 1 for each stack. One point is removed with a day of [[Rest]] (or more quickly with [[+Medicine]]).

A creature with enough drain to decrease their max hp to 0 dies.

Some circumstances, such as when a [[healing]] effect also inflicts drain for a powerful effect, you can reasonably ignore the current HP reduction.
