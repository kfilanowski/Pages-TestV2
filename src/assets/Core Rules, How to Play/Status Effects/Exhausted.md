Functions as [[Fatigued|fatigue]], but it can only be removed by through [[Rest]].

While exhausted, doing anything that causes you to become fatigued will instead inflict [[drained]] 5. The most common way to gain the exhausted condition is to not rest for a night. Exhaustion is considered a [[permanent status]] effect.


## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Using Exhaustion</span>
Exhaustion is a stacking debuff that is intended to be the primary method you inflict general debuffs on the players when travelling through treacherous terrain, hustle and rush during [[overland travel]] or when they refuse to [[rest]].

Some of these things mention how they inflict exhaustion, but others don't. When they don't...
1. Try and think of a time frame (every time they do x, or every y days). 
2. Then have all characters roll [[Fortitude]] saves, deciding on a DC based on conditions (usually somewhere around 10-15). Characters that fail are exhausted.
3. Give characters that prepare effectively bonuses, like [[Advantage|adv]] for bringing copious amounts of water into deserts, or +2 for having a thick coat for the cold. Some factors may eliminate the need for a roll entirely. 
4. For every time they roll one of these saves, increase the DC by a few points.
