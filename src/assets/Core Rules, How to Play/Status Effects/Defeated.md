A defeated creature is [[Knocked Down]] and cannot do anything more than a weakly speak while at or below 0 HP. [[Main Character Status|Main characters]] may crawl at a rate of 10 feet per turn, or move at full speed when another creature uses [[Aid]]. 

When recovering from the defeated condition, a creature immediately loses the knocked down condition and instead becomes [[Dazed]] for 1 round. 

See [[Defeat]] for more details.
