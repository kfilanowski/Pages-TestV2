Creature loses [[Concentration]].

This creature is [[Knocked Down]], can take no actions, and cannot be awakened unless it takes damage, is subject to an offensive ability or an ally uses the [[Aid]] action to awaken them. The creature is also [[DD]] and suffers -10 [[+Perception]].

If the creature is asleep through natural means, it can use perception to wake up by noticing something amiss.
