An ability that reduces maximum [[Mana]]. Many features throughout the game allow for more powerful effects, but do so at the cost of mana burn. Unless otherwise specified, mana burn decreases only your maximum mana and is not an additional cost to an ability. For example, something like [[cure wounds]] costs 1 mana. You decided to mana burn to overheal, it still only costs 1 mana, but your max mana is also reduced by 1.

The default value for mana burn is 1. 

Mana burn can stack with itself, eventually reducing your max mana to 0. Having 0 max mana inflicts the mana starvation [[Affliction]].

An ability with no mana cost that has mana burn decreases your current mana by an equal value as well.

Bestiary creatures that don't use mana instead [[Drained|drain]] equal double to the amount they mana burn.
