You consider all creatures enemies. Every turn, roll a d10 to determine how you act. Many effects allow you to increase the dice roll with a will save. For confusion effects that last more than 1 round, each subsequent d10 roll gets 2 added to the result.

| Roll | Effect                                                                                        |
| ---- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1-2  | Attack nearest non-[[Companion]] creature once with a [[Simple Attack]] as a standard action. |
| 3-4  | Harm yourself for 2d8 Crush [[Damage]] as a [[Standard Action]].                              |
| 5-6  | Consume all movement to wander 15 feet in a random direction.                                 |
| 7-8  | Babble incoherently as a Standard Action.                                                     |
| 9-10 | Confusion ends.                                                                               |
The confusion result is the only thing the creature does on their turn, ie if you roll a 5 you wander and then end turn.
