Through loud noises or physical trauma the inner workings of a creatures auditory receptors have been damaged.

- You can hear things, but in very muddied and muffled forms, or your hearing is overwhelmed by a loud ringing sound. You cannot understand speech or determine the direction of sounds. This makes you [[Immunity|immune]] to any [[Languages|language]] effects or similar effects that require your comprehension to function.
- You have [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on [[+Perception]] checks and saves made against [[sonic]] effects.
- Whenever you cast a [[spell]], there you must make a DC 10 [[luck check]] or you mess up the words of power and the spell [[Fizzle|fizzles]].
