An effect with the concentration tag requires some degree of upkeep from the caster and typically has a duration. Concentration effects end if the caster is [[defeated]], [[dazed]], put [[Asleep]], or otherwise completely prevented from taking any actions. You can also cease concentration as an instant action.
If you cast a spell with concentration, you must hold the staff in order to maintain your concentration. Being disarmed of a staff you are using to concentrate ends the effect. You can normally only have one concentration effect at a time.
