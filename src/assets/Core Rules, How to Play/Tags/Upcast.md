Upcast functions differently depending on whether it is attached to a martial or magic technique. For martial techniques, you spend additional mana and treat it as a higher level technique for a greater effect.

Spells have universal thresholds where, if your [[Caster Level]] (CL) is high enough, you get bonus effects. These usually happen every 2, or every 4 caster levels, with some spells, they have a unique threshold that unlocks entirely new effects.
