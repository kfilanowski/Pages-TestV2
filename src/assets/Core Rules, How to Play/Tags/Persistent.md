Persistent Effects have an initial effect and an effect that persists over time. This type of effect cannot deal its persistent effect on the same round as its initial effect (usually). Persistent effects end preemptively if the user is [[Defeated]].

There are some effects, like the [[Skyman]]'s Elemental breath that have an <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">initial effect</span> that is different from the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">persistent effect</span>. Such effects usually happen if the enemy stays within the area at the end of their turn. This is a special exception to persistent tag not being able to deal damage multiple times in the same round.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Dev Note:</span>
Persistent effects that do the same things as their initial effect (like [[Wall of Fire]]) are not something you want happening multiple times a turn. Don't let players weasel their way into dealing double damage.
