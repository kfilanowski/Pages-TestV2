**Curse** effects inflict permanent debilitating conditions on creatures. Curses cannot be dispelled, but they all have some way to get rid of them. This usually requires some convoluted action most people wouldn't do unless prompted. Can be identified using the [[Identify Affliction or Curse (P)]]  feature in the [[+Medicine]] skill.

Many curses include visible marks on the creature, even if not explicitly stated in the ability description.
