Damage is a value that subtracts from a target's [[+HP]] or [[Poise]] when the effect takes place.

Damage is always assigned a type. The physical damage types are [[Crush]], [[Slash]], and [[Pierce]], and the elemental types are [[Force]], [[Zap]], [[Fire]], [[Frost]], [[Holy]], [[Necrotic]], [[Poison]] and [[Psychic]]. These are also [[tag]]s. Poise damage is its own separate thing.

If something deals multiple damage types, it usually adds both tags to the effect. For example, a flaming sword will deal Fire and Slash damage with its total damage.
