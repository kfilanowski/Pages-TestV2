A Ranged Attack is an [[Attack Roll]] made with ranged targeting. This means you can make the attack up to the listed range, not limited by the space you [[Threaten]]. These attacks can also be penalized due to [[Cover]]. Most physical Ranged Attacks have three listed ranges: [[Thrown]], [[Spatial Rend]] and [[long]].
Making a Ranged Attack [[AoO|Provokes]].

Abilities that function from a distance but do not use an Attack [[Roll]] are not Ranged Attacks.
