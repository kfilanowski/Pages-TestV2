An natural attack is an [[Attack Roll]] that is made with a part of a creatures body that naturally dangerous, such as claws or fangs. Exotic creatures have natural attacks that can be all kinds of things, like spitting thorns or tentacle whips.

Natural attacks are usually [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]], but can sometimes be [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]].

Natural attacks always have the [[Finesse]] property, letting them use [[DEX]] to attack and damage.
