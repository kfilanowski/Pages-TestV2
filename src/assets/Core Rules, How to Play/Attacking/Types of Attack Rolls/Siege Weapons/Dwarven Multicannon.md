Dwarven multicannons are incredibly feats of engineering that allow for rapid fire siege weapon attacks against groups of enemies with some severe limitations to offset the rapid damage output. Only 2 people are needed to upkeep a multicannon, with one maintaining a steady supply of cannonballs (the standard action) and one person calling the shots (the FRA). If infrastructure is not set up to automatically load the cannon, a creature can only load 3 cannonballs a turn. The cannon holds a total of 6.

The cannon itself has legs that keep it stable on the ground while its head rotates in a regular pattern in a circle on an axis. This axis is typically left untouched, allowing it to shoot in all directions. It can only shoot once in a direction each round (northeast, north, northwest, southwest, south, southeast). This makes it exceptional when surrounded in targets. The axis can be tilted as a standard action to accommodate for a [[High Ground]] or low ground position, but it limits the cannon to firing only 3 times per round in that general direction.

As with all [[+Siege Weapon|siege weapons]], all creatures beyond the first are treated as though taking the [[Aid]] action to boost attack.
