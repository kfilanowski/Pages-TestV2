Siege weapons are attacks that come in the form of enormous weapons of war. This includes [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]] like ballista, cannons and enormous rocks, as well as [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]] like battering rams.

Siege weapons are typically placed a tier above most forms of attack, and thus ignore many defensive abilities like [[Buckler|parry]], [[Wind Wall]] or [[+Shield Actions|shield actions]].
