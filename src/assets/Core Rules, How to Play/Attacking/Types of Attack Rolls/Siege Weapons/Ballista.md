Since ballista are so time consuming to load, it usually requires a team of 4 people to fire a shot each round. 1 person to load the bolt and aim, 2 to crank the bolt back and 1 to call the shots.

The three people loading are treated as taking the [[Aid]] action to increase the attack bonus of the person calling the shots, essentially granting the maximum +6 bonus with a full team. Once loaded, the person calling the shots makes an [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Attack Roll]].
