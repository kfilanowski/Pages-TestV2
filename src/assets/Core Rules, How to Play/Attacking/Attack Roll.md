Attack rolls are any type of harmful effect targeting a creature that requires rolling. This can be spells, swords or trying to push your foe off a cliff.

Most attacks assume you use a weapon, a [[Natural Attack]] or an [[Unarmed Attack]].

Default:

d20+ (lvl) +(ability score) +/- (circumstances)

In general AC is not a zero sum endeavor. If you successfully meet the AC number, you deal full damage. if you roll 10 higher than the AC number, you [[Crit]]. If you roll under the AC number, but not by more than 5, you get a partial hit as you [[graze]] your opponent with your attack.

Generally speaking, you can only make a single attack with your standard action, but creatures with [[Multiattack]], and special cases like [[Classes/Fighter/Fighter]] or [[Martial Artist]], can make multiple on their turn.
