Special attacks allow for more versatility in play style. Weapons, class abilities, [[Technique Rules|techniques]] and
feats can grant these special attacks. Special attacks have a global cooldown of 1 round, meaning they can usually only be performed once per turn.

Special attacks universally have the [[martial]] tag. 

A special attack that does not originate from a technique also has the [[recharge]] tag, with a cooldown of 1 minute (unless otherwise stated). The most common form is the [[Base Special Attacks]]. When used out of combat (for the utility), you can use them an unlimited number of times. With no cooldown.

Some techniques will say something to the effect of "does damage" and then goes on. This means the damage of a held weapon.
