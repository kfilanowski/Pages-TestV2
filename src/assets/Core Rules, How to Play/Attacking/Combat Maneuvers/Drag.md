Grab onto a hold of a creature and immediately pull them adjacent to you. You can then move up to 20 ft while holding onto them, and they move into any space you move into during this movement (essentially following behind you during this movement).

This is reduced to 10 feet if they are larger than you and you do not possess enough [[Carry Weight]] to drag them (typically you can drag double your carry weight). 

You can choose any space within your natural reach to place the creature in at the end of your drag (both pulling adjacent and positioning a creature around you count against the distance you can drag).

Your movement is [[Elusive]] against the target of your drag.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special</span> - You must use at least one prehensile limb to initiate a drag on a creature, unless your weapon has the [[CM]] (Drag) property.
