Simple attacks are only rolling attack and damage, typically with no special effects tied to them. The most common form of simple attack is an [[AoO|Attack of Opportunity]].

This terminology is important to distinguish since many features will limit attacks by making them simple attacks as opposed to [[Special Attack|special attacks]]

Special attacks allow for more versatility in play style. Unique weapons, class abilities, techniques and feats can grant these special attacks. Special attacks can usually only be performed once per turn.
