Critical hits occur when you roll 10 higher than a creature's [[AC]]. Crits deal 1.5x damage.

Its also worth noting here that all damage multipliers are additive.

For example, [[Groups|group]] type enemies like swarms takes 1.5x damage from [[AoE]]. If you [[Base Special Attacks|cleave]] and crit, you deal 2x damage.
