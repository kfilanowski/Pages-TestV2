Graze is an attack that is not a complete miss. When you roll below the targets [[AC]], but not by more than 5, then the attack does half damage. A graze does not apply any [[on-hit]] effects, like [[Bleeding|bleed]].

Unless otherwise specified, a graze is considered a miss.
