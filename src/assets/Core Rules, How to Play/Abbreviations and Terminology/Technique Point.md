A technique point gives you access to the next tier of a technique tree equal to the number of points you have invested in it, from 1 to 6. Most classes get technique points on odd levels, and all player characters get technique points on even levels.

You are capped at how many technique points you can put into a tree by your [[LvL]]. Level 1 characters can only have access to tier 1 tech, level 3 get tier 2 and so on, as presented in graph below.

| Technique Level (and points required) | Character Level |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| 1                                     | 1               |
| 2                                     | 3               |
| 3                                     | 5               |
| 4                                     | 7               |
| 5                                     | 9               |
| 6                                     | 11              |
