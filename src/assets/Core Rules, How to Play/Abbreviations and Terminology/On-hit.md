On-hit effects only happen when you successfully match or exceed a creature's [[AC]] and deal damage to a creature (AKA it is not negated by [[DR]]/[[Resistance]]).
The effect does not occur on a graze.

When a creature has an on-hit effect that has a saving throw attached to it like the [[Grounding]], weapon quality, the creature makes the save only once if they are hit multiple times. Each application beyond the first applies a -2 penalty to the save.
