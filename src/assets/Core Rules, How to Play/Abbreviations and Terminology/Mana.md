Mana is the resource that enables all sorts of things. For the purposes of this explanation, mana is primary used to fuel [[Technique Rules|techniques]]. It also fuels mana class abilities and is a crafting component. Mana itself has value.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Mana Basics</span>
In order to use a technique, you must spend mana equal to the tier of technique. Cantrips have an effective tier of 0 and do not cost mana. While not in immediate danger (such as combat or a chase), you regenerate 1 mana per turn, or 10 mana per minute.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Technique Cap</span>
Techniques have many different action types, and so that means that you could theoretically have extremely powerful turns using high level [[buff]]s as a swift action, powerful [[Special Attack|special attacks]] and free action techniques like [[Focus Punch]]. To prevent this, a cap is introduced. The cap is (your highest level technique+1).

There is <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">not</span> a cap on how much mana you can spend in a turn. Go wild with class features, magic items and metamagic feats in the [[+Magic Feats]] section.
