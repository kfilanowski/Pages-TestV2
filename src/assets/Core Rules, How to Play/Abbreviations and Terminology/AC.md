Armor Class (AC) is the measure of how difficult it is to hit you. Targets must meet or exceed your AC with an [[Attack Roll]] to [[Damage]] you.

By default, your starting AC is 10, and you add your [[LvL]] and [[Armor]]. [[DEX]] also gets added, but see [[Armor]] for how dex interacts with armor. It is also adjusted based on [[Circumstance]].
