Reach determines how far a creature can make [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]] from. Some features and equipment, like using a weapon with the reach property, increases your reach for attacks, but it does not increase your reach for other features.

Natural reach determines what you character can reach without the aid of any magic or extraordinary feature. You can interact with objects within your natural reach. Any attacks you make without added features are assumed to have this natural reach. See [[Size]] for how it affects your reach.
