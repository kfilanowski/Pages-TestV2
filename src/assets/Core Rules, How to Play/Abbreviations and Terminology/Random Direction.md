Some effects in the game launch creatures, but do so in unpredictable ways, like [[Temperamental Tempest]] and [[Rift Bolt]]. To simplify things, I will present a unified method to help you determine what that direction is. Because the direction isn't taking into account terrain, it could very well move a creature directly into a wall. All directions are valid.

Nick Nacks is expected to take place on a grid, either with hexes or squares. As such there are already a limited number of directions. For hexes that is 6, for squares that is 8. For hexes, roll a d6. For squares, roll a d8. Rolls of 1 should be directly up, with each space moving clockwise being 1 higher. 

Since most of the time horizontal movement is all that matters, directions pushing up or down should not be considered unless you're feeling wild.
