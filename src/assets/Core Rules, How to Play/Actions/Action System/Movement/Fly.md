Fly [[Land Speed|Speeds]] allow [[Movement]] in any direction, treating spaces moving directly into the air as [[Difficult Terrain]].
- A creature that is [[Entangled]] or [[Grounded]] cannot continue flight.
- A creature that takes [[+HP]] damage from an [[Attack Roll]] or [[AoE]] effect while flying receives [[Clumsy]] 1 until they land, at which point the clumsy is removed.
- Starting to fly is a [[Swift Action]] that [[AoO|Provokes]] as if leaving a [[Threaten|Threatened]] space. Some mobility effects that place you into the air like [[Sky Uppercut]] or [[Extreme Speed]] can reasonably bypass this action requirement.
