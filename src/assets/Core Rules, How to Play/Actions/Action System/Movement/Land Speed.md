Land speed refers to the generic speed most creatures use to walk around.

Default land speed is 30 ft. 
[[+Races|race]], [[+Class]], Equipment, and [[Status]] can change this.
Some actions grant extra speed.

You may have other forms of movement that manifest in terms of different Speeds - you may use multiple forms of movement on the same turn, but only up to the maximum amount for the given speed. As an example, if you have a [[Fly]] 40 and a land speed of 30, you may walk 30 feet and then fly another 10.
