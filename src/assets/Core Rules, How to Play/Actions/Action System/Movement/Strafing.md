Strafing is any movement in a [[Threaten|threatened]] space that is not a direct path closer to the target. Usually this means moving to get into [[Flanking]], or trying to position yourself in a better spot to [[Shove]] for instance. Strafing [[AoO|Provokes]] just like exiting threatened spaces.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Dev Note:</span> The purpose of this rule is to get melee characters leeway when moving up to creatures, but discouraging free movement around someone. It would be pretty silly if you could move 10,000 feet in circles around someone, but then step away from them and only THEN could they attack you.
