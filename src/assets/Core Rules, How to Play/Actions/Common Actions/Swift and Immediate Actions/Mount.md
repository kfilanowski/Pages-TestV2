Hop onto a willing creature's back, using their movement instead of your own.

If you start combat mounted, your position in [[Initiative]] matches theirs.

You can also dismount as a immediate action. You subtract whatever movement your mount used on their turn from your own for the round you dismount.

If you are inflicted with any form of [[Forced Movement]] that knocks you more than 5 ft away from your mount, you are automatically dismounted. If your mount is knocked more than 5 ft away from you,  you can choose to either hold on tight or hop off (requiring an immediate action). If your mount is [[Knocked Down]] or [[poise]] broken, you are set to 1 poise.
