Provide one of the following bonuses to an ally within your reach for 1 round (up to 3 times):

+2 to [[Attack]] 
+2 to [[+Saving Throw|Saves]]
+2 to [[AC]]

Alternatively, remove one stack of the following conditions: [[Clumsy]], [[Shaken]], [[Sickened]]. It can also remove the [[charmed]] or [[Knocked Down]] conditions. You can also use aid to [[breakout]] any ally trapped by objects.

You can aid a defeated creature when not in combat, bringing them to 1 hp. Extreme circumstances may require more assistance to restore a creature particularly out of it (such as medicine or magic).
