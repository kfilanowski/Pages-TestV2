Your ability to turn tides and benefit from unlikely outcomes.

- You get a number of [[Boons]] equal to your LUK score 
- You add LUK to [[Luck Check]]s. 
- You can add LUK to a [[Roll]] as an [[Immediate Action]] (that did not already add LUK). You must roll to add luck, so you cannot add it when you take 10 (such as when using skills for income) or take 20


- Negative LUK give you a bane per point of negative luck
- Your [[DC]] decreases by your negative luck
- Subtracted from luck checks
