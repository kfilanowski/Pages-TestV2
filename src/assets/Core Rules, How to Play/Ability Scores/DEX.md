Your agility, hand-eye coordination, and general sneakiness.

- You add DEX to your [[Reflex]] Save. 
- Depending on your choice in armor, you can add your dexterity to your [[AC]], but you always add it to [[Poise]]
- You add dexterity to [[Attack]] and [[Damage]] with ranged weapons and light weapons (but not at the same time as strength)
***
- Negative DEX subtracts from these values.
