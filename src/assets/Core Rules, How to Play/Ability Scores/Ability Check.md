An ability check is something that can reasonably be covered by an ability score, but cannot be associated with any particular skill. For example if you forget something important out of character, or if out of character you aren't thinking about something, the GM might have you roll an [[INT]] check to see if your character recalls it.

In such scenarios, you roll and add the relevant ability score and anything that can be added to ability checks.

[[Luck Check]] is its own separate thing that has other factors that can influence it.
