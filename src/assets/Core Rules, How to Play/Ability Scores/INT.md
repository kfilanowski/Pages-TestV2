Your knowledge, magical expertise, and logic capacity.

- You gain an additional point of [[Memory]] per point of INT
- You gain an additional [[Skill Proficiency]] per point of INT
- You gain two additional [[Languages]] per point of INT
- You gain 1 [[Mana]] for each point in intelligence
- You add your INT to you [[Caster Level]]
***
- Negative int subtracts from these values. In particular, you lose max mana equal to your negative int.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Intelligence and Sentience</span>
A creature is considered sentient if it has at least -2 intelligence. These creatures typically do not speak, but can understand at least 1 language.

Animal intelligence is -3 intelligence. Creatures with animal intelligence only understand simple commands (if anything). They are usually limited to their instinctual urges.

Mindless creatures possess no will of their own. They act completely on instinct.
