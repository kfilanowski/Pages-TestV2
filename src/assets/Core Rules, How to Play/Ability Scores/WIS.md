Your faith, decision making, habits, diligence, and discipline.

- You add WIS to your [[Will]] Save. 
- You add your wisdom to [[+Perception]], the skill all creatures get for free
- You can pray to your god for guidance (see below for details). You can use [[prayer]] a number of times per day equal to your wisdom bonus. You can also use it for [[Invoking Deities]].
- Positive wisdom increases GBP gains, giving you [[deific boons]] boons earlier and in higher quantities
***
- Negative WIS subtracts from these values
- Negative wisdom represents a complete lack of faith in most gods or being rejected by them, you cannot gain GBP (unless in service of Frogg Saran)
