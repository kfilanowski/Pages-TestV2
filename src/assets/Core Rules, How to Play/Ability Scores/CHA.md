Your leadership and personal magnetism.

- Positive charisma enhances your [[Companion]] creatures. Any creature that takes a companion slot gains your CHA to their [[Attack]] (unless you are [[Defeated]])
- With positive charisma, you get a [[+Teamwork Feats|teamwork feat]]
- For every point of CHA, you can grant another creature one of your teamwork feats. To do this, you must spend 1 hour doing training exercises with the creatures you intend to team up with. (allies can't use while you are defeated)
***
- You lose a companion slot with negative CHA.
- Negative CHA reduces your companion's Accuracy
- You cannot benefit from teamwork feats with negative charisma
