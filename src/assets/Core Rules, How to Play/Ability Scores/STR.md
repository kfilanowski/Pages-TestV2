Your fortitude, athleticism, and endurance.

- You add STR to your [[Fortitude]] Save. 
- You add STR to your [[Poise]]
- You add +100 [[Carry Weight]] for each point of strength
- Add +1 [[Inventory|inventory slot]] per point of str
- Every point of strength increases your [[Jump]] distance by 5 ft.
***
- Negative STR subtracts from your Fortitude Saves.
- Negative STR halves your Carry Weight.
- Subtract -1 inventory slot per negative point of str
