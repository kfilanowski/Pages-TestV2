As a standard action you can cure creatures of status ailments to a creature within reach. You can remove all [[sickened]] stacks, [[Bleeding|bleed]] and [[blurred vision]]. The DC of removing any of these conditions is the DC of the ability that inflicted them. If they have no DC, then assume the class/offense trait DC. When you succeed in providing first aid to heal a condition, the creature heals 1 HP ([[Healing]]).

Performing first aid without a first aid [[Tools|kit]] imposes a -5 penalty.
