Common modifiers to make the task more difficult can include…
- The surfaces you are interacting with are wet or moving.
- You are swimming in substances other than water that are more grimy or viscous\
- Extra reinforcements to enhance the structural soundness and prevent breakage

Common modifiers to make the task less difficult can include...
- You are climbing at a >90 degree angle, or the surface was designed to be climbed\
- Flotation devices (driftwood, floaties)
- Age or fundamental flaws/weaknesses in the structure

How much of something you want to destroy can also influence how difficult it is. If you want to tear a brick out of a wall, that would make breaking the stone easier. If you want to break down the entire wall, that is much more difficult.
