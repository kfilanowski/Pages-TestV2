aliases:
  - detect intrusive presence
---
You can use insight to determine when a creature is under duress from a spell or some other kind of effect that has a lasting impact on their mind, such as if they are [[charmed]] or under the effects of [[Possess Body]]. You must actively search for this presence by speaking with a creature for 1 minute. Roll insight vs the DC of the effect. If the effect has no DC, assume 12+inflicting creature's level.
