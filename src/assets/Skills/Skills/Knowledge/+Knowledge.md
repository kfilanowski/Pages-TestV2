All knowledge uses [[INT]].
What you know. Knowledge is broken up into many different skills based on subject and each one has deals with the [[+Monster Lore]] of specific creature types. Monster lore isn’t its own skill, but it has its own functions separate from the rest of individual knowledge skills. If a subject or creature falls into multiple categories, then players can choose whichever they like more, but an individual character can't roll multiple checks with different skills.
# <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">ALL knowledge requires proficiency to roll.</span>
