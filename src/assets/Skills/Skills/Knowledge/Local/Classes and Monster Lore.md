Your GM is inevitably going to pit you up against [[fighter]]s and [[Beast Trainer]]s and all kinds of other creatures with classes.

If you succeed, you learn the creatures level and everything about its [[+Class]] and [[archetype]].

If you exceed this DC by 5, you learn everything about the creature's fighting style, such as feats and [[Technique Rules|techniques]] they often use.
