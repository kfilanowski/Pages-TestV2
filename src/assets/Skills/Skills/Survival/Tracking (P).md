You can attempt to follow footprints, broken branches, scent trails and other disturbed natural elements to figure out where a creature has been and where it’s going. With ideal conditions you can follow trails made days or even weeks ago (if you don't believe me, look at dino footprint fossils :^). The longer it's been since the creature has been there and the less ideal the conditions, the higher the DC.
