You can store light weapons in all kinds of places. As a swift action you can draw up to two concealed weapon to attack a creature with. If they fail a perception check vs your sleight of hand, they are [[DD]] this turn. 

If you're in a crowded area, or in some kind of standoff where you aren't quite fighting your enemy yet, you can even use this skill as a replacement for stealth to surprise your enemy. The enemy must be aware of you in order use a concealed weapon, otherwise it is stealth.
