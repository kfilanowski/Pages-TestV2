aliases:
  - pick lock
---
Open locked objects with a DC dependent on the complexity of the lock. Mages and very skilled locksmiths might have nearly impossible locks while common doors are child's play. The more difficult the DC, the longer it takes to open a lock, with an upper limit of 30 seconds. Failing a check has no consequences unless time is short or there are other security measures in place, allowing you to [[Taking 20|take 20]].

Picking locks without thieves' [[Tools]] imposes a -5 penalty.
