As a swift action, roll deception against a creature within reach. DC (10+target's [[LvL]]) or (10+target's [[+Insight]] bonus) whichever is higher. If successful, your opponent is [[DD]] until the end of your turn, or until after your first attack is resolved. 

This is to allow offensive (bigger attack, no parrying etc) and defensive (avoid AoO, deny retaliation strikes) uses for feint.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note - </span>
It is intended that both attacks from [[Dual Wielding]] benefit from feinting.
