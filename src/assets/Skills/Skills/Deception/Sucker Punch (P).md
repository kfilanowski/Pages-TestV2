If you are in some kind of standoff where you aren't quite fighting your enemy yet, you can use this skill as a replacement for stealth to surprise your enemy with a melee attack. The enemy must be aware of you in order sucker punch, otherwise it is stealth. Doesn't actually require unarmed a strike.

Certain situations where you are not able to get close to your aggressors, such as having bows pointed at you on from a garrison above you, make sucker punching impossible.
