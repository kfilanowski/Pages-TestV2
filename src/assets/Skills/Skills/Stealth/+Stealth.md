Moving around while remaining unseen. If someone is looking for you in your immediate vicinity and you don't want to be seen, you roll stealth. Uses [[DEX]].

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special: Stealth and size</span>
Size provides no numerical benefit or penalty.  The only thing that size effects relative to stealth is your ability to gain cover: Larger creatures have a harder time figuring out places to hide, while smaller creatures find it very easy.
