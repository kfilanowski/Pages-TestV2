For clarity's sake...
Everything written in <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Green</span> is a skill.
Everything written in <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Red</span> is a combat talent you can unlock under that skill.
Everything written in <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Blue</span> is a utility talent you can unlock under that skill.
Abilities that are preceded by a number have a level requirement.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Prayer (Knowledge Religion)
</span>
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Protection Prayers</span> [[Su]] - Gain an additional point of [[prayer]] and 5 [[Deific Boons|GBP]] in a god of your choice, or [[Frogg Saran]] if your [[WIS]] is -1 or lower.
  When you would harmed by an attack or fail a saving throw, you can choose to negate the attack or pass the save by spending prayer as an immediate action.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Piety (Su) </span>- Gain an additional point of prayer and 5 [[Deific Boons|GBP]] in a god of your choice, or [[Frogg Saran]] if your [[WIS]] is -1 or lower.
  By spending prayer as a swift action, you can give yourself a +2 bonus to all d20 rolls until the start of your next turn.

You must still be able to provide odd specific ingredients, such as the tongue in a [[Tongues Potion]].
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">(5) Chug</span> - You can drink a [[+Potions|potions]] once per turn as a free action.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Fishing (Profession Fisher)</span>
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Reel 'em In</span> - You can use a fishing rod to finely manipulate objects. You can manipulate anything you could normally carry, replacing any [[Thievery]] check with profession (fisher). Range 30 ft.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Patience</span> - You get a +2 bonus to attack when you attack as part of a [[Prepare]] action.
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">(4) Fish are Friends (Su)</span> - Using the power of fishing, you can convert aquatic creatures into friends. By catching a specific type of fish with a DC 30 profession (fisher) check, you can get a bonus to an +2 associated skill check when using the fish as a tool, as though they successfully used the assist other action. Unique fish might be able to assist in other ways, this is meant to apply to anything, within reason. This fish transcends its fishy existence to survive outside of water to assist you.  It cannot be attacked, but it can be stolen. This kills the fish. A few examples of weird fish upgrades below…
Hammerhead - Smithing, construction, you name it
Pufferfish (or other poisonous) - For use in creating poisons
Monkfish - Good for meditation
Archerfish - You can trip with your ranged attacks
Sharks - Underwater tracking
Bass - Good for dropping sick beats
Seahorse - Makes for a good underwater mount

- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Axe Man </span>- You gain a [[climb]] speed equal to your [[land speed]]. If you wield an axe, this speed doubles.

Sentient trees are immune to treebane.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Mining (Profession Miner)</span>
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Shaft Mining</span> - As a standard action, you can mine a 5x5x5 ft cube, effectively allowing you to burrow underground. You can only use this ability on unworked stone/earth etc. You must wield a pickaxe to do so.
  Whenever you use profession (miner) to make money, you're essentially [[Taking 20]].
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Break In</span> - You can use [[+Profession]] (miner) as a substitute for [[+Athletics]] to break things with [[Brute Force]] or [[Breakout]] as long as you wield a pickaxe. You get a +4 bonus to do so.
- (<span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">8) Vibrations (Su)</span> - As a standard action, you can slam a pickaxe into the ground to gain [[+Blindsense]] ([[Tremorsense]] 30 ft) for 1 round. This allows you to see the general shape of things like buildings and caves, even through walls and floors.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Construction (Knowledge Engineering)</span>
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Bridge the Gap (Su)</span> - While you wield a hammer, you have a “bridge speed” equal to your [[Land Speed]]that lets you quickly nail together planks and walk along them. This bridge is solid enough for creatures to walk on and lasts until the end of your turn, or the start of your next turn if you want your allies to follow. Costs 2g per foot bridged (30ft=60g).  You can continue the bridge indefinitely, even as previous sections fall, effectively ignoring gravity, but you must at least start attached to something.
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Affordable Housing</span> - With an hour of work, you can build a makeshift house with natural materials like dirt, wood and stone. You can remove T1 [[Afflictions]] with general cures by resting in such a house.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">(3) Comfort</span> - By creating a home worth at least 2k gold, you can get a boost by resting in this home. You and any allies get temp HP equal to half your skill roll. By spending the gold again, you can make home improvements to improve roll again to gain more temp hp.
  You can spend 40k gold to take 20 and maximize your comfort.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Hunter  (Survival)</span>
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Speak to Me (Su)</span> - You gain the [[Wild Empathy]] feat. You are under the effects of [[Speak with Animals]].
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">(3) Box Trap</span> - With 10 minutes of preparation you can set a box trap for a specific creature, or a type of creature.  You leave a trail of desirable stuff to a box with a stick propping it up. The creature makes a perception check (DC 15+your [[+Survival]] bonus). If they succeed then they notice the unusual and untrustworthy setup. If they fail they are trapped and [[Entombed]] in the box for one minute. Creatures with 0 or more [[INT]] get [[advantage]] on their perception check, and can escape the box using any means at their disposal. Athletics DC to [[Breakout]] is equal to the perception check to notice.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Agility  (Acrobatics)</span>
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Boost</span> - You can [[dash]] as a swift action. You can dash as a free action, but it makes you [[fatigued]].
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Endurance Training</span> - +10 [[Land Speed]].
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Parkour </span>- You can [[jump]] as a free action 1/turn. When you jump, you ignore [[fall damage]] from 60 ft of falling. When you take fall damage, you take 1 damage per 10 ft you fall.
- <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">(4) Shortcut (Su)</span> - You can squeeze through the tightest of spaces and the smallest of cracks. Even with only a centimeter of space, you can slide through it. You can even comfortably sit in spaces like these indefinitely. You can't effectively navigate while squished in this way, making it only usable to get through very thin gaps, like doors and windows.

- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Dubstep Ignite</span> [[recharge]] - When a creature survives one of your attacks with 5 or less hp, you know. You can spend an instant action to deal 5 [[true damage]] to them. Usable 1/minute.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">(5) Solves Everything </span>[[Sp]] - You gain [[fire blast]] as a 1/day innate technique. You increase its range to 120 ft. At level 9 this is usable at will.
