The Hero is someone who is born for adventure. They have an incredibly diverse skill set to accomplish their goals. The quests of the world seem to gravitate towards them, making sure they are never idle.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Combat Style</span>
Choose one of the following 3 options to specialize in...

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Melee</span> - You gain 10 hp per level instead of 8. Gain [[martial specialization]]. Choose a martial technique tree. You gain a technique point in it at level 1 and every odd level after. You also gain a [[Multiattack]].
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Multiattack</span> - Make 2 [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ranged</span> - Gain martial specialization. Choose a martial technique tree. You gain a technique point in it at level 1 and every odd level after. You also gain a multiattack.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Multiattack</span> - Make 2 [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Magic</span> - Gain [[magical specialization]]. Choose 2 magical technique trees. You gain a technique point them at level 1 and every odd level after. Increase your memory by 2.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Home Teleport</span> ([[Su]]) ([[Teleportation]]) ([[recharge]])
By spending a week in a specific location, such as a house, clearing, or guild hall, you can designate it as home. By spending 1 minute concentrating on a ritual, you can teleport yourself and up to 8 creatures in physical contact with you back home.

Requires 1 week to recharge. If the ritual is interrupted, this ability does not require recharge. Does not require line of sight.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - All Skill</span>
Gain 3 [[Skill Proficiency|skill proficiencies]]. You gain the [[Advantage]] on skill checks you are proficient in.
Every level you also gain a skill proficiency from the list below. Each of these is associated with a particular Runescape skill. You must be proficient in a skill in order to gain its skill unlocks.

At this level and levels 3, 5, 7, 9 an 11 gain a skill unlock from the combat skills.
At this level and levels 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10. gain a skill unlock from the utility skills.

Skills: [[+Profession]] (Arson, wood cutting, mining, fishing, cooking, farming), [[+Knowledge]] ([[+Religion]], [[Arcana]], [[Engineering]], [[Alchemy]]), [[+Survival]], [[+Acrobatics]]
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Quest!</span>
Heroes are called to adventure, but there are a handful of quests that are rites of passage for true heroes. More information can be found in the quest subsection.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Ditto</span>
Every hero proudly presents their finest victory in the form of a pet ditto: An ooze creature that can mimic the appearance of a creature that the hero has defeated in battle. It functions as a familiar. It knows a number of transformations equal to your level. If you choose to teach it a new form and it has no more slots, it forgets a previous one permanently. New transformations must at least match the level of its highest known transformation. It starts play only knowing 1 transformation of a level 0 creature. A ditto can never transform into a creatures with class levels.

Your ditto becomes a level 0 copy, losing all defense traits (outside of base stats), offense traits (outside of its attacks) and feats. Its stats are adjusted to be a level 0 creature, effectively subtracting its [[LvL]] from things like AC, saves, poise etc. Its hp is 20. Its size is always tiny. It can only communicate in methods the creature it transforms into could.

If it aids you in combat, it gains the [[companion]] tag. You can also use your familiar to do a handful of other useful things.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Spying</span> ([[scrying]]) - You can sense through your companion at will.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Assistant</span> - Your companion can use the aid action to assist you for any skill checks you are proficient in.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">To My Side</span> (teleportation) - You can concentrate for 3 rounds to teleport your companion to your side.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Gear Swap</span>
You can choose to also swap any worn equipment, such as armor or magic items, when you take the swap gear action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Combat Style+</span>
Gain a new upgrade based on the combat style you chose at level 1, or choose a different combat style you did not choose at level 1. If you choose a different level 1 combat style, its boons are retroactive, granting you technique points/[[+HP]] you would have gained.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Melee</span> - As a swift action, you can enter one of 3 stances as a [[buff]]. They last until you gain another buff and you can swap between them whenever you like.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Aggressive </span>- Deal 10 poise damage [[On-hit]] with [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]], or 20 with 2h weapons.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Accurate</span> - Get a +2 bonus to [[attack]] with melee attacks.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Defensive</span> - Get a +2 bonus to [[AC]]

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ranged</span> - As a swift action, you can enter one of 3 stances as a buff. They last until you gain another buff and you can swap between them whenever you like.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Long Range</span> - Increase your range by 15 on all ranged attacks.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Accurate </span>- Get a +2 bonus to attack with ranged attacks.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Rapid</span> - You can make a [[Simple Attack]] as a swift action.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Magic</span> - As a swift action, you can enter one of 3 stances as a buff. They last until you gain another buff and you can swap between them whenever you like.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Long Range</span> - The listed range of your spells increases by 10.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Accurate </span>- Get a +2 bonus to attack with [[Spell Attack|spell attacks]]
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Rapid</span> - You gain the [[Quicken Spell]] trait, usable with any [[+Cantrips|cantrip]], but it costs 2 [[Mana]].
