A Fiend Shifter is someone who has stolen the secrets to the powers of [[Fiends]], slowly letting this power overtake them. They only grow into a more terrifying monster capable of combating even the most terrifying the fiendish realms have to offer with the very same power they wield.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
Your technique specialization varies by [[Archetype]].
You gain 1 technique point this level and 2 levels after.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - The First Seal</span>
You add fiend to your type, in addition to any others you possess.

As a free action, deal 1 [[true damage]] to yourself to break the first seal. This gives you bonuses based off your archetype. This lasts for 1 minute, or until you exit combat (or something similarly strenuous), whichever is longer.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Shifter Claws</span>
You possess claws on your hands that you can use to make [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]]. They deal 2d8 slashing damage if your hands are empty, or d10 if they are not. Any other natural attacks that you gain through this class share this damage, but often with a different damage type. These attacks can be used interchangeably with claws.

Your claws share the properties of a single weapon in your possession, such as magic enchantment, a magic focus' ability to cast spells, or special attacks. You can bond to a new weapon with a 1 minute ritual requiring concentration and contact with the weapon.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Multiattack</span>
2 claw attacks.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Fiendish Growth</span>
Gain a fiendish growth from your chosen archetype, or the universal list. Get another one at level 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - One of Us</span> [[Su]] [[Polymorph|poly, true]]
The physical transformation into a fiend shifter is a dramatic one that can draw unnecessary attention. You gain a specific form reminiscent of your original form that you can revert back to as a standard action. Breaking the seal or using a feature gained through fiendish growth ends this disguise.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1- Skill</span>
You get [[+Planes]] as a bonus skill with a +4 bonus.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Deific Boons</span>
You start play with 10 [[Deific Boons|GBP]] for any gods that are [[Demon Lords]], unless your [[WIS]] is -1 or lower.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Telepathy (Su)</span>
You can speak directly into the minds of other creatures you can see. A creature must have an intelligence score of at least -2 in order to be telepathically communicated with. A creature can telepathically project its thoughts to several creatures in the nearby vicinity, even if it cannot see them. Once a creature initiates a telepathic conversation, the other creature can communicate back using telepathy. The creature that initiated the telepathic conversation can choose to end it whenever they wish.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Hell and Back</span> [[Sp]] [[recharge]]
You gain [[Hell and Back]] as an [[Innate Technique]], usable 1/day.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Second Seal (Su)</span>
You break the second seal, improving the ability that you gained at first level.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Shadow Realm (Su)</span>
You can banish a willing or helpless creature to [[The Demon Realm]]. The creature is unable to return through any means other than a naturally existing portal. The Fiend Shifter can telepathically communicate with banished creatures. They can also stun themselves to observe them, as though floating overhead.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Final Seal (Su)</span>
You break the final seal, perfecting the ability that you gained at first level.
