You form a close bond with a beast companion and mold your fighting style around fighting in tandem with it. You gain special abilities that allow you to establish a closer bond with them. Fighting individually you are fairly weak, but together you can unleash powerful combo attacks.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
You have [[magical specialization]] and [[martial specialization]].
You gain 2 [[Technique Point|technique points]] at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after.
These technique points can be used on the [[Shroomlette]] and [[Techniques/Martial Techniques/Pupper/Pupper]] trees.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Beast Companion</span> [[companion]]
You gain the service of a specific animal to aid you on your journey. It grows more powerful as you do. More rules on beast companions in the [[Beast Companion]] section.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Tandem Strike</span>
When you take the attack action, your beast companion can also use its attack action to attack the same creature. You both get +2 to [[Attack]]. This bonus also applies if you make an [[AoO]] against the same creature. This attack counts as one attack for the purposes of applying [[DR]]. Some forms of offensive actions that don't require an attack roll, such as maintaining a [[Grapple]], can reasonably also grant this boon to the other member of the duo.

When you use this feature and one strike is enough to defeat the creature, the other is consumed and you cannot use your companion's strike on another.

All special attacks gained through this class deal an additional d6 per odd LV dmg if you and your beast companion both land an attack using tandem strike. You cannot combine these special attacks with other special attacks, even if your companion is the one performing the "special" part of the attack.
If one of your attacks is a [[Crit]], then you crit this additional damage as well.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Teamwork, Dreamwork</span>
Gain a [[+Teamwork Feats|teamwork feat]]. Your beast companion shares all of your teamwork feats.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Specialized Training</span>
Select one of the following options that will shape your companion. 
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Mount</span> - Beast companion grows to size large. You can ride bareback (no saddle) without issue. 
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Guard</span> - Companion gains the [[Bodyguard]] feat.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Watch</span> - Companion gains the [[Ready for Anything]] feat.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Lucky </span>- Companion gains +1 [[LUK]]
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Druidic</span>
You learn the secret [[Languages|language]], druidic. In text form it can only be seen by other creatures that know druidic. You can essentially write secret messages by touching your finger to any natural object (typically boulders and trees) and writing. Any message written in this way can be erased by a creature that knows druidic with ease.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Feat</span>
Gain the [[Convenient Companion]] feat.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Deific Boons</span>
You start play with 10 [[Deific Boons|GBP]] for the [[Earth Mother]], unless your [[WIS]] is -1 or lower.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Nature Stride</span>
When traveling through natural terrain that restricts your movement, such as thick snow or dense jungle, you and your beast companion's speed are not affected. All allies that can see you or your beast companion also gain this benefit. After not seeing either of you for a few minutes, your allies lose this benefit.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3 - Beast Trainer Bond</span>
You can speak with your beast companion as though you shared a language. Other creatures cannot understand you unless they can speak with animals.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Adaptation</span> [[Mana Burn]]
You can mana burn any amount to grant an equal number of [[Polymorph List|adaptation points]] to your beast companion indefinitely. When you rest, this adaptation fades and you must reapply it.
