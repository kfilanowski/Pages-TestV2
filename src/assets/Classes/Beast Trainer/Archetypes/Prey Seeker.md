[[Beast Trainer]] [[Archetype]].

Prey seekers use beast companions to harass their opponents, keeping them at a distance so you can hit them from afar. Your companion can rush back to your aid when needed.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Protector</span>
Your movement is [[Elusive]] against creatures your beast companion threaten.

Your beast companion gains the following special attack.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: To My Side</span> - The beast companion can move 30 ft closer to you and attack a creature threatening you. If its current speed is less than 30 ft, this special attack's additional movement decreases to that reduced speed.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
You gain a technique point in the [[Marksman]] tree at this level and every two levels after.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Special Attack: Baited Arrow</span> ([[healing]])
Attach a tasty treat to your next ranged attack. On hit, apply a treat. Your beast companion gains [[Seeking]] against the creature for 1 minute. Your beast companion heals (3+[[LvL]]) on hit on their first attack against this creature. 
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Feat</span>
Your beast companion gets the [[Sentinel]] feat.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Bothersome Buddy</span>
You get a +2 bonus to [[Attack]] against creatures your beast companion [[Threaten|threatens]], as though you were [[Flanking]].
Your beast companion gets the following special attack.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Bother</span> - Your companion [[Taunted|taunts]] a creature [[On-hit]].
