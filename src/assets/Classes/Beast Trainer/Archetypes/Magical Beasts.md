[[Beast Trainer]] [[Archetype]].

Magical Beasts use their casting to enhance their beast companions. They are able to combo their spells with their beast.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)"><br>This archetype replaces Tandem Strike</span>

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Buffed Up</span>
Your beast companion benefits from any technique you have prepared with the [[prepared]] tag.
Whenever you use a technique with the [[Buff]] tag, you can also affect your beast companion with it.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Natural Synergy</span>
The DC for your spells is 2 higher against any creature your beast companion has attacked this turn. You get advantage on [[Spell Attack|spell attacks]] against any creature your beast companion has attacked this turn.
You automatically exclude your beast companion from any damage or [[CC]] that your techniques inflict.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
Choose a magical technique tree. You gain a technique point in this tree at level 1 and every two levels after.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Exploit Weakness</span>
Whenever a creature fails a save against one of your techniques, it [[AoO|Provokes]] from your beast companion.
