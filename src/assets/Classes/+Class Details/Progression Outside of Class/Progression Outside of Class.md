Just so its all in one place.

[[Boons]]/[[Banes]] - Gained at level one as a result of having positive or negative luck.

[[Feat Functionality|Feats]] - Gained at level 1, and every odd level after.

[[Technique Point|Technique Points]] - Gain one that can be used on any school of techniques at level 2, and every even level after.

[[Ascendance]] - Unique template applied at level 12.

[[+Achievement]] - Can be gained at any time, even retroactively, when certain conditions are met, varying based on the achievement [[Fall Damage]]
