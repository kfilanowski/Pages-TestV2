You have a special weapon that you use to vanquish your enemies. It's name, legendary. It's purpose, divine. This indestructible weapon will be passed down from champion to champion for all of eternity.
(if dual wielding is your thing, no issue in making it twinned weapons)

Gain the following benefits…
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Bonus Feat</span> - Gain the [[Bonded Weapon]] feat, applying only to your legendary weapon(s).
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Indestructible</span> -  This weapons destruction can only be brought about through a series of complex rituals, requiring the intervention of deities or some other incredibly powerful force of destruction, determined upon ascension.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Intelligent</span> - It may or may not be able to communicate, but it can at least understand language and perceive the world around it. Its bonded wielder can receive knowledge from it through language or psychic impressions conveying the information. It has a +20 knowledge bonus concerning monsters of a specific type ([[plants]], [[fiends]] etc.). Gain [[Bane]] against this creature type.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Unique Property </span>- Each legendary weapon is unique and has something that aids and defeating its favored foe. Some examples below.

| Name               | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Destined Death     | Creatures slain by this weapon are turned to ash and their souls are absorbed by the weapon. They have no afterlife and cannot be brought back from the dead unless the weapon is destroyed.<br>                          |
| Like Butter        | This weapon can destroy almost anything, cutting through/crushing stone and metal with ease. It has the "transmundane" level of toughness (see [[Breaking Objects]]).                                                     |
| Detected           | This weapon shutters when in the presence of a specific creature type within 100 ft, even through [[Polymorph]] effects and [[Total Cover]]. The wielder gets an innate sense of the direction to any such creatures.<br> |
| [[Special Attack]] | Something like Elden Ring's weapons somber weapons/ash of war. Get creative.                                                                                                                                              |
