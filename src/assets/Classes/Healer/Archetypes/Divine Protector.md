[[Crust]]
Divine protectors are incredibly frustrating healers that not only excel at healing wounds, but in preventing them altogether through the use of divine shields.

Replaces a single technique point you get at level 1. Your lay on hands loses the [[overheal]] tag and cannot grant temp hp.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Technique </span>
You gain a technique point in the protector tree at this level and every 2 levels after.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Divine Shield (Su)</span>
Gain a [[Divine Shield]] that naturally reforms after not taking damage for 1 minute. As a standard action you can refresh your divine shield.

As a standard action, you can give an ally within reach a divine shield. These shields last 10 minutes.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Blaze of Glory (Su)</span> [[recharge]]
When you are defeated, you go out in a blaze of glory. All your allies within 60 ft (minus yourself) are immediately granted a divine shield.

This can only happen once a minute.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Front Lines</span>
While you have a divine shield, your actions are [[elusive]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Rally</span>
Movement of allies (except you) within ??? is [[elusive]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Divine Army</span>
If you spend 1 mana per creature affected, creatures you heal with holy arrow or channel energy gain a divine shield.
