Healers are mages that combine natural talent, a breadth of experience, and a compassion for life that results in abilities that lift spirits and mend wounds, both literally and metaphorically. A healer's mere presence commands a high degree of respect, as even a single healer behind the scenes can turn the tides of a battle. For this reason, healers are often involuntarily pulled into conflicts that do not concern them, as every government, army, church, and adventuring group seeks out their abilities, and every enemy of said groups seek to eliminate them.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Technique</span>
You have [[Magical Specialization]].
You gain 2 technique points at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after.
These technique points can be used on the [[Brazzeria (T)]] and [[goomshroom]] trees.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Healing Touch</span> [[Sp]] [[Healing]] [[Overheal]]
Touch a creature to heal (3+[[Caster Level|CL]]) hp. If you [[Mana Burn]] 1, you may touch yourself as a swift action. Temp hp granted by this ability lasts 10 minutes.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Channel (Sp) (healing) (Overheal)</span> [[Recharge]]
You can unleash a pulse of energy at your maximum power, healing all allies within 60ft for 
(5+2*[[Caster Level|CL]]) hp.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Holy Arrow (Healing)</span> [[Sp]] [[Holy]] 
As a standard action, conjure a magic bow and make a ranged attack with 20ft range. The target takes holy damage equal to Healing Touch. If this attack passes through allies, apply healing touch to them. Holy arrow also heals yourself.
This can be used through a staff.
This ranged attack counts as being made from a bow.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Skill</span>
You are proficient in the [[+Medicine]] skill and get a +4 bonus to it.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Innate Casting</span>
You can innately cast [[death ward]]
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Deific Boons</span>
You start play with 10 [[Deific Boons|GBP]] for any gods that are not [[Demon Lords]], or are otherwise overtly evil, unless your [[WIS]] is -1 or lower.
