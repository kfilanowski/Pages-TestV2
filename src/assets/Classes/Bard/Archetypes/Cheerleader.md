Cheerleader bards excel at bringing their allies up when times are the toughest with encouraging words and healing effects.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Performance: Raise Morale</span>
The cheerleader only ever gets a singular performance, and allies can choose to gain any one benefit from the performance, deciding at the start of their turn. Below are level requirements for providing a particular benefit.
1 - <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Raise Morale</span> - Increase allied speed by 10 and give them advantage on saves against fear
1 - <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Inspire Courage</span> - +2 attack
4 - <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Song of</span> [[Healing]] - Restore 3 hp.
1 - Raise Dongers - ???
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Inspire Competence</span>
A cheerleader can use the [[aid]] action as a swift action. They are always grant the improved +2 bonus when aiding in a skill check through their tunes.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Finale: Purging Finale (healing)</span>
You automatically remove all non-[[Permanent Status]] effects from an ally within 30 ft of you. They can take a swift or immediate action as an instant action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Finale: Reviving Finale (healing)</span>
Restore (3\*lvl) hp to all allies within 30 ft. [[Defeated]] creatures healed are not [[dazed]] on their next turn.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Finale: Bass Drop (healing)</span>
The music sticks with your allies even after the performance ends. They gain (5*[[LvL]]) [[temp hp]]. This temp hp disappears at the end of your next turn.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Finale: Staccato of Surrender</span>
Enemies within 60 ft are [[pacified]] for 1 round (will negates). When they are attacked by you or your allies, they get a new save against the effect.

[[Minion]] creatures instead lose the will to fight completely, unless they are [[Companion|companions]].
