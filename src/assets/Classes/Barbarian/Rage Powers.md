Despite the name, rage powers, unless explicitly stated, do not require you to be raging to use.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Skill-Based Raging</span>
Choose a skill. You gain a +2 bonus to that skill and can use it while raging.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Intimidating Glare</span>
By spending 30ft of movement, you can make an [[demoralize]] a creature. If you successfully demoralize a creature that is already shaken, in addition to increasing the shaken penalty, you also [[Frightened|frighten]] them for one round.
A creature frightened in this way cannot be frightened again for 1 minute.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ferocity</span>
Gain [[ferocity]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Flesh Wound</span>
When you are defeated, it only takes 1 minute for you to regain 1 hp.
You are [[staggered]] after you are healed from 0 hp instead of [[dazed]]. You only become [[drained]] 2 when [[defeated]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">No Escape</span>
When an enemy creature leaves your [[Threaten|threatened]] spaces, you can spend an immediate action to chase after them up to 60 ft.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Quick Reflexes</span>
When a creature attempts to [[Avoid AoO (P)|avoid AoO]], you get a +4 bonus to your reflex save for the opposed check.
Get a +4 bonus to [[attack]] made as AoOs.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Strength Surge</span>
Once per rage, add (2+lvl) to an [[+Athletics]] check as an instant action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Bonk</span>
Whenever you defeat a creature with a melee attack, they are automatically [[entangled]] as you smash them into the ground. If they regain consciousness, they must first escape before they can move. They can [[Breakout]] as a swift action. Failure means they stay entangled.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Fight On</span>
An instance of damage must be at least higher than your level (after reduction) in order to defeat you. When this ability procs you take no damage.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Charger</span>
Gain [[powerful charge]]. You can move through creatures and [[difficult terrain]] during your charge.
