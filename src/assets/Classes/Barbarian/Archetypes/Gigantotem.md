Become large and in charge.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Totem Feature - Embiggen</span> [[Su]]
When raging, your size increases to large. At level 6 you can choose to instead become huge. At level 10 you can choose to become massive.
## <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Rage Powers</span>
Numbers are level requirements
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Gigantic Impact</span>
Whenever you take the [[jump]] action, create a shockwave affecting all creatures within your natural reach of where you land. Creatures are made [[clumsy]] 1.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Giant Steps</span>
The ground quakes with each step you take. Whenever you take the [[dash]] or [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]] action, you can move through other creatures squares and your movement is [[elusive]], even through other creatures' spaces. Creatures you move through take (5+2*[[LvL]]) crush damage (reflex halves).

You cannot attack a creature you step on with this ability.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Juggernaut</span>
You get a +4 bonus to [[+Athletics]] to [[Breakout]] or [[Brute Force]] while raging. Every time you break something with athletics, you gain +1 Wrath.

When you take the charge or dash action, you can make athletics checks to destroy or displace obstacles such as doors, walls and other barriers as a free action any number of times as long as you continue to succeed. If you initiate combat by doing this, the enemy is [[DD|surprised]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Lesser Creatures</span>
Can only be used against creatures smaller than you.

You can innately use the [[Foe Toss]] technique at will.
