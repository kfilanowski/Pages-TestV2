[[Fighter]] [[Archetype]].

For when you really don't want to think about complicated class features.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Superior Stats</span>
You gain a bonus feat every level. Every time you gain a technique point, you can choose to instead turn it into a feat.

You get access to the following feats, each of which can be taken any number of times.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ability Increase</span> - Increase an [[Ability Scores|Ability Score]] by +1 (up to +3)
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Super Strength</span> - Increase [[STR]] by +1 (up to +5).
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Super Armor</span> - Gain +1 [[AC]] (up to +3).
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Power Stance</span> - Gain +3 [[Poise]].
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Paladin Cosplay</span> - Get a +1 bonus to [[+Saving Throw|Saves]] (up to +3).
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Superior Genetics</span> - Get an additional major feature from your [[+Races|race]].
