[[Fighter]] [[Archetype]].

Wind Swordsman are focused on being annoying and slippery with an incredibly powerful signature attack: Steel Tempest. Their abilities are centered around mobility to get into a better position to use this attack.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Steel Tempest</span> [[Su]] [[Air]]
Once per round when you land a [[melee attack]] against a creature, gain a stack of gathering storm. With two stacks of gathering storm, you can use the following special attack. This special attack can be used any number of times in a combat.

Your attack whips up a whirlwind, targeting all enemies in a 60 ft line. Compare your attack roll to the ac of all creatures in the line. Enemies are [[Staggered]] [[On-hit]] for one round and take an additional d6/odd lvl damage.

You cannot gain gathering storm stacks in the same turn you use steel tempest.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Flow</span>
Keep a running tally of how far you move. Every time you move 60 ft, you gain a protective barrier  that gives you DRA (3+1/2x[[LvL]]) for the next instance of damage you take.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Sweeping Blade</span> ([[Elusive]])
Once per enemy per round, unless your speed is 0 or you are unable to make attacks, you can choose to dash in a straight line through a enemy within reach as a free action, appearing directly on the opposite side of the creature adjacent to them. This movement doesn't count against your movement each round. Any movement around a creature you target with this ability is also elusive for this turn.

You can combine sweeping blade with steel tempest, creating a new unique effect. You can either attack a 60 ft line as usual, or you can choose to attack a 10 ft radius around you at your destination.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Wind Wall</span> ([[Sp]]) ([[recharge]])
You can innately cast [[Wind Wall]]. You can use wind wall as an immediate action, but its size is decreased to a 15x15 square, its duration changes to 1 round and its range decreases to reach. It does not impede you or your allies' movement or attacks.

The penalty to ranged attacks also applies to the DC of any ability that must physically pass through the wall. It would not apply to a spell like [[charming gaze]], but it would apply to an effect like [[bestial claw]].

You can only use this ability again after 1 minute passes.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 -Ride the Wind</span>
You can use sweeping blade against a single target you attack with steel tempest, appearing on the opposite side of them from your current position.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Already Dead</span> ([[Teleportation]])
As an immediate action, you can teleport adjacent to any creature you can see within 30 feet that gets inflicted with a debilitating status effect (such stagger, poisoned, sickened) and make a simple attack against them. This ability must be used in response to the status being inflicted, it cannot be used against a creature that merely happened to be status'd.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Last Breath</span>
Creatures hit by steel tempest are [[dazed]] for one round (fort reduces to stagger).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Storm of Steel</span>
Steel tempest's line attack increases in width. Instead of a 60 ft line that is 5 ft wide, it is 15 ft wide, essentially targeting all spaces adjacent to the line.

When combined with sweeping blade, the AoE increases to a 20 ft radius around you at your destination.
