[[Fighter]] [[Archetype]].

Your armor is a second skin to you. Your armor is also your first skin. The iron knuckle has 2 "phases" to their battles. The first is a walking tank with multiple layers of armor, durable and stubborn until you get to the squishy _single layer_ of plate armor underneath. The second is a nimble warrior that gets a boost of adrenaline, feeling light as a feather just after their outer shell is cracked open.

Iron knuckles are practically invincible to being attacked head on while layered up, and must be ganged up on. Mages will find a much easier time breaking the outer layer.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Metallurgy</span>
You are fused with a set of standard metal plate armor. You can enhance this armor with new metals and enchantments with a more involved crafting process. You are permanently treated as though you are wearing [[Armor|heavy armor]]. It is part of you and cannot be separated without causing permanent life-threatening damage to you.

Because this armor is a part of you, it being destroyed hurts you. Whenever your armor is destroyed/degraded you are [[Drained]] 10.

You add construct to your creature types. You gain proficiency in the profession (blacksmith) skill.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Layer Up</span>
You can choose to put on an additional layer of heavy armor over the first, providing you with the following benefits after 1 minute of preparation.
- 18+[[LvL]] AC (instead of the 16+LvL given by normal heavy armor)
- [[DR|DRA]] 2 (instead of the 1 given by normal heavy armor)
- 20 [[Poise]]
- You benefit from the [[Quake]] technique constantly
While layered up you also take a -10 penalty to your speed and cannot [[fly]] or [[hover]].
Enemies can remove <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">layer up</span> by dealing damage that drops you below half hp.
It takes 3 rounds to remove layer up manually.

It requires a set of heavy armor in order to use layer up. When an enemy removes layer up, you cannot use that particular set of heavy armor to layer up again until you [[Repairing Equipment|repair]] it. Be sure to pick it up after the battle!
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Light as a Feather</span>
After carrying the weight of incredibly heavy metal armor during the heat of battle, to have the burden removed suddenly fills you with new vigor. When an enemy removes layer up from you, you become <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">light as a feather</span>, gaining the following benefits until combat ends.
- You gain (10+2*[[LvL]]) [[temp hp]] ([[healing]]), potentially preventing you from dying from single instances of damage.
- Your speed and [[jump]] distance increases by 10 until combat ends (or you layer up again)
- You are affected by the [[Unbreakable Advance]] technique until combat ends (or you layer up again).
- You gain access to the sudden strike special attack until combat ends.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Sudden Strike</span> - Advance 10 ft towards an enemy ([[elusive]]) and make a [[melee attack]]. [[On-hit]] the target is pushed back 5 feet and [[knocked down]]. Deals an additional (d6 per odd LvL) damage.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Bully</span>
You gain the [[Power Pose]] feat. When you use it, it deals 15 [[poise]] damage instead of 10.

If you are <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">layered up</span> then you can use it as an instant action 1/turn. You can use power pose against [[AoO]]s an unlimited number of times as an instant action.

You can choose if you want to leave a creature at 1 poise, or poise break them with this ability. If a creature is poise broken by this ability then they are made [[clumsy]] 2, or you can push them 10 ft back.

While <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">light as a feather</span>, when a creature misses you with an AoO, or you successfully use acrobatics to evade AoOs, you can make a bully attack as an instant action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Bull Rush</span>
If you are <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">layered up</span>, when you shove a creature, you can move with them to increase the shove distance by your remaining movement.

While <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">light as a feather</span>, you gain [[powerful charge]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Adrenaline</span>
If you are <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">layered up</span> then you are [[Immunity|immune]] to [[Fatigued|fatigue]].

You gain the innate technique [[adrenaline burst]] while <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">light as a feather</span>.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Invincible</span>
You are immune to attack rolls made by [[Minion|minions]] while <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">layered up</span>. Any minion that attacks you is instead set to 1 poise automatically as it bounces off of your metal body. This is treated as a miss.

While <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">light as a feather</span>, you gain [[resistance]] to damage from failed saving throws (aka you always take half damage, but if you fail, you still suffer any additional effects).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Back Massage</span>
Whenever a creature misses or [[Graze|grazes]] you, they are knocked back 10 ft. If you also poise break them with Power Pose as part of the same attack, you can push them 20 ft.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Special Attack: Iron Wave</span>
Strike at a foe, dealing an additional 6d6 (21) damage. If you are <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">layered up</span>, gain temp hp and poise equal to the total damage dealt. If <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">light as a feather</span>, also immediately layer up again until this temp hp is lost.

This poise recovery cannot exceed your poise maximum.
