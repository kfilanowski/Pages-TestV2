## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
You have [[martial specialization]].
You gain 1 [[Technique Point|technique point]] at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after. These technique points can be used on any martial tree.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Superior Attack</span>
Once per any turn where you're capable of taking the attack action, you can make a simple attack as a free action, or as an additional simple attack if you do take the attack action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Weapon Training</span>
You have +2 to [[Attack]] with [[Weapon Attack|weapon attacks]], [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]] and [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed strikes]]. 
You have DR 5 vs disarm.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Armor Training</span>
Armors requires 2 less [[STR]] to use. You ignore the [[+Stealth]] penalty to heavy armor.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Physical Training</span>
Gain athletics as a skill with a +4 bonus. You can [[Breakout]] an ally as a swift action, regardless of the action it usually took to breakout.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Bravery</span>
You have [[resistance]] to [[fear]] effects.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Always Ready</span>
When combat starts, if you are below full hp, heal to full HP.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Baby Slayer</span>
When an enemy is a [[minion]], you automatically know just by looking at them. Gain an additional superior attack that can only be used against minion enemies.
You are immune to being grazed by minions
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Smash From Air</span>
Gain [[Combat Reflexes]] as a bonus feat. If you already have it, gain a bonus feat. 
You can use an attack of opportunity to swat a ranged attack out of the air that targets either you or an ally within reach. Roll an attack roll. If your attack roll matches or exceeds theirs, the attack is negated. 
You take a -4 attack penalty to attacks opposing [[+Siege Weapon|siege weapons]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Striking</span>
Your [[Weapon Attack|weapon attacks]], [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]] and [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed attacks]] gain additional damage equal to their damage dice.
Examples: 
2h weapon @1=2d8, @6=4d8
Light Weapon @1=d6, @6=2d6
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Weapon Spirit</span>
As a standard action you can enchant 2 different [[+Weapons]] (including [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed attacks]] and [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]]) you hold with any single quality from the [[Equipment and Economics/Equipment Types/Gear Upgrades/Magic Weapon Properties/+Magic Weapon Properties|magic weapon properties]] section, except [[Bane]]. You can enchant 2 different weapons, and give them different enchants. You can enchant new weapons, but the old ones lose their enchantments.

Weapons cannot have typically more than 1 enchantment, unless they have multiple enchantment slots.
