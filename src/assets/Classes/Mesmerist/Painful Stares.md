Effects with a number require a specific level.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Staring Daggers</span>
Your stare is painful in the most literal sense. When triggered, the creature takes an additional d4+CL (2+CL) pierce damage.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Stink Eye</span>
Your stare fills the creatures nostrils with a powerful stench. When triggered, the creature becomes [[sickened]] 1. This cannot cause a creature to become higher than sickened 1.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Bloodthirst</span>
Your stare causes the creature to believe they are hemophiliac. The immediately begin [[bleeding]] for d6 damage.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Ice Cold Stare</span> [[frost]]
The creature believes ice creeps across its body. When triggered, the creature becomes [[frosty]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Dirty Look</span>
Your gaze holds a deep unsatisfied hunger for the creature so powerful it forces them to avert their gaze from you. When triggered, they are [[DD]] against you specifically until the end of your next turn.
