## [[Crust]]

[[Rogue]] [[Archetype]].
An expert thief, a blur of shadows around the battlefield taking everything their opponents have and stepping back before they realize what happened.
Your very shadow steps out to reach into people's pockets when they aren't looking.

**1 - Shadower**
You have a sentient, autonomous manifestation of dark magic and inner corruption, acting as your loyal, silent partner. It appears as your shadow, sticking by your side and manipulating objects alongside you. It grants you a proficiency and a +4 bonus in Sleight of Hand, and allows you to attempt to steal from anyone regardless of their attentiveness. Your ~={red}Cunning Actions=~ have +10 reach when assisted by it.

You can choose to make this shadow appear or disappear once per round as a [[Free Action]], but you must use Sleight of Hand normally while it isn't helping you.

**1 - Pickpocket**
Critical hits and successful Poise breaks drop coins around the target. When collected, it's as much money as the damage dealt; if you collect them, it's worth quadruple. You can collect the coins as a free action, but other creatures must spend an action to collect or otherwise move the money. Before it's looted or moved, the coins are ~={red}Blood Money=~. 

This ability represents taking something away from your opponent with each hit. If you use it while sparring or against allies to "farm" money, they may need that money back to replace the golden tooth they lost, jewelry you pilfered, the kidney you stole etc.

[[DM]] discretion.

**1 - Cunning Action: Steal**
You can use ~={red}Cunning Action=~ to perform Thievery. You can use Steal to produce ~={red}Blood Money=~ once per creature, in addition to taking something from them.

**1 - Coin Explosion**
As an action, explode ALL of your ~={red}Blood Money=~, dealing your 2d8 (9) Crush and Poise damage to enemies within 10ft of a coin (Reflex halves). Getting hit by multiple increases the damage by d8 (4) per additional coin they're hit by.

This kills the Blood Money.

**2 - Band of Thieves** [[Companion]] [[Summoning]]
You can easily find and hire goons to call upon for aid. These are humanoids with half your [[HP]] (including when summoned), and +1 [[DEX]] and [[LUK]]. They are minions of half your level. They have no other abilities or statistics of their own (no techniques, feats, class features, etc).

You may equip them as you please, and Summon them as a ~={red}Cunning Action=~ as if by a Summoning Contract. They take their turn on your turn - the player controls them.

They can create ~={red}Blood Money=~ for you using the same methods as you.

The first two goons are free, but additional goons or replacing goons costs 500g each. 
*You can train specific commoners or intelligent level 0 creatures to be your goons and be summon-ready, with DM discretion - but far more work on the player's part is required to justify anything with class levels, such as investing in real summoning contracts and extensive training.* 

**4 - Grand Larceny**
When you use the [[Steal]] action, you can [[Steal]] from everything in reach.

**6 - Coin Collection**
Gain ~={green}+20%=~ of your character's current wealth as bonus. The value of your share of all future loot, as well as money earned through Profession, is increased by 20%.
Up to GM discretion to determine what counts, to avoid farming wealth with recurrent sales and repurchases.




**Talent - Assaulter** [[Teleportation]]
As a Special Attack, Teleport to an enemy that is DD (up to your speed in distance) and Attack them.
(Minimum level 4)

**Talent - Moneybag**
You can spend a minute and 1000g to create a special, enticing, particularly jingle-y bag of money. 
When placed on the ground, enemies with a concept of money will be Fascinated by it (Will negates). 
It can be exploded if undisturbed, and counts as your level in Blood Money coins if exploded.
You can only have one at a time.
