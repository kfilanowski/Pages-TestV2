The Skyman is a class defined by the signature ability to speak the tongue of dragons and manifest incredibly powerful effects from their words. There's also a healthy amount of jank. It is based on a Bethesda game after all.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
Choose either [[Martial Specialization]] or [[Magical Specialization]].
Then choose a single technique tree within your specialization. Gain a [[Technique Point]] in that tree at this level and every odd level after. 
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Shout</span> [[Su]] [[Recharge]]
You learn draconic and ancient draconic as a bonus [[languages]].
You can spend 1 mana as a swift action in order to shout. The effects of which vary based on the shout. Pick a shout from the [[Shouts]] page at this level, and at level 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10.

You can shout once per minute and cannot do so while [[Silenced]]. Some shouts may have additional restrictions.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Blessing of the Mountain Goat</span>
The skyman is adept at maneuvering vertical terrain. You can comfortably [[Balance]] on the smallest of ledges and walk on and up practically vertical walls. This is essentially like having a [[Climb]] speed equal to your [[Land Speed]], but you need not use your hands.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Blessing</span>
Your birth was accompanied by the prominence of a particular constellation. Choose a blessing.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Blessing of the Lovers</span> - Get a +2 bonus to all [[Skill Check|skill checks]].
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Blessing of the Steed </span>- Get +1,000 [[Carry Weight]]. You can take the [[Swap Item]] action as a free action once each turn, even if it is not the start of your turn.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Blessing of the Mana Well</span> - You get 1 [[Magic Resist|MR]]. When this ability reduces damage, recover 1 mana.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Talented</span>
Choose a talent from the talent list. Gain another at level 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10.
