Shouts have level requirements listed next to them.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Unrelenting Force</span> [[Sonic]]
Your shout manifests as an incredibly loud shockwave that launches your enemies away from you. Deal 2d6 (+d6 @3 and each odd [[LvL]] after) sonic damage to creatures in a 30 ft line in front of you and launch them back 5 ft per d6 you roll (fort halves damage and converts knockback into 20 [[Poise]] damage).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Elemental Breath</span> [[Persistent]]
Your shout manifests as a [[Breath Weapon]] of an elemental damage type. Choose between [[Fire]], [[Frost]], [[Zap]] and [[Poison]]. Deal 2d8 (+d8 @3 and each odd [[LvL]] after) damage of that type in a 60 ft line or a 30 ft cone (reflex halves). The ground beneath where you shouted is suffused with this element for 3 rounds. A creature that moves into or ends their turn in this area takes 10 damage.

Creatures that were struck by the elemental breath do not take this persistent damage unless they end their turns in the area.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Spirit World</span>
Become [[Incorporeal]] for 5 rounds. If you take any action other than movement, you lose your incorporeal status.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Whirlwind Sprint</span> [[Elusive]]
Your shout manifests as a violent wind that carries you forward. Move 60+(5 per level) ft in a straight line. Creatures in this line or adjacent to it are set to 1 [[Poise]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Dragonrend</span>
Automatically inflict the [[Grounded]] condition on creatures in a 100 ft line for 1 minute. If they are currently airborne, they plummet, provoking [[AoO]]s and taking [[Fall Damage]].

[[Dragons]], as well as any dragon adjacent creatures, are also made [[Clumsy]] 2.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Storm Caller</span> [[Zap]]
Mimic the effects of [[Call Lightning Storm]], but it is not concentration. When you initially shout, and as a free action on each subsequent turn, you can call down a bolt of lightning. After you call down 2+(1/2 [[LvL]]) bolts of lightning, this effect ends.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Bend Will</span> [[Charm]] [[Companion]]
Charm a creature within 100 ft that is a [[Minion]] to you for 24 hours. When you say the first syllable to this shout, any targetable creature is highlighted in your vision. If none are within range, then you stop the shout before it activates. You lose the action, but the shout does not recharge.
While a creature is charmed in this way, they are willing to perform menial tasks for you, so long as they are not inherently dangerous (ie excavate this volcano). If you are in the heat of battle, they will fight by your side.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Slow Time</span>
Functions as the [[Slow Time]] technique.
