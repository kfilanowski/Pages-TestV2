Your access to spinnerets gives you incredible mobility. 

Alters shapeshift

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Shapeshift</span>
You get 1 less adaptation point.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Clingy</span>
You get the climb and cling upgrades from the [[Polymorph List]] constantly.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Spinneret</span>
You have a spinneret in each of your wrists. These highly specialized organs allow you to produce a silk that is incredibly strong, sticky and flexible. All class features surrounding webs involve using these spinnerets. All of them require at least one of your hands to be free, and some require both of them and are labeled as such.



## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Special Attack: Web Shot</span>
Make a [[Thrown]] attack. [[On-hit]] the creature is [[entangled]]. They can roll to [[Breakout]] as a swift action, or tear their way out automatically.
