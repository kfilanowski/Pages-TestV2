A thunderlord's specialty is disruption. Knocking people around with rushing wind and cacophonous attacks that completely ignore the poise system.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Blown Away</span>
Whenever you deal [[zap]] damage, [[sonic]] damage or *crush* damage from an [[air]] effect, you push the creature you damage 10 ft directly away from you. If the effect already pushed the creature, it pushes them an additional 10 ft.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - AS: Reverberating Strike</span> [[sonic]]
Deal an additional d6+CL (3+CL) thunder damage and energize your foe [[On-hit]] for 1 round. They take an additional 2d6 (7) sonic damage if they move of their own volition, no matter the method.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Talents</span>
Gain one at level 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10. Numbers indicate level requirements
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Avatar Form</span> [[Su]] [[buff]] [[recharge]]
As a swift action, conjure a larger version of yourself made entirely of crackling storm clouds to linger over your head. 
- Your [[Reach|natural reach]] increases to 20 ft during your turn. 
- Blown Away doubles in power to knock people 20 feet away from you
- Whenever you make a [[Melee Attack]] during this time, your avatar causes an immense shockwave at the point of impact. Creatures within 20 ft of the original target are knocked 20 ft away from the target 20 ft and are knocked down (fort negates knockdown).
## <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 80)">Talents</span>
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - AS: Wind Strike</span> [[air]]
Strike an enemy, dealing no additional damage, but instead surrounding in a tempest that cuts into them as they move for one round. They take d8 (4) slashing damage for every 5 feet they move, no matter the method.
Despite not dealing any damage, this attack still procs Blown Away [[On-hit]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Reverberations (air)</span> [[Sp]]
At the start of your turn as a swift action, you can spend 1 mana to unleash powerful winds upon creatures within 10 ft of you, dealing 2d6 (7) crush damage (fort negates).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Thunderous Smite (Sp) (sonic)</span>
If you spend 1 mana as an immediate action when you take damage, you can blast your attacker with a sonic blast if they are within 30 ft. They take 2d6+CL (7+CL) sonic damage (fort halves).
