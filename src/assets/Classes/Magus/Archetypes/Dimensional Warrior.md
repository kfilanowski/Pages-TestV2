A dimensional warrior's specialty is mobility through blatantly overpowered abilities that allow them to teleport on a dime and strike opponents through magic proxies.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Echo</span> [[Su]]
By spending 1 mana as a swift action, you can cause a colorful copy of yourself made of pure mana to appear in a space you can see within 30 ft. You can make a [[Natural Attack]], [[Unarmed Attack]] or  [[Weapon Attack]] from the position of this copy instead of your own. This copy [[Threaten|threatens]] as you would and consumes your [[AoO]]s. Echos cannot provide [[flanking]].

If you create an echo while another exists, the old one disappears.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Warp Speed (Su)</span> [[Teleportation]] [[Mana Burn]]
You gain a "warp" speed, allowing you to teleport instead of walking, swimming etc. Your warp speed is equal to your [[Land Speed]]. You mana burn any turn you use it.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Me Too! (Su)</span>
When an opponent uses a [[Teleportation]] effect within your [[reach]], you can teleport with them as an immediate action. If they are out of your reach, you can use a standard action to open a portal to follow them.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Talents</span>
Gain one at level 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10. Numbers indicate level requirements

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Playing With Portals (Su) (teleportation)</span> [[Concentration]]
By spending 1 mana as a swift action, you can place a portal on an adjacent roughly flat surface and have a linked portal open up along another such surface within 60 ft of you that you can see. the portal is about 5 ft tall and half as wide. Creatures cannot pass through this portal, but anything else can. This could mean arrows, spells or precious cargo. 
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Harm's Way Out (Su) (teleportation)</span>
After an ally takes damage within 60 ft of you, you can choose to spend 1 mana as an immediate action to swap places with them.
