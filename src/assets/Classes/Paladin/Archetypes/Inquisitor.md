Inquisitors crush wills as hard as they crush skulls. By marking people for judgement, you can send them deep into the depths of despair.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Skill</span>
Gain [[+Intimidation]] as a bonus skill with a +4 bonus.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Judgement</span> [[Su]] [[Recharge]]
Mark an enemy within 30 feet for judgement as a swift action. Some type of glowing visual effect manifests on/around the creature while they are judged. All allies get a +2 [[Attack]] against this creature.

The first time you make a [[Melee Attack]] against a marked enemy, you can attempt to [[Demoralize]] them as a free action [[On-hit]]. If successful, they are [[Frightened]].

When this enemy is defeated, this ability immediately recharges. If you are unable to defeat this creature within a minute, the mark of judgement disappears from your target and judgement recharges.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Multiattack</span>
Make 2 attacks against a creature that is [[Shaken]] or [[Frightened]]. If your first attack inflicts one of these conditions, you can make another.

This ability is meant to be forgiving, so both attacks need not be made against qualifying creatures, only 1.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Find the Faithful (Su)</span>
While in a settlement, you can see a faint glowing trail leading to anybody who shares your faith. If one exists, you can also see a path leading to a place of worship, such as a temple or an altar dedicated to your deity. These paths are visually distinct from one another.

By spending a standard action to examine a creature, you can tell whether or not they share your faith.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Truth in the Sword (Su)</span>
By gazing at a creature's reflection of the metal surface of your weapon, you can get an abstract visual representation of how many they have killed and how they feel towards it. How many is usually represented with none, one, several, many, countless. How they feel is usually represented with joy, indifference, regret and anguish.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Crush Conviction</span>
Whenever you make a creature shaken 4, they completely lose the will to fight (will negates). This effect manifests differently depending on their [[LvL]] relative to yours.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Minion</span> - Minion creatures change sides and fight for you
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Lower that your LvL</span> - Creature is [[Frightened]] and cannot take any actions other than [[Dash]], [[Disengage]] or something similar until they have [[Total Cover]] from you and are at least 60 ft away.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Your LvL or Higher</span> - Creature is [[Frightened]].
