Invincible champions always heal and never die. Invincible champions are pure tanks with superb ability to draw aggro.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Invincible champions get lay on hands at level 1, and it replaces the base feature.</span>
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Seal of Blood</span>
You can use the [[Bloodletting]] technique at will. It does not pop your divine shield.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Lay on Hands</span> [[healing]]
As a swift action, spend 1 mana to heal yourself for (5+[[LvL]]) hp.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Bragging Rights</span>
Your constant boasting of how impossible you are to defeat irks your enemies. Allies get +2 [[Attack]] against creatures taunted by you.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Multiattack</span>
Make 2 attacks against a [[taunted]] creature. If your first attack taunts, then you can make the second attack.

This ability is meant to be forgiving, so both attacks need not be made against qualifying creatures, only 1.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Special Attack: Audacious Charge</span>
Make a [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]] attack against an enemy. [[On-hit]] the target is [[Taunted]] for 1 minute. At the end of each of the taunted creature's turns, they can make a [[Will]] save to end the effect.
You can use this special attack at will.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Mocking Shout</span> [[Mind-Affecting]]
By spending 1 mana as a standard action, shout out to any enemies within 20 ft of you, insulting them for their weakness or cowardice. They take a -2 penalty to [[attack]] against any creature other than you until they deal damage to you. While this penalty persists, they treat all spaces moving away from you as [[Difficult Terrain]].

By spending 2 mana, you can target a single creature with this ability as a swift action, but (will negates).

This ability counts as a [[Taunted|taunt]] to fulfill your multiattack condition.
