The ultimate knight in shining armor. Dash into battle with blinding speed to pluck your allies from danger while riding on your noble steed. Your enemies will regret ignoring you to attack innocent bystanders.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Technique</span>
You gain a point in the [[Techniques/Martial Techniques/Pupper/Pupper]] tree this level and every two levels after.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Innate Technique</span>
You gain [[Sanctuary]] as an innate technique, usable at will.
As an instant action, you can use sanctuary when an innocent bystander would be directly targeted by an enemy by an offensive ability or attack.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Noble Steed </span>
You gain the [[Pet]] feat. It also gains the [[Beast Trainer]] special training feature. Specifically the mount bonus.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Special Attack: The Cavalry Arrives</span>
When you [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]] attack a creature, any ally or innocent noncombatant it [[Threaten|threatens]] can immediately step away from that creature until they exit its reach. Their movement is [[elusive]].
You can use this special attack at will.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Multiattack</span>
Make 2 attacks against a creature [[Threaten|threatening]] an ally that isn't yourself or your noble steed.
Make 3 attacks against a creature threatening an innocent noncombatant.
You can also make this multiattack against a creature who targeted a creature with a harmful ability and you saved them with Heroic Dash or if you used Sanctuary on an innocent.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Heroic Dash</span> [[Recharge]] [[Elusive]]
As an immediate action, you can move up to your speed, or your mounts speed if mounted, in response to an ally (other than you/mount) being subject to an attack or is caught in an [[AoE]] effect. You can move your ally as though taking the [[carry]] action as part of this movement. If this places the ally out of range of the attack or AoE of the effect, then they suffer no harm from it.

This ability can be used once a minute. If you target an innocent non-combatant, it has no cooldown, and can be used while on cooldown from saving another creature.

You can take the [[Carry]] action as a swift action.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span> 
Even if something does not directly harm an innocent bystander, doing something like torching a house, or doing some comically evil villain thing like activating a slow moving cage into lava could still qualify.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - My Hero</span>
You can take the [[Carry]] action as a free action.
When you carry an innocent bystander, you immediately [[Dash]]. You must drop them off in a location they have [[Total Cover]] from all enemies. They gain a [[Divine Shield]], are affected as per [[Sanctuary]] and do not become [[Clumsy]]. If they are [[defeated]], you can use Lay on Hands as a free action on them.
