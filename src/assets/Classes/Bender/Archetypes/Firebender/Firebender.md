## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Elemental Blast: Fire</span>
Your damage is fire.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Elemental Movement: Boost</span>
Increase [[Land Speed]] by 10.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Elemental Defense: Inner Fire</span>
Gain [[resistance]] to [[fire]] and [[frost]] damage.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Elemental Intuition: Firebending</span> [[concentration]]
You can physically manipulate fire within 30 ft. Unlike water and earth, fire cannot create structures, nor can it lift enormous quantities of matter. You can ignite objects as well as move or snuff out flames of (5 ft cube per [[LvL]]). By touching objects, you can drastically increase their temperature or absorb extreme heat.

Initiating and maintaining concentration on firebending requires a standard action each turn.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3 - Elemental Intuition: Charge</span>
You can spend 1 [[mana]] and decrease your speed by 30 to add your [[LvL|Level]] to damage on the next [[Su]] ability from the bender class that deals fire damage.
At level 5 you can also apply this to spells with the [[zap]] tag from the [[Clairvoyance (T)]] tree.
This has a distinct audio and visual effect that imposes -10 penalty to [[+stealth]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">5 - Elemental Intuition: Firebending Adept</span>
Your blast or elemental wave damage can deal fire or zap damage interchangeably. You gain resistance to zap damage. You can manipulate the flow of electricity similar to how you can manipulate fire.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">7 - Elemental Intuition: Dragon's Breath</span>
By spending 1 mana, you can use fire bending techniques from your mouth instead of hands or feet.
Reduce elemental wave's mana cost by 1. Your elemental wave becomes a 60 ft line or 30 ft cone when used from the mouth.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">9 - Elemental Intuition: Jet Propulsion</span> [[Mana Burn]]
As a swift action, increase your speed by 30 for one round. During this turn you gain a [[fly]] speed. At the end of your turn, you plummet.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Elemental Intuition: Firebending Master</span>
Your flames change color. Fire damage you deal ignore fire [[resistance]].

## <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Pyromancer's Curse</span>
All of your effects with the [[fire]] tag create a bonus effect. 
- Whenever you activate an ability with the [[fire]] tag, you leave a trail of fire behind you that sets creatures [[ablaze]] (reflex negates).
If the effect is [[AoE]] then also... 
- The area is also ignited in flames, causing all creatures/objects in the area to be set [[ablaze]]. 
The fires you create persist for a minimum of 1 round, even in environments hostile to fire. In other environs, like forests or urban areas, the fire can slowly spread. Some environments combustibles, like gaseous mines or oil rigs can have extreme effects.
You cannot be set ablaze by your own abilities.
