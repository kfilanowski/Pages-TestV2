Features with a number require being at least that level. (24 total features)
Reminder: All effects are [[Su]] and have the [[water]] or [[frost]] tag as appropriate
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Versatile Blast</span>
Your elemental blast can deal pierce, crush and slash damage interchangeably.

You can use elemental blast to perform combat maneuvers from a range. All combat maneuvers you do with your blast get a +8 bonus.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Riposte</span> [[Elusive]]
You can also [[Buckler|parry]] attacks aimed at allies within your blast range.
When you successfully parry an attack, you can make an attack against the creature as an instant action, including using your blast. You can riposte against any attackers within 30 ft of you.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Soothing Touch (1)</span> [[healing]] [[recharge]]
Using clean water, touch a creature to heal (blast damage) hp. This ability has a 1 minute cooldown.
You can choose to give this the [[overheal]] tag, but it also gains [[mana burn]].

Clean water counts as a medicine [[Tools|kit]] for you.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Mist (1)</span> [[concentration]]
You permanently memorize the [[Fog Cloud]] technique. By spending a standard action each turn, you can cause it to follow your movement, or the movement of another creature of your choosing within 30 ft.

You can also spend a standard action to increase its size, essentially adding another fog cloud adjacent to the first one.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Drench (1)</span>
As a swift action, give a creature within range of your Elemental Blast the [[wet]] condition. If it is an enemy (reflex negates). This can effectively wash a creature clean for any features where that is relevant.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Plant Bending</span>
You can move loose vegetation with your bending such as vines or other long flexible plants. This does not function against particularly dry plants like tumbleweed or dead plants. Against plant creatures, your blasts have a +2 bonus to [[attack]] or the DC as appropriate.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Breathing Bubble (1)</span>
As a standard action, you can bend water around the head of a creature within reach. A protective bubble forms around it, allowing it to breathe freely underwater for 1 hour.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Water-Ish Bending (1)</span>
You can use water bending to manipulate any liquids in the [[Alchemical Items]] section as though using the [[Catapult]] technique, but it deals your blast damage instead of its standard damage.

When you use the [[Administer Potion]] action, you can use waterbending to administer it as a swift action. This also makes it so it does not consume the target's immediate action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Ice Slick (2) (concentration)</span> [[Persistent]]
Completely freeze over a surface in slick ice. Freeze a 10 ft radius, centered on a point within 30 ft, or a 30 ft cone. Creatures inside of this radius take blast damage and are [[knocked down]] (reflex negates). Moving through an iced space knocks down creatures (reflex negates). You can avoid falling by treating icy spaces as [[difficult terrain]] or by rolling to [[Balance]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Icy Hold (2)</span>
Creature within 30 ft takes blast damage and is made [[frosty]] or [[entangled]] (reflex halves dmg and negates status). 
[[Breakout]] to escape entangle as a swift action. 1 fire damage frees the creature automatically.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Imbalanced</span>
Whenever you use your elemental wave feature, creatures are made [[clumsy]] 2 on a failed save.
When you land your elemental blast, creatures are clumsy 2. You cannot benefit from this if you use a [[Technique Rules|technique]] to augment your elemental blast.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Soothing Waves (2) (healing) (elusive)</span>
A wave of water gently washes over you and allies within 30 ft of you. All allies heal hp equal to half your blast damage. Allies can choose to ride the waves to the edge of the radius of this ability, moving directly away from the origin you used this ability from.

You can use the repositioning effect of this ability whenever you like, but a creature can only be healed by this ability once each minute.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Push OR Pull</span>
Remove the mana cost of elemental wave.
Creatures that fail their save against your elemental wave are pushed away from you or pulled towards you 10 ft (your choosing for all affected creatures).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Fickle Weather</span>
You permanently memorize the [[Snowscape]] and [[Rain Dance]] techniques without costing memory. During rain or snow, you can use your [[Swim]] speed, regardless if it is heavy enough to make puddles.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Water Pressure</span>
Your water attacks are considered to be of adamantine toughness for the purposes of [[Breaking Objects]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Ice Wall (3) (concentration)</span>
Create a barrier made out of ice with enough water. Creatures cannot see through the wall. You can make this barrier in different shapes. At least one section of the wall must be within reach.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Igloo:</span>   10 ft radius dome, complete with door.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Castle:</span> 5 ft radius wall 10 ft high.
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Wall:  </span>  10 ft tall, 30 ft wide.

Creature can use [[Brute Force]] as a standard action to break through a 5x5 section of a wall (or more for larger creatures). Alternatively an attack that does 10 crush damage or 5 fire damage destroys a 5x5 section of the wall.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Favored Terrain (3)</span>
As a swift action, you can use waterbending to create icy terrain that lasts until the end of your turn, or until the end of your next turn. This can be used to make things like bridges, ramps, stairs or to create [[Total Cover]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Water Prison</span>
You permanently memorize [[Aqueous Orb]] without spending memory. The orbs speed is 60.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Cryofreeze (4)</span>
You can freeze a [[defeated]] creature, giving them [[stasis]] permanently. Must take at least 30 fire damage to unfreeze. You can unfreeze them as an instant action if they are within 30 ft.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Whirlpool (4) (concentration) (persistent)</span>
Choose a point on the surface of the water within 60 feet of you. Water within 30 ft rapidly starts to twist and turn toward that point, carrying anything within. Creatures within the area are immediately caught in the whirlpool (fort negates, putting them out of the AoE at nearest point). When the creature is caught, and every round thereafter, they take your blast damage (crush) and are pulled closer to the center at a random speed (roll dice to determine which side they end up on). After the second round of being caught, they are completely submerged underwater.

Each round a creature can attempt to [[Breakout]] as a [[FRA]] to escape the whirlpool's area.
You are immune to the effects of your own whirlpool.
All creatures in the area are made wet.
Creatures with a [[Swim]] speed get [[Advantage]] on saves against whirlpool.

Special: If you use this outside of a body of water, its [[DC]] decreases by 2 and its radius decreases to 15 ft.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Icy Prison (4) (recharge)</span>
[[Entombed|Entomb]] a creature in ice (reflex reduces effect to frosty). An attack that does 10 crush damage or 5 fire damage destroys the ice and frees the creature. An entombed creature can breakout as a standard action to escape.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Waters of Life (5) (recharge) (healing)</span>
By spending 1000g to perform a special ritual, requiring 10 minutes of concentration, you can bless a small vial of water with incredible healing capabilities. You can use this blessed water to bring a creature back from the dead at 1 hp if it died within the last minute. You can innately tell when a creature is too far gone to heal with this.
You can only have one vial of this blessed liquid at a time.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Octopus Stance (5) (recharge)</span> [[Buff]]
As a standard action, enter octopus stance for 10 minutes, causing 8 tendrils of water to form around you. As an instant action, you can take a shield action or parry by sacrificing a tentacle.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Tsunami (5) (recharge) (concentration) (persistent)</span>
Cause an enormous and powerful wave of water to wash over the world. Form a wave 60 ft wide and 30 ft high that originates from your space and launch it in a direction. It moves 60 feet each round on your turn for 3 rounds before crashing. When the wave passes over a creature, it gets picked up by the wave (fort negates). When it gets picked up and every round after it takes blast damage and is entombed in the wave. A creature can attempt athletics with a -4 penalty as a FRA to escape, getting deposited somewhere near where they were trapped. If a creature is within the wave when it crashes, it takes 50% increased damage and is [[dazed]] for 1 round.

You can choose to ride the wave if you wish. The wave deals massive damage to structures and carries objects unless they are securely rooted in the ground. All creatures in the area are made wet.

Special: If the waves starting point is outside of a body of water, its DC decreases by 2, its height decreases to 15 and its width decreases to 30.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Blood Bending (5) (concentration) (recharge)</span>
Take control of the body of creature within 30 ft of you (assuming a significant portion of its body composition is made of water like humanoids), [[Stunned|stunning]] it for 3 rounds (fort reduces to [[Staggered|stagger]] for 3 rounds). As part of initially using the ability, and each round it is staggered/stunned by this ability as a swift action, you can cause the creature to attack its friends. If the creature is stunned, you can move it up to its speed and make [[Simple Attack|simple attacks]] or take the [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]] action. If it is staggered, you can only force it to make a simple attack.
