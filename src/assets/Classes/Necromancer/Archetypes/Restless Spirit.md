## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
You have [[Martial Specialization]]. 
You gain an additional technique point at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after.
The technique points can be used on any martial tree.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Drain Hope</span> [[Su]] [[Recharge]] [[Healing]] [[Necrotic]] [[Overheal]] 
By spending 1 mana a swift action, make a [[Thrown]] attack. [[On-hit]] apply a tether linking you and the target creature. At the end of your turn it takes 2d6+CL (7+CL) necrotic damage and you heal an equal amount. This tether lasts 3 up to 3 rounds. If the creature moves more than 60 feet from you, the tether breaks.

When you miss, or when the tether breaks, this ability cannot be used again on your next 2 turns.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Death Defied (Su) (recharge)</span>
When you are [[defeated]], you can instead choose not to be. Your body vanishes and transforms into an [[incorporeal]] and [[invincible]] spirit version of yourself, cleansing all non-[[permanent status]]. While in this form you can continue to float around the battlefield with a 30 ft [[hover]] speed. At the end of your 4rd turn defying death, you return to life at 50% of your max hp.

While in this form you can move and speak, but you can only take 2 actions: [[Simple Attack|simple]] [[melee attack]], or drain hope. They have unique effects while defying death. Melee attacks reduce a creatures speed by 15 on hit, but deals no damage. Drain hope immediately recharges when you enter death defied, and has its resource cost removed. It does not deal damage or heal you, but for each round you drain, you return to life 1 turn sooner.

After you use death defied, you acquire a unique [[Tier 3]] [[Affliction]] and Drain Hope immediately recharges.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Hades' Watchful Eye</span> - You cannot use death defied.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Cure</span> - You must pay your respects to the god of death. This requires performing a 1 week ritual in a place of worship dedicated to Hades, or spending 1 week tidying up places where the dead are kept.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Bonkers</span>
You can use the [[Base Special Attacks|cleave]] [[Special Attack]] any number of times each combat. It is not limited to only slashing weapons.
The first time you land a melee attack each round, gain a stack. When you have 2 stacks, your next [[weapon attack]] gets the following bonuses. You can choose not to unleash this attacks power when you attack to save it for a more suitable moment.
- Get a +10 bonus to [[attack]]. 
- The attack echoes to nearby enemies. Targets all enemies within 10 ft of the original target. If the attack already hits multiple targets (like with cleave), then it cannot strike the same target twice.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Wraith Walk (Su)</span> [[teleportation]]
You can let your spirit temporarily leave your body by spending 1 mana as a standard action. When you do, you immediately [[dash]] and become an [[Invincible]] [[Incorporeal]] spirit, similar to death defied. Your body remains in the position you used this ability from.

At the end of your turn, your body teleports to the position of your spirit.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Children of the Grave (Su)</span> [[Curse]] [[Companion]]
You can apply a curse to an enemy by spending 2 mana as a swift action. The curse has no effect until the creature is [[defeated]] or killed. When said condition is filled, its spirit briefly leaves its body and fights by your side. The spirit uses all of the creatures stats, but it can only use its [[multiattack]], or make a single [[simple attack]].

It spawns with only 50% of its max [[+HP]] and disappears after 1 minute. The spirit persists even if the creature recovers from the defeated condition or death. A spirit can only spawn from a creature once each day.

If you are not present when the spirit is summoned, you receive a mental alert and can immediately begin [[scrying]] and control the spirit. If you already have 2 companion slots filled because of your undead, one is put in [[stasis]] until the spirit leaves.

The curse is removed by touching a gravestone and saying "F."

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Improved Wraith Walk</span>
You can use wraith walk as a swift action, and can maintain wraith form for up to 3 turns. Using it as a swift action increases its mana cost increases to 2. When you move directly through a creature, it becomes [[Sickened]] 2 (fort negates).
