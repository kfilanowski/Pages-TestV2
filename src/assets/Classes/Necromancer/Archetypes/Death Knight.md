For when casting isn't as important to you and you want to get in there and slap some fools with your boners. This variation of the class has a lot more survivability and is geared towards being a raid boss.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Techniques</span>
You have [[Martial Specialization]] and [[Magical Specialization]].
You gain an additional technique point at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after.
The technique points can be used on any martial tree or the [[Vampirism]] tree.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Vengeful Spirit</span> [[Su]] [[teleportation]]
When an undead you control within 30 ft of you is [[defeated]], you can teleport to its space as an immediate action (with larger creatures choose which space), and its remains teleport to your previous position. You can also make a [[Simple Attack]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Bathe in Blood (Su)</span> [[healing]]
Whenever a creature within 30 ft of you takes [[Bleeding|bleed]] damage, you heal 3 hp.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Special Attack: Brow Slicer</span>
Strike just above a foes eyes to cause them to take d6 (4) bleed [[On-hit]]. Causes [[blurred vision]] for as long as they are bleeding.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Death Strike</span>
Whenever you strike a creature, you drain blood from its wounds. If the creature is bleeding, it immediately takes bleed damage and extends the duration by 1 round (if it has one).
In the case where the attack itself inflicts bleed, this ability does not proc unless the target was already bleeding.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Boiling Blood (Su)</span>
Spend 1 mana to choose 3 creatures within 30 ft of you. Their blood melts out of their veins and start bleeding for 2d6 (7) damage (fort negates).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Rally Servant (Su)</span>
By spending 2 mana as a swift action, you can command an undead you control to attack a foe you made a [[melee attack]] against this turn.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Special Attack: Heart Strike</span>
You cause a creature to bleed internally, making it harder to heal. [[On-hit]] inflict a bleed effect that deals 2d6 (7) damage. The DC to remove the bleed is 5 higher.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Deathrattle (Su)</span>
Undead you control gain a [[Deathrattle]].
When an undead creature you control is defeated, it attempts to cling to nearby foes with its undying breath. An enemy (of your choosing) within its natural reach is [[entangled]] (reflex reduces to 15 [[Poise]] damage). Creatures can [[Breakout]] as a swift action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Death Grip (Su)</span> [[necrotic]]
Spend 4 mana to dash 30 feet without provoking (even into the air) and make a [[grapple]] check with a +4 bonus. If you succeed, you carry the creature down into the ground and slam them violently. They take 5d8+CL (22+CL) necrotic damage, appropriate [[Fall Damage]] and the grapple ends.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">8 - Touch of Corruption (Su)</span>
[[On-hit]] with a [[Weapon Attack]], [[Natural Attack]] or [[Unarmed Attack]], you can spend 2 mana to add touch of corruption as an additional effect (instant action). 
The creature is [[drained]] 10 (fort halves).
You can also make touch of corruption as a [[Melee Attack]] with no other effects to remove the saving throw attached.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Death Coil (Su) (necrotic) (healing)</span> [[Recharge]]
Spend 5 mana to unleash a skull of dark energy at a creature within 30 ft. The creature takes 6d8+CL (27+CL) necrotic damage (will halves).

The skull tears a chunk of the creature's spirit and you can direct the skull at an ally (also with 30ft of you) to heal them for the damage dealt.

Usable 1/minute.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Plague Strike (Su)</span>
When you defeat a creature on your turn, you can use your Animate Dead feature as an immediate action, provided it is within your [[reach]].
