## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Major - Friend Crafter </span>([[companion]])
You have created a fragile companion meant to run in, tank some hits, and go out in a blaze of glory. Use beast tamer's [[beast companion]] as a baseline for what your creature should be with the following changes…
- It can be a plant, ooze, humanoid, fey, or cryptid.
- It gains 2 initial adaptation points and gains none from leveling.
- It's base HP is 10+4*[[LVL]].
- It's intelligence is -2 and it can speak a language of your choosing, but not very well
- It loses the evasion, scent, lost companions and one liner features of beast companion
It gains the following special ability...
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Kaboom</span> ([[fire]]/[[Poison]]) - Using your standard action, or when it is defeated, your creation explodes violently. Deals (10+3*[[lvl]]) fire or poison damage (chosen when exploding) in a 10 ft radius to ALL creatures (reflex halves).
- You have [[evasion]] vs your creation's explosions.
- You can recreate your companion by spending 3 rounds crafting, good as new. It maintains its memories and harbors no ill will towards you for its repeated destruction.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Thesis</span>
You grow accustomed to your research blowing up into your face. You make it blow up in new ways, adding more variety. When your creation explodes, you can choose one of the following explosion types.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Earthshatter</span> [[earth]] - Damage changes to crush. Groundborne creatures are [[knocked down]] (reflex negates)
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Bigger Boom (fire/poison) </span>- AoE increases to 20 ft radius
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Cold Snap</span> [[frost]] - Damage changes to frost. Creatures are [[frosty]] (reflex negates).
- You are [[Immunity|immune]] to your creation's explosions
- Recreating your companion takes 2 rounds
