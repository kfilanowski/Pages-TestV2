## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Major - Enhancer</span> ([[Polymorph|poly, chimeric]])
Your feline fascination drives your research forward. You can distill the essence of cats to be complimentary with humanoids. You can use alchemy to create "catgirl potions" for 50g with the following effects.
- [[Nightvision]]
- Claw attack (d8 slash)
- Reduce [[fall damage]] to 1 per 10 ft fallen. You cannot become [[drained]] from falling.
- [[Compression]]
- The ability to speak with cats
- Cosmetic changes such as cat eyes, ears, tail, whiskers, some fur
The effects of the potion last 1 hour. You have [[Advantage]] on any [[Skill Check|skill checks]] against any creature affected by one of your nekomancy potions.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Thesis</span>
Your nekomancy potions last 24 hours.
When you make a nekomancy potion, you can choose to add one of the following benefits…
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Cat Form</span> ([[Polymorph|poly, true]]) - Gain the ability to assume the form of any type of cat and back. Transforming is a standard action. Your size changes as appropriate. Your claw damage increases to 2d6 while transformed. You can speak while in cat form.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ideal Form (poly, true)</span> - Gain the ability to assume the form of an attractive female human with secondary cat features and back. Transforming is a standard action. Your size changes as appropriate. You get advantage on [[+Persuasion|purrsuasion]] while transformed.
