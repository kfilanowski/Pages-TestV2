Alchemist is a hybrid class with the potential to branch in many directions depending on the desired play style. Using the power of SCIENCE you can create friends, enhance your friends, yourself or you can manipulate the world around you in a variety of creative ways.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Technique</span>
You have [[Magical Specialization]] and [[Martial Specialization]]
You gain two technique points at level 1, then another two points every 2 levels after.
These technique points can be used on the alchemy tree. Choose another tech tree. They can also be used on that tree.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Alchemaster</span>
You get the [[Alchemy]] skill for free and a +4 bonus to alchemy checks.
The [[+Saving Throw|Save]] DC of the items you create, even ones without alchemical inspiration, increase to your save DC (if it is higher).
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Alchemical Inspiration</span> [[Mana Burn]]
You can make AND drink, administer, activate or throw an alchemical item as a standard action.
You can use this feature with items that have a craft [[DC]] of 10+(your alchemy bonus) automatically. Anything higher requires a roll. Failures consume materials.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Toxin Enthusiast</span>
You have [[Resistance]] to poison damage, being [[Venom|envenomed]] and [[+Toxins]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Major</span>
Every alchemist is defined by their (often nonsensical) specialization. In order to make you, the player, feel like you are on the cutting edge of research, you can break the usual rules of the game and create entirely new class features with a specific focus. This can include things like researching how to gain additional limbs or genetically engineered catgirls.

More details in the [[Alchemist Majors]] section.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Thesis</span>
Upgrade! Abilities granted by your major are improved, loosening the restrictions.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">11 - Grand Discovery</span>
Abilities granted by your major are perfected, eliminating all but the most extreme restrictions.
