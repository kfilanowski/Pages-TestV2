_Potion man, sitting at your stand trying to expand, but they taste so bland._
You are a master of using potions to aid you allies. You can bottle your own techniques into potions to share them with allies.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Extracts</span>
You can spend a point of alchemical inspiration to bottle your knowledge. Choose a single technique you possess with the [[buff]] or [[Prepared]] tags. You can give an ally access to this technique as though using the [[Administer Potion]] action.

If it is a prepared technique, it lasts for 10 minutes. If it is a buff, it lasts its usual duration. Effects that are normally only target "self" are usable on others through extracts.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Explosive Healing</span>
[[Healing Potion|Healing potions]] you create using alchemical inspiration heal an additional 3 hp. You can throw a healing potion at an ally or a spot on the ground to have its healing juices wash over them, affecting a 5 ft radius around its impact zone.

[[Mana Potion|Mana potions]] also share this explosive quality, but restore no additional mana.
# <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Extract Injection</span>
Extracts you create with the prepared tag now last until rest.
Your ally no longer needs to take a immediate action to consume the extracts.
