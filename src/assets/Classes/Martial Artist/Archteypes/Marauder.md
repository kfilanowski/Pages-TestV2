## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Body Tether</span> [[Su]]
You have magically conjured ropes or chains, long hair, a silk spinneret, or some other flavored abstraction that you can use in place of rope or chains. You can only use your body tether in one way at a time; as soon as you do something else with it or stop touching it, the previous one ceases to exist. 
For example, you can tie someone up with your body tether, or use *Swing Line* with it, but it only lasts until you let go. This restriction can be ignored by simply using a real tether from your inventory, such as rope.
This tether can be as long as your movement speed.
Body Tether requires an empty hand to use in combat.
Body Tether counts as an [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed strike]].
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Scorpion's Grip</span>
You can increase the range of your [[drag]] or [[bully]] maneuvers to 30ft. This makes it a [[thrown]] [[ranged attack]]
If you succeed at the drag, the effect changes to moving the target adjacent to you.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Swing Line </span>
When you [[Jump]], you can also attach a tether to something you can target within a distance equal to your remaining movement speed. This functions exactly as if a rope was tied to the point, with you holding the end, and can be used as such. 

This tether only lasts as long as you are using it (such as for the purposes of other creatures climbing it) unless you use rope or a similar tether from your inventory or the environment. 
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Displacement Focus</span>
Gain the [[Maneuver Master]] feat.
Whenever you inflict [[forced movement]]...
- You can move the target 10ft farther. 
- You may move 10ft, which doesn't count against your total movement and is [[elusive]] against any targets that you inflicted forced movement on this turn.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Skill</span>
Gain a +4 bonus and proficiency in [[+Athletics]], [[+Acrobatics]] and [[+Stealth]]
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Lifeline</span> [[Elusive]]
Target an ally within 30ft. Using both your and an ally's immediate action, you can pull them to your side - assuming you can lift them, and there are no impassable barriers. 
You can also use this to grab an object or unconscious target with your Immediate Action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Weave</span>
You can [[Dash]] and [[Hide]] with the same Swift Action.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - Speed Infusion (Su)</span> 
Grant a touched ally +1 [[AoO|Attack of Opportunity]] per round (similar to and stacking with [[Combat Reflexes]]) for 10 minutes. Up to 3+[[CHA]] allies can benefit at the same time.
This effect ends when you are [[defeated]].

You gain this benefit passively.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4 - The Leash (Su)</span>
A creature restrained by you with a tether is [[pacified]] as long as you're holding the leash, and are forced to follow your movement orders.
