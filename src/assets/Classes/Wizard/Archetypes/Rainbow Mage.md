Rainbow Mages are masters of all magic. They get access to far more magic at once than any other caster.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Mage's Paradise</span>
You get an additional [[technique point]] each level, but it must be used on a tree you have no points in.
# <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 80)">Arcane Discoveries (Rainbow Magic)</span>

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Combo</span>
If you case a [[spell]] from a tree you have not cast this combat, gain +1 [[Caster Level|CL]]. When you cast a spell that you have cast this combat, you lose this CL boost. The boost depletes before determining the results of the spell (ie I cast Fire Blast for a +1 CL boost, then next turn I cast it again. I do not get the CL boost for the second cast.)
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Spice of Life</span> 
If you case a [[spell]] from a tree you have not cast this combat, reduce its [[mana]] cost by 1.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Rainbow Magic</span>
Get access to [[Taste the Rainbow]] as an [[Innate Technique]].
When you hit level 10, get access to [[Tastier Rainbow]] and [[Rainbow Beam]] as 1/day innate techniques.
