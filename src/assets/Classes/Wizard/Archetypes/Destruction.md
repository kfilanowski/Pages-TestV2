Destruction wizards look to win battles by unleashing enormous amounts of damage, usually to large groups of foes.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - With 2 Hands!</span>
While wielding a [[Staff Component]] in each hand, you increase your [[Caster Level|CL]] by an additional 1 for spells that deal damage. This increases to 2 for spells of your school specialization.

# <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 80)">Arcane Discoveries (destruction)</span>
Unless otherwise stated, arcane discoveries for destruction wizards only function on spells that deal damage.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Artillery Mage</span>
You can spend mana to increase the range of a spell you cast. For each point of mana you spend, you can increase the spells range by 10.

Spells with a range of reach gain (reach+10\*mana spent) range. If the spell is a [[melee attack]], it turns into a [[ranged attack]].

Does not function for spells without a listed range.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Precision Strike</span>
Whenever you cast a spell that has a listed AoE, your allies within that AoE are not targeted by the negative effects of the spell (including you!).

Woe to the destruction wizard who believes he has outwitted [[Frogg Saran]] by evading the many aspects of his spells that backfire. You can still use the techniques, but casting a Frogg spell demands unforeseen consequences. If you do not play along, you will make him your enemy.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Threatening Magic</span>
When you wield a [[staff component]], you [[threaten]], regardless of if it is a weapon. In place of [[Simple Attack|simple attacks]] when a creature provokes an [[AoO]], you can cast [[+Cantrips]].
