## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Cutting Edge of Time</span>
You must select [[God Mode]] as your technique tree used with Cutting Edge.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Alternate Timeline</span> [[Recharge]]
Whenever you roll a [[Skill Check]], reality branches into two possible outcomes for you. While not in combat, you can rewind to the moment just before you rolled this check and [[Advantage|Reroll]] to try and reach the alternate timeline.

You can also do this if you simply say or do something that you wish to undo, such as triggering an alarm.

You can only do this for rolls/events that happened within the last minute. If you [[Drained|drain]] 10, you can ignore the recharge tag. At level 11, this no longer has the recharge tag, but you can still only rewind a particular roll/event once.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Skill </span>
Gain the [[+History]] skill with a +4 bonus.

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6 - Accelerate</span>
Whenever you affect an ally with a beneficial spell, like [[Cure Wounds]] or [[Astral Infusion]], you can spend 3 [[mana]] as an instant action to also affect them with the [[Fast Forward]] spell. You can only affect 1 ally with beneficial [[AoE]] effects.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Contingency</span> [[Contingent]] [[Mana Burn]]
You can ignore the usual restriction placed on effects with the contingent tag and have 2 active at once. They cannot have the same trigger (or triggers that are effectively the same like "Ragnar inhales" and "Ragnar exhales").

The timelines briefly converge, giving you access to another version of yourself that comes to your aid under a very specific condition. By spending 10 minutes scanning the multiverse, you can come up with a very specific set of instructions for yourself when the time is right. This essentially acts as a [[Prepare]] action that you set well in advance, but it can only be used to cast a [[Spell]] of up to level 3.

While this spell is contingent, you are mana burned equal to its level. This contingency manifests as a unique timepiece that must exist in a visible position on the caster, such as on the wrist or around the neck. If it is not visible, then the contingency cannot take effect.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10 - Chronoshift </span> [[recharge]]
As an immediate action, you can spend 6 mana to cause a [[defeated]] ally within 60 ft to go back in time to just before they were defeated. You put them in [[stasis]] until the start of their next turn. They are then set to ([[Caster Level|CL]]) [[+HP]] and treated as though they had not been defeated.

This can even be used on a creature that has been killed outright if it happened within the last round.
