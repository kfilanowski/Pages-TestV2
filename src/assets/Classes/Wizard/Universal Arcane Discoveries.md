Can be taken by any type of wizard.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Elemental Mage</span>
Whenever you cast a spell with an elemental damage tag, like [[Fire]] or [[Frost]], you can choose to gain [[Resistance]] to that damage type as an immediate action.
Whenever you take damage from an effect with such a tag, you can choose to gain resistance to that damage type after the effect is resolved as an immediate action.
Either effect only lasts 1 minute, and is replaced the next time you gain resistance to an element through this feature.
