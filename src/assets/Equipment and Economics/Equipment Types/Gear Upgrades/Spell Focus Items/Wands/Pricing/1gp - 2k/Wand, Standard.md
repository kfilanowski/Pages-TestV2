## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2,000g</span>

This piece of enchanted wood is infused with mana from those adept at magic casting, allowing you to channel their experience through it.

While wielding a wand, you can increase the [[Caster Level|CL]] of a spell you cast by 1 as a swift action.

A wand comes with 1 [[+Cantrips|cantrip]] that you can [[Innate Technique|Innately Cast]] while you wield it.
