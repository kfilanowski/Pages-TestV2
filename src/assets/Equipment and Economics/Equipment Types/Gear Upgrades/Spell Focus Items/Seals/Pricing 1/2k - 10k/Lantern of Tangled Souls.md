## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span>

Tags: [[Su]], [[Light]], [[Elusive]]

*This seal has an obvious supernatural element thanks to the oddly colored light it emanates. On closer inspection, the light source appears to be spectral creatures floating around inside.*

Gives off light as a [[Torch]]. Can be extinguished or lit as a free action 1/turn.

While lit, you can spend 1 mana as a swift action to project the lanterns light to a space within 30 feet you can see. It lingers there for 1 round, the spirits reaching out towards allied creatures. If an ally reaches over and touches the origin of the light, they become [[Incorporeal]] and are immediately dragged adjacent to you, placing them on the side of you they were originally. When they arrive, they become corporeal again.

If you ever move more than 30 feet away from the projected light, it retracts back to the lantern.
