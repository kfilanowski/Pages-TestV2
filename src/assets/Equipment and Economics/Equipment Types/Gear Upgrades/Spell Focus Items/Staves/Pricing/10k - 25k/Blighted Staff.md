## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">12,000g</span> 

*The Blighted drained the strength of his enemies.*

This 2h battle staff causes any [[Status|status effect]] you inflict with a [[spell]] to cure your own.

The staff can cure any effect you inflict or the following (once per spell)...
- [[Shaken]] 1
- [[Clumsy]] 1
- [[Sickened]] 1
- [[Blurred Vision]]

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note - </span>
Any status you inflict means you have to make a creature suffer that particular status. For example if you are [[frosty]], you can cast [[Ice Beam]], and if you land it, you remove the frosty. The creature must actually suffer the status effect, so it does not function if it is [[Immunity|immune]].
