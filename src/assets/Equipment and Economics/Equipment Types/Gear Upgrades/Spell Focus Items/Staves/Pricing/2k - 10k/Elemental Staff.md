## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3,000g</span>

This staff has a mana crystal affixed to its end, shimmering with a color that indicates the type of element it favors. All spells with the elemental tag shared by the staff get +1 [[Caster Level|CL]]. 1/day it can reduce the mana cost of such a spell by 1.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Manathirst 12</span>
1/day you can spend 1 mana to change the elemental damage type of the spell to the stave's elemental affinity.

The standard staff is a 1h crush weapon.
Also comes in a battle staff and light variant, like most staves.
