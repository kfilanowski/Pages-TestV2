## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">9,000g</span>

*The dagger seems uninteresting unless placed in the hands of a skilled mage, where its true power is revealed.*

Whenever you cast a [[Spell]], this dagger is empowered. It gains a bonus to damage equal to 
(the spell's [[Caster Level|CL]])-(your [[LvL]]). This damage bonus is stored until you make an attack with the dagger, which then vanishes at end of your next turn.

The knife is a light pierce weapon with the [[Conceal]] and [[Chink Magnet]] property.
