## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span>

*This knife is molded after the weapons typically wielded by [[Moogen, The Murderer]]. A black blade whose hilt bears a resemblance to [[The Eye of Moogen]].*

- By closing one of your eyes, you can use the knife as a proxy for your own vision, allowing you to peek around corners using the blade. 
- The knife seems to enjoy cutting into flesh and can be used to administer [[First Aid (P)|first aid]].
- Against a creature you administer first aid, or [[On-hit]], you getting a brief image in your head of the last creature the target killed, if any.

The knife is a light pierce weapon with the [[Chink Magnet]] and [[Versatile]] (slash) property.
