## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span>

*This knife makes the wielder feel uneasy, like it knows what its thinking.

Whenever you [[Crit]] a [[DD]] creature, the knife randomly chooses a [[Technique Rules|technique]] the creature knows to memorize, up to your highest tier technique known. While you wield the knife, you also have that technique memorized without spending memory.

The knife has a will of its own, and will memorize new techniques if you keep critting whether you want it to or not.

The knife is a light pierce weapon with the [[Conceal]] and [[Chink Magnet]] property.
