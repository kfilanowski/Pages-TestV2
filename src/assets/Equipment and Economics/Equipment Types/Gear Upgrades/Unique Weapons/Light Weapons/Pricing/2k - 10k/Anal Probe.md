## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3,000g</span>

This baton looks unimpressive until you press a button on its side to coat it in a strange liquid and electrify it as a swift action. It can remain electrified for 1 minute before it must recharge for 5. When you thrust it, it seems to magnetize to the target's orifices. You must hold it in 2 hands to keep it electrified.

It is a 1h light club when not electrified.

When electrified it instead deals 2d6 zap dmg. It also gains a special attack.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack:</span> Terrestrial Exploration
Shove the probe in whatever hole the creature is most uncomfortable with. They are [[clumsy]] 2 [[on-hit]].


<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span>
You can use [[Engineering]] to craft this item instead of [[+Profession]].
