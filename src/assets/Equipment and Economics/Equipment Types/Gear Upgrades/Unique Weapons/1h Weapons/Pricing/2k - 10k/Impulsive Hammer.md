## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">7,500g</span>

*This hammer is eager to smash, and prefers to be swung overhead, resisting other types of swinging motions.

When the wielder lands a [[Crit]], the hammer releases a shockwave. The creature you attacked and any enemy within 10 ft are automatically [[Knocked Down]].

The hammer is a 1h crush weapon with the [[CM]] (topple) and [[One and Done]] properties.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
I'm normally all for changing weapons around to suit your needs, but this weapon should absolutely not be allowed to made as a light weapon with the Chink Magnet property. That would be *dangerously cheesy.*
