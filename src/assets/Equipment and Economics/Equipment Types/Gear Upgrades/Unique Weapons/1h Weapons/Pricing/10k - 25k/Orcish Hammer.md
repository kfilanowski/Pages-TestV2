## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">22,000g</span>

*This hammer is infused with the power of the wind itself by orcish shamans.*

It feels practically weightless while you swing it through air, despite its otherwise unwieldy size. Its wielder can innately cast [[Storm Step]] 1/day. 
The wielder also gains this special attack...

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Windfury</span> -
Attack twice in rapid succession, applying DR only once.

The hammer is a 1h crush weapon with the [[Strings Attached]] and [[One and Done]] quality.
