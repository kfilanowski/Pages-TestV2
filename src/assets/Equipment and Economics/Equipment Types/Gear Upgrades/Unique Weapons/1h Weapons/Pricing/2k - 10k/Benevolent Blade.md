## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2,200g</span>

*This silver sword has a hilt that shines a radiant gold. 

This weapon is used to non-lethally dispatch creatures when defeating them since it has no chance of potentially killing them. When you [[defeat]] a creature with this weapon, you can spend 1 mana to give them the [[Time Out]] condition.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Lifesteal</span> [[Healing]]
On hit heal (2+[[LvL]]).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Benevolence (healing)</span>
Strike at an ally as the blade transforms into a warming radiance. It grants the attacks damage as [[temp hp]] and has no harmful on hit effects.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Manathirst 22</span>
You can use the Lifesteal and Benevolence special attacks at-will.
