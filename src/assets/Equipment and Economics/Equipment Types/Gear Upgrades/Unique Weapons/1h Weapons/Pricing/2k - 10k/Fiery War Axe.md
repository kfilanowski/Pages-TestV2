## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">5000g</span>

*Favored by veteran [[Fighter]]s, this axe is the color of obsidian, with a blade that looks white hot. 

When pointing the weapon at any creature that is considered a [[minion]] to you, it ignites in flames. Against any such foe, the axe deals an additional d10 fire damage.

The axe is a 1h slash weapon with the [[One and Done]] and [[CM]] (topple) property.
