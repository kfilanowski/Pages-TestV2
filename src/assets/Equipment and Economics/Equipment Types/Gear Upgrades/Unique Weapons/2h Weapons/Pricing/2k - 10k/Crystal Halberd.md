## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">5,000g</span> 

*This elven halberd is practically weightless and can be swung with incredible speed.*

The crystal halberd is so lightweight that it is practically weightless. Wielders must use [[DEX]] to attack with it, or risk breaking it. This does not decrease its base damage.

If you can speak elven, you can whisper to the weapon to unlock the its true potential with the following special attack.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Rapid Strike</span> - Make two attacks against the same creature, the second with a -4 penalty.

It has [[improved reach]] quality and the [[Unique Special Attacks|outmaneuver]] special attack.
