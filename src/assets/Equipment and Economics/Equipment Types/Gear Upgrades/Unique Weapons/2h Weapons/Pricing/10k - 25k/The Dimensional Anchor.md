## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">20,000g</span>

A literal anchor, clearly for a smaller vessel, that weighs 50 lbs.

- The anchor passively alerts whoever holds it whenever teleportation occurs within 100 ft of it. It also alerts the holder when they are within 100 ft of a portal. This detection functions through solid barriers, but not [[Lead]].
- Whenever you are subject to a [[Teleportation]] effect against your will, you can slam the anchor into the ground as an immediate action to negate it.
- Enemies the anchor strikes are [[Anchored]] [[On-hit]] for 1 minute.

The anchor is a 2h crush weapon with the [[Unique Special Attacks|magic crash]] special attack and the [[CM]] (drag) quality.
