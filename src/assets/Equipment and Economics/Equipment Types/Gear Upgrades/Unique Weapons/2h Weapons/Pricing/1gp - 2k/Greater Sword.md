## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,000g</span>

*An oversized blade with swings so clumsy it would give any self-respecting blacksmith a heart attack.*

This sword's swings are always going to hurt someone which each swing. If you [[Graze]] or hit a foe with a weapon, it deals an additional 1 damage. If you miss, you become [[Clumsy]] 1.

The sword is a 2h slash weapon with the [[Unique Special Attacks|determination]] special attack and the [[Versatile]] (crush) quality.
