## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">7,000g</span>

This long, pointed piece of wood is covered in a sticky sap. The wielder cannot change weapons using [[Swap Item]] without first spending a standard action to attach it to another object and pulling it off.
However the spear is very fickle. Whenever you [[disarm]] a creature, it leaves you behind and hops directly into your target's open hand.

It is a 2h pierce weapon with the [[Reach]], [[CM]] (disarm) and [[Attached]] properties.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span>
You use [[Alchemy]] or [[+Nature]] to craft this item instead of [[+Profession]].
