## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span>

*This curved iron sword looks as though the black iron exterior could peel off at any moment and is covered with patches of rust.*

The sword has a -2 penalty to damage. When used to cut down creatures that can bleed, it changes. The iron in the blood repairs the weapon and then some. [[On-hit]] the rust fades and the blade sharpens, adding +1 damage, up to 4 times for a total of +2 bonus damage.

The sword is a 2h slash weapon that has the [[Bleed Weapon]] and [[CM]] (dirty trick) property.
