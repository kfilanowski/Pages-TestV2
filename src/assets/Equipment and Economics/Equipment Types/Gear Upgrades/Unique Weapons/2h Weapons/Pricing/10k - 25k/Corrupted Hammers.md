## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">12,000g</span> 

*The Corrupted is relentless. His repeated strikes debilitate foes.*

[[On-hit]] against a creature who is [[Clumsy]], reduce the creature's speed. The speed reduction is 5 per stack of clumsy they possess. This takes into account if the attack itself inflicts clumsy (ie [[Base Special Attacks|concussive strike]]). This penalty lasts until the clumsy is removed.

They are a pair of light crush weapons with the [[thrown weapon]] property and the [[Unique Special Attacks|hack n smash]] special attack.


Part of the following [[+Unique Item Sets]]
- [[Corrupted Set]]



<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note</span> - The intention behind this weapon's effect is that the speed penalty will not stack with itself after hitting multiple times. It's more you apply a unique status that slows by 5 per clumsy stack.

Ex. Frost giant is clumsy 2. I bonk him with hammers once and they get -10 speed. Hitting him 20 more times doesn't decrease his speed any more until I make him clumsier.
