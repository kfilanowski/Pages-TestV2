## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,000g</span>

*This club has metal plates at its end, each face inscribed with magic sigils to enhance this heavy weapons power.*

Unfortunately, ogres' propensity for randomness creates a different definition of power than most. While the you are [[Threaten|threatening]] multiple enemies, you get a +2 bonus to [[Attack]], but you decide which enemy you attack randomly among all creatures you threaten.

The club is a 2h crush weapon with [[Unique Special Attacks|bewildering blow]] special attack and the [[CM]] (shove) quality.
