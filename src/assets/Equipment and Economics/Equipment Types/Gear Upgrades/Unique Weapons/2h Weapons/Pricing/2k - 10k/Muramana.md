## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span> 

Tags: -
*This spear has a blue glowing sheen to it when held by a creature. The more mana the creature has, the more brightly it shines. Creatures with incredibly high mana cause the spear to ooze liquid mana.*

As long as you have not spent any mana this turn, you get a bonus to damage based on your current mana. For every 5 mana you currently have, you deal an additional 1 damage.

It is a 2h pierce weapon with the [[Reach]] and [[Strings Attached]] properties.
