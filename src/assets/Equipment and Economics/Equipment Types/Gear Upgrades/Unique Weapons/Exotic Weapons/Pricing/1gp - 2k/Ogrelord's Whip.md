## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,800g</span> 

Unlike the standard [[Whip]], this whip is capable of threatening its full [[reach]] off turn, but only your allies. When you make an [[AoO]] against an ally, it always does 5 slashing damage and increases their speed by 20 this round. You also gain a charge, up to 3. Each charge you can spend as an free action to increase the bonus of your next attack by +2 (max +6).

Charges are lost when you roll initiative.
