## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6,000g</span>

*This gun has a barrel that grows to comically large size at its tip.*

You get a +4 bonus on survival checks to [[Tracking (P)|track]] any creature you have hit or grazed with this weapon in the last week.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)"><br>Special Attack: My Brand</span> - [[On-hit]] apply a brand to a creature, physically marking them. You and your [[companion]] creatures' attacks do an additional d6 damage until the end of your next turn.

The rifle functions as a 2h [[Gun]].
