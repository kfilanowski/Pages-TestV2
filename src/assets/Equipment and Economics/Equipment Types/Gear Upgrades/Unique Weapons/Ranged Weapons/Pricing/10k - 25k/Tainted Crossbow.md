## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">12,000g</span> 

*Against the Tainted, there is always a bolt midflight on its way to its target.*

The tainted crossbow is a 2h ranged weapon with the [[Reload]] property, but it removes the restriction of only firing once per round. It can be fired 4 times before it needs to be reloaded.

It has [[long]] range and the [[Unique Special Attacks|rapid fire]] special attack.
