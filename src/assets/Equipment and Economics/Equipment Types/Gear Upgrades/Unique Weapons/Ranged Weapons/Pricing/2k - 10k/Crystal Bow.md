## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6,000g</span> 

*Initially one might question making a bow out of rocks, but the elves that make them put these magical bows to great use.*

Tags: [[Su]], [[Force]]
A crystal bow does not require any form of ammunition in order to fire. When the wielder pantomimes pulling an arrow, one forms of pure magic force.

If you can speak elven, you can whisper to the weapon to unlock the its true potential with the following special attack.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Crystal Shard </span>- This attack deals [[true damage]].

It has [[long]] range and the [[Unique Special Attacks|volley]] special attack.
