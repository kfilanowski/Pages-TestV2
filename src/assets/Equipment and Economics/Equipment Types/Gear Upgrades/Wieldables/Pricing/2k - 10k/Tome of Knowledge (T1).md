## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">7,000g</span>

*The tome is on the smaller side and decorated in accordance to what knowledge is contained within. While its pages are in view, you feel a cascade of new knowledge dance through your brain, only for it to disappear the moment you put it in your pocket.*

The tome of knowledge provides you with the knowledge to use any single level 1 [[Technique Rules|technique]], chosen when the tome is created. To use the knowledge within, you must study it every morning when you prepare your techniques, it it consumes 2 [[Memory]] slots instead of 1.

You can only activate the technique when you wield the book in your hand.
