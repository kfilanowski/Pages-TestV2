Tags: [[Recharge]], [[Su]], [[Polymorph|poly, chimeric]]

By spending 1 mana as a swift action, you can cause long spider legs to pop out of your back. You can use these legs to [[Climb]] using your [[Land Speed]] without using your hands or legs, allowing you to freely swing weapons, cast spells etc while climbing.

These legs last one minute, and cannot be summoned again for 1 minute after they retract.
