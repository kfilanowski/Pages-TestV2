Tags: [[Su]], [[Teleportation]]

You can rub a symbol on the armor in a specific pattern as a standard action in order to get sucked into the armor, which clatters to the ground. You appear in a tiny extradimensional space with breathable air, but little else. Objects separated from your person in this space fade away into nothing within a few seconds.

You cannot perceive anything in the outside world while in this space and you can exist here indefinitely, though you will eventually get hungry/thirsty. By holding up a finger and making the same motions to activate the armor, you can reappear wherever the armor is. If the armor is in a space you can't fit, you appear in the nearest space where you can. This also happens if the armor is destroyed or [[Dispel|dispelled]].

You do not need to see your destination to use this effect.
