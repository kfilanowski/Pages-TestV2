Tags: [[Su]], [[Glamer]]

This armor enchantment allows the wearer to feed outfits to the armor. When you do, you can spend a standard action to change the armor to appear as that particular outfit, or back again.

When you feed a new outfit to the armor, the old outfit pops out. You can also turn the armor upside down while it is not being worn to shake the outfit out.

An outfit is generally considered to contain footwear, legwear and some type of body wear. Can also include things like hats, gloves, glasses etc. You can even feed it suits of armor to match uniform of some local guards for instance.
