Whenever the wearer takes damage, a mark is left behind on the armor or on the wearer's skin showing the attacker's name, if it has one. If the wearer takes damage from a few dozen creatures, the names start to overlap and become illegible.

If the attacker is wearing a [[+Disguise]], then the wearer must make a [[+Perception]] check with a DC of (10+attacker's disguise bonus). Failure indicates the name is illegible.
