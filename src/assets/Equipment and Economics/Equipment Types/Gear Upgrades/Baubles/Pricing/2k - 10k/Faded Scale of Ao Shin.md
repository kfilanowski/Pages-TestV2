## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span>

*Sometimes bestowed upon the followers of [[Ao Shin the Storm Dragon]] as a gift, but this one has lost its luster. For a single scale it is still quite large, taking up most of the hand if placed on an empty palm. Rubbing it slightly causes this gold and blue scale to crackle with electricity.*

Spend 1 mana to gain access to the following [[special attack]], usable at-will.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Lightning Strike</span> - Electrify your next [[melee attack]]. [[On-hit]] the electricity arcs to another target within 15 ft. All secondary targets take the entirety of the attacks damage as lightning damage (reflex halves). They do not suffer any of its [[on-hit]] effects. You choose the targets.


<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Quest</span> - Receive the favor of the Ao Shin to improve this item. Granting the following benefits.

You can remove the special attack tag from lightning strike, allowing you to combine it with the effect with other special attacks. When you do so, you increase the amount of mana you spend from 1 to 6.

When you make special attacks that target multiple creatures (like [[Base Special Attacks|cleave]], or [[Kazekenshin]]'s steel tempest), the additional lightning bolt strikes cannot target the same creatures as the attack roll(s) made in the special attack.
