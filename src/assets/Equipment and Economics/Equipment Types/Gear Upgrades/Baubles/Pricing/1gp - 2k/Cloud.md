## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,500g</span>

As an instant action, you can cause a solid cloud to manifest underneath your feet. The cloud lasts until the end of your next turn. You can use it to negate [[Fall Damage]], or to give yourself a boost, allowing you to jump twice with a single [[Jump]] action.

This has a 1 minute cooldown.
