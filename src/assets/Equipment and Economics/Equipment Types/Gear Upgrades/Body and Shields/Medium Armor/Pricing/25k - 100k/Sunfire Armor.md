## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">60,000g</span>

*This breastplate comes with a cape. Both pulse with light, which intensifies in the heat of battle.*

You emit [[Light Level|Bright Light]] at a range of 40 ft, and [[Light Level|Dim Light]] for 40 feet past that.
