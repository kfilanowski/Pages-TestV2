## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">55,000g</span>

Tags: -

*This enormous metal shield is plated with shinier metals, but on in inside is made of nigh indestructible metal using forging techniques perfected by generations of dwarven smiths.*

You can use any  [[+Shield Actions|shield actions]] against [[+Siege Weapon|siege weapons]] without your shield breaking. The shield cannot be broken through conventional means, nor does it rust.
