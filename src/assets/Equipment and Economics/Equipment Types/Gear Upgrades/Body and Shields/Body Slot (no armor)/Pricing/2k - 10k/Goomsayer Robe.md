## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3,000g</span>

Tags: [[Su]], [[Glamer]]

*This robe has very long sleeves and a hood that casts a shadow over the face. It has colors that match those of a [[Goo Dragon]]. Typically only worn by [[Goomsayer]]s (agents of the [[Shroomlette]]) and their allies.*

Goomsayer robes allow any creature of medium size or smaller to obscure their appearance completely, appearing as a generic robed figure, provided they wear the robe with the hood pulled over their face. Even bright lights are unable to pierce this hood. It droops over the legs, and even conceals other features that could be revealed through traditional clothing, like horns or tails.

For creatures that aren't humanoid, it allows them to appear as though they were humanoid shaped. Effectively turning them into a robed figure. The robe allows the user to manipulate objects as though they had hands. If the wearer does have hands, they do not appear visible even when you peek into the sleeves. Held objects appear attached to the robe.

If a creature really wants to reveal who you are, they can either do it covertly through [[+Sleight of Hand]], or overtly through [[poise]] breaking you, revealing your head under the hood.
