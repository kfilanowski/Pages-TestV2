## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2,500g+</span>

Tags: -
Can count as non-armor, [[Armor|light armor]], [[Armor|medium armor]] or [[Armor|heavy armor]], increasing the price accordingly.

*The diving suit comes in a wide variety of styles and sizes, accommodating for all types of armor users.*

The diving suit is an air-tight suit designed for underwater use. It allows the wearer to breathe underwater for 30 minutes before they need to resurface and recharge. They also ignore penalties for [[Underwater Combat]].
