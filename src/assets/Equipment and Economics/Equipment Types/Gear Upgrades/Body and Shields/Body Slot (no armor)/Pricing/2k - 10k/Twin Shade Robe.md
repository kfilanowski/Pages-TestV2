## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3,000g</span>

Tags: [[Su]], [[Recharge]]

*Ghosts decorate this otherwise dark colorless robe. When not looking directly at it, they seem to shift to and fro.*

As a swift action, you can spend 1 mana to release the [[Incorporeal]] ghosts inside of this robe. They move 100 feet each round towards the nearest non-allied creatures that are at least [[LvL|Level]] 0. When they make contact with a creature, they disappear and return to the robe. 

If the ghosts travelled at least 30 feet, creatures touched have their speed reduced by 15 and become [[Glowing]] for 1 round. Regardless of distance, you see a bright glowing outline of the creature for 1 minute, even through solid terrain.

Once used, it takes the ghosts 10 minutes to recharge their spookyness.
