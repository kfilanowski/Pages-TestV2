## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,000g</span>

A fuzzy patch to place over your eye that wraps around the head, lined with material to block even the harshest light and the pocket-est sand.

You take a -2 penalty to [[+Perception]]. When you are afflicted with the [[Blurred Vision]] or [[Blinded]] condition, you can move the patch over to to the other eye and ignore the condition. 

Certain effects that don't directly target the eyes, like [[Curse of Blindness]] make an eyepatch useless.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
You need 2 eyes for this to work. You can buy normal eye patches to cover wounded/missing eyes for 1gp.
