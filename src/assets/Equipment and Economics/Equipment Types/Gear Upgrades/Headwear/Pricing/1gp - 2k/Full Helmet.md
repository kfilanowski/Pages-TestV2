## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,000g</span>

Counts as [[Armor|heavy armor]].

A full helmet completely covers the head, including the face, leaving only slits or holes for vision/breathing. Many are decorated with plumage or cosmetic royal/animal iconography.

Full helmets block your peripheral vision, giving a -2 penalty to [[+Perception]]. Gives a +1 bonus to [[Fortitude]] saves.
