## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,000g</span>

This is a [[The Black Market|black market]] item.

This mask is a unique item that can only be created by a [[Maligner]]. It looks as thought the skin on someone's face was removed with careful precision and perfectly preserved.

When you wear this mask, you partially transform into that creature, which counts as a [[+Disguise]] and grants a +4 bonus [[Skill Check|skill checks]] to appear as that specific creature. Putting it on is a [[FRA]], but removing it is a [[Swap Item]] action. It does not change your clothes or equipment.

It is virtually impossible to acquire masks of a specific creature, considering they are usually harvested from fresh corpses. Masks that are in circulation are typically of deceased individuals. 

Masks of particularly famous, influential, beautiful or rare creatures are often much more expensive. Even more so if the creature still breathes.
