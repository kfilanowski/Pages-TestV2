## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span>

Tags: [[Holy]], [[Light]], [[Recharge]], [[Persistent]], [[Su]]
Counts as [[Armor|medium armor]].

This helmet constantly emits light, as a [[Torch]].

When you take damage, you can choose to spend 1 mana to turn off this light as an instant action to burst in a burning radiance, causing shining orbs to litter the nearest space occupied by your attacker, where it then continues to emit light. Creatures other than you that move into this space take 5 Holy damage. This is a [[Persistent]] effect.

You must move into this space to collect the orbs to restore the light on the helm. If you fail to do so, the helm becomes inert and must be recharged through exposure to sunlight for 7 days.
