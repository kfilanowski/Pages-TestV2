## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">33,000g</span>

Tags: [[Su]]

*These translucent, almost ethereal chains were originally designed by escaped prisoners who refused to allow themselves to be recaptured by wandering wardens. Now anybody with a vendetta against a specific creature can benefit from them.*

To activate any of the Free Man's Chains abilities, you must first conduct a ritual to bind yourself to a specific creature. This ritual requires 10 minutes of concentration where you require the creature's name or alias and at least a basic idea of their appearance. Once you perform this ritual, you cannot perform it again until you [[Defeat]], kill or capture the creature you are bound to.

Against this creature you gain [[DR|DRA]] 3. Whenever you would be [[Stunned]] or [[Dazed]] by this creature, you are instead [[Staggered]].

If the creature you are bound to every becomes incarcerated, dies or some other form of extreme circumstance, the chains react. You aren't aware of what happened, but you are aware that you can perform the ritual again.
