## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2,000g</span> 

*An elaborate tattoo that depicts a sword embedded in the ground. By making the motion of pulling it out, the tattoo disappears from the body and appears in your hands.*

You can get a tattoo that functions like a magical sheathe for a single weapon, a ranged weapon with ammunition, or 2 light weapons. The tattoo must be visible in order to function, and it must be at least the size of your hand. This can cause some complications if you wanted to keep the weapon hidden by putting it on your ass but you are a heavy armor user. The tattoo is not bound to a single weapon, and will change to more closely depict whatever weapon is stored within.

In all other ways, it functions as though it was your hip, allowing you to freely take the [[Swap Item]] action with any weapon(s) within.

A creature can peel your skin off to remove the tattoo, causing the weapon to fall out.
