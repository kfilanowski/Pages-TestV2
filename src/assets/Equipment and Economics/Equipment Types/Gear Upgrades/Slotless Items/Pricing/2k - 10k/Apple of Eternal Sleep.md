## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">6,000g</span> 

*What a tasty looking apple!*

It looks and tastes like a normal apple. Creatures with the [[+Nature]] skill can roll a DC 16 check to realize this apple's unusual nature. A creature with [[Scent]] can detect it is unusual as well.

A creature who eats even a single bite of this apple will fall asleep almost immediately, absorbing the magic from the apple. While asleep, the creature is frozen in time, cannot age or die of thirst/hunger or even lack of air. The creature cannot be woken up unless kissed by someone with royal blood.
