## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">5,000g</span> 

*A floating brain in a jar catches your eye. Before you get a chance to ask about it, a voice comes from it asking "what are you lookin' at?"*

The brain vat is the method of capture of choice for particularly cruel alchemists. In order to create a brain vat, the vessel must be prepared long before the brain itself is extracted. This means you must craft the item in advance, only adding the brain as the final component. To safely extract the brain from a creature, it must be alive or freshly killed. You must roll a DC 10 [[First Aid (P)|first aid]] check, otherwise the brain might have some defects.

When you successfully extract a brain, you can safely store it away on your shelves. The vat itself comes with an apparatus that allows the brain to "hear" the world around it, and one that allows it to respond back in its original voice. Both features have a switch that allow you to turn them off and on, letting you mute, deafen or both.

Requires swapping out the brain juice for new fresh ingredients every 6 months or so. Forgetting leads to a very insane brain.
