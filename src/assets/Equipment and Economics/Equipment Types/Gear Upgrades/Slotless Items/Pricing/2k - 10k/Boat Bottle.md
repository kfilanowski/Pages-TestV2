## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">4,000g</span> 

Tags: -

*A relatively mundane looking bottle with a few scribbles on it.*

This bottle can be activated by spending a minute in direct contact with a boat you own. After this minute the boat shrinks down in size to fit inside the bottle and is placed in [[stasis]]. Any creatures on board are deposited on the ground/in the water where the boat was. You then specify a password/phrase to activate the boat. When you do so, the boat pops out of the boat and splashes into the nearest body of water. If none are present, nothing happens.

Once a ship is freed from the bottle, it turns to dust.
