## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">7,000g</span> 

Tags: [[Fear]]

This flag is inscribed with symbols of [[Demon Lords]], prominent criminal organizations or evil mages. Those that exist within 30 ft of the banner while it is planted (a standard action) hear whispers from it, causing them to become [[shaken]] 1, unless they are allied with the group the banner's iconography showcases.

This has no effect on creatures that are already shaken. If the creature was not already shaken when it entered the area and it leaves the area, the shaken the banner inflicts immediately dissipates as the whispers vanish.

A creature can roll a DC 15 [[+Athletics]] check to push the banner over and disable its effect.
