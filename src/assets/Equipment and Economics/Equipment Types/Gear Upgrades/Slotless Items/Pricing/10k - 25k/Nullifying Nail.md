## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">12,000g</span> 

*An oversized nail made of adamantine, separated from its brothers and sisters, seems a bit overpriced for just a single nail.*

By spending 3 mana as a standard action, you can roll an [[Brute Force]] athletics check to impale this nail into a solid surface. You make this check with [[Advantage]] if you wield any type of hammer.

While embedded, ALL creatures within a 30 ft radius of the space you embedded the nail in have a chance their [[Spell|spells]] [[Fizzle]]. Every time they cast a spell, they must make a [[Caster Level]] check, rolling the CL of the spell against a DC of (10+your [[LvL]]).

Any creature can remove the nail as a standard action by beating your athletics roll.
