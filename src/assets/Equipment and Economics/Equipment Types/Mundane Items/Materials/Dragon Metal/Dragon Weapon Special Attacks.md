<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Sever</span> - This attack gains +2 attack. [[On-hit]] sever the connection a creature has to their deity. They cannot [[Invoking Deities|invoke deity]] nor can they use [[Prayer]] or [[Deific Boons]] until they [[Rest]]. 

Similar abilities that rely on divine power like [[Paladin]] smite are unaffected.
