## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">80g</span>

Tools typically come in sets catered to a specific task, such as fishing, lockpicking or medicine. Some tasks require the use of tools, others only give a penalty if you don't have tools.

Tool kits weigh 10 lbs and require 2 [[Inventory|inventory slots]]. Some tasks, such as smithing, require even more advanced tools you can't carry around like a forge.
