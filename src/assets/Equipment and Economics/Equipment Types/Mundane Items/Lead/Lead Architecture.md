Lead structures are the most expensive, but most effective way of making sure an area is safe from scrying. This is done by lining the building materials with layers of lead during construction, or as a later add on. [[Scrying]] attempts inside of lead structures always fail. Even going near the structure causes scrying magic to be faulty, obscuring the senses.

A singular room costs <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">25,000g</span>

An average home costs <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">60,000g</span>

Large or complex structures such as prisons, dungeons or palaces have a starting price of <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">150,000g</span>, which can easily increase based off the scale of the project.

As long as the building is intact, the risk of [[Lead|lead poisoning]] is basically zero. During times of strife though, the lead can poison the air, making it unsafe to breathe.
