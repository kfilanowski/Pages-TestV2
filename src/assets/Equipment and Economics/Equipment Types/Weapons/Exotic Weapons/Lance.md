## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Lance - Pierce, 2h, Reach (10 lbs, 500g)</span>

- While mounted, a lance can be wielded in one-hand while still benefiting from 2h damage.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Joust</span> - When you make this attack while mounted and during a [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]], your movement is [[elusive]] against your target. [[On-hit]] dismounts the target.
