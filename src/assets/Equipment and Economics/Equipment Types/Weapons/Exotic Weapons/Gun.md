Guns come in 3 variants: Pistol, rifle and blunderbuss

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Pistol - 2d6 pierce, Reload (5 lbs, 500g)</span>

- [[Short]] range
- Requires an even more complex reloading process than crossbows. Their reload requires a [[FRA]]
- Pistols are 1h weapons, but they cannot be used to dual wield
- Also comes in a 2h rifle version that has the 2h property and does 2d8 dmg (10 lbs, 800g)

## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Blunderbuss - 2d6 pierce, 2h, Reload (10 lbs, 800g)</span>

- Requires an even more complex reloading process than crossbows. Their reload requires a [[FRA]]
- Simultaneously attacks every creature in a 15ft cone, comparing a single attack roll to all affected creatures' [[AC]]
- Does not provoke [[AoO]]s from shooting in melee
- Not compatible with [[Special Attack|special attacks]] or features that increase range (such as [[Artillery]])
