## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Harpoon - d10 pierce, 2h, Bleed (15 lbs, 500g)</span>

- [[Thrown]] weapon
- Harpoons come with a particularly strong 40 ft rope attached, allowing you to drag the weapon back to you in case you miss (standard action). Holding onto this rope after you toss uses a hand.
- Harpoons stick into the target. The bleed from this weapon cannot be stopped until after it is removed with [[Breakout]] as a swift action. The target also can't move farther than 40 ft away from you without forcing you somehow.
- While embedded, you can make a [[drag]] attacks against the target without penalty from being at a range. The target can do the same against you.
