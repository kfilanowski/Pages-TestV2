## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Boomerang - Light, Thrown (5 lbs, 500g)</span>

- Boomerangs return to the user when thrown automatically, allowing multiple attacks with a single thrown weapon.
- Boomerangs curve, allowing you to hit opponents with total cover, so long as you aim for the correct space
- Boomerangs do not return when caught with something like a [[goo dragon]]'s sticky quality
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Wait For It...</span> - Throw a boomerang at an unusual angle, initially missing a target. At the start of your next turn make an attack against them, but they're [[DD]].
