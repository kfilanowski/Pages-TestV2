An improvised weapon can be anything relatively dangerous when thrown or swung. It could mean a stick, a rock, a chair or a shiv. Improvised weapons function as one of the following weapon types.

Light (shiv)- D6 dmg, [[Light Weapon]] property
1h (stick)- d8 dmg
2h (chair)- 2d8 dmg
Throwing (rock) - D6 dmg, [[Thrown]]

After any turn you make an attack with an improvised weapon, roll a DC 10 [[Luck Check]] to see if it breaks.
