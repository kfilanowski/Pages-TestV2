Weapons come in 4 main categories: light, 1-handed, 2-handed, ranged.

The weapon type determines how many additional qualities it can have, its weight and its damage. I've made a handful of example weapons in the next page. Exotic weapons break the mold. All weapons are given points based on their type, they are all listed here for your convenience.
