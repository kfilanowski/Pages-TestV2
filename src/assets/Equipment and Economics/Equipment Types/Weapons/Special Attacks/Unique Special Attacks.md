This section is dedicated to helping you fill in the "[[special attack]]" weapon quality, or for giving unique weapons special abilities. See the [[Base Special Attacks]] section for the special attacks weapons get by default.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">All Melee</span>

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Determination</span> - Focus your conviction.  You get a +4 bonus and [[Seeking]] to your next attack with this weapon by the end of your next turn. You can also choose to not attack at all with this special attack, granting your next attack with this weapon a +10 bonus instead.
If you choose to delay this bonus, Determination does not consume your special attack next round.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Stacking Strike</span> - Make an attack. If this [[Defeated|defeats]] the creature, stacking strike permanently gains 1 damage. Weapon specific, so you have to stack weapons individually. You cannot farm stacks passively and must gain them in combats you actually roll out.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Bewildering Blow </span>- [[On-hit]] creature is [[confused]] for one round (will negates).

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Magic Crash</span> - Roll an attack roll in place of a [[Dispel]] check against a magical effect you can attack, including one on a creature.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Meteor Swing</span> - On hit your target is unable to make [[AoO]]s for 1 round.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ankle Slicer</span> - On hit your target has -15 speed for 1 round.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Lifesteal</span> ([[Healing]]) - On hit heal (2+[[LvL]]).

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Throat Slicer</span> - On hit [[Silenced|silence]] a creature for one round (fort negates).

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">All In</span> - On hit instead of rolling for damage, you deal maximum damage. If you graze, you instead miss entirely. When you miss, you take 20 poise damage.

## Weapon Specific
The above special attacks can be generalized to any type of weapon. Even the "slicer" special attacks could be achieved just as effectively if you crushed a throat or foot. In general I try to be lax with what weapons you give your character so mechanics to not interfere with flavor. However, the following weapons will specify a type of weapon you lock into, and are incompatible with each other.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Volley</span> - Fire many shots at once, targeting a 15 ft cone in front of you. Compare a single attack roll to all creatures in AoE.
This requires a bow, which is a 2h ranged weapon without the reload property.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Rapid Fire</span> - Fire two shots in rapid succession at the same creature, each with a -2 penalty to attack.
This requires a bow, which is a 2h ranged weapon without the reload property.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Wild Strike</span> - Swing around a flail without concern for the consequences. Deals full damage on a graze. If you miss, you instead treat the attack as if you did half damage to yourself.
This requires a flail, which is a 1h or 2h melee crush weapon.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Hack n Smash</span> - If a creature takes a [[+Shield Actions|shield action]] in response to this attack, they are made [[clumsy]] 2 on hit. They cannot use shield actions until this clumsy is removed.
This requires a hammer, which is a 1h or 2h melee crush weapon.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Outmaneuver</span> - While not being flanked, move 5 ft [[elusive]] and make a melee attack.
This requires a polearm, which 2h melee reach weapon.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Dig Deep</span> - Embed your weapon into your foe punch it in further, making them [[clumsy]] 2. They can remove it as a standard action, or a swift action with [[+Medicine]] proficiency. You let go of the weapon as part of using this special attack.
This requires a pick style weapon, which is a 1h or 2h melee pierce weapon.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Execution</span> - Finish them. If this attack [[Defeated|defeats]] the target, they stay defeated for at least 1 minute unless someone administers [[First Aid (P)|first aid]]. 
This requires a heavy sword or axe style weapon, which is a 2h slashing weapon.

Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Overrun</span> - Move your opponent 5 ft in a direction that isn't towards you on hit.
This requires a heavy ramming or hammer type weapon, which is a 2h crush weapon.
