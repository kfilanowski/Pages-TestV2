All weapons come with some of these special attacks, based on the damage type they deal, or their weapon type (ranged, melee, shield etc).

Special attacks by default recharge after being out of combat for 1 minute. When used out of combat (for the utility), you can use them an unlimited number of times.

You can swap weapons to gain more special attacks, but you cannot use special attacks with the same name more than once in the same combat. Most special attacks require a melee attack, unless the weapon is ranged.

The ALL in these subcategories refer to only manufactured weapons, not [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]] and
[[Unarmed Attack|unarmed attacks]].
## All <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Slash</span> Melee
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Lacerate</span> - On the hits apply d6 [[Bleeding|bleed]] damage for 2 turns (fort negates)
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Cleave</span> - Attack two separate creatures you [[threaten]] from your current position.
## All <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Crush</span> melee
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Concussive Strike</span> - On hit apply [[clumsy]] 1.
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Backbreaker</span> - Deal 15 [[poise]] damage on-hit.
## All <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Pierce</span> Melee
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Lunge</span> - Increase your reach by 5 ft for this attack and all others made this turn
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Pierce Defense</span> - Ignore [[DR]]. If your opponent uses an immediate or instant action in response to this attack, gain +4 attack.
## All <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ranged</span>
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Maim</span> - On hit reduce the creature's speed by 15ft for one round (fort negates).
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Snipe</span> - Ignore -2 penalty to attack for a long ranged shot
## All <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Shields</span>
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Endure</span> - Slam a target with your shield and continue to hold it up, focusing on them. Gain [[resistance]] to all damage from this target for 1 round, even if you miss.
Special Attack: <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Shield Bash</span> - Inflict clumsy 1 on hit.
