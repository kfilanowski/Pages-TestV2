Mana is an naturally occurring, invisible and hard to use resource akin to air. Crystallized mana is mana condensed into a pure and physically tangible form. Crystallized mana is usually formed by using special rituals to extract mana from creatures, inflicting [[mana burn]] on them. This is usually referred to as [[Mana Donation]]. 

Crystallized mana is a resource that is used in crafting magic items (located in the [[gear upgrades]] section). It is also often a resource sought out for by larger societies in order to participate in large scale magic infrastructure like [[teleportation circles]], or to perform powerful rituals, usually to justify types of [[GM Magic]] for story elements.
