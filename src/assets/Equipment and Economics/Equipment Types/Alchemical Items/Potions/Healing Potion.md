## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">700g (DC 18)</span>

The jizzle when it is not acclimated to a particular creature consumes resources from the creatures body to immediately speed up recovery.

Immediately restores 5 hp.

By increasing the DC by 4, you can double the price and increase its healing by 5. This doubling in price/+4 DC is cumulative (DC 22 for 700g, DC 26 1400g, DC 30 for 2800g etc)

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">T3 and above potions are considered rare items due to their high craft DC.</span>
