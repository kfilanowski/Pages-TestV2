- Gain [[Poise]] equal to the [[AC]] the armor gives.
- Heavy armor imposes a -2 penalty to stealth checks.
- Dex column decides how much [[DEX]] you get to add to AC.  If it has no value, your dex doesn’t add or subtract from your AC.

|                                                      | AC  | Dex       | Weight | Str Requirement | SQs   |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | --- | --------- | ------ | --------------- | ----- |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Light</span>  | 1   | Limitless | 10     |                 |       |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Medium</span> | 4   | 1         | 25     | +1              |       |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Heavy</span>  | 6   | -         | 50     | +3              | DRA 1 |
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
If you do not meet the armor's strength requirement, your speed is reduced by 30 (minimum 5).
