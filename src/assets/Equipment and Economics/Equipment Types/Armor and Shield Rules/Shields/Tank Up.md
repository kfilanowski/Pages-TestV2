Shield Types: Tower
Action: 15 ft of movement

- Plant your shield to gain [[Total Cover]] from one particular direction. Essentially defends you from all attacks originating from a cone in that direction. A creature within 5 feet of you behind your shield gains [[Cover]], but only from a direct line. On subsequent turns reduce your movement by 15 to maintain this.
- You can choose to attack from behind the shield, but it gives cover instead of total cover.
- Using other [[+Shield Actions]] ends tank up.
- You can only use tank up once per turn, forcing you to stick to a particular direction.
- You cannot tank up in the same turn you [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]].
