- Shields give you [[poise]] and additional combat options called [[+Shield Actions|shield actions]]. 
- Shields also double up as basic 1h weapons with d8 base damage.
- If you do not meet the [[STR]] requirement, you cannot use [[+Shield Actions|shield actions]]

|                                                            | Poise | Weight | Str Requirement | SQs        |
| ---------------------------------------------------------- | ----- | ------ | --------------- | ---------- |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Heavy Shield</span> | 5     | 15     | +1              | CM (Shove) |
| <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Tower Shield</span> | 10    | 40     | +3              | CM (Shove) |
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
If you un-equip a shield, you immediately subtract the bonus poise it grants (min 1).

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span> 
If you wield a shield in two hands, you increase your effective [[STR]] by two for determining if you can wield it.
