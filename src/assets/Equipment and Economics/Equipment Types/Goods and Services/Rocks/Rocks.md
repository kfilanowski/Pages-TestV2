It's not a boulder, it's a rock! Rocks are the premier transportation and shipping vehicles in the world of Nack. They are incredibly fast, cheap and can plop down anywhere and look natural because they're rocks.

Rocks are construct creatures that have their programming optimized for navigation and high speed transportation. More details in the stats subsection.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Pricing</span>

A standard rock costs <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1,500g</span>.

If you would only like to rent one, you are typically limited to predesignated paths, such as state roads. It costs <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">10g</span> for short trips within 600 miles, <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">30g</span> for something between 2-6 days and <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">80g+</span> for anything longer.

Prices for augments are included below. Using augmented rocks multiplies adds <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">50g</span> to the price to rent them, with particularly expensive ones being even more expensive or unavailable for most customers.

If the rock is lost, destroyed or damaged, the owner/company that provided the service expects compensation for replacements/repairs.
## <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Augments</span>

All prices listed are in addition to the base 1,500g price.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Offroad</span> - 2,000g
Capable of traversing average and less than ideal conditions at full speed. Some regions that require movement types not available to a rock may still impede its speed.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Climbing/Swimming</span> - 1,000g
Rock is capable of using its full speed while climbing or swimming. Each sold separately.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Armored Plating</span> - 10,000g
Adds +5 ac, +5 to all saving throws, +10 poise and +20 hp.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Flight</span> - 60,000g
Rock is capable of using its full speed while flying.
