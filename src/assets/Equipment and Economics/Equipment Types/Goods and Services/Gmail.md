People in Nack do not have phones or radio, and therefore need to communicate with people somehow. A common method is the [[Sending]] spell, but that is often limited in nature, and requires specific magic knowledge and knowledge about who you contact. 

Enter [[Gobbo Mail]] (shortened to G-Mail), a company that comprises of (mostly) goblins that let you send letters to places all around the world. One can purchase their services for <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">20g</span> at any number of locations around the globe. They exist in all metropolis and city size settlements, and many villages (see [[Populations]]). You can also purchase stamps for the same price that effectively are you preemptively purchasing services to skip any lines, or pay for the responses of people you send letters to.

They have the gobbo guarantee that their letter will be delivered in 3 days or less anywhere on the planet. Almost anywhere. Particularly dangerous locations may require more money to convince the couriers, or such requests may be outright denied. The methods they use to travel such distances are closely guarded trade secrets.
