Roll a d100 and add 20. The result you get is the percentage of the way through your life essentially. Multiply this value by the average lifespan of your chosen race.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Example</span> - Human with a roll of 75

70(.75)=52 years old

The added 20 is to basically make sure you aren't a child.
