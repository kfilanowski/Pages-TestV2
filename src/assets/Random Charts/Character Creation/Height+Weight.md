Roll a d10 and then flip a coin. The d10 represents the variance as a percentage and the coin flip decides up (heads) or down (tails). If you roll a 1 or 10, you can roll again.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Example</span> - Human are average 5'6 and weigh 150. I roll a 10 and get heads.

66\*1.1=72 in or 6 ft, 150\*1.1=165 lbs

If you roll two 1s or 10s, you can choose to be ludicrously tall or smol.

Some people do not have weight correlate exactly with height, so you can choose to roll weight separately if you're feeling like having a chance of being incredibly wide and short or tall and thin.
