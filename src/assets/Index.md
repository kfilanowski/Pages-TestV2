# <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">What is Nick Nacks?</span>
![[NICK NACKS showcase n logo.png]]
Nick Nacks is my very own fantasy tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG). My biggest sources of inspiration are Pathfinder 1e and Divinity Original Sin 2.

Most of the rules in Nick Nacks are centered around combat. I expect you to be traveling through dangerous wilderness, delving into dungeons and stealing treasure from dragons. Some rules, such as [[Skills]] and [[+Minigames]], allow you to resolve actions outside of combat. The [[+Chase]] and [[Drinking Contest]] and [[D420]] are examples of such things.

Where I think Nick Nacks shines is its customization options for players and GMs alike. Instead of having traditional spell lists, I made techniques for both martials and mages, each with their own group loosely centered around a theme such as [[Pyromancy]], [[unarmed]] fighting or [[necromancy]]. Anybody can specialize in any list using a pool of limited points. This means a large degree of depth and complexity, with lots of potential builds.

I have plans to make far, far more classes, subclasses, bestiary creatures, feats and more!
