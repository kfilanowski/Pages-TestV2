Combat [[Feat]]

Gain the ability to perform a 10 minute ritual that allows you to bond with 2 specific [[weapon|weapons]].

You cannot be [[disarm|Disarmed]] of these weapons.

When you throw these weapons, they automatically [[teleportation|Teleport]] to your hand after the attack is resolved, if desired.

If these weapons somehow manage to leave your possession otherwise, you can spend a swift action to have them [[teleportation|Teleport]] to your hand.
