Pose a caveat to resurrection magic that imposes a severely limiting factor, such as only being able to revive a creature shortly after it died with some kind of resource attached to it. Maybe even make it a limited resource by tying it to physical items, like scrolls with this effect attached to it.

EFFECT

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Range|Reach|
|Target|One dead creature|
|Duration|instantaneous|

DESCRIPTION You breathe new life into a recently deceased creature. If the creature has died within the last minute, they are brought back to life at 1 hp and drained 1.
