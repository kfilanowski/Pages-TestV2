Provide one of the following bonuses to an ally within your reach for 1 round as a [[Standard Action]]:

+2 to [[ACC]] against foes you both threaten
+2 to [[Saving Throw|Saves]]
+2 to [[AC]]

Alternatively, remove one stack of the following conditions: [[Clumsy]], [[Shaken]], [[Sickened]], or [[Knocked Down]].

Note: Many [[Entangled]] effects can be removed using [[Athletics]]. Use the Aid action to assist an ally in overcoming these conditions.
