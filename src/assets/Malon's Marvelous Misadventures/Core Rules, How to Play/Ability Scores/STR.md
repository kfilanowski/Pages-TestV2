**Strength (STR)**: Your fortitude, athleticism, and endurance.

>You add STR to your [[Fortitude]] Save. 
>You add STR to physical attack [[Damage]], such as with weapons and unarmed attacks. 
>When you wield a weapon in two hands, you add twice your STR to damage.

>You add STR to [[ACC]] when using a [[Combat Maneuver]].

>Every point of STR increases your [[Carry Weight]] by 50%.

>Negative STR subtracts from your Fortitude Saves. 
>Negative STR halves your Carry Weight.
