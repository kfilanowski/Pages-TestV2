**Luck (LUK)**: Your ability to turn tides and benefit from unlikely outcomes.

>You add LUK to [[AC|Avoid]]. 
>You add LUK to [[Potency]]. 
>You add LUK to [[Luck Check]]s. 
>You add LUK to [[Critical Damage]].

>You can add LUK to a [[Roll]] as an [[Immediate Action]] (that did not already add LUK).

>Negative LUK subtracts from Avoid, Potency, Luck Checks, and Critical Damage.
