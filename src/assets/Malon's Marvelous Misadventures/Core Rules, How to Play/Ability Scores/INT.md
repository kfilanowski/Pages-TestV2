**Intelligence (INT)**: Your knowledge, magical expertise, and logic capacity.

>You add INT to [[SP]] 
>You gain an additional [[Technique]] Point.
>You gain an additional [[Skill]] [[Proficiency]] per point of INT

>You gain two additional [[Language]]s per point of INT (listed in the Skills section under [[Linguistics]])

>Negative INT subtracts from Spell Power
   You lose a Skill Proficiency per negative INT
