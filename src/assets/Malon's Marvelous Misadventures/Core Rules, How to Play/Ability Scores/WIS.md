**Wisdom (WIS)**: Your faith, decision making, habits, diligence, and discipline.

>You add WIS to your [[Will]] Save. 
>You add WIS to your [[Mana]].
>You gain 50% more [[XP]] per point of WIS. 
>You gain a [[Feat]] per Wisdom. 

>Negative WIS subtracts from your Will Save, Feats, and reduces your XP gain to 50%.
