**Dexterity (DEX)**: Your agility, hand-eye coordination, and general sneakiness.

>You add DEX to your [[Reflex]] Save. 
>You add your DEX to your [[ACC]] and [[AC]]. 
>You add DEX to [[Initiative]].

>Every point of DEX adds +5 to your [[Speed]] and [[Jump]].

>Negative DEX subtracts from these values.
