| Human Nature | Humans are an iniquitous bunch, a uniquely talented group blessed with incredible intellect and twisted by the flames of ambition. Despite their mundane hairless forms, their demeanors span across every extreme of the humanoid experience, their tales of conquest and glory reaching every corner of the world. The overwhelming strength of Human ambition causes them to be among the greatest heroes and most dastardly villains, the most talented craftsmen, the most passionate lovers, the richest, the most prideful, and every other extreme. |
