Races are a classification similar to creature type, mainly tailored towards players. They are theÂ various sapient species and beings that inhabit the setting. When creating a character, players typically choose a race to determine their character's physical appearance, unique abilities, cultural background, and narrative possibilities.

Most races have a racial language, but also know common. All creatures are assumed to have a base speed of 30ft, know [[Language|Common]], and be Medium in [[Size and Reach|Size]] unless otherwise stated.

The lore used to describe races is to differentiate a given race from the others. It also gives people a starting point if they don't know how to roleplay or world build with a specific race.

The descriptions you find here (or in any source really) are tailored to a specific setting, or are the brain child of some other fantasy obsessed weirdo. They do not need to match how a specific race exists in your world if you have something else in mind.
