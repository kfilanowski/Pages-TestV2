You worked in a job that required a specialized form of training, such as an alchemist, a doctor or a blacksmith. Over the years of your work you have accumulated a few tools of the trade.

Select one of the following presets, or make one that's similar. Regardless of the selected trade, you have a place of work that you can return to that views you favorably, has masterwork equipment available for use, and that you can get ~={green}+10% [[Wealth]]=~ from with related sales and deals.

~={orange}Doctor=~ - You start off with a medicine kit, a dose of [[troll oil]], and 3 doses of [[Bloodblock]].

~={orange}Alchemist=~ - Start off with an alchemist kit, a [[love potion]], and 2 [[Alchemist Fire|alchemist fires]].

~={orange}Craftsman (weapons/armor)=~ - Start off with a set of combat ready equipment. You can start off with
- Heavy [[armor]] and a [[weapon]] 
- Medium armor, 3 melee weapons, a ranged weapon, and a [[shield]] 
- Light armor, 6 light melee weapons, 2 ranged weapons, and a shield
