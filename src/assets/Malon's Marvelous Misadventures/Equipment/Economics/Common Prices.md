[[LV]] 1 Characters start off with 3000g to spend. Higher level characters should get more gold, determined by the DM and as appropriate for the sort of campaign being run.

The following are prices of basic equipment. Recall that basic equipment is the lowest quality available. This is budget equipment suitable for militias, thugs, and beginner adventurers.
