Daily income, typically through the use of a skill, like profession, perform or alchemy.

All income is based around a formula which calculates a yearly salary (y) based on your skill bonus (x).

~={orange}y=10000+x^2 * 250=~

Salary assumes you work for 5 days a week, 50 weeks a year. Divide this value by 250 to figure our your daily income. The chart below does this formula for you for most expected values. Anything lower than a +0 bonus to the task at hand makes a 5k salary, or 20g daily.

This formula assumes a prosperous nation with a lot of economic opportunity and a population with many thousands of people per city. In poorer and less populous regions, consider using the following multiplier for all goods and services.

~={orange}Developing=~ - Multiply all values by .75

~={orange}Poor=~ - Multiply all values by .5
