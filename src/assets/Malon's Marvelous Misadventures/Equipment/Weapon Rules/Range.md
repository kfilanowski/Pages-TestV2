Weapons with ranged capabilities (thrown or otherwise) will also have a listed range. Ranges come in an X/Y format, with the X range increment being the accurate range ([[Close Range]]), and the Y being second range increment ([[Wide Range]]), taking a -4 penalty.

| Range Category | Value  |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Thrown         | 20/40  |
| Short          | 40/60  |
| Long           | 60/120 |
