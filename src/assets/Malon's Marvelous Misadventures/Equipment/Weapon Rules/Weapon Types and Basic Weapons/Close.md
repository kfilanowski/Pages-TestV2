Close Weapons are a group of [[Weapon|Weapons]] that are "1-handed". 
These represent punches, kicks, claws, and headbutts that can be used in the closest of combat, even when pinned. These attacks can be with one's own [[Natural Weapon|Natural Weapons]], or with weapons augmenting their limbs.
