Trigger weapons are a group of [[Weapon|Weapons]] that can be 1-handed or 2-handed. 
These are weapons with handles that are loaded beforehand and then fire their ammunition with a trigger. 
Trigger weapons do not add [[STR]] to damage.

The following are the [[Basic Weapon|Basic Weapons]] in the Trigger group.

| Name     | [[AS]] | [[Damage]] | Damage Type | Description and [[Weapon Qualities]]         |
| -------- | ------ | ---------- | ----------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Crossbow | 1      | 2d8        | P           | 2H, Long Range<br>Uses [[Ammunition]] (Bolt) |
| Pistol   | 1      | 2d6        | P           | Short Range<br>Uses Ammunition (Bullet)      |
