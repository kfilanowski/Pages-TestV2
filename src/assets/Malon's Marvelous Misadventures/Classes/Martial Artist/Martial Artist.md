**~={blue}1 - Techniques=~**

You gain 1 [[Technique]] point at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after. These technique points can be used on anything.

You gain full progression in [[Warfare]] and [[Martial Arts]].

**~={blue}1 - Flurry=~**
Action-Attack [[Martial Arts]] [[Technique|Techniques]], Combat Maneuvers, and Unarmed Attacks gain +1 [[AS]].
[[Unarmed]] Attacks deal d4 [[Damage]] rather than 1.

**~={blue}1 - Core Strength=~**
While wearing Light or no [[Armor]] and not using a [[Shield]], you gain +5 [[Speed]]/[[LV]] and +6 [[AC]] vs [[Combat Maneuver|Combat Maneuvers]].

**~={blue}1 - Fighting Style=~**
Gain a [[Fighting Style]] [[Feat]].
