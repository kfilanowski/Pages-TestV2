[[Fighter]] [[Archetype]].

Stand stalwart in the face of danger, an immovable obstacle that your opponents must fight tooth and nail to merely walk past, let alone survive. Outside of combat your massive skill bonuses can allow you to sense what others don't, and sniff out liars.

Based on the Maplestory class of the same name.

**~={blue}1 - No Escape=~**
All effects that grant [[Elusive]] [[movement]] do not function against you while you wield a reach weapon, allowing you to make [[AoO|AoOs]] against a much larger number of creatures.

If a creature tries to move around you safely using [[Acrobatics]] while you wield a reach weapon, you add your ~={red}Evil Eye=~ bonus to your [[Reflex]] against them.
