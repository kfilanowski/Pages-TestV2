**~={blue}1 - Techniques=~**

You gain 1 [[Technique]] point at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after. These technique points can be used on any martial tree.

You gain full progression in [[Warfare]].

~={blue}**1 - Superior Attack**=~
Once per turn, on any turn where you're capable of taking the [[Attack Action]], you can make an [[Attack]] as a [[Free Action]].

~={blue}**1 - Armor Training**=~
Armors/shields require 2 less strength to use. You ignore stealth penalties from heavier armors.

~={purple}**1 - [[Endure]]**=~
[[Standard Action]], [[At-Will]]. Stand up and [[resistance|Resist]] all incoming damage for one round.

**~={purple}1 - [[Fury]]=~**
[[Immediate Action]], [[Recharge]]. Move 5ft and [[resistance|Resist]] physical damage for one round.

**~={blue}2 - Bravery=~**
+4 to [[Saving Throw|Saves]] vs [[Fear]] effects.

**~={blue}2 - Baby Slayer=~**
When an enemy is a minion, you can tell just by looking at them. Gain an additional ~={red}Superior Attack=~ that can only be used against minion enemies.

**~={blue}4 - Smash from the Air=~**
Gain [[Combat Reflexes]] as a bonus [[feat]]. If you already have it, gain a bonus feat. You can use an [[AoO|Attack of Opportunity]] to swat a [[Ranged Attack]] out of the air. [[Roll]] a melee [[Attack]] roll. If your attack roll exceeds theirs, the attack is negated. You must decide to use this before the roll is made. You take a -4 penalty to [[ACC]] against opposing siege weapons.

**~={blue}6 - Attack Speed=~**
Gain +1 [[AS]] with all [[weapon|Weapons]].

**~={purple}8 - Weapon Spirit=~**
[[Standard Action]], [[Recharge]]. You can enchant weapons you hold with any quality from the [[Magic Weapons]] section. You can enchant 2 different weapons, and give them different enchantments. You can enchant new weapons, but the old ones lose their enchantments.

Weapons enchanted in this way cannot have more than 2 enchantments.
