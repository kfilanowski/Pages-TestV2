[[Achievement]]
==Requirement== - Get defeated by a critical hit and survive in 3 different encounters. Or get crit 3 times in the same combat and survive.

==Reward== - The scars you get from battle are deeply entrenched in your memory. Your memories of combats you have encountered cannot be magically removed. By touching them, you can perfectly recall past events, as long as they happened in the thick of combat. Other creatures can touch your scars to share in the experience of the memory
