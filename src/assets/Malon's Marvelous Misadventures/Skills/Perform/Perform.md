[[CHA]]-based [[Skill]].

Proficiency in Perform only applies to specific instruments or Performance types. The bonus for having this skill only applies to the chosen types. 
Examples of Performances include instruments like flutes, pianos, or drums, physical displays such as singing and dancing, and displays of charm such as comedy.

Used for [[Performance]] and [[Economics]].
