[[WIS]] or [[INT]]-based [[Skill]]. [[Proficiency|Trained]] individuals can use any ability score for Economy uses if doing so has a justification in-character.

Professions can be anything used to provide a service or create a product. When you gain a profession, you choose one such specialization, such as Profession ([[Cooking]]).
