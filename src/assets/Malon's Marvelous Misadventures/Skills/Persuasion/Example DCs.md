Common modifiers to make the task more difficult can include racism, your questions/requests being offensive or closely guarded secrets by the target, and not being well-liked by the person/people you are persuading.

Common modifiers to make the task less difficult can include being well-liked by the person/people you are persuading, holding a position of authority over them, or it would benefit the person you're speaking to to reveal the information you're looking for.
