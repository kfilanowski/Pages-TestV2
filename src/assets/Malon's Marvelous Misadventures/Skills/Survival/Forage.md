Creatures [[Proficiency|Proficient]] in [[Survival]] can spend an hour in a wilderness area with suitable sources of food and water to create a [[Food|Field Ration]].

Alternatively, this can be used to search for specific [[Ingredients]] that are native to the area.

In areas that are unsuitable for life, such as deserts, a skill check is needed to succeed at this action. Failure indicates that the time is wasted.
