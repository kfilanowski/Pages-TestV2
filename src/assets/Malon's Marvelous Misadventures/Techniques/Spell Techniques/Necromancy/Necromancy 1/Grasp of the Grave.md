Tier 1 [[Necromancy]] [[Spell]]
[[Action-Attack]], [[At-Will]]
[[Earth]]
[[Transmutation]]

| Effect      |                       |
| ----------- | --------------------- |
| Range       | 60                    |
| Target/Area | One Grounded Creature |
| Save        | Reflex                |
| Duration    | -                     |
The target must save or be [[Entangled]] by grasping arms from the earth. These arms share your statistics - when hit with an attack, or if they fail a save against a damaging effect, they disappear.
The target can also use [[Athletics]] against your [[Potency]] to escape.

[[Upcast]] 1 - Target an additional creature.
