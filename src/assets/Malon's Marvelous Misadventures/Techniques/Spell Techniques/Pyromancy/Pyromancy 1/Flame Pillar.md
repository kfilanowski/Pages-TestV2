Tier 1 [[Pyromancy]] [[Spell]]
[[Standard Action]], [[Recharge]]
[[Fire]], [[Concentration]], [[Persistent]]
[[Evocation]]

| Effect      |                                      |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Range       | 30                                   |
| Target/Area | One Space, and a 15 Ft Line Above It |
| Save        | Reflex                               |
| Duration    | 1 Minute                             |
The targeted space erupts, causing a pillar of flame to appear. Creatures in this area, and that pass through it, take 2 [[SP]] [[Fire]] [[Damage]] (Reflex halves). Creatures that end their turn in the pillar are set [[Ablaze]].

[[Upcast]] 1 - +1 SP Damage.
