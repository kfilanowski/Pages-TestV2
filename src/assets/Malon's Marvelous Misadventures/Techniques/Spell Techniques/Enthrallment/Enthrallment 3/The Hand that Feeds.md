Tier 3 [[Enthrallment]] [[Spell]]
[[Action-Attack]], [[Recharge]]
[[Mind-Affecting]]
[[Illusion]]

| Effect      |                      |
| ----------- | -------------------- |
| Range       | 30                   |
| Target/Area | 1 Companion Creature |
| Save        | Will                 |
| Duration    | 1 Round              |
You bring forth all of the negative memories and feelings a companion has for its leader. The target [[Companion]] is [[Taunted]] by its leader for 1 round ([[Will]] negates). 
This ability cleanses all other taunt effects currently affecting the companion creature if the spell is successful.

[[Upcast]] 2 - You can target an additional Companion.
