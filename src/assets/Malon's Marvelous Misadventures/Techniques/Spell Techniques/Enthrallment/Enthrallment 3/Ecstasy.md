Tier 3 [[Enthrallment]] [[Spell]]
[[Action-Attack]], [[Recharge]]
[[Mind-Affecting]]
[[Illusion]]

| Effect      |              |
| ----------- | ------------ |
| Range       | 30 Ft        |
| Target/Area | One Creature |
| Save        | Will         |
| Duration    | 2 Rounds     |
You fill a creature's mind with pleasure, causing it to lose focus. The creature is [[Dazed]] for 1 round and [[Staggered]] the round after (Will reduces to Staggered for 1 round).

If the target creature could reasonably be sexually attracted to the caster, then the [[DC]] is increased by 1. If the caster is naked, or barely clothed, the DC is increased by an additional 1. 

[[Upcast]] 1 - Target one additional creature.
