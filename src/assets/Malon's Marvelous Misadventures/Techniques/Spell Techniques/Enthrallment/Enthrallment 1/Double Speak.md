Tier 1 [[Enthrallment]] [[Spell]]
[[Instant Action]], [[At-Will]]
[[Subtle]]
[[Illusion]]

| Effect      |              |
| ----------- | ------------ |
| Range       | 30           |
| Target/Area | One Creature |
| Save        | -            |
| Duration    | -            |
You change the sounds of your speech before they reach the target creature, causing them to hear something different than what you said. For every sentence you speak, you can cause them to hear a different sentence.
This allows you to speak to two different people at the same time and tell them two different things.
