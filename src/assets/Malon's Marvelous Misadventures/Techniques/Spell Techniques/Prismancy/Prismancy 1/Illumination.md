Whenever you use a Light spell to hit a creature with an [[Attack]], or they fail a [[Saving Throw|Save]] against it, they begin [[Glowing]] for 1 round.

When you apply Glowing to an enemy, you or an ally can Attack the target to end the condition immediately and deal an additional [[SP]] [[Fire]] [[Damage]].
