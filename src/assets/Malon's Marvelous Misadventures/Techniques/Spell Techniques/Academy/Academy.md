[[Spell]] [[Technique]] Tree.

In the heart of every large city, the scholars and the wealthy gather around places of magical research to stay relevant in a competitive and difficult world of magic. These places focus on imparting general knowledge of best practices and methodologies to their students, a basis from which they can further the research and development in their field of magic. 

Those who choose to immerse themselves in the Academy skill tree embark on a journey of rigorous study and practical mastery, gaining a profound understanding of the skills and self-defense techniques needed to take on the occult world beyond the city walls.
