When targeted by [[Ammunition]], roll [[Reflex]] against the target's [[Potency]]. If you succeed, you negate the attack, and can fire it back at them with a [[Ranged Attack]] if you have the right weapon (such as a bow for a caught arrow, or an empty hand for a dart).
