[[Buff]]

| Range       | 30       |
| Target/Area | One Ally |

You have a watchful eye of the actions around you, and you shout to your allies to warn them.

When an ally [[Attack|Attacks]], you can grant them +2 [[ACC|Accuracy]] after the roll. When an ally is attacked, you can grant them +2 [[AC|Avoid]] after the roll.

You cannot use this on yourself.

[[Upcast]] 1 - The bonus increases by 1.
