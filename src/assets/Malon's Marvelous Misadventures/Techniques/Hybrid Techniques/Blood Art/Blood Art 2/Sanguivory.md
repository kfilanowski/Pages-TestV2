Rend the blood of the target to replenish your own, restoring 10 [[HP]].
This [[AoO|Provokes]], and kills target [[Defeated]] creatures. 
This qualifies as eating for the purposes of benefiting from a [[Long Rest]].

A given corpse can only be eaten once. Its [[Food]] drops are destroyed.
