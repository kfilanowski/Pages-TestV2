Caster level (CL) is typically equal to the creature's level, though certain effects can change it. CL determines the effectiveness of many spells.

This stat is also relevant in resolving what happens when casting goes head to head with each other (such as with [[dispel magic]]), when using [[healing]] to staunch bleeding or when [[light]] and [[Dark]] magic meet.

If dual wielding [[Staff Component|spell focus]]es, get +1 CL.

All boosts to CL affect [[+Cantrips]], even if they normally only affect specific types of spells.
