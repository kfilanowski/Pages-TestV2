Jump into the air and bring your heel down upon your target's shoulder...Or something close to it at least. This [[Unarmed Attack]] deals an additional d8 (4) damage and breaks your the bones leading to your opponents arm (fort reduces to [[sickened]] 1).

While their bone is broken, every time they use the arm, such as using a [[+Shield Actions|shield action]], making a [[Natural Attack]] or casting a spell using a held [[Staff Component]], they take d8 (4) [[True Damage]] and become sickened 1.

This effect can be removed with [[First Aid (P)|first aid]].
