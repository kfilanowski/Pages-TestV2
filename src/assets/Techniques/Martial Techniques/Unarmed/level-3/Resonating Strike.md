Unleash a sonic blast at a creature as though using a thrown weapon attack that deals your [[Unarmed Attack]] damage as sonic damage. This attack has [[Seeking]]. [[On-hit]], as long as that creature stays within 60 ft of you, you know it's exact position and gain seeking against it until the end of your next turn. 

At any point before the end of your next turn, you can spend an immediate action to dash to their location and to make an [[Simple Attack|simple]] unarmed strike against them. They must be within 60 ft and you must have [[line of effect]].
