Help an ally snap out of it with the back of your hand. Deal 1 [[true damage]] and immediately wake them up from [[Asleep|Sleeping]] or free them from [[Fascinated|Fascination]]. If they are [[confused]], add 2 to their rolls.

If they are [[charmed]] or [[frightened]], they get a new save against the effect.
