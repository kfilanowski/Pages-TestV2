If you do not take [[+HP]] damage or get [[poise]] broken by the start of your next turn, you can make an [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed strike]] with +10 [[Attack]] that deals an additional (2*[[LvL]]) dmg at any point during your turn as a free action.
