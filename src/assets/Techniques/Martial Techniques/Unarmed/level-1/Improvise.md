Level 1 [[Unarmed]]
Tags: -
Action: Immediate

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
Scramble to find something to take the hit for you against an incoming attack. Decrease their [[Attack]] by 2 and the object breaks. They maintain this -2 attack penalty as their weapon gets stuck in the object. They can remove it as a swift action.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
Must have a free hand to use this ability. There must also be suitable object with which to block the attack, such as a piece of furniture.
