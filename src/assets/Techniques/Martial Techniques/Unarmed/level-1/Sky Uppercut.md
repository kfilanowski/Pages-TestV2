<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
You must be on the ground to use this ability.

As part of using this technique, you can leap 60 ft into the air directly up and make a [[Melee Attack|melee]] [[Unarmed Attack]]. You are [[Immunity|immune]] to [[fall damage]] during the turn you use this technique. You knock the creature into the air 10 ft, dealing fall damage.

If you can [[fly]], you can choose to stay airborne at the peak of your leap. If you have [[glide]], you can use it to adjust your landing location. If your opponent has either of these qualities, they ignore the fall damage if they can use them.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- For each additional mana spent, knock the creature 10 ft higher into the air.
