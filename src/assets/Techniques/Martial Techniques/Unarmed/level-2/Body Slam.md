<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
This attack must be performed from above the target or combined with a [[jump]] action.

Crash onto your foe. This [[unarmed attack]] inflicts [[Knocked Down|Knockdown]] on you and your target and deals an additional d8 (4) dmg. If the target is already knocked down, it is instead [[staggered]] and takes an additional 3d8 (13) dmg.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
