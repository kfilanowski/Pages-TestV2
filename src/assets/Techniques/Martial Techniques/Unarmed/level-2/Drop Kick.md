Jump into the air and kick your foe with both legs at full force. They take an additional d8 (4) damage. If they are not clumsy, they become [[clumsy]] 1 [[On-hit]]. If they are clumsy, then you can choose to make them [[knocked down]] [[On-hit]] instead of making them more clumsy. 

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
