Level 1 [[Cataclysm]]
Tags: [[Buff]]
Action: [[Swift Action]]

| Range          | -          |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self       |
| Duration       | 10 minutes |
| Save           | -          |
Increase the effects of gravity on your body, causing you to stay firmly planted on the ground, sometimes to your detriment.

You cannot [[hover]] or [[Fly]]. You gain [[Advantage]] on any save that would physically move you from your position or knock you down. Creatures have [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on attacks made against you that would move you from your position or knock you down.

If used underwater, you sink to the bottom 60 ft per round.
