Level 1 [[Cataclysm]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Upcast]]
Action: [[Special Attack]]

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | -          |
Charge your [[Melee Attack]] so it leaves behind a lingering effect, causing the target to experience the gravity of a larger planet. [[On-hit]] the target is [[grounded]] and is affected as though under the effects of Tier 1 High [[Gravity]].

As a swift action, you can end the grounded debuff and pull the creature 30 ft closer to you.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- For each additional mana spent, you can pull the creature an additional 15 ft.
If you spend at least 3 mana, the creature is affected as per T2 High Gravity.
If you spend at least 6 mana, the creatures is affected as per T3 High Gravity
