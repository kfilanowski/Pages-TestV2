<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
You must be touching the ground to cast this spell.

Tenderize the ground to send your foes flying. Roll to [[Topple]] all foes within your [[reach]] with a +4 bonus. Compare the result of the attack to the [[Poise]] of all enemies. Against all enemies you successfully topple, the creatures are knocked into the air for 1 round.

While airborne, the creature has a speed of zero and creatures get a +4 bonus to [[Attack]] against them. Anything that removes [[Knocked Down|Knockdown]] can also pull the creature out of the air. At the start of your next turn, the creatures fall and are [[Knocked Down]].
