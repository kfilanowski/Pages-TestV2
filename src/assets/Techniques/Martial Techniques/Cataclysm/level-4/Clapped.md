You call forth a portion of your fiendish patron to crush your enemies. Two muscular arms overlap your own as you clap a foe within range.

Make two [[Melee Attack|melee]] [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed attacks]] against a creature with a +2 bonus to [[Attack]]. Each attack deals 18 crush damage. Creatures hit by at least one attack are [[staggered]] for 1 round [[On-hit]]. Creatures hit by both strikes are [[dazed]] for 1 round on-hit (fort reduces to [[Staggered]]).
