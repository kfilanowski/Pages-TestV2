Call upon your fiendish patron and punch the ground. An enormous fiendish fist appears above your target and slams down upon them.
This can have 2 different effects...

<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Attack</span>
Make a [[Melee Attack|melee]] [[special attack]]. It has greatly increased range, deals an additional d8 (4) dmg and an equal amount of poise damage. If this poise breaks, it inflicts [[Knocked Down|Knockdown]]. If the creature was flying, they are sent down to the ground, taking [[fall damage]], in addition any other effects [[On-hit]].

<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Save</span>
Let the patron do all the work as a standard action. The targeted creature takes 3d8+lvl (13+lvl) crush damage and is sent down to the ground if flying, taking fall damage (reflex halves dmg and negates knocking out of the air). The target also takes poise damage equal to damage dealt.  If this poise breaks, it knocks the creature down.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Deals an additional d8 (4) damage per mana spent. 
If you spend at least 4 mana, the technique's range increases to 60 ft.
