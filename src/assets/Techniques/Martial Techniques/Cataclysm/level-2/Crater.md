<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span>
You must have [[High Ground]] on your opponent to use this technique. A [[jump]] is sufficient to meet this criteria.

Slam your opponent with such force that the ground underneath you cracks and splinters upwards, dealing an additional d8 (4) dmg. [[On-hit]], a 10 ft radius circular wall of earth forms with you and your opponent at the center inside. The wall is 20 ft tall on the inside and sloped on the outside. Any creatures in spaces where the wall forms are shunted away.

A creature can break through the wall with [[Brute Force]] as a standard action. You can attempt to break through it as a free action (1/turn) with the same check.

If you or your opponent is larger than medium, than the walls radius increases by 5ft per size category above medium.

If you or the target leave the arena, the wall crumbles.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional point of mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) dmg and increase the DC to destroying the wall by 2.
