You make an [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed strike]] or [[natural attack]] against a creature, aiming straight for the abdomen while shouting "GUT CHECK." The attack does an additional d8 (4) dmg and [[On-hit]] if the creature has just swallowed something recently, they vomit it out, negating its benefits or drawbacks.

Also ends pregnancies (fort negates).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If you spend at least 3 mana, the creature is also [[sickened]] 1, and cannot consume anything without getting rid of the sickened first.
