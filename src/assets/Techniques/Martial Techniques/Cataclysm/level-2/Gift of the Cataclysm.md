Your devotion manifests into gifts from your fiendish patron. Get a +2 bonus to [[+Athletics]] checks and damage made with [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed attacks]] and [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]].

As part of using this technique, you must make a DC 15 check to [[Brute Force]] destroy a nearby or handheld object. If you fail, the buff immediately ends.
