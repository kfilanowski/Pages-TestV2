Shout at your enemy to create a sonic projectile and scare them off. Make a [[thrown]] attack that deals 3d8+lvl (13+lvl) sonic damage. The target is [[frightened]] for one round (will reduces to shaken 1).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every point of additional mana spent on this ability increases the damage by d8 (4). 
If you spend at least 3 mana, its range increases to [[Short]] 
If you spend at least 5, [[Long]].
