<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
You must be touching the ground to cast this spell.

You smash the ground violently, causing it to crack and crumble under your strength. The area becomes [[difficult terrain]]. Creatures on the ground or [[Hover|hovering]] in the area take 4d8+lvl (18+lvl) crush damage and are [[knocked down]] (reflex halves damage and negates knock down).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
