Level 3 [[Cataclysm]]
Tags:
Action: [[Special Attack]]

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Fort       |
On-hit or after grappling a foe, crack a creature's bones in one of their limbs in order to impede them and cause great pain. The creature takes 2d8 (9) true damage and has one of their limbs rendered useless (fort turns damaged limb into [[Staggered]] for 1 round).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Arm</span> - Creature is unable to use arm
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Leg </span>- Creatures speed is reduced by 15. [[Clumsy]] 1 permanently.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Wing</span> - Creature is unable to [[fly]].
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
Other limbs can be selected, disabling natural attacks like tentacles and such. These are just the most common examples.

The damaged limb can be healed with a DC 25 [[+Medicine]] check, taking 1 minute.
