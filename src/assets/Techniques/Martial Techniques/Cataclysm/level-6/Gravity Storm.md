You channel your power such that it forces everything nearby to bend to your might. You passively exert a tangible gravitational pull to everything within 60 ft, causing loose objects weighing less than 100 lbs to immediately be pulled into the storm, throwing them into an orbit around you. Loose objects weighing between 100 and 1000 lbs are slowly dragged towards you.

The movement of any enemy within 60 ft of you that is moving farther away from you is considered [[difficult terrain]]. All creatures within the [[aura]] are [[Grounded]].

As part of using this technique, and at the start of every turn as a free action, you can choose to exert this force, pulling all creatures in range 10 ft closer to you. At the end of each of your turns, if a creature is within your [[Reach|natural reach]], it must roll a DC 20 [[+Athletics]] check or buckle under its own weight, causing it to take 4d6 (14) crush dmg and gain [[Clumsy]] 1.
