Action: Standard

You call forth a portion of your fiendish patron to strike fear into the hearts of your enemies. Its visage overlaps your own as you let out a monstrous roar.

Creatures within 30 feet of you take 6d6 (21) sonic damage. 
They are also [[frightened]] for 1 minute (will reduces frightened to shaken 1). Each round at the end of their turn, creatures can roll another save to reduce the frightened condition to [[shaken]] 1.
