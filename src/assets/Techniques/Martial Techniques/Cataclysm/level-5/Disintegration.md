| Range          | Reach                |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature or object |
| Duration       | 3 rounds             |
| Save           | Fort                 |
Attack with such violent force that the target is evaporated. Roll a [[melee attack]]. [[On-hit]] the target starts to disintegrate (fort reduced duration to 1 round). Disintegrating creatures take 3d6 (10) true damage at the start of each turn and are [[Staggered]]. Creatures defeated by this damage are turned to dust.

If you target an object, you can evaporate a 5x5x5 ft cube matter of solid or lesser toughness, turning it to dust. Tougher objects sustain heavy damage in the same area.
