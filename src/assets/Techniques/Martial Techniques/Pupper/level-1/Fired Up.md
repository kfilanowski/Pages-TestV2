Level 1 [[Pupper]]
Tags: [[Fire]], [[Healing]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach  |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 ally |
| Duration       | -      |
| Save           | -      |
You coat your hand in fire and deal 2 fire damage to an ally (other than yourself) as you slap it on the back.

You can remove any condition that can reasonably be melted away, such as getting entangled by [[Pin Down]] or the creature is [[Frosty]].
