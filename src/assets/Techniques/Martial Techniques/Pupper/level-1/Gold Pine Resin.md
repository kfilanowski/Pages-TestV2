Tags: [[Buff]], [[Holy]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach                                         |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 weapon or 2 [[Light Weapon\|light weapons]] |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                      |
| Save           | -                                             |
Coat your weapon(s), [[Unarmed Attack|unarmed attacks]] or [[Natural Attack|natural attacks]] in syrup that possesses a golden radiance. These buffed attacks deal entirely [[Holy]] damage, its usual damage, or both, whichever is most beneficial.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast 3</span> - Whenever you strike a [[Minion]] undead with this weapon, it is instantly destroyed (will negates). If destroyed, it is also treated as though it was doused in [[Holy Water]].
