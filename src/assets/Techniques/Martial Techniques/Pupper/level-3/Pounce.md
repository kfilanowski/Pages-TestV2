Take the [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]] action. After the charge is resolved, make an additional charge against a foe you have not attacked this round, making only a single [[Simple Attack|simple]] [[melee attack]] against them. You turn after making the first attack. You can [[jump]] once as a free action during this charge.
