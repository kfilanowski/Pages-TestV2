| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 3 rounds |

Create 3 copies of a held melee weapon, [[natural attack]] or [[Unarmed Attack]] that float around your head. While these persist, gain +2 [[Attack]]. As part of casting using this technique, and as a swift action each subsequent turn, you can make a [[Simple Attack|simple]] [[melee attack]] with that weapon and consume a copy. This attack functions exactly like making a simple attack with the weapon normally, but deals an additional d8 (4) dmg.
