You create a transparent but clearly visible wall that partially unweaves magic that passes through it. Any [[spell]] or supernatural (Su) effect that passes through the wall has its [[Attack]] bonus and [[DC]] decreased by 2. If an ability requires going through the barrier to have [[Line of Effect]], it is considered to be "going through" the barrier for targeted spells without projectiles like [[Charming Gaze]] or effects with an aura (like [[Techniques/Martial Techniques/+Warfare/level-3/Targeted|Targeted]]).

You can also choose to form the barrier into a hemisphere centered on you with a radius equal to half the walls dimensions.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Per each additional mana spent, increase the dimensions of the wall by 10 ft (20x20, 30x30 etc)
