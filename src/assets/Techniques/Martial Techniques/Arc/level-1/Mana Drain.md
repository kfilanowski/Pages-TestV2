You radiate a 10 ft [[aura]] that drains the mana from living things. Every time a creature spends mana within this aura, it spends an additional point of mana. If the ability costs no mana, such as an innate spell, its [[Caster Level|CL]] is reduced by 2.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast 4</span> - Double the penalties of the aura (+2 mana expenditure, -4 CL for innate spells).
