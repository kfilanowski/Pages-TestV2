Action: 3 rounds

| Range          | Reach                                         |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | A spot on a flat continuous surface<br>120 ft |
| Duration       | 24 hours                                      |
| Save           | -                                             |
When you place this sigil, you must designate a location within its AoE on a surface visible from where it is placed. Unlike most other sigils, a teleportation trap is not triggered by moving within a certain radius of it. A creature that teleports within 120 feet of the sigil, even without direct [[line of effect]], is immediately affected by the trap and teleported to this predesignated location instead of their intended destination (will reduces effect to [[clumsy]] 1).

If you place a Teleportation Trap while you already have one active, the previous one disappears.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast 6</span> - The sigil becomes permanent and its AoE increases to 300 ft.
