Level 4 [[Arc]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Sigil]]
Action: 3 rounds

| Range          | Reach                                 |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered around a point |
| Duration       | Until Rest                            |
| Save           | Will                                  |
You inscribe a anti-magic rune on the surface of an object. Whenever a creature of at least small or larger size enters this area, the sigil triggers. All creatures within 20 ft of the sigil are siphoned (will negates). At the end of each round, creatures get a new save against this effect.
