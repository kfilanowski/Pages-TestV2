If you land this attack against a magically created [[companion]] creature, such as an undead or a summoned creature, you automatically deal the attack's damage as [[True Damage]] to the creature that created the effect too if they are within 100 ft of you.

In the case of a creature summoned by a [[Summoning Contract]], it damages whoever summoned them, not the real body of the creature being summoned.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4). 
If you spend at least 3 mana, the attack damages all the companion creatures, in addition to their creator/summoner.
