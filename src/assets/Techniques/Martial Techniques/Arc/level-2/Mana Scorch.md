You weaken the creatures connection to the mana of the universe. They [[Mana Burn]] 2 and lose an additional 2 mana (will negates mana burn). Mana Scorch curses can stack.

Against a creature with only [[Innate Technique|innate techniques]], it instead removes a 1/day spell of up to 2nd level until the curse is lifted.

By not casting a spell for a full 24 hours, the curse ends.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Increase mana burn and mana loss by 1 per mana spent. Or increase the level of 1/day spell that can be consumed by 1 per mana spent.
