Whenever a creature uses a supernatural ability or spell on one of its allies within the aura, you can decide to become the target of the effect as an immediate action. For AoE effects (like [[Healer]]'s channel energy), you can only steal the effect from one target.

Unless you identify it with [[+Monster Lore]] or [[Identify Spell Being Cast]], you're gambling. Any effect that requires a saving throw that you steal (like a [Blight](Blight.md)) you automatically fail.
