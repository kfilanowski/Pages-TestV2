An ally can walk into a space you occupy to use a [[+Shield|shield]] you wield as a springboard. You give them a [[jump]] action with additional movement and height equal to your [[Reach|natural reach]]. If they are a larger size than you, then you need to possess exceptional strength or [[carry weight]] to do this.
