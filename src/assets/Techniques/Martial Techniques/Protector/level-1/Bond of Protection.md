Level 1 [[Protector]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Dismissible]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 ally     |
| Duration       | Until Rest |
| Save           | -          |
You siphon your allies' pain into your own body with this bond. You take half of their [[+HP]] damage as [[True Damage]] (with odd damage numbers, round down). This technique can defeat you. You cannot take more damage than you have hp (ie if you have 1 hp and your bond takes 100 dmg, they take 99, you take 1).

The damage redirected to you is considered self-inflicted damage for effects that trigger "when damaged by an enemy."

If you do not have line of sight to your ally, or if you are more than 100 ft away for more than 3 rounds, the bond is temporarily suppressed until such conditions are met.
