Level 1 [[Protector]]
Tags: [[Elusive]]
Action: Immediate

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
When a creature harms another within 30 ft, you can immediately move adjacent to the ally, become the new target of the effect, and automatically get hit by the attack. 

If you use this technique against an effect that also targets you or includes you in its area, you suffer the effects for both yourself and the target you spared, potentially taking damage or suffering other consequences twice.
