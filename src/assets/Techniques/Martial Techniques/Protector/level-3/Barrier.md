Level 3 [[Protector]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft         |
| -------------- | ------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20x20 ft wall |
| Duration       | 3 rounds      |
| Save           | -             |
Erect a transparent barrier that blocks movement and [[Line of Effect]]. These effects only apply to enemies and allies can freely attack and move through the barrier.

The barrier can be harmed, and has 5+(3*[[LvL]]) hp. It can also be broken with [[Brute Force]] (DC 30). When its hp is depleted or a creature otherwise breaks it, the barrier shatters completely. Unless the ability/attack in question is a [[+Siege Weapon|siege weapons]] or it specifically mentions it destroys structures/barriers, like [[Calamity]], the entirety of the attack is negated. In such cases, these attacks can target creatures on the other side of a barrier, but must deal enough damage to break through it to actually harm their intended target.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">TL;DR</span> - If the ability doesn't mention the ability to destroy structures, then pretend it is a real wall. Even if it shatters as a result of the attack, attacks and [[AoE]] effects without a duration are halted along this barrier and do not "spill over" when the barrier is broken.
