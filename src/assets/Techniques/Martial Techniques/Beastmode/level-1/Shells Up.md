While this technique is prepared, you have a large shell on your back. You cannot be [[Flanking|flanked]].

By spending 1 mana when you take the [[total defense]] action, you can slip into your shell completely. This prevents you from taking any instant or immediate actions, but increases the the defense granted by the action to [[DR|DRA]] 4.
