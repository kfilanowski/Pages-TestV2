Assume the form of a tiny monkey indefinitely. You can exit this transformation as a standard action.

While in this form, you gain the following traits from the [[Polymorph List]]
Speed - Climb
Smol - Tiny
Prehensile - Tail
Attack - Bite (P)
Bother

While in this form, you gain 2 abilities. Both usable as a standard action.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">One of Us (poly, true)</span> - While in monkey form, you reach over and touch a creature with your tiny monkey hands. The creature also turns into a tiny monkey (fort reduces to stagger 1 round).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ohhh Banana</span> [[Mind-Affecting]] - You procure a shiny golden banana that hovers in the air for 1 minute. All creatures you turned into a monkey are automatically fascinated. Other monkey/ape-like creatures  are fascinated (will negates).
