You can detach and regenerate lost limbs/organs, and in general recover much faster. 

You can cause pieces of your body to detach as a free action, such as your hands or legs. Basically anything that isn't your head or your torso. When you do so, you are [[drained]] 3. If you are able to re-attach this limb, you recover the drain.

You recover 2 stacks of drained every days instead of 1, as though receiving [[Long Term Care (P)|long term care]] (stacks). A lost limb/organ regrows within 3 days.

When you are [[defeated]], you automatically regain 1 hit point after 10 minutes.

As a swift action, you can heal 10 hp, regrow a limb instantly and make yourself drained 5.
