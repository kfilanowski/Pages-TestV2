Grow massive powerful jaws and take a chomp your opponent with a [[Melee Attack]]. It deals an additional d8 (4) damage and you latch onto them [[On-hit]]. Until the start of your next turn, you stay adjacent to this target as it now needs to drag you along for the ride. You can decide which space you end adjacent to them after the creature moves.

Anything that prevents you from moving prevents the other creature from moving as well (such as being [[entangled]]. If you are poise broken you detach.
