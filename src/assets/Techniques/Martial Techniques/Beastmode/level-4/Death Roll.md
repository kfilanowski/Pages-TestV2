<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
Can only be used against a grappled or knocked down creature.

You chomp down onto a creature with excessive force and spin them around. If the creature is [[Grapple|grappled]], they are [[knocked down]] and take an additional 3d8 (13) damage and are made [[clumsy]] 2. If they are knocked down, you initiate a grapple, they take an additional 3d8 (13) damage and are made clumsy 2. In either case, they are also [[Held Down]].

If the target is neither grappled nor knocked down, you can attempt one such maneuver as a free action on hit.

When you maintain a grapple against a creature after using this technique against them without the grapple ever being broken, they are made [[clumsy]] 2.
