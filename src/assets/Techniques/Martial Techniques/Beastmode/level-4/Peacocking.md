You grow an elaborate and colorful set of feathers, patterned wings or a wide hood like that of a snake to temporarily entrance your enemy. The creature you direct this display towards becomes [[fascinated]] for 1 round (will negates).

You cannot use this technique against a creature you have attacked this round.
