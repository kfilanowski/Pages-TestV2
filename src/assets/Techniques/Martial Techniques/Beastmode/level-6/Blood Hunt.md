While this technique is prepared, you can smell everything as long as it bleeds. You gain the [[Shirshu]] Elite Tracker and Sightless Blindsense traits. These only function against wounded creatures, or against creatures whose blood you have smelled before.

You see a faint misty red trail leading directly to whatever you [[Tracking (P)|track]], automatically succeeding on survival checks to track creatures whose blood you have smelled before.
