Level 3 [[Warfare]]
Tags: [[Fear]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -                              |
| -------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered on user |
| Duration       | 1 round                        |
| Save           | Will (see text)                |
Lift your weapon into the air and let out a battle cry. Enemies of a lower [[LvL]] than you are [[frightened]] for 1 round. Enemies of your level or higher are frightened for 1 round (will reduces to [[shaken]] 1).

After a creature is the target of this technique, it cannot be affected by it again for 1 minute.
