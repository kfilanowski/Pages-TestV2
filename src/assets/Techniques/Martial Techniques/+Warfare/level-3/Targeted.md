A prototypical target appears on your back, and on your head. Enemies are [[taunted]] by you (will negates). Taunted creatures get an additional save at the end of their turn.

[[Mindless]] creatures and creatures with -2 or less [[INT]] take a -2 penalty to their save. Even if they aren't taunted, creatures in the aura take a -10 penalty to [[+Perception]] checks versus anything that isn’t the targeted creature.

A creature that is affected by this technique cannot be taunted by it again for 1 minute.
