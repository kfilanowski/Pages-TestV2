Level 4 [[Warfare]]
Tags: [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach       |
| -------------- | ----------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | All enemies |
| Duration       | 1 round     |
| Save           | -           |
Start rapidly spinning and make a [[Simple Attack]] against all foes within your [[reach]]. You cannot move after initiating this technique. Until the start of your next turn, you have +2 [[AC]]. Any foes within your reach at the start of your next turn are automatically attacked again.
