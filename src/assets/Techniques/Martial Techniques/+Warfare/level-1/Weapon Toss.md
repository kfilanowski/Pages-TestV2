Level 1 [[Warfare]]
Tags: [[Su]]
Action: Special Attack

| Range          | [[Thrown]] |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | Until Rest |
| Save           | -          |
Toss your weapon, temporarily giving it the thrown property.

If it is a piercing weapon, the foe is [[Entangled]] [[On-hit]]. The creature can [[breakout]] to remove it as a swift action, or remove it automatically by spending a standard action.

When you throw the weapon, a copy of it appears in your hand, hit or miss. The weapon you throw disappears on a miss or after it is removed.
