Level 1 [[Barbarism]]
Tags: [[Buff]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
Wipe the blood, sludge or ectoplasm off of a creature and apply it to yourself to get yourself in the mood for battle. You must initially target a defeated creature or yourself if you are below 10 hp.

After you apply the blood to yourself, gain +15 speed, a +4 bonus to [[+Intimidation]] checks. When you [[crit]], you deal an additional 5 damage.
