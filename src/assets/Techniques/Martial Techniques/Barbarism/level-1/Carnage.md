Level 1 [[Barbarism]]
Tags: [[Buff]]
Action: Instant

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
The more blood that flows from battle, the more you are pushed forward with reckless abandon. When you [[defeat]] an enemy you can activate this buff, you take a -1 penalty to [[AC]] and get a +2 bonus to [[Attack]]. The next 2 times you defeat an enemy, you get this benefit again.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
You cannot receive another buff effect or end this buff while under the effects of Carnage.
