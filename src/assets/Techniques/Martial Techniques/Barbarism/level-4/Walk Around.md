Level 4 [[Barbarism]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]], [[Concentration]]
Action: [[FRA]]

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
You can walk around. You spend all of your actions moving and doing nothing else, ending your movement at some point within 30 feet of where you started walking around. Naturally this behavior befuddles the creatures around you. All enemies within 60 feet of this initial starting point are [[confused]] (will adds 4 to d10 roll).

The [[DC]] is 2 higher for creatures that [[AoO]] you as part of this movement.

You may continue spending your turns walking around to continue confusing new creatures as long as you want, the same creature cannot be confused more than once.

This has no effect on creatures not engaged in combat with you.
