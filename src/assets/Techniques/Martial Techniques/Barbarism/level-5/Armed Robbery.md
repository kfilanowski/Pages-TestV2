Make a [[grapple]] or [[disarm]] attack with a +4 bonus. If successful, instead of the usual effects, tear off your opponents arm causing, them to [[Bleeding|bleed]] 2d6 (7) damage and be [[drained]] 5 (fort removes drain/bleed and reduces "disarm" to dislocated arm, which deals 5 [[True Damage]] to you every time you use it, such as to attack with a weapon in that hand, cast a spell with a staff in that hand or take a [[+Shield Actions|shield action]] with a shield in that hand. [[First Aid (P)|First aid]] can fix this condition).

If you successfully disarm or grapple, make a [[Simple Attack]] against them with their own arm as a free action. This counts as a light weapon. If your hands are already full, you can choose to sheathe or drop weapons/items in order to make this extra attack.
