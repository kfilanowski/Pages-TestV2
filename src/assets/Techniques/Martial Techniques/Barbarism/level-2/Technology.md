You can activate things using the [[+Athletics]] skill that normally require another skill. This could mean twisting the doorknob off of a door to open it, forcing open a book that requires a password gleaned through [[arcana]] or activating a portal that normally required [[+Planes]].

This usually involves damaging or breaking the object.
