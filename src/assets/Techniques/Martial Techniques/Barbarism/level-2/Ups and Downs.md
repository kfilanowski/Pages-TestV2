Level 2 [[Barbarism]]
Tags: [[Upcast]]
Action: [[Special Attack]]

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
On hit deal an additional d8 (4) damage and smash a creature into the ground, or throw them directly up into a low roof within 10 ft. In either case, the creature is [[entangled]] until they [[breakout]] as a swift action. If you throw the creature into a roof, they are also unable to see and interact with the floor below until they escape.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
