<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note -</span> 
This technique requires [[Extraordinary Strength]].

Make a [[grapple]] attack. Instead of grappling them, you make a bonus [[thrown]] weapon attack using the poise broken creature as ammunition. Can only be thrown in the first [[Range Increments|range increment]].

The attack always deals full damage to the poise broken creature. If you [[graze]] the secondary target, it does not change the damage against the initial target. If you [[crit]], both targets are both crit. All creatures affected take an additional d8 (4) damage.

If you are already grappling the creature when you initiate the attack, you don't need to grapple them again and can instead skip straight to the thrown attack.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
