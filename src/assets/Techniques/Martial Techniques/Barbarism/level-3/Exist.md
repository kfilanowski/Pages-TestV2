Level 3 [[Barbarism]]
Tags: -
Action: Instant

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
As part of the initiative roll you can choose a creature within range. That creature is [[taunted]] (will negates). To that creature, your very existence infuriates them and you must be stopped at all cost. At the end of each of their turns, they get a new save to end the effect.

If you are at or below 50% hp when this ability triggers, increase the [[DC]] by 2. If on subsequent turns, you take no actions on your turn other than movement, the DC increases by 2.

If this creature is defeated, then you may choose a new creature to affect with this ability (no action required, happens immediately).
