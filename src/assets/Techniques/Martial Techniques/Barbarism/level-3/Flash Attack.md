Level 3 [[Barbarism]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Teleportation]], [[Force]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Free

| Range          | 30 ft              |
| -------------- | ------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | A threatened space |
| Duration       | -                  |
| Save           | -                  |
You can teleport 30 ft, as long as you appear in a [[Threaten|threatened]] space. Any creatures you threaten take 2d8 (9) force damage and an equal amount of [[poise]] damage.

After you activate this ability, your turn immediately ends.
