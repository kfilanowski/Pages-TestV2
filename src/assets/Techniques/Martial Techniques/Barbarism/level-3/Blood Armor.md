Bathe in your own blood. While below 50% hp or if you took [[Bleeding|bleed]] damage this turn, gain +2 [[AC]]. If you stop bleeding, or heal above 50% hp, the buff remains, but you lose the AC bonus until you meet the techniques requirements.

If you are below 10 hp, gain +4 AC.
