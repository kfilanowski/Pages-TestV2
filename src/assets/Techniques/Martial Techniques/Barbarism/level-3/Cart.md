You manifest a cart of your own design as a standard action. Every time you manifest this cart, it is the same cart. The cart is capable of holding a dozen or so people and has an [[Extradimensional Space]] with 8 [[Inventory|inventory slots]]. You can cause this cart to vanish back to the cart realm as a standard action.

While attached to and dragging your cart, your [[land speed]] doubles for [[overland travel]]. To you, the contents within the cart are completely weightless. To anybody else, it weighs 2k lbs.

The cart lasts indefinitely, but disappears while you don’t have this technique memorized. Anything placed inside of the card when it disappears are deposited onto the ground.
