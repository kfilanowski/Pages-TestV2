Your eyes glow and your resting emotional state is angry.

The first time you are [[defeated]] and the condition is not negated or delayed by another feature, such as [[ferocity]] or [[Diehard]] you can choose to activate this contingency.

You become completely [[Immunity|immune]] to death/defeat. Any hp damage in excess of your current hp is  instead converted into [[Drained]] and increased by 5 (22 damage? 27 drain. 1 damage? 6 drain). When your drain matches your max hp, you instantly die.

When combat ends, or as an instant action, you can choose to end the effects of this technique. You are defeated and unconscious until you are no longer drained.

Your [[+HP]] can be increased through [[Healing]] effects during the combat, decreasing how quickly the drain accumulates, but you are always defeated after the effects of this technique wear off, regardless of your current hp.
