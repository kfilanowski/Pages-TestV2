Level 5 [[Scoundrel]]
Tags: -
Action: [[Special Attack]]

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 3 rounds   |
| Save           | Fort       |
Strike at a creature's tendons dealing an additional 4d8 (18) dmg and applying a unique [[Status]] (fort negates). For every 5 feet they move, they take 2 [[True Damage]]. If the target is [[DD]], this has no save.

Certain effects like teleportation, telekinetic movement or anything else not based on physical locomotion can bypass this effect.

This wound can be healed with a DC 25 [[First Aid (P)|first aid]] check, or by succeeding on a DC 25 [[Caster Level|CL]] check with a [[healing]] spell/effect.
