Level 6 [[Scoundrel]]
Tags: [[Recharge]], [[Teleportation]]
Action: Immediate

| Range          | -          |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft      |
| Duration       | 1 creature |
| Save           | -          |
You can choose to vanish when affected by any type of effect originating from a creature that you can see within range. At the end of the target's turn, you rapidly teleport and make [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]] against them and up to 3 other creatures within 30 ft, each dealing an additional 4d6 (14) dmg. You end this flurry adjacent to original target.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note</span> - The 4d6 bonus damage only gets added once per creature, even when [[Dual Wielding]].
