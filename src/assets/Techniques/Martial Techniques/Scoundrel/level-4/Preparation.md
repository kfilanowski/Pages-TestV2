Level 4 [[Scoundrel]]
Tags: [[Teleportation]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -       |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self    |
| Duration       | 1 round |
| Save           | -       |
Enter a defensive stance, giving you +4 [[AC]], +4 [[+Perception]] for 1 round and resetting your [[poise]]. When an attack made by a creature you can see misses you or fails to break your poise, you can choose to lose these bonuses to teleport within 5 feet of them and strike them with a [[melee attack]].

They are [[DD]] for this attack and take an additional 3d8 (13) dmg.
