Level 2 [[Scoundrel]]
Tags: -
Action: Immediate

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
When a creature attempts to manipulate an item, such as drinking a potion or opening a door, stop them. You automatically slap the item out of their hands onto the ground, jam the door or otherwise prevent the creature from utilizing equipment that can't be used to make attacks or cast spells.
