<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
Can only be used while at or below 50% hp, and only in response to taking damage.

Inflict [[Knocked Down|Knockdown]] on yourself and roll [[+Disguise]]/[[+Deception]] with a +2 bonus. The nearest creature rolls [[+Perception]] against you to determine if you're down for the count and everybody else takes their assessment as fact. You lose the knockdown status at the start of your next turn and your next attack against a creature makes them [[DD]].

This ability only works once each combat. Creatures that have seen you do it in other combats get [[advantage]] on their perception.
