Make a [[grapple]] attack with a +4 bonus that also makes them [[Bleeding|bleed]] for 2d6 (7) if successful. While grappled your target is also [[silenced]] and cannot remove the bleed.

You do not need to spend any actions to maintain this grapple. Your [[drag]] attacks automatically succeed against them while grappled. This resets the creatures [[poise]].
