Fire an arrow that deals an additional 2d8 (9) damage and embed it into a key spot on the targets body on hit with a variety of effects.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Hand</span> - Creatures suffers a -2 penalty to [[Attack]] using weapons/casting spells through that hand while the arrow remains.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Leg</span> - Creatures [[land speed]] is reduced by 15 and it cannot [[dash]], [[jump]] or [[Core Rules, How to Play/Actions/Common Actions/Full Round Actions/Charge]] while the arrow remains.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Etc</span> - You can target other regions of a creature's body, such as their jaw, or a tentacle to apply the -2 attack penalty to that attack specifically, or a wing/fin to restrict the movement of a swimming or flying creature.

The arrow can be removed as a standard action without issue. It can be removed as a swift action by taking 2d8 (9) true damage. If you have [[+Medicine]] proficiency, removing as a swift action deals no damage.
