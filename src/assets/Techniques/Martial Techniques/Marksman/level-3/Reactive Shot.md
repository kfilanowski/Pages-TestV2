Level 3 [[Marksman]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard 

| Range          | 60 ft                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 round                                         |
| Save           | -                                               |
Mark an area with a glowing circle and take aim. Until the end of your next turn, you can automatically make a [[Simple Attack|simple]] [[Ranged Attack]] against any creature that moves into, within or out of the area, only firing a shot 1/turn. Such attacks deal an additional d8 (4) dmg.

The attacks must still be within range of a ranged weapon you wield, and you must have a method of reloading after each shot.
