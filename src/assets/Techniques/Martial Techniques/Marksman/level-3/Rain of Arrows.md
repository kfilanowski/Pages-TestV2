Level 3 [[Marksman]]
Tags: [[Su]], [[Persistent]], [[Upcast]]
Action: [[Standard Action]]

| Range          | 60 ft                                                   |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 5 ft radius cylinder, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 round                                                 |
| Save           | Reflex                                                  |
Fire a single powerful shot directly up into the air, when it falls it splits into many projectiles. The area is considered [[difficult terrain]] until the projectiles cease. Creatures in the area takes 4d8+LvL (18+LvL) damage (reflex halves).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) dmg and increase the duration of the effect by 1 round.
