Fire a single powerful shot at a creature that crashes on impact. The target takes an additional 2d8 (9) damage and is pushed back 10 ft [[On-hit]]. Creatures within 10 ft of the impact zone are also knocked back 10 ft away from the target creature (fort negates).

This attack is considered to be from a [[+Siege Weapon]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For each additional mana spent, increase the knockback distance of both effects by 5 ft.
