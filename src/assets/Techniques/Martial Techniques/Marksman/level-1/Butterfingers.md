Make a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[disarm]] attack with a +4 bonus. If successful, the disarmed item continues flying along with the projectile until it lands, either in a surface, or in the ground at the end of its range. Attacking from different angles can cause different behaviors, like with [[High Ground]] it won't go particularly far. It requires a swift action to yank it free.

You can also use this technique against unattended objects for a similar effect.
