Level 4 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Mana Burn]]
Action: [[FRA]]

| Range          | 120 ft                                  |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius burst, centered on a point |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                |
| Save           | Reflex                                  |
You form a literal cannon made out of shimmering magical force that is immobile and automatically comes loaded with mana from casting this spell. As part of casting the spell, you can fire the cannon, dealing 5d8+CL (22+CL) force damage in a 10 ft radius at the point of impact (reflex halves). These cannon shots count as [[+Siege Weapon|siege weapons]].

You or any ally can spend 4 mana as a standard action to again fire the cannon at a new point (still using your CL). But the cannon can only fire once each round.

You mana burn 4 mana while this spell is active. When the spell ends, the mana burn reduces to 1.
