Level 4 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Light]], [[Force]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Varies   |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | Reflex   |
Call down ray(s) of light from the heavens onto your foes. The spell’s [[Caster Level|CL]] increases by 2 at night with a direct view of the night sky. 40 ft around the area the spell impact becomes bright light for one minute.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Shower</span> - Target a 30 ft line in the spells area. Creatures in the area take 5d8+CL (22+CL) force damage (reflex halves).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Strike</span> - Target a single creature. The creature takes 5d10+CL (27+CL) force damage and is [[blinded]] for 1 round (reflex halves and reduces blinded to [[Blurred Vision]]).
