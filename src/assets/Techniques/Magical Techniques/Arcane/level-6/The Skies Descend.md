You mark a position on the ground and a dark shadow forms over a 20 ft radius, and an bright and shining object hurtling towards it is visible from the sky. At the start of your next turn, a small star lands on this spot, dealing 7d8+CL (31+CL) force damage and [[Dazed|dazes]] for 1 round (reflex halves dmg and reduces daze to [[Staggered]]).

The star then sends a shockwave out to affect all other enemies within a 100ft radius. They are automatically treated as though they pass their save.
