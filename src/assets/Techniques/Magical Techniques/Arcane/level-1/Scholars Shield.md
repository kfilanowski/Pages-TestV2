A shield made of vibrant magical force rotates around your body. You can take [[+Shield Actions]], but only against [[Spell Attack|spell attacks]]. Gets a few additional options
* Can also be used to block [[force]] effects that normally automatically hit (such as [[lucky star]] and [[magic missile]]) as an immediate action
* Can also be used to grant a +2 bonus to a [[+Saving Throw]] against a spell as an immediate action

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special: </span>This technique is compatible with the [[Shield Focus]] feat.
