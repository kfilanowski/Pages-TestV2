| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 3 rounds |
| Save           | -        |
3 small pale white orbs emerge from your staff and float around you. At the end of each of your turns, an orb bumps into to a creature within your [[Reach|natural reach]]. A single creature can only be hit by 1 orb a turn, but can be affected by multiple over several turns.

When an orb bumps into a creature, it pops. If multiple creatures are within your reach, multiple orbs pop. When all three orbs have popped, deal (3+CL) force damage to all creatures within your natural reach and gain (3+CL) temp hp.
