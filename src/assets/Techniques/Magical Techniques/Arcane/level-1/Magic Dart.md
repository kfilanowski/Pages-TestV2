Level 1 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | -          |
Make a ranged attack with a dart that deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) force dmg. The dart sticks inside of them, magnetizing other magic effects to it. Creatures get a +2 bonus to [[Spell Attack|spell attacks]] against this creature within 1 minute.

This dart can be removed as a swift action while wielding a [[Staff Component]] by making a DC (5+CL) [[Caster Level]] check. If the attack was a [[Crit]], it cannot be removed. It is automatically removed if targeted with [[Dispel Magic]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase your attack bonus with the dart by 1.
