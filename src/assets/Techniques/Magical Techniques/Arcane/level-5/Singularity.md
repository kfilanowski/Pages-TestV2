Level 5 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Persistent]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 10 ft                 |
| -------------- | --------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | A space on the ground |
| Duration       | 3 rounds              |
| Save           | Fort                  |
Choose a point in space to manifest a dark sphere with a selective gravitational pull. Immediately upon casting, and as a swift action each subsequent turn, 3 creatures (or objects weighing less than a ton) of your choosing within 100 feet of the singularity are pulled 30 feet closer (fort reduces to 10ft). Any creature in an adjacent space is considered touching it, dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) force damage.

Pulling objects can also be used as an attack form dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) damage of an appropriate type (reflex halves).
