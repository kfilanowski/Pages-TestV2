Tags: [[Force]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20x20ft wall, anchored on the ground |
| Duration       | -                                    |
| Save           | Reflex                               |
You create a wall of shimmering force. It is transparent, giving you line of sight, but blocks [[line of effect]] and absorbs all attacks that attempt to pass through it. It has 50 hp and dissipates if this hp is lost.

A creature can attempt to pass through the wall by making a reflex save as a swift action. Failure deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) force damage to them and prevents them from passing through.

A wall of force can be destroyed with [[Brute Force]] with a DC of 40.
