Level 5 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Recharge]]

| Range          | 60 ft           |
| -------------- | --------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self+1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round         |

You gently float into the air and the cradle yourself with energy that takes the shape of a full moon. You enter [[stasis]] until the start of your next turn.

As a free action at the start of your next turn, launch the moon at an enemy within range. Roll a
[[Ranged Attack|ranged]] [[spell attack]] to deal 6d8+CL (27+CL) force damage. The creature also gains [[weakness]] 5 to force damage for 1 minute.
