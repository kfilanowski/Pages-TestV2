Level 2 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                                   |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature<br>10 ft radius, centered on target creature |
| Duration       | -                                                       |
| Save           | Reflex                                                  |
Make a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]] by firing a magical crystal at a creature, dealing 3d8+CL (13+CL) force dmg. Regardless if it hits or misses, it explodes in the targets space, [[Knocked Down|knocking down]] all creatures within 10 ft (reflex negates).

If your attack lands, the target has [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on its save. If your attack is a [[Crit]], the target gets no save against the knock down effect.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase your attack by 1.
