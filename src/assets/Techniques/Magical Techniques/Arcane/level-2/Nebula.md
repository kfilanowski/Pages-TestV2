Level 2 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Light]], [[Force]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -          |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft cone |
| Duration       | 3 rounds   |
| Save           | -          |
Conjure a cloud of stardust that illuminates in a 20 ft radius. It obscures vision beyond 5 feet, granting [[Partial Concealment]]. Anything beyond 5 feet grants [[Total Concealment]]. At the end of the spell's duration, it ignites dealing 3d8+CL (13+CL) force damage to all creatures in the area.
