Conjure an oversized gavel from the tip of your staff and slam it down onto a creature within reach as a [[Melee Attack|melee]] [[Spell Attack]]. They take 4d10+CL (22+CL) force damage and are [[Knocked Down]] [[On-hit]]. Creatures (other than you and the original target) within a 10 ft radius of the target take this damage and are knocked down (reflex halves dmg and negates knockdown).

If you [[Crit]] with the gavel attack, the creature is also made [[Clumsy]] 2.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, get +1 attack.
