You can cause 3 copies of a held melee weapon made entirely out of magic force to form slowly, float above your head and follow you at a fixed distance. You can launch them at targets of your choosing. Make a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]]. It deals the damage of a standard melee attack but as force damage.

You can fire any number of these weapons in a turn, but none can target the same creature more than once. You can also choose to hold any number of weapons for later turns, where they can be fired as a free action.

Any attacks that result in a [[crit]] are not consumed and return for use next round.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, conjure an additional copy of the weapon to form above your head.
