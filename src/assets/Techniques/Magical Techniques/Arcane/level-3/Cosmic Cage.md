Level 3 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Concentration]]
Actions: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius, centered on a creature |
| Duration       | 1 round                              |
| Save           | Will                                 |
You create a cage like structure at the edge of the radius of this spell. Creatures can still see in and out of the cage, and still have [[line of effect]]. The cage provides no [[Cover]].

If a creature moves in or out of the cage, willingly or unwillingly, it is [[dazed]] for 1 round (fort reduces effect to [[staggered]]) and its movement is halted. A creature can only be affected by the spell once.
