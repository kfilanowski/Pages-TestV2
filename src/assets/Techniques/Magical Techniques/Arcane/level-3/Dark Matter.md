Level 3 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                          |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 5 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | -                                              |
| Save           | Reflex                                         |
You mark a position on the ground and a dark shadow forms over it, indicating great danger to those nearby. At the start of your next turn, a chunk of condensed cosmic energy lands on this spot, dealing 4d12+CL (26+CL) force damage (reflex halves).

If a creature's speed is zero (such as from being [[stunned]] or [[entangled]]) it has [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on its save.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the spells range by 5 ft.
