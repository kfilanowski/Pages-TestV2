Level 3 [[Arcane]]
Tags: [[Force]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 2 rounds   |
| Save           | Reflex     |
Fire a magical projectile that homes in on a specific target and follows it around, damaging each time it passes through. The projectile moves 30 ft each round and stops when it reaches the target's space, dealing 3d8+CL (13+CL) force damage (reflex halves). If the target does not move out of range, it takes the damage repeatedly at the start of each of your turns.

The projectile moves in a straight line from its starting position to the target. If it impacts solid terrain during its travel, it dissipates harmlessly. It cannot harm any creature other than the original target.

Any attack resulting in a [[crit]] returns back to you to be fired again on later turns.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, the spell lasts an additional round.
