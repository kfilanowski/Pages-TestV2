Level 3 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Fire]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                         |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 5 ft radius, centered on a point on a surface |
| Duration       | -                                             |
| Save           | Reflex                                        |
You throw an enormous ball of fire that attaches to something, then explodes forcefully, dealing 4d8+CL (18+CL) fire damage and [[Knocked Down|knocking down]] creatures struck (reflex halves dmg and negates knock down).

You can target one specific creature with a [[Ranged Attack|ranged]] [[spell attack]]. If you land it, the creature's space is the center of the explosion, and the DC is 2 higher for that creature. If you miss, the fire falls directly down and then explodes when it first impacts a surface.

This also destroys flammable undergrowth, sends small objects flying, starts fires, disperses atmospheric conditions, and otherwise causes the effects one would expect from a fiery explosion.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase range by 5 ft.
Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the explosion radius by 5.
