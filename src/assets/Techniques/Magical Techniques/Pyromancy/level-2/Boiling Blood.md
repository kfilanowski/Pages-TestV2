Level 2 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Fire]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 3 rounds   |
| Save           | Fort       |
Ignite a creatures insides, making them convulse in pain. They are [[staggered]] and take (CL) fire damage each round at the start of its turn (fort negates stagger). At the end of each turn, save again.

Creatures that are already [[ablaze]] take a -4 penalty to their save.
