Level 1 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Fire]], [[Buff]], [[Persistent]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You light yourself on fire and the fire hungers for things to burn. At the end of your turn, you take 3 fire damage. Whenever a creature is within 5 ft of you, they take 3 fire damage.
Your [[land speed]] increases by 5 ft.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the damage taken and dealt by 1.
Every 4 CLs, increase your speed by 5.
