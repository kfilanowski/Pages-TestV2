You toss a mote of flame that hungers for more if it successfully consumes its target. Make a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]] against a creature that deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage. If it [[Defeated|defeats]] the creature, it make another attack that deals the same damage to another creature of your choosing within 30 ft of the target. This can continue indefinitely.

Upcast - For every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, gain +1 attack.
