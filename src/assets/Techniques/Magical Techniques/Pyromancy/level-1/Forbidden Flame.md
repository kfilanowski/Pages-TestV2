Level 1 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Fire]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
Convert all of your mana into heat with a spark. Cause the air to explode in a creatures face, dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage. You automatically spend all of your [[mana]] when you use this technique. For each mana spent beyond the first, increase the spells CL by 1.
