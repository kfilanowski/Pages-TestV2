Level 1 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Fire]], [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]], [[Persistent]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | One Space, 30ft high |
| Duration       | 1 minute             |
| Save           | Reflex               |
Ignite a circle on the ground and cause a pillar of flame to erupt from it. Creatures in the area of the pillar take 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, create an additional pillar.
