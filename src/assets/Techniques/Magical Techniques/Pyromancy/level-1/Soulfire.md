Level 1 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Fire]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Reflex     |
Your mana ignites and you release this uncontainable energy at an enemy. The target takes 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage and are set ablaze (reflex halves damage and negates ablaze). Your [[Memory]] decreases by 1, forcing you to choose a technique to forget. If that spell was currently active as a [[buff]] or [[concentration]] effect, it immediately ends.

This debuff ends when you [[rest]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase range by 5 ft.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Pro Tip:</span>
For creatures with innate techniques, consider rendering one of their at will abilities inaccessible.
