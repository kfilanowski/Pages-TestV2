Level 4 [[Pyromancy]]
Tags: [[Persistent]], [[Concentration]], [[Fire]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                      |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft line or 5 ft radius circle, See text |
| Duration       | -                                          |
| Save           | Reflex                                     |
Cause a wall of flame to ignite within range. The wall is 20 ft high and 5 ft wide. The line, does not need to originate from you, allowing you great creativity in placement. The line must be in one continuous direction, or in a circular formation. It can move along changes in elevation, so long as that elevation is not taller than the wall itself.

Creatures inside of the wall when cast, or that move through it take 5d8+CL (23+CL) fire damage (reflex halves).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the length of the line and range by 10 ft. 
Every 30 additional ft can increase the radius of the circle by 5 ft.
