Your staff transforms into electricity and coats your hand. Make a [[Melee Attack|melee]] [[spell attack]] against a creature that deals d10+CL (5+CL) zap damage. The creature cannot make AoOs against you this turn [[On-hit]].

If the creature is wearing metal armor or is sufficiently metallic, get a +4 bonus to [[Attack]]. Medium armor and heavy armor are metallic by default.
