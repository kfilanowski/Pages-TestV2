Tags: [[Necrotic]]

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | -          |
Make a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]] against a creature with a bony spectral hand. Deals d10+CL (5+CL) necrotic damage and applies [[Bone Chilled]] for 1 round.

If the target creature is within your natural reach, you can make this attack as a [[melee attack]]. If you are [[Grapple|grappling]] a creature, you can cast this spell as an option to maintain this grapple.
