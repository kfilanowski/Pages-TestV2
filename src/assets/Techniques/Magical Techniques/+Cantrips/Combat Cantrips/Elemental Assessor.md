You fire a bolt of energy that rapidly changes color and consistency to match varying elements. Make a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[spell attack]] against a creature that deals d10+CL (5+CL) [[True Damage]]. If the creature possesses a [[Weakness]] to an elemental damage type, this spell instead deals that type of elemental damage, changing its tag as appropriate.

If a creature has multiple elemental damage weaknesses, this spell does multiple damage types, amplifying its damage with each weakness they possess.
