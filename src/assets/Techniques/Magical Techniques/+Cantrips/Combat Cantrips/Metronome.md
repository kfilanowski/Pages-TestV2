Wiggle your staff and cause a random effect from the following list. Roll a D6.

1. Bury yourself underground until the start of your next turn, effectively putting you in [[stasis]] against anything that isn't an [[earth]]-based attack. If you attack a foe on follow up turn, they are [[DD]].
2. [[Confused|Confuse]] yourself. Immediately roll on confusion chart, then end the confusion.
3. Cause a banana peel to appear under a random creature within 30 ft of you. They are [[knocked down]], (reflex negates).
4. Your tongue becomes long, powerful and elastic, allowing you to make a free [[drag]] attack with a range of 30 ft.
5. Move 60 ft in a straight line of your choosing [[elusive]], stopping when you hit a solid surface. Creatures along the way take d6 (3) crush damage
6. You and an enemy within 30 ft of you are set [[ablaze]] (reflex negates for you both).

You cannot cast this cantrip on consecutive turns.
