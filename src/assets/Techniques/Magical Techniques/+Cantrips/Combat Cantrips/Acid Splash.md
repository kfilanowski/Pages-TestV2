Your hand, or your staff/seal turn a sickly green color and start bubbling. You flick it towards a creature, making a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]], and a sticky glob of caustic goo flies at them. Deals [[Caster Level|CL]] poison damage. 
[[On-hit]], this damage happens again at the end of the creatures turn unless they are [[wet]], or otherwise remove the acid through some kind of washing.
