Tags: [[Earth]]

| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |

You cause the ground underneath a creature to react unfavorably to their movement. If the targeted creature moves at all on their next turn, a piece of the ground intercepts their movement. They take d10+CL (5+CL) bludgeoning damage and they have their speed is reduced by 15 until they spend a swift action bitching and moaning about it.
