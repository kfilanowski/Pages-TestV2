You create a visible barrier above your head that shields you from the elements. You are protected from things such as rain and sunlight. Gain [[Cover]] from attacks that originate above you. Creatures that huddle next to you can also share this benefit.

Certain effects that mimic weather can potentially be mitigated by this spell such as [[call cheese storm]] and [[solar eclipse]].

Average doorways are considered [[difficult terrain]] while this spell is active.
