| Range          | Reach  |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -      |
| Duration       | 1 hour |

You create a small spectral animal of significance to you. This animal follows basic commands and can roll survival for [[Navigation]] using your bonus. It cannot interact with the physical world and disappears if it takes damage. Casting this spell again causes any other instances of it to end.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the duration by another hour.
