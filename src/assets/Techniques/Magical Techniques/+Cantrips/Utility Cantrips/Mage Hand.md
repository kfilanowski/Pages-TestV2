You create a ghostly ethereal hand that can pick up items and perform simple tasks such as opening doors, pulling up pants or tipping over buckets. It can carry up to 10 lbs and has a +0 bonus to [[+Athletics]]. The hand cannot do anything that takes longer than one turn to perform unless you add the [[concentration]] tag and focus on its actions with yours each turn.

You can use the hand to perform a [[bully]] or [[shove]] combat maneuver with a bonus of +1.
