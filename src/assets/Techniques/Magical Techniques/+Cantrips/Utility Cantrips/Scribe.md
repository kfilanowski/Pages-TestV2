[[+Cantrips]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard
Focus: A writing utensil and something to write on

| Range          | Reac                   |
| -------------- | ---------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 piece of written tex |
| Duration       | -                      |
| Save           | -                      |
You touch a book, scroll or other form of writing and give the text a faint glow. The utensil moves on its own to copy down the content you targeted onto the paper, parchment etc. that you provided as a focus. It copies down the text at a rate of about 1 page of an average book per minute in your handwriting. If you are trained in the [[+Linguistics]] skill, you can duplicate the handwriting.
