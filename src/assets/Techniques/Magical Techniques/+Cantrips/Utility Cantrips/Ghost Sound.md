[[+Cantrips]]
Tags: [[Figment]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft          |
| -------------- | -------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -              |
| Duration       | Up to 1 minute |
| Save           | Will           |
You create a volume of sound that rises, recedes, approaches, or remains at a fixed place. You choose what type of sound is created when casting it and cannot change the sound.

The sound mimics whatever you want. It can sound like a cafeteria, crowd, waterfall, lion roaring etc, but cannot produce intelligible speech, or sound like a specific person. It can be very loud, but cannot be loud enough to cause harm or otherwise affect anything physically.

When exposed to the apparent location the sound originates from, a creature can roll a will save to realize it is fake.
