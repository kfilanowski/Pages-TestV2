Level 1 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Curse]], [[Upcast]]
Action: 1 minute
Focus: One gold piece

| Range          | Reach          |
| -------------- | -------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 water source |
| Duration       | -              |
| Save           | -              |
You stand at the edge of a well and fill a coin with malefic energy. By dropping the coin into the well, it subtly causes it to become poisonous. Creatures that drink from it become [[drained]] 6 (fort halves).

This curse can be removed by removing the coin from the well.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Weird scaling...
At CL 4 - Affects small bodies of water, such as lakes and creeks.
At CL 8 - Affects large lakes and 1 mile of river.
At CL 16 - Affects an entire river, regardless of its length.
