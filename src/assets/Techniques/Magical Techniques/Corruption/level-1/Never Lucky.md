Level 1 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |

Cause a creature to be unlucky. They next time they roll an [[Attack]] or [[+Saving Throw|saving throw]], they roll it at [[Advantage|Disadvantage]].

Outside of combat, this has the [[subtle]] tag, but only affects [[Skill Check|skill checks]], [[Ability Check|ability checks]] and [[Luck Check|luck checks]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 8..
The creature's next 2 rolls are at disadvantage.
