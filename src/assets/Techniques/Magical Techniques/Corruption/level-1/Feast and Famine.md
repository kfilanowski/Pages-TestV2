Level 1 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Healing]], [[Overheal]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft            |
| -------------- | ---------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Inanimate Plants |
| Duration       | -                |
| Save           | -                |
You wave your staff in a circle to absorb the life from any inanimate plants in the area. You heal yourself for 4+CL hp and automatically end all [[Bleeding|bleed]] effects. This kills the plants.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
This spell only functions if you are not in a form of desolate terrain. Desolate could include places like arctic terrain, desert the deep ocean or many urban areas.

If it's iffy, you can always roll a [[Luck Check]]! (with a DC dependent on terrain)
