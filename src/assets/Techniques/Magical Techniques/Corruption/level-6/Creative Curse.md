You can inflict a curse upon a creature with very few limitations, listed below.
- It cannot deal [[+HP]] damage
- It cannot inflict a [[Status]] effect
- It cannot match a pre-existing curse [[Spell]]
- Once you inflict a particular type of curse with this spell, you cannot inflict it again, or anything similar (ie "you can't get within 10 ft of me" then the next time its "you can't get within 15 ft of me")

You can choose to remove the curse at an instant action at any time, or apply a very convoluted method of removing the curse. Other than that, it requires a DC 40 [[+Medicine]] check, or some type of incredibly powerful [[GM Magic]].
