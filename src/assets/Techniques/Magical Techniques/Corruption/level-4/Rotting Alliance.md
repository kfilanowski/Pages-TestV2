Level 4 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Curse]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft       |
| -------------- | ----------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 2 creatures |
| Duration       | -           |
| Save           | Will        |
You curse 2 creatures with a rot that accelerates their demise when they exist near one another. When a creature with this curse exists within 15 feet of an ally that is also cursed during its turn, it takes 4d6 (14) necrotic damage and is [[drained]] 5. This is a [[persistent]] effect.

Each cast of this spell is not mutually exclusive. Multiple castings of this spell have the potential to include more creatures in this effect.

This curse is removed by the cursed creatures all signing their name on something like paper or parchment without damaging each other in the process, and then burning it.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the number of creature's targeted by 1.
