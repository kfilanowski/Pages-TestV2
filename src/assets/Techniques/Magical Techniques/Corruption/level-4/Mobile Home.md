*Give new life to your house!*

You spend the cast time of this spell wandering around your house, placing magic sigils on floors walls and ceilings. During this wandering you assign a passphrase to activate the magic. At the end of the cast time, you activate all of them and they fade away, causing the house to slowly animate. 

Whenever you speak the passphrase, the house grows legs, either literally from nothing, using the roots underneath the house or whatnot. Your house now functions as a [[Rock Stats|rock]], but it moves much more slowly (40 speed, or ~10 mph our of combat) and obviously has a lot more storage space, scaling with the size of your residence. Its [[+HP]] is (10*[[Caster Level|CL]]). If it is [[defeated]], your house immediately crashes onto the ground, causing severe damage to anything inside. Creatures inside when this happens take 20 crush and [[poise]] damage. This also happens if your house is subject to an effect that [[Dispel|dispels]] it.

Your animated house has a [[INT]] score of +0, and it understands any languages you know. It can perceive the world around it as though it had eyes and ears, and can decide on its own whether or not it wants to let people into its doors, furniture, windows etc. It still obeys its master at the end of the day.

While on the move, objects within the house may jiggle around as the house takes its giant steps, but they will not fall off their surfaces. Creatures can even comfortably rest within the moving house.

When not in motion, the house's legs will sink into the ground, appearing in all ways as a normal house until it meddles with someone trying to get in or steal your things. It can perform the following action once each turn as a standard action, using your [[DC]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Banish</span> [[Su]] [[teleportation]] - Forcefully eject a creature out of the house (will negates).

You can only have one Mobile Home activated at once. The homes may change, but the animating force of the home remembers you regardless of where it inhabits.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
The spell asks you to have a deed of ownership to make it so you can't just steal people's houses with this spell. If you made it yourself, or obviously received it as some kind of gift/prize without documentation, that should suffice.
