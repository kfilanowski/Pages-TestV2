Level 3 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Poison]], [[Persistent]], [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                        |
| Save           | Fort                                            |
You cause a purple or green mist to spread over and area. Creatures inside cough and wheeze, becoming sickened 1 the first round of exposure. If the creature is already sickened, then they instead take 3d8+CL (13+CL) poison damage (fort halves).

You have [[Immunity]] to your own cast of corrupting mist.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 12...
You can increase the duration of the spell to 1 hour. Every round it persists, it spreads an additional 10 ft. It only spreads in one direction 180 degrees.
