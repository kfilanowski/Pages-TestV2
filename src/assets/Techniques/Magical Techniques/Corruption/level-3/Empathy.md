Level 3 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Curse]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                   |
| -------------- | ----------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 victim and one vessel |
| Duration       | -                       |
| Save           | Will                    |
Choose two creatures within range and decide one to be the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">victim</span> and the other to be the <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">vessel</span>. For the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">victim</span> (will negates). Any time the <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">vessel</span> takes damage, the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">victim</span> takes half that damage as [[True Damage]]. This damage does not reduce the damage the <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">vessel</span> takes, only adding an additional instance of damage to the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">victim</span>.

To remove the curse, the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">victim</span> and the <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">vessel</span> must wear some type of footwear owned by the other. For unlucky souls to be empathetically bound to a creature that doesn't or can't wear footwear, they must kiss. Each requires a standard action (and cooperation).


Example.
Vessel takes 9 damage from an attack (after all damage modifiers like, saving throws and [[DR]]).

So vessel takes 9 damage.
Victim takes 4 true damage.

BASICALLY, at no point is the damage reduces or split between the creature. One creature essentially takes damage every time another creature takes damage.
