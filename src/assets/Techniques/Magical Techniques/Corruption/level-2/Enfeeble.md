Level 2 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Curse]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
With heavy arms, the creature struggles to bring the full force of their attacks and spells. Whenever the creature deals damage, that damage is decreased by 2 (unless it is [[true damage]]). Damage over time effects are not affected by this curse.

While a non-minion creature within 60 ft is afflicted with this curse (and you are its caster), you get [[advantage]] on fortitude saves.

In order to remove this curse, a creature must "sheathe" and "unsheathe" a weapon 3 times without doing anything else. In combat this requires a FRA.
