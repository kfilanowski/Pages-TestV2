Level 2 [[Corruption]]
Tags: [[Curse]]
Action: 1 minute
Focus: 1 inert mana crystal

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
The curse of the harvester is a tool used by creatures to remotely collect mana. Because of the cast time, the creature must either be willing or defeated to be the target of this spell. You link a creature to the inert mana crystal used as a focus. You also specify a time of day.

Every day at the specified time, the target is [[Mana Burn]]ed until they only have 1 mana left, as though they had donated mana. The harvester receives (this mana value-1), which they can use as they see fit.

This curse can only be removed by destroying the inert mana crystal used as the focus for the initial casting. The harvest-ee can innately tell if a crystal is bound to them by looking at it, feeling it etc.
