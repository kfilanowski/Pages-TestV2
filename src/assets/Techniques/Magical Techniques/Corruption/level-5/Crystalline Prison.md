This spell used to seal people away, usually powerful individuals, so their mana can be harvested for large scale projects. When cast, you start a complex series of incantations to cover a creature in the material component of the spell, causing them to be [[Entombed]] permanently. Once complete the creature can do little more than wiggle slightly. They can hear muffled sounds from the outside, but not to an extent that what they hear is intelligible. The creature's weight doubles and they are frozen in whatever pose they were sealed in. Most details about the creature's appearance are lost on the rather crude rocky covering, making it hard to identify them.

While in this state, they are treated as willing subjects for an [[Mana Donation]].
