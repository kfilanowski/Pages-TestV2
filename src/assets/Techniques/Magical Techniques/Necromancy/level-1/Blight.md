Level 1 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: [[Necrotic]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | Fort       |
You briefly allow a creature to experience the joy of decomposition. They take 2d8+CL (9+CL) necrotic damage and are [[sickened]] 2 (fort halves dmg and negates sickened).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, target an additional creature.
