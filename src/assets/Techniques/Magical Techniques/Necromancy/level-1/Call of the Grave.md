Level 1 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: [[Upcast]], [[Earth]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Reflex     |
Cause several arms to erupt from the ground and grab onto a creature. The creature is [[entangled]] (reflex negates).

The hands share your AC, and have poise equal to their AC. [[On-hit]], when the hands are [[poise]] broken, or with a successful [[Breakout]] as a standard action (DC=their AC) they retreat back into the ground.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, target an additional creature in range.
