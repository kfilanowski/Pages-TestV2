Level 1 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: [[Companion]]]
Action: Standard
Focus: 1 instrument

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 corpse   |
| Duration       | 10 minutes |
| Save           | -          |
Raise a skeleton from a corpse whose only calling is to jam. Give it the instrument used as the focus of this spell and it will play it with a bonus of (CL+2) whenever you ask. When not playing, it follows you around.

If you give the skeleton a trumpet, its bonus increases to (CL+4).
