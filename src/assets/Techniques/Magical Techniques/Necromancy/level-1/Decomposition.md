Level 1 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                   |
| -------------- | ----------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 12 corpses, or 1 undead |
| Duration       | -                       |
| Save           | -                       |
You cause a group of corpses to rapidly experience decomposition, turning in them into skeletons almost instantly. Perfect for cleanly storing in your closet.

If cast on an undead creature, they instead take 2d6+CL (7+CL) true dmg. This can happen accidentally if you happen to target an undead buried amongst corpses. If you target multiple undead accidentally, 1 randomly takes the damage.
