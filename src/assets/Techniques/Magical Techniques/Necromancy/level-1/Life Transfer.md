You transfer fundamental life energy between yourself and the target. Choose one.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Heal</span>
You can inflict [[drained]] upon yourself in order to heal the target. You become drained, up to your [[Caster Level|CL]] and you heal double that in [[+HP]] to the target, or remove an equal amount of drain from the target.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Harm</span>
Alternatively, you can [[Mana Burn]] to inflict drained stacks you possess onto another creature. This requires an attack roll and removes up to [[Caster Level|CL]] drain from yourself to inflict it on your target [[on-hit]]. If you miss, you do not mana burn. You must be drained to use this variant of the spell.
