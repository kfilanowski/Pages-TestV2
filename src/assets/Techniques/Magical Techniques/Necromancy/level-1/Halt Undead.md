Level 1 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 undead |
| Duration       | 3 rounds |
| Save           | Will     |
You command an undead creature to bend to your will, and you will it to stand still. It is [[entangled]] (will negates). An intelligent undead creature gets a new save at the end of each of its turns.

If an undead [[companion]] is affected by this spell, you can [[aid]] them if they are within 30 ft to free them, barking a new command.
