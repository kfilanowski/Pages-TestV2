A corpse reaches out and attempts to [[Topple]] or [[Shove]] a creature within the [[Reach|natural reach]] it had in life. The attack bonus the corpse uses is your [[Caster Level|CL]]. The target is [[DD]] for this maneuver.

When used out of combat, this has the [[subtle]] tag.
