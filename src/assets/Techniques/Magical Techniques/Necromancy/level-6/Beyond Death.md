When you are [[defeated]] or killed, you can choose to spend 6 mana to enter a ghost state. While in this state, you can freely speak, use [[Su]]pernatural abilities and cast [[Spell|spells]], but your speed is 0 and you cannot make [[Weapon Attack]]s, [[Natural Attack]]s or [[Unarmed Attack]]s.

At the end of your next turn, your spirit reunites with your body and this effect ends.

Your ghost remains tethered to your body. You cannot move on your own without the aid of magic, but other creatures can physically move your body to adjust the position of your ghost.

This effect can only happen once each day.
