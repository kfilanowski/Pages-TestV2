Level 2 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: 
Action: 1 minute
Material: 1 skull of a [[+Races|race]]

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -        |
| Duration       | 24 hours |
| Save           | -        |
Speak aloud a specific task and channel your energy into a skull. At the end of the duration, the skull disintegrates. Around you emerges several skeletons from the ground. The exact number varies, but cannot be more that a dozen.

Depending on the task you asked of the skeleton, you are granted the bare minimum number of simple laborers that are required to do things like operate a ship or move many small objects. They dutifully perform the task they were commanded when the spell was cast. When the job is complete or the spell ends, they crumble to dust.

The skeletons effectively have -1 in all of their [[Ability Scores]] and no [[Skill Proficiency]]. They cannot perform the [[Aid]] action or aid in combat at all. If they take damage, they clatter into pieces and reform after 1 round, unless that damage was [[Holy]] damage.
