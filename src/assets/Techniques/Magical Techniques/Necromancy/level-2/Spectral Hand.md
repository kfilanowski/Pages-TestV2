You summon an ghostly bloodied hand that you can used to deliver melee attacking spells from a range. [[Spell Attack|Spell attacks]] with a range of reach gain a range of 30 ft. You can make these attacks as [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]] or [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]]. Defaults to melee.

The hand ambiently floats around you when not in use, but you can choose to pocket it if you don't want creatures to see it.
