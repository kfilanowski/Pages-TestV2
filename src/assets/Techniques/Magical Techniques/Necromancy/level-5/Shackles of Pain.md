Level 5 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | Will       |
You share your pain with another creature, represented by ethereal shackles that connect you two (will negates). While within 60 ft of this creature, you take half damage, and the other half of the damage is transferred to it as [[true damage]]. If the creature leaves this range, the shackle breaks.

Spaces moving away from the caster are considered [[Difficult Terrain]] to the target.
