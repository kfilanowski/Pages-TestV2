Level 5 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Curse]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | 60 ft            |
| -------------- | ---------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 willing undead |
| Duration       | 2 rounds         |
| Save           | -                |
A surge of necrotic energy imbues an undead creature, giving it a very noticeable aura. It takes a -5 penalty to [[+Stealth]] and becomes [[Invincible]]. It is afflicted with a curse that destroys it at the end of its second turn.
