Level 4 [[Necromancy]]
Tags: [[Mana Burn]], [[Healing]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
You can remove practically any non-[[Permanent Status]] ailment at the cost of your own magic. You mana burn to remove it, increasing in amount based on the status ailment.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">1 - Minor Status</span> - Asleep, bleed, blurred vision, charmed, entangled, fatigued, fascinated, grounded, knocked down, shaken/sicken/clumsy stacks, frosty, wet.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">2 - Major Status</span> - Bone chilled, ablaze, blinded, confused, envenomed, frightened, staggered, silenced, taunt.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">3 - Extreme Status</span> - Dazed, pacified, stun, stasis.

You can change the mana burn into [[Drained|drain]] to cast this spell as a swift action.
