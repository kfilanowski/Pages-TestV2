Give your undead [[Companion|companions]] within the spells area a +4 bonus to [[Attack]] this turn.

Choose one specific creature within range of the spell. All undead under your control rush this target down, moving up to it and making a [[Simple Attack]]. If the undead can make ranged attacks, it need not move. This is in addition to whatever actions they take on their turn.
