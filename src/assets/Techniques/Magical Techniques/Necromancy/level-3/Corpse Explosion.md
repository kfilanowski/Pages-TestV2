The effect, range and cast time of this varies depending on what you target.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Allied Undead (Immediate)</span> - You can cast this freely on any undead you control, even in response to one being destroyed. The creature bloats and cracks before bursting, dealing 3d8+CL (13+CL) necrotic damage (reflex halves). This kills the undead.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">A Corpse (Standard) </span>- You can cast this spell on any corpse. Its range increases to 60 ft, its AoE increases to 10 ft but its cast time increases to a standard action.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your CL is at least 12...
You set off a chain reaction, causing all corpses in the spell's [[AoE]] to also explode, and the corpses they effect and so on. A creature can still only be damaged by a single explosion.
