| -------------- | ---------- |

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
This spell can only be cast when [[Concentration|concentrating]] on something.

Release your mind from the strain of concentration, channeling this raw mental energy into a projectile you launch at a target within range. Make a [[Ranged Attack]] that deals 3d10+CL (16+CL) psychic damage. This spell immediately ends the spell you were concentrating on, immediately restoring the ended ability's mana cost (if it had one).

If you are concentrating on multiple spells at once, you can end both spells to restore more mana.
