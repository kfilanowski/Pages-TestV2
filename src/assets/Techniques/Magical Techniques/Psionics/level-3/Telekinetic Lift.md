You exert your psychic power to manipulate heavy unattended objects within 30 ft. You can lift objects weighing up to (100*[[Caster Level|CL]]) pounds, having them float in the air around you. Lifting objects with this spell has the [[concentration]] tag, but otherwise does not restrict your movement, occupy your hands etc.

As a standard action, you can drop an object weighing a few hundred pounds on a creature to deal 2d8+CL (9+CL) damage (reflex halves). The damage type varies depending on what you drop, usually crush.

Increase carrying capacity to (1000 x CL) lbs.

If your CL is at least 20...
