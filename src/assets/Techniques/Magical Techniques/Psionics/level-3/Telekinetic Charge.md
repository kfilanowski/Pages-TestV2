Lift an ally, including yourself, into the air using telekinesis and fling them anywhere within reach of the spell. If they are within [[Reach]] of an enemy at the end of the movement, they can make a [[Simple Attack|simple]] [[Melee Attack]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the range of the spell by 5 ft.
