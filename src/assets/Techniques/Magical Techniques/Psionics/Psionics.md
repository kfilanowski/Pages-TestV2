The psionics tree is something that has been developed piecemeal over long periods of time, culminating into its current form under the guidance of an entity by the name of [[Santa]].

The psionics tree uses psychic powers to gather information and out maneuver foes before the battles even begin. If it does come down to a fight, you can crush foes' minds or toss them around with [[telekinesis]]. Long distance communication is easy with spells like [[dream]] and [[sending]]. Creatures won't be able to keep secrets from you with access to spells like [[detect thoughts]] and [[memory movie]].
