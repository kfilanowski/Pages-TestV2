You exert your psychic power to such an extent that you are able to manipulate heavy objects and toss creatures around against their will. Your eyes glow and you emit a constant repulsive force pushing away loose objects nearby.

For the duration of the spell, you gain a [[hover]] speed equal to your [[land speed]]. You can use this speed to [[fly]] during your turn as a free action.

Upon casting the spell, and every turn after as a standard action, you can perform one of the following actions.
