Level 6 [[Psionics]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]]
Action: 1 minute
Material: 1 bucket of [[Lead Paint]]

| Range          | Reach                   |
| -------------- | ----------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 [[Helpless]] creature |
| Duration       | -                       |
| Save           | -                       |
Force the lead paint used as a material component to cycle through the creatures circulatory system, with the highest concentrations settling in the creature's brain and the rest being vomited out.

You completely remove all memories a creature has, making them a blank slate. This includes people they knew, events they experienced and locations around the world. If it can fall under the category of knowledge, they forget it. They still understand basic mechanics of the world, like gravity, temperature etc.

As a result of this complete mind wipe, the creature will not know basic things about their own life, such as their family, their name or "how they act." At first they will likely not express very strong emotions or opinions of things as they reshape their ideas on them. Occasionally minor details from the creature's subconscious will emerge, but without context, they will likely be written off as dreams.

This effect cannot be removed through conventional means, such as [[Dispel]] or [[+Medicine]]. It requires a complex series of tasks related to the creature's past. To even begin the process, the creature must be exposed to...
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">A person of significance</span> - This doesn't need to be a specific person, but most people have at least one person they are close to: A lover, a child, a teacher, a priest etc. They must have a conversation with this person.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">An event of significance</span> - Nonspecific. They must go to the scene where something major happened in their life, such as a near-death experience, a place where a great tragedy they experienced happened or a remote place they went on a pilgramage to when they were younger.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">An object of significance</span> - Nonspecific. They must witness an object of great significance to them, such as an artist's magnum opus is on display, or a warriors [[Legendary Weapon]].

With each step, the creature's hallucinations and dreams will become more vivid and memorable, potentially providing clues to the next step in the process. With all three, you can finally perform a medical procedure to remove the lead from their brain, requiring a DC 30 [[First Aid (P)|first aid]] check. Failure makes the effects of this spell irreversible outside of [[GM Magic]].
