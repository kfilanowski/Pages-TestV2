Level 1 [[Psionics]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
With a touch, communicate a vast quantity of information by showing the target brief memories and psychic impressions. You can communicate the broad strokes of an experience you had, including important locations, people, events and feelings. The experience must be shorter than 1 hour.

Casting on an unwilling creature requires an attack roll.
