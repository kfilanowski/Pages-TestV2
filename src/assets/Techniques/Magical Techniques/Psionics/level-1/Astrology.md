Level 1 [[Psionics]]
Tags: [[Recharge]]
Action: 1 minute
Material: 1 scroll or parchment

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note:</span>
In order to cast this spell, you must know the target's date of birth, and have access to the night sky.

When the spell is complete, you convert a scroll or parchment into a Horoscope. The target can read the Horoscope to gain insight into some of the strengths and failings they may have within the next 24 hours. As long as they have the horoscope on their person, they may add 2 to the natural die roll on any skill check. This cannot exceed the natural 20 limit of a d20. After they use this benefit, the horoscope disappears.

A creature that cannot read cannot benefit from their Horoscope. A creature may only benefit from Astrology once per day.
