You can perform one of the following actions, manipulating matter with your mind.

| Name      | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Toss      | Briefly take hold of a creature's body (fort reduces to 20 [[Poise]] damage) and throw them across the battlefield. You move them 30 ft and they take CL crush damage.                                                                                                                   |
| Crumple   | Briefly take hold of a creature's body (fort reduces to 20 [[Poise]] damage) and slam them against the ground. They are [[knocked down]] and take 5d8+CL (22+CL) crush damage                                                                                                            |
| Artillery | Lift large objects or numerous small objects by breaking stone off of walls, shattering branches into needles etc.<br>Toss these objects at your foes. Creatures within range in a 10 ft radius take 5d8+CL (22+CL) physical damage (reflex halves) based on the objects that you threw. |
| Maneuver  | Functions as [[telekinetic maneuver]], but with a bonus of (5+CL). Cannot [[grapple]].                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
