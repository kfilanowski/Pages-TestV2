You see everything as it truly is.

- You automatically disbelieve all illusions, such as [[Figment|figments]], [[Pattern|patterns]] and [[Glamer|glamers]]
- If a creature is under the effects of a [[polymorph]] effect, you can see the creature's true form
- You can see [[Invisible]] creatures
- You gain [[Darkvision]]
