Level 2 [[Brazzeria (T)]]
Tags: [[Healing]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach                          |
| -------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature other than yourself |
| Duration       | -                              |
| Save           | -                              |
You roll a caster level check to immediately end a harmful [[Status|status effect]]. The DC is (10+[[Caster Level|CL]] of the effect). For non-spell effects its (10+inflicting creature's [[LvL]]). If the status is removed, the creature heals 5 [[+HP]]. If you do not remove a status effect, it does not heal hp.

This spell cannot be used in order to remove permanent status effects, like [[Drained|drain]], [[Exhausted|exhaustion]], a [[curse]] or an [[affliction]].
