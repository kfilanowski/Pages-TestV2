Your staff emits light, but it is a dim violet color. The radius of the spell turns darkness into dim light.

More importantly, certain substances are highlighted while within this light. Bodily fluids and chemical spills are the most common substances, but savvy alchemists can create ink that also shows up under black light.

Black light only functions in [[Light Level|Dim Light]] or [[Light Level|Darkness]].
