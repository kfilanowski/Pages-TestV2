Tags: -
Action: 1 minute

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 ally     |
| Duration       | Until Rest |
| Save           | -          |
This spell creates a visible spectral golden hand that wraps around the bonded creature's hand.

You can use effects with a range of reach on the ally you are bonded to, targeting them with its beneficial effects, as long as they are within 30 ft and you have line of sight. This functions both ways.
