Level 1 [[Brazzeria (T)]]
Tags: [[Lewd]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                  |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 2 creatures within 30 ft of each other |
| Duration       | 1 round                                |
| Save           | Will                                   |
When you cast the spell, you choose two targets. The initial target you inflict the horny upon. The second target doesn't get affected by anything, but rather determines where this horniness is directed towards. The initial target must spend the entirety of its next turn moving towards the secondary target and then caressing, hugging, or kissing them (will negates). The target of this affection becomes [[clumsy]] 1.

A creature gets a +4 to its save if it would not normally be sexually attracted to the secondary target. By succeeding on a [[+Monster Lore]] check against a creature, you can determine what an appropriate secondary target for this spell would be.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the spells range by 5.
