Level 1 [[Brazzeria (T)]]
Tags: [[Healing]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
Magically close wounds and replace lost blood. The creature gains 5+CL hp and is cured of [[Fatigued|fatigue]].

By adding [[Mana Burn]] equal to spell level you give it the [[overheal]] tag.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s allows you to target an additional creature.
You can move in between delivering the spell to reach more than one creature.
