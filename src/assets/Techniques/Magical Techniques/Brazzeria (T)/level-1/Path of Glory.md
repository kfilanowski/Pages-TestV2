Tags: [[Healing]], [[Concentration]], [[Persistent]], [[Upcast]]

| Range          | -                |

You bless the ground that you walk on. Your movement this turn leaves behind a glowing golden radiance in any space you moved through. Allies that exist in or move through one of these spaces restore 1 hp. This effect cannot happen more than once per turn.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the healing by 1.
