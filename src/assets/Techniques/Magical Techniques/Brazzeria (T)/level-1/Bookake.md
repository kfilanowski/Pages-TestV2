Level 1 [[Brazzeria (T)]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach                     |
| -------------- | ------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 book, or similar object |
| Duration       | -                         |
| Save           | -                         |
You apply the mark of Brazzeria and close the book. Between its pages ooze an incredibly effective gooey adhesive that keeps it closed. You can also choose to seal off only a specific section of a book, such as a chapter or a singular page. The caster must apply a password to the book that dispels the goo when spoken.

A caster may roll arcana to [[Identify Magic Item]] to determine the password (15+CL). An application of [[universal solvent]] automatically dispels this effect.
You can also roll a DC 40 athletics check to [[Brute Force]] it open.
