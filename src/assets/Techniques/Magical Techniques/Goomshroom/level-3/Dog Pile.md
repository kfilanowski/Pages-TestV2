Level 3 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Elusive]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | Reflex     |
Cover the ground underneath your foe in sticky goo, [[Entangled|entangling]] them for 1 round (reflex reduces to -15 speed).

The goo lashes out and yanks your allies closer. Any allies can choose to move 30 ft closer to a creature targeted by this spell as an instant action.
