Level 3 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Water]], [[Concentration]], [[Persistent]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -        |
| Duration       | 3 rounds |
| Save           | Reflex   |
You open a portal to the elemental planes to generate a large size (10x10 ft) orb of water. Immediately upon casting the spell, and as a swift action each turn, you can move the orb up to 25 ft in a straight line. If the orb moves through a creature's space, then it is carried along for the ride and made [[wet]] (reflex negates). Creatures that are huge get [[Advantage]], and massive creatures are immune.

When picked up or at the end of each turn it is carried, creatures take 4d8+CL (18+CL) [[poise]] damage and are made [[clumsy]] 2. Creatures can [[Breakout]] as a swift action. Aquatic and [[amphibious]] creatures make this check with [[Advantage]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the speed of the water orb by 5.
