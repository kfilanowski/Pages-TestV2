Level 3 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Recharge]], [[Weather]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -                                 |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 mile radius, centered on caster |
| Duration       | 1 hour                            |
| Save           | -                                 |
You dance a jig. After you complete your dance, clouds roll in and it starts to lightly rain after 1 minute. After the spell ends, the weather returns back to what it was before casting.

All weather inducing spells do not function underground.
