Level 2 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Healing]], [[Elusive]]
Action: [[FRA]]

| Range          | -       |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self    |
| Duration       | 1 round |
| Save           | -       |
Your body oozes with a soothing and sticky goo. Take the [[carry]] action and increase your speed by 20 this turn. You ignore carried creatures' weight as though it were your own and do not damage you allies' [[poise]]. Any creature carried recovers [[Caster Level|CL]] hp. You can rescue up to 2 allies. At the end of your turn, they detach in an adjacent space of your choosing, or in your possession.

If a creature is directly preventing an ally from moving, such as when being [[Grapple|grappled]], then you cannot rescue them. For situations like being stuck in a web, a suitable check (like a [[Breakout]]) can be rolled to rescue.
