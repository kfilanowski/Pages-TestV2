Level 2 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Water]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                                          |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | One 5ft diameter hemisphere, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                                       |
| Save           | -                                                              |
You cause a hemisphere of water to suddenly form in an area. This water has no inherently negative effects, but [[Aquatic Combat]] can be a problem for some and a boon for others.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, the diameter of the hemisphere increases 10 ft.
