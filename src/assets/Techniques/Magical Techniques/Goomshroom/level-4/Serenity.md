Level 4 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | -                                      |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft radius burst, centered on caster |
| Duration       | 3 rounds                               |

You compel the enemies around you to cease fighting. Enemies are [[pacified]] (will negates). They get a new save against this effect each round.

If you or one of your allies ally harms a pacified enemy, the effect ends on that enemy.
