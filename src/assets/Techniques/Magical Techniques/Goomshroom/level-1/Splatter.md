Level 1 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                        |
| Save           | -                                               |
Spit a slimy ball at the ground that splatters on impact. Creatures in the spells area at the time of casting, or that walk into the spells area are affected by it. Moving out and then back into the spell can affect the creature multiple times.

Choose one:
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Greasy</span> - Creatures are [[knocked down]] (reflex/[[+Acrobatics]] negates). Treat the grease as a [[Slippery Surfaces|slippery surface]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Gooey</span> - Creatures are [[entangled]] (fort negates). Athletics as a swift action to remove the goo. Treat the goo as a [[Sticky Surfaces|sticky surface]].
