You exude an aura that allows creatures to exist in worlds they are unaccustomed to. Every creature that can see you (or sense you) gains the [[amphibious]] quality, allowing them to comfortably adapt to either land or sea, no matter which they normally prefer. Sea creatures gain a 30 ft [[land speed]] and land creatures gain a 30 ft [[swim]] speed.

This quality persists for as long as they stay near you, but it does not immediately disappear. The boon persists for (10*[[Caster Level|CL]]) minutes after the creature cannot see you.

Most places of worship dedicated to [[Shroomlette]] are persistently affected by this aura.
