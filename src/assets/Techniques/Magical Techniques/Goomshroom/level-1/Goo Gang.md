Level 1 [[Goomshroom]]
Tags: [[Figment]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | -          |
You create 3 slimy copies of yourself that relentlessly follow a creature of your choosing (even into areas you couldn’t go, such as into the air). While they persist, they each take up a single space adjacent to the creature the most optimally to provide [[flanking]]. If they are targeted with a hostile attack, spell or take any damage, they disappear. Their movement is [[elusive]].

These duplicates are treated as though they have all [[+Teamwork Feats]].
