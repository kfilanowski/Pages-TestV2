Level 1 [[Nack]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -        |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You summon a relatively average looking fox at a point you can see. At your command, you can cause it to make noises typical of a fox. After which, it explodes violently in a tiny fiery mushroom cloud.

This does not cause damage to anybody or anything, nor does it dirty anything.

If you do not cause it to make noise, it disappears within 1 minute.
