Level 3 [[Golden Order]]
Tags: [[Holy]], [[Persistent]], [[Light]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -          |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft cone |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | Reflex     |
You throw many radiant rings that deal 4d8+CL (18+CL) holy damage (reflex halves).

The rings persist at the end of the cone, forming a line that automatically deals their damage to creatures that move through their spaces. At the start of your next turn, the rings return very quickly directly back to you in a straight line, dealing half the damage again automatically (no save).

If any of the rings impact solid terrain (such as a wall) on either the way out or the way back, they halt in place. Any that don’t continue on their usual path.

The discs give off light in a 20 ft radius.
