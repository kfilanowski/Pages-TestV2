Level 1 [[Golden Order]]
Tags: [[Concentration]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | 30 ft                         |
| -------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 aberration, undead or fiend |
| Duration       | 1 minute                      |
| Save           | -                             |
You sing out to the creature, inflicting [[drained]] equal to your [[Caster Level|CL]] while you concentrate. If it is defeated while this spell is active and it does not have [[regeneration]], it is instantly and utterly consumed in holy flames, leaving behind only ashes. If it has regeneration, this spell will cause them to become [[dazed]] for one turn upon revival.
