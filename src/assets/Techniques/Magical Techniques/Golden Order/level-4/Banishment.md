Level 4 [[Golden Order]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                         |
| -------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 aberration, undead or fiend |
| Duration       | -                             |
| Save           | Will                          |
You point towards a creature and send it back from whence it came (will negates), with the effect differing slightly depending on what you target. You can only use banishment on a creature who your CL exceeds their [[LvL]] by at least 8, or it is a [[minion]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Aberration:</span> The creature is sent back to its original planet. If it did not have an original planet, it gets transported to the moon.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Undead: </span>It is immediately [[Entombed]] in the ground underneath it. It can [[Breakout]] as a [[FRA]], but the DC is increased by 5.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Fiend:</span> The creature is sent back to [[The Demon Realm]].
