Level 2 [[Golden Order]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Language-Dependent]], [[Light]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | -          |
You recite an oath with your target, bolstering their courage in the face of darkness. The target sheds light out to 20 feet, granting them a +4 bonus to [[+Intimidation]] vs [[Aberrations]], 
[[Undead]] and [[Fiends]] 

Allies within this light get [[resistance]] to [[fear]], [[Light Level|darkness]], and effects that inflict the [[Blinded]] or [[Blurred Vision]] status effects.
