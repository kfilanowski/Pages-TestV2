Level 2 [[Golden Order]]
Tags: [[Holy]], [[Light]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                              |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius burst, centered on a point in the air |
| Duration       | 2 rounds                                           |
| Save           | Reflex                                             |
You manifest a shining light and cause it to flash brightly before extinguishing. Creatures are blinded for two rounds (reflex negates). A creature can make a new save against this effect at the end of its turn.

[[Aberrations]], [[undead]], [[fiends]] and creatures with [[light sensitivity]] take 10 holy damage (no save).
