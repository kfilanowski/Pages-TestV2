Level 3 [[Cryomancy]]
Tags: [[Frost]], [[Elusive]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -          |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft line |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Reflex     |
Unless your speed is 0, rapidly advance forward in a straight line riding along a tiny glacier, dealing 4d8+CL (18+CL) frost damage and an equal amount of [[poise]] damage to any creature you move through (reflex halves). If a creature is poise broken from this, they are pushed 15 ft away from the line in a direction of your choosing.

The area of the spell becomes an icy [[Slippery Surfaces|slippery surface]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the distance you travel by 5.
