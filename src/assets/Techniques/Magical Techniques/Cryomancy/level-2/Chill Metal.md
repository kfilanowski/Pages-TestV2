Level 2 [[Cryomancy]]
Tags: [[Frost]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft (60 ft)                                                            |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 metallic object of up to one 5x5x5 ft cube, or one attended metal item |
| Duration       | -                                                                        |
| Save           | -                                                                        |
Rapidly chill a metallic object. If it is not currently attended, it automatically happens. Creatures that touch this item for whatever reason are made [[Clumsy]] 1 by rapidly spreading frost.

If the object is attended, then the creature can choose to drop the object as an instant action to negate the effects of the spell. If they do not drop the object or cannot, such as with metal armor, the creature takes ([[Caster Level|CL]]) frost damage and making them [[Clumsy]] 1 at the start of each of their turns and the item cannot be separated from them until heat is applied or you cease concentration.

You can also use this spell to target a creature that is made of metal, functioning as though they cannot drop the object.

Sometimes creatures have features that rely on heated metal. Such features are disabled while they are affected by this spell.

If the caster is ever more than 60 ft away from a chilled creature, the spell ends.
