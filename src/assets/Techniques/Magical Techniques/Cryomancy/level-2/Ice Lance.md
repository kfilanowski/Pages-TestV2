Level 2 [[Cryomancy]]
Tags: [[Upcast]]
Actions: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
Turn your staff into a deadly ice spike and make a [[Melee Attack|melee]] [[Spell Attack]] against a creature that deals 3d8+CL (13+CL) pierce damage.

If the target is [[entangled]] by a [[frost]] effect, has [[weakness]] to frost or is [[frosty]], you make the attack with [[advantage]] and it does 3d10+CL (16+CL) pierce damage.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase your attack by 1.
