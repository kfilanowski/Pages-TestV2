Snowflakes permeate the air, chilling creatures to the bone. The spell leaves behind a cloud that persistently inflicts [[weakness]] 2 to frost damage to all creatures without frost [[resistance]] in the cloud.

Your allies gain frost resistance while within the area of powder snow.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the weakness by 1.
