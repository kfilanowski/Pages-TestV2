Tags: [[Upcast]]

| Target/[[AoE]] | Self    |
| Duration       | 1 round |
| Save           | -       |
You cover yourself in a thin protective ice barrier. You gain [[resistance]] to the next instance of [[+HP]] damage that is not crush, slash or pierce.

Once you take damage from any source, minus damage over time effects like [[Bleeding|bleed]] or self inflicted damage, the ice barrier shatters and frost armor ends.

If you are [[ablaze]] when you cast this spell, instead of the usual benefits, put out the flames.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 16...
The resistance granted by this spell does not go away until you take crush, slash or pierce damage.
