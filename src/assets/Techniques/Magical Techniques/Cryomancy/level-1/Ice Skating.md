Level 1 [[Cryomancy]]
Tags: [[Buff]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 10 minutes |
| Save           | -          |
You coat the bottoms of your feet/footwear etc. with ice, allowing you to slide along cold terrain with ease. While moving across ice or snow, your gain a "skating speed" of 40 or equal to your [[Land Speed]] if it is faster. You ignore the negative aspects of moving across [[Frosty Terrain]].
