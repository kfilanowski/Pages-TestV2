You sculpt snow into a more friendly shape, and your new friend does his best to aid you. A snow man is an elemental that shares all of your ability scores, AC and saves. He has half of your max hp and 20+CL poise. He shares your [[LvL]], but is always treated as a [[minion]].

He cannot wield weapons, and his only form of attack is a single snowball [[Thrown]] attack every turn that deals 2d4 frost dmg. If there is no snow present, he must take 3 true damage to throw this snowball. He has a 30 land speed, but when not moving along snowy or icy surfaces, his speed is 15.

You mana burn equal to the mana spent on this ability. You can choose to burn any amount of mana, increasing snow man's damage by 2 for each additional mana burned. When the snow man is destroyed, or when the spell ends, the mana burn is removed (but it does not restore current mana).
