| -------------- | ---------- |

You toss a snowball at a creature, aiming for their weak points. Make a [[ranged attack]] that does 2d8+CL (9+CL) frost damage. They also suffer one of the following effects (fort negates).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Eyes</span> - Creature has [[blurred vision]] for 1 round.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Mouth </span>- Creature is [[staggered]] for 1 round.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase range by 5 ft.
