Level 1 [[Cryomancy]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | 30 ft                        |
| -------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 medium or smaller creature |
| Duration       | -                            |
| Save           | -                            |
You cause a snowy barrier to appear to protect a creature. The barrier appears all around the creature, protecting them from all sides, but not from attacks with [[High Ground]]. They gain [[Cover]] as long as they do not move from their space. If you stop concentrating or if the fort is hit with fire damage, it crumbles.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, you can increase the size of creature you can protect, or number of smaller creatures you protect by 1.
