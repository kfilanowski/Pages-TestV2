Level 5 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Zap]], [[Recharge]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft       |
| -------------- | ----------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | All enemies |
| Duration       | 1 minute    |
| Save           | Fort        |
Your shout echoes like thunder across the battlefield as you call down dozens of lightning bolts to strike all enemies you can see within range. Creatures take 6d8+CL (27+CL) zap damage and are [[staggered]] for 1 round (fort halves damage and negates stagger).
