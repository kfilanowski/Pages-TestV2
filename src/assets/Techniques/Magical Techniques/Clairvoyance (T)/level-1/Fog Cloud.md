Level 1 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Air]], [[Water]], [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                        |
| Save           | -                                               |
Cause a fog cloud to roll into the area and linger for a bit. Creatures outside of the fog cloud can only see 5 ft into it. Creatures inside of the fog cloud can only see 5 ft in front of them. Even still, such creatures have [[Partial Concealment]].

Creatures beyond 5 ft of fog have [[Total Concealment]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 12...
The fog becomes much thicker. Creatures treat it as [[difficult terrain]]. It can also soften falls.
