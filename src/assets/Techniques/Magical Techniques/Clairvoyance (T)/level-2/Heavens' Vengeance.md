Level 2 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Zap]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
A dark cloud spawns above your head that crackles with electricity. It is angered whenever you take damage, and deals 3 zap damage to the creature that inflicted the damage if it is within 30 ft.

This can only happen once each turn.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the range the spell can retaliate from by 5 ft.
