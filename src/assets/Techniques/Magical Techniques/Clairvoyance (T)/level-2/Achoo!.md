Level 2 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Air]], [[Elusive]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Fort       |
Your nose twitches before you unleash a mighty sneeze. You knock yourself and a creature back 5 feet in opposite directions. They are [[staggered]] for one round and take 2d8+CL (9+CL) crush damage, or two rounds with 3d8+CL (13+CL) crush damage if they impact a solid surface (fort halves dmg and negates stagger).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the range you knock you and your target back by 5 ft.
