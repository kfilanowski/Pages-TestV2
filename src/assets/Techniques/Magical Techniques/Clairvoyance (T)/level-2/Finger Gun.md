Level 2 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Air]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Fort       |
You point your finger at someone and then snap your fingers with your thumb pointing towards the sky. You unleash a powerful concentrated stream of air at a creature, requiring a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]]. The attack deals 3d8+CL (13+CL) crush damage. The creature is [[staggered]] for 1 round (fort negates).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the spells range by 5 ft.
