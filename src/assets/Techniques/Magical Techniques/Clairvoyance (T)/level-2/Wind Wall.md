Level 2 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Air]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Varies   |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
All [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]] passing through the wall take a -4 penalty to [[Attack]] unless they originate from a [[+Siege Weapon]]. Exceptionally small creatures such as insects cannot pass through the wall. The wall can also reasonably protect against things stopped by wind like swarms or harmful gases.

Moving through the wall costs 15 ft of movement. Creatures that opt to stand inside of a wind wall are [[clumsy]] 2 until they exit.

You can choose to make the wall in 2 shapes.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Wall</span> - 20x40 ft rectangle
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Castle </span>- 10 ft cube
