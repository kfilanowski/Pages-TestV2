Level 2 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Zap]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
While this spell is active, you crackle with electricity. Your [[Melee Attack|melee attacks]] deal an additional 2 zap damage and apply a mark to enemies [[On-hit]]. As a standard action while this spell persists, you can end it with one big stream of electric current. Marked creatures you have [[line of effect]] to automatically take 3d8+CL (13+CL) zap damage, even if you can't see them.

This gets rid of the mark and requires you to re-apply it for subsequent casts.
