Level 3 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]], [[Zap]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | Fort       |
As part of casting the spell, and as a standard action every turn after, you call down a lightning bolt to strike a specific creature within 60 ft of you. The creature takes 2d8+CL (9+CL) zap damage (fort halves).

If cast outdoors, the damage dice increases to d10.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, target an additional creature.
