Level 3 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Zap]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | See text |
| Duration       | -        |
| Save           | Fort     |

Lightning erupts from your fingertips and bounces around the battlefield from enemy to enemy. You initially target a single creature, then the lightning bounces from that target to another target of your choosing (that you can see) within 5 ft. You affect a total of 2 creatures. Each creature affected takes 4d8+CL (18+CL) zap damage and is [[staggered]] for one round (fort halves damage and negates stagger).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the number of creatures targeted by 1, and the distance they can be from each other by 5 ft.
