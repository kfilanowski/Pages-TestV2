Level 3 [[Clairvoyance (T)]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Air]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You surround yourself with a tempest that you can command. You get advantage on saves against air effects and +2 [[AC]] against [[Ranged Attack|ranged attacks]]. Spaces within your [[Reach|natural reach]] are considered [[difficult terrain]]. Harmful gasses, smoke, fog etc is blown directly away from you, clearing areas you move into.

As a standard action while the spell persists, you can make an [[Ranged Attack]] against a creature within 30 ft with a gust of wind. This attack can be a [[Melee Attack]] if made against a creature within [[reach]]. It deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) crush damage and an equal amount of poise damage. If this breaks the creatures poise, you can choose to affect it as though you had successfully poise broke them with a [[shove]] or [[Topple]].
