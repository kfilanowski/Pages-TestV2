Level 2 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Fear]], [[Psychic]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | Will       |
The fear of failure looms over the target creature (will negates). They take a -2 penalty to [[Attack]] and opposed [[Skill Check|skill checks]]. Whenever they [[graze]], miss or fail an opposed skill check they initiated, they take d6+CL (3+CL) psychic damage.

Outside of combat this gains the [[subtle]] tag and gives creatures disadvantage on their next skill check made in front of a crowd. Its duration changes to 24 hours. Once they roll the check, the spell ends.
