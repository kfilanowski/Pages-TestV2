Level 2 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 3 rounds   |
| Save           | Will       |
You add an extra load to all mental actions related to a creature’s speaking, causing their speech to be slowed and slurred (will negates). In order to speak at all, the creature must spend a swift action instead of a free action (ex sharing information with [[+Monster Lore]]). In order to cast a spell that normally requires a swift action, the creature must spend a standard action. In order to cast a spell that normally requires a standard action, the creature must spend a [[FRA]].

This increase in action cost can reasonably apply to any other actions based on speech as well.
