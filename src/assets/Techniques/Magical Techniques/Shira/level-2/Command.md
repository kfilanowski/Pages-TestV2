Level 2 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Language-Dependent]], [[Mind-Affecting]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | Will       |
You give the subject a single command, which it obeys to the best of its ability at its earliest opportunity (will negates). You may select from the following options. The spell specifies if their actions provoke [[AoO]]s. The creature still gets its turn.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Approach</span> - On its turn, the subject moves directly towards you 30 ft, provoking AoOs

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Drop It </span>- On its turn, the subject drops whatever it is holding. It can’t pick up any dropped item on the same turn it drops it.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Kneel </span>- The subject is [[knocked down]]. 

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Begone</span> - On its turn, the subject moves 30 ft directly away from you, provoking AoOs.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Praise Me</span> - The subject babbles on about your greatness, effectively making them [[Silenced]] for 1 round.

If the subject can’t carry out your command on its next turn, it defaults to kneel.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 8...
The spell gains the [[subtle]] tag. When used out of combat, the spell gets new effects.
- [[Minion]] creatures do not get a saving throw.
- The effects of the spell last 3 turns instead of 1. Creatures under the effects of the spell cannot perform hostile actions towards you or your allies while affected.
