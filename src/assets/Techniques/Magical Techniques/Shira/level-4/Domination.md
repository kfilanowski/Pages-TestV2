Level 4 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Charm]], [[Concentration]], [[Recharge]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 minute   |
| Save           | Will       |
You link your mind with a creature and enforce your will upon it. For the duration of the spell the creature is [[Dominated]] (will reduces effect to [[staggered]] 1 round). You can [[Telepathy|telepathically]] give orders to the dominated creature. They must follow these orders unless they are very obviously self-harming (ex. KYS NOW). For all intents and purposes, this creature is considered your ally as long as they are dominated. The creature holds back and cannot use any [[Special Attack|special attacks]], or cast any spells that are level 2 or higher.

At the end of each turn, the creature gets a new save against the effect.

The spell ends if any of your allies harm the target (including you).

This spell can only be cast 1/minute.
