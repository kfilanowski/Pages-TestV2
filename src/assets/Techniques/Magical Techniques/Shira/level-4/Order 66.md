You plant subconscious cues into a [[helpless]] or willing target. After completing the cast time, you cause the target to forget you cast this spell. At any point during the following month, you can trigger the true effects of this spell by speaking a passphrase in a manner where the target can clearly hear you as a standard action. For 1 minute the creature is [[Dominated]], aiding you in combat, attacking your enemies as you direct them. Once this effect is triggered, the contingency ends.

The contingent aspect of this spell applies to the caster, not the target creature. A tattoo of the number 66 appears somewhere on the creatures skin where it is visible. If covered, it moves position until it is not. If completely covered, the spell ends.

The spell leaves behind subtle cues, allowing those trained in [[+Insight]] to realize the creature is affected by this spell (caster's DC+4). Creatures only ever get 1 check to realize this enchantment is in place.
