| -------------- | ------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 helpless creature |
| Duration       | -                   |

Worm your way deep into a creature's psyche and take control of their body. Functions as [Domination](Domination.md), but must be cast on a or [[helpless]] creature. The domination lasts indefinitely. The creature does not receive new saves and must be freed through other means, magical or otherwise. Thrall is considered a [[Permanent Status]].

If a friend, ally, respected superior, family member etc spends a standard action to sway the creature, it gets a will save against the effect with [[Advantage|Disadvantage]]. If at least three creatures spend their turn doing this at once, the save is not at disadvantage. Certain types of people, such as people related to the controlled creature's destiny or true love can give a creature advantage on their save.

Uniquely, creatures enthralled do not gain the companion boost to attack unless they are a [[Minion]].
