Level 3 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 companion creature |
| Duration       | 1 rounds             |
| Save           | Will                 |
You bring forth all of the negative memories and feelings a companion has for its leader. The target companion is [[taunted]] by its leader for 1 round (will negates). It does not use any [[recharge]] abilities while taunted in this way. This ability removes all other taunt effects currently affecting the companion creature if the spell is successful.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, you can target an additional companion creature.
