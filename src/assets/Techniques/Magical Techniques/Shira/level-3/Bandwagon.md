Convince a creature to perform a very specific action (will negates). Specifically something you or another creature has done in this creatures presence within the last few minutes. For example, you could take a drink from a wine glass and then compel another creature to drink from theirs. If the activity caused any form of obvious harm, social repercussions (like getting naked in a public space) or similar consequences to the creature that initially performed it, the spell [[Fizzle|fizzles]].
