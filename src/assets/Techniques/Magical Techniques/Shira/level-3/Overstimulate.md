Level 3 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 3 rounds   |
| Save           | Will       |
You flood the creatures senses with stimuli, overloading them (will negates). They have -15 speed, [[blurred vision]] and any [[+blindsense]] they have is disabled. They have [[weakness]] 2 to all damage.

An affected creature can make an additional save at the end of each of their turns. If [[wet]], this additional save is made with [[advantage]].
