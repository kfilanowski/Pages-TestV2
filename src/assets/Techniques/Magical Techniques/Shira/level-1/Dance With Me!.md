Level 1 [[Shira]]
Tags: [[Charm]], [[Elusive]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | Will       |
You extend your hand to a creature and offer to dance. You immediately move 15 ft in a direction of your choosing and the creature is [[Charmed]] by you during its next turn. It cannot move while tangled in your routine, but it can otherwise act normally (will reduces effect to 15 speed penalty).

<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Unique:</span> If you have the [[+Profession]] (dance) skill, then you can move 30 ft in a direction of your choosing on a failed save.
