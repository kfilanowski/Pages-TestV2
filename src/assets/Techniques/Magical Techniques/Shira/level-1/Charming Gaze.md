Tags: [[Charm]], [[Subtle]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | Varies     |
| Save           | Will       |
You lock your eyes onto a target and make them [[Charmed]] (will negates). If you're currently engaged in combat with the creature, the spells duration is 3 rounds. If you are not, the spell's duration is 8 hours. Entering combat against them shortens the remaining duration to 3 rounds.
