Level 6 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Light]], [[Concentration]], [[Recharge]], [[Weather]]
Action: 1 minute

| Range          | -                                 |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 5 mile radius, centered on caster |
| Duration       | 5 minutes                         |
| Save           | Reflex                            |
You alter the composition of the heavens in such a way to mimic the effect of a solar eclipse. All creatures caught in the radius of the spell when it is first cast without proper eye protection are [[blinded]] for the duration of the spell (reflex reduces to [[Blurred Vision]]). Creatures in the spells radius take a -4 penalty on [[+Perception]] even if they aren't blinded.

What constitutes protection either covers the eyes completely, or are glasses specifically designed to block the powerful light of the eclipse. The caster can create a pair of these glasses for 100g.

Solar eclipse ends automatically if [[Lunar Eclipse]] is cast in its range, cancelling each other out.
