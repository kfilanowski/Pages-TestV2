Level 2 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Buff]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You summon a swarm of bees that intuitively follow your orders without need for speech. The bees won’t enter water. You must maintain a tether to them using the swarm almost like an additional limb that if separated, immediately disappears.

You take a -5 penalty to [[+Stealth]] checks and you have a [[hover]] speed of 30.

The bees hover around you and can perform a few sets of tasks...
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Interact (action varies)</span> - The bees can perform tasks with a high degree of precision such as picking up objects and opening doors. They can perform any task you could perform with your hands (including skills like [[+Sleight of Hand|SoH]]) at a range of 20 feet
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Sting (standard)</span> [[Venom]] - Automatically deal (2+[[Caster Level|CL]]) pierce damage to a single creature within 10 feet. They are also [[staggered]] for 1 round (fort negates).
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Protect (immediate)</span> - The bees move to block an attack, giving you a +4 bonus to ac or +4 bonus to a reflex save. This subtracts 5 rounds from the duration of the spell
