Level 2 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                            |
| -------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | A wall 20 ft high and 20 ft wide |
| Duration       | 1 minute                         |
| Save           | -                                |
You summon a flock of sheep that quickly pile up on top of each other to form a fluffy impassable barrier. If you summon the wall in a space occupied by a creature, the creature can choose which side of the wall they end up on. The sheep are completely impervious to all forms of attack unless it is a [[+Combat Maneuver]]. The wall has 20 [[poise]]. When poise broken, a 5x5 section of the wall falls apart and the sheep that occupied that space disappear. If the creature who broke through the wall was larger, the hole is also large enough for them to fit (10x10 large, 15x15 huge etc).

In order to interact with the wall, it requires maneuvering through the unruly sheep. You must beat the casters spell [[DC]] to [[balance]] on or [[Climbing|climb]] the wall.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase one of the dimensions of the wall 5 ft.
