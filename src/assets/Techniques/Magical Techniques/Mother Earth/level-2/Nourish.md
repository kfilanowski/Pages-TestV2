You infuse plants with life, with a variety of effects.

Choose one:
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Cultivate</span> ([[recharge]]) - You cause a single dying plant to become vibrant, putting it on the correct path for future growth. If you [[Mana Burn]], you can even accelerate its growth one stage. Seed>seedling>sapling>adult.
  If you target creatures that are [[Plants]], you can instead grant them 5+[[Caster Level|CL]] [[Temp HP]]. This gives the spell the [[Healing]] tag.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)"><span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Fertilize</span> (recharge)</span> - Tracts of land become more habitable for plants, enriching soil and removing contaminants, assuring future growth. You target 1 square mile.  Contaminators might not be too happy…
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Constrict</span> - An enemy within 30 ft must roll a reflex save or be [[entangled]]. Creature can spend a standard action to [[breakout]].
