Level 2 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -                                                                |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground, or yourself<br> |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                                         |
| Save           | -                                                                |
All the nearby plants snap to attention, ready to receive orders. As a swift action each turn, you can command a nearby inanimate plant to [[Bully]] a nearby creature by bending its trunk or swinging its branches. Its bonus is your [[Caster Level|CL]]. It gets [[Advantage]] if the creature is currently inside of the attacking tree, bush etc.

The spell name assumes trees, but it can be any type of vegetation, even the grass someone stands in. What the [[Size|Reach]] of the plant is, is based on its size. Trees are generally pretty tall, giving them ~15 feet of reach while bushes, tall grass and such have much less.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 8...
You can target 2 creatures to bully as part of the same action.
