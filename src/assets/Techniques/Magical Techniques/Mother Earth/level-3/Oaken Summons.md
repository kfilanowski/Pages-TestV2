Level 3 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Companion]], [[Healing]], [[Recharge]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -        |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You grow bark on the exterior of your skin and to summon a [[treant]] to your aid. Gain (3*[[Caster Level|CL]]) [[temp hp]] when you cast this spell. The treant understands druidic and does its best to aid you.

When you lose this temp hp, the treant disappears.

This spell can only be cast 1/minute.
