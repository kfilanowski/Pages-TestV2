Level 3 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Companion]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                 |
| -------------- | --------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | A point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 minute              |
| Save           | -                     |

You cause a vibrant [[thorn spitter]] to sprout from the ground. It does not have access to any [[Special Attack|special attacks]]. It mindlessly shoots your enemies for the spells duration, focusing a target you choose when you cast, or the nearest creature if the first target leaves the flowers range. It loses 10 hp each turn. When it is defeated, it wilts and crumbles to dust.

If the flower is summoned in an area that otherwise wouldn’t sustain it, such as underground or in stone, it loses 25 hp at the end of each of its turn.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 8...
The flower gains access to its special attacks.
