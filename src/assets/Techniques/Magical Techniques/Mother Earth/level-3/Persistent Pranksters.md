Level 3 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft    |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -        |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You summon a group of playful fairies that fly around the battlefield and bother your enemies. They do not have any hp, but they do have 25+[[Caster Level|CL]] poise. If they are ever poise broken, they disappear.

On your turn they can [[fly]] at a speed of 30 feet and make a [[+Combat Maneuver]] against a creature. The result of the combat maneuver is always 20+CL. You can direct them to focus on specific creatures.
