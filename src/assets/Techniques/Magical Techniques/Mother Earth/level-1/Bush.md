Level 1 [[Earth Mother]] 
Tags: -
Action: Swift

| Range          | 30 ft              |
| -------------- | ------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 unoccupied space |
| Duration       | 1 minute           |
| Save           | -                  |
You cause a bunch of tall grass to spring up. This space is overgrown and is considered [[difficult terrain]]. It also provides [[Cover]] to any attacks made into or through it. Despite being only a 5x5x5 ft patch of dense brush, a creature of an size can fit inside of it.

You can take the [[hide]] action in or behind this bush as a free action the turn you cast this spell.

The bush withers and fades after 1 minute, or 3 rounds if the environment couldn't otherwise sustain it. Dealing any slashing or fire damage to it destroys the bush.
