Level 1 [[Earth Mother]] 
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | -                                               |
| Save           | -                                               |
Enemies within the spell's area have plants suddenly grow in the chinks of their armor and around their feet, tangling their joints. They have their speed reduced by 15. The plants can be destroyed by dealing 1 slashing or fire damage to them, or 10 damage of any other type.
