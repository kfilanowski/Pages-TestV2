| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |

Shower a foe with a thick cloud of toxic plant dust. Creature takes d6+CL (3+CL) poison damage. The powder also has an additional effect (fort negates).

Choose One:
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Poison Powder</span> - Creature takes an additional (3+CL) poison damage at the end of its turns (fort ends effect).
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Rage Powder</span> - Creature is [[taunted]] by you for one round.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 8, gain the following options.

<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Sleep Powder</span> - Creature is put [[Asleep]] (fort reduces to [[Drowsy]]).
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Stun Spore</span> - Creature is [[dazed]] for 1 turn.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Deadly Poison Powder</span> - Creature takes an additional 2d6+CL (7+CL) poison damage at the end of its turns (fort save after damage is taken ends effect).
