Level 4 [[Mother Earth]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 10 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Fort       |
Shoot a precise string of webbing/vines at a creature. They are [[entangled]] and you may slam them against the ground into any position within the spells range to deal 5d6+CL (17+CL) crush damage (fort negates damage and repositioning effect). Creature can remove this automatically as a swift action or [[Breakout]] as a swift action to remove entangled.
