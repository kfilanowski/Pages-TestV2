| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 mile radius, centered on caster |
| Duration       | -                                  |

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
This spell can only be cast in a densely forested area (such as a rainforest or woodland). With special training, you can learn to cast it in other areas where life is plentiful, like coral reefs.

You enter a trance while you slowly become one with nature over the cast time. Once you have completed the cast time, your spirit separates from your body and it is free to wander and explore.

There is no limit on on how long you can remain in this trance, but staying in for many hours leaves your body hungry and tired. While in spirit form you have a sixth sense for detecting water sources and when the forest is being significantly damaged within the spells area. Someone cutting down a tree isn't enough to trigger this, but a large scale lumber organization cutting away is. When you detect one of these things, you can appear overhead to observe. Other creatures cannot see you unless they can also see invisible creatures or ghosts.

At any point, you can decide to speak. You can make your voice soft or loud and you can make it seem to emanate all around the area, giving you [[advantage]] on [[+Intimidation]]. You can also cause any of the nearby creatures to exhibit any behavior natural to them, such as being completely silent, making noise or perching overhead. They won't engage with dangerous situations at your request.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - If your [[Caster Level|CL]] is at least 16...
