Level 5 [[Frogg Saran (T)]]
Tag: [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -                                |
| -------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 30 ft radius, centered on caster |
| Duration       | 3 rounds                         |
| Save           | -                                |
A bunch of storm clouds roll in, even while indoors, and rain hundreds of frogs upon the area. The situation grows more dire with each passing round at the start of your turn.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Round 1</span>
A mere frog drizzle. The area is considered [[difficult terrain]] to any non frog creatures. Creatures with at least T1 [[Deific Boons]] from [[Frogg Saran]] also ignore this difficult terrain.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Round 2</span>
A frog downpour. The area is considered impassable terrain for any non frog creatures. Any non frog creatures still in the area are [[entangled]], unable to effectively move through the lake of frogs. Creatures with at least T2 [[Deific Boons]] from [[Frogg Saran]] also ignore this entangling.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Round 3</span>
A frognado. The frogs, and any creature in the area of the spell is lifted into the air and thrown 30 feet in a random direction. The frogs quickly dissipate afterwords. Any non-frog creatures and objects are [[staggered]] for 3 rounds and take 6d10+CL (33+CL) crush damage. Creatures with at least T3 [[Deific Boons]] from [[Frogg Saran]] also ignore damage/stagger (but not the knockback).
