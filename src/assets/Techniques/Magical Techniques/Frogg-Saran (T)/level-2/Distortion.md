Level 2 [[Frogg Saran (T)]]
Tags: [[Teleportation]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 3 rounds   |
| Save           | Will       |
You immediately cause a creature to look distorted, blinking in and out of existence and seeming to regularly warp back in time a fraction of a second (will negates). Every time the creature takes [[+HP]] damage, they immediately teleport 5 ft in a [[Random Direction]]. If this would put them in a solid object, they are set to 1 [[poise]] and are ejected to the nearest available unoccupied space.

If you target an ally, you can reduce the cast time of this effect to a swift action, and it gains the [[buff]] tag.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, the distance of the teleportation is increased by 5 ft.
