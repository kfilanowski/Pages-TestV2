Frogg is always watching out for you, protecting you when you need it most. Whenever you take the [[Total Defense]] action, you also turn into a solid golden version of yourself, putting you in [[Stasis]]. At the end of an enemy's turn, roll a d6. On a 6, this stasis ends, but the total defense does not.

If you do not lose this stasis by your next turn, you don't get a turn, as though you had spent it doing nothing other than using total defense again.

If you are not currently in combat, you can still turn golden, but it only lasts 6 seconds.
