You laugh maniacally and revel in the chaos. All creatures in the radius of the spell MUST make a [[simple attack]] against the nearest creature. If they do not have a means to attack, such as not being in range, then the spell does not affect them. The order that creatures make the attacks is as follows: 

The caster > their allies > neutral parties > their enemies.

The caster is allowed to use a [[special attack]] instead of a simple attack.
