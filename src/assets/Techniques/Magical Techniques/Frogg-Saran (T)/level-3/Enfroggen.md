Level 3 [[Frogg Saran (T)]]
Tags: [[Polymorph|poly, baleful]], [[Curse]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
You transform a creature into a frog. The <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">caster</span> is unable to attack, cast any spells that affect this frog or otherwise harm it in any way, even when magically compelled to do so.  The creature gains the following qualities from the [[Polymorph List]].

| Mad Hops | Increase the distance the [[jump]] action brings you by 5 ft. You can jump vertically as high as you can jump horizontally. |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Tongue   | You can make [[Drag]] attacks as [[Melee Attack\|melee attacks]] at range of 30 ft during your turn.                        |
| Smol     | Decrease to size tiny.  Decrease reach to 0.                                                                                |
| Speed    | Gain a [[Swim]] speed equal to [[Land Speed]]                                                                               |
Another creature must spend their standard action to kiss the frog to transform it back. The un-frogged creature is [[dazed]] on its next turn if they did not take at least 1 turn as a frog. They are [[charmed]] by the creature who kissed them for 8 hours. This secondary effect is a [[charm]] effect.
