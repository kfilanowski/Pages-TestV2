Level 1 [[Frogg Saran (T)]]
Tags: [[Buff]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | -          |
Cause the creature to surge with unbelievable power as their body rapidly deteriorates. They get a +4 bonus to all d20 rolls, non-[[Spell Attack]] damage rolls and [[Caster Level|CL]]. This bonus cannot be added to something twice. After the buff ends, they take 50% of their max hp in [[true damage]].

If the creature is not [[Defeated]] from this damage, they become [[Drained]] 2.
