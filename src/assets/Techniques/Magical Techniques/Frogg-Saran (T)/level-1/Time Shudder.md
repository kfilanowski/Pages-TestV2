You emit waves of entropy, hindering or helping ALL creatures nearby (including you). Immediately upon casting and at the start of your turn unleash a pulse to determine the effects of the spell for one round. There is a 50% chance to Hinder and there is a 50% chance to help.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Hinder</span> - Creatures are [[staggered]]
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Help </span>- Creatures gain an additional 30 speed and their movement is [[elusive]]
