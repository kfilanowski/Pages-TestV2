This spell manifests itself as a rainbow box floating above your head with a question mark on it.

Before you would normally be affected by a hostile (non-[[+Cantrips|cantrip]]) spell, you change the nature of the magic into something completely different (will negates). The spell changes to another spell of the same level with you as the primary target or origin point of the spell, regardless of its typical range/targeting. Make saves as appropriate.

You are considered "affected" by a spell when it either targets you or included in an [[AoE]]. This can only trigger in direct response to the spell being cast, not later being affected by it (like walking into a [[Wall of Fire]]).

Some spells may have other restrictions like material components. Ignore them.
