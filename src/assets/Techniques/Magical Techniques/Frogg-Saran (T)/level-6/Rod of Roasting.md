Level 6 [[Frogg Saran (T)]]
Tags: [[Fire]], [[Healing]]
Action: Standard
Focus: 1 staff

| Range          | Reach        |
| -------------- | ------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 60 ft radius |
| Duration       | 3 rounds     |
| Save           | Reflex       |
You plant your staff into the ground and it ignites in flames. The flames take the shape of a frog and start laughing maniacally, telling a joke or saying proverbs unfit for the current situation.

At the end of each of your turns, the frog launches an intense fire blast at a creature the rod has line of sight to within 60 ft, chosen randomly between all creatures. Creatures takes 10d8+CL (45+CL) fire damage (reflex halves). Touching or trying to physically move the stick also triggers this effect on the creature that touched it.

If you are the last one standing within a 60 ft radius of the rod when the spell ends, you refund the the mana cost of this spell, you gain a [[luck dice]], your staff returns to you and you gain 60 temp hp.
