Level 5 [[Prismancy]]
Tags: [[Light]], [[Fire]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -          |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 60 ft line |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Reflex     |
Fire a rainbow that deals 6d8+CL (27+CL) fire damage and applies [[Glowing]] to enemies (reflex halves dmg). If the enemy was already glowing, they instead have [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on all d20 rolls for 1 round.

Instead of harming allies, it grants them a Lucky Rainbow - visible as an aura around them. They may consume this rainbow to [[Advantage|Reroll]] any one attack, save, skill check or [[ability check]]  within a minute, after which it disappears.

A creature may only have one Lucky Rainbow on them at a time. It lasts one minute.

Also creates bright light in its area for a round.
