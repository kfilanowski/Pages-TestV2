Level 4 [[Prismancy]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Light]], [[Fire]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You become a being of pure light, allowing you to move through glass and obstacles that do not block light (such as wire fences) freely.

You shed light in a 20-foot area. This form can be suppressed while in areas of magical darkness, but you automatically attempt to [[dispel]] any such area at the start of your turns.

Sighted creatures that attack you are considered to have [[blurred vision]] against you, and creatures that successfully [[Melee Attack]] you or that [[Grapple]] you are set [[ablaze]] (reflex negates). No matter how many attacks a creature makes, they cannot be forced to make multiple saves a turn.
