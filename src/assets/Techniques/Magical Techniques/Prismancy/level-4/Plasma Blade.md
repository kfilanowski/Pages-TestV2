Level 4 [[Prismancy]]
Tags: [[Buff]], [[Fire]], [[Light]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -                                 |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Held melee slash or pierce weapon |
| Duration       | 1 minute                          |
| Save           | -                                 |
Imbue a weapon in hand with searing light, changing its damage to d6+CL (3+CL) Fire damage. It becomes weightless. It gives off [[Light Level|bright light]] within 20 feet.

As part of casting this spell, you may make a [[Simple Attack|simple]] [[Spell Attack]] with this weapon. Every turn after, you can make another simple attack as a swift action. If you make any additional attacks with it, it consumes an additional 4 mana with each swing.

Apply [[glowing]] for 1 round [[On-hit]] with this weapon.

If the plasma blade leaves your possession, this spell ends after one round.
