While this spell is active, you are wreathed in an elemental damage type of your choosing. You gain [[resistance]] to this damage type. 1/turn when you take damage, you can cause the spell to retaliate as an instant action, dealing ([[Caster Level|CL]]) damage of your chosen damage type to the creature that did the damage.
