Level 1 [[Academy]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]], [[Force]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -          |
| Duration       | Until Rest |
| Save           | -          |

You create a magical copy of a non-magical shield or weapon that you think of that doesn't require ammunition, such as a light weapon or a 2h weapon. It deals force damage instead of its usual damage.

If you have an open hand, you can cause the weapon to appear in your hand and you can make a [[simple attack]] with it as part of casting the spell.

If you cast this spell while you have already conjured gear, the previous gear disappears.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, create an additional piece of equipment.
