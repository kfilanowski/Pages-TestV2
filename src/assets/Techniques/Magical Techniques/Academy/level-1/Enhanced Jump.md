As part of casting the spell, you can take the [[jump]] action.
Casting this spell allows the caster to jump as high and far as their movement speed. When you jump, you ignore [[fall damage]] from a fall of 30 ft or less. If you attack an enemy that is in midair during this time, your movement is [[elusive]] when falling after jumping up to them.
