Tags: [[Sigil]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach                                |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, emanating from a point |
| Duration       | Until Rest                           |
| Save           | -                                    |
You inscribe a magic rune on the surface of an object. Whenever a creature of at least tiny or larger size enters this area, the sigil triggers, creating one of 2 effects you decided on casting. In either case, you are mentally alerted and awakened if sleeping.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Silent Alarm</span> - Only you are mentally alerted.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Loud Alarm</span> - A loud sound starts to play and repeat, like beeping. Creatures within 300ft can hear it in ideal conditions.
