The spell can be used in two different ways.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Dispel </span>- You roll a [[Caster Level]] check to immediately end a magical effect. The DC is (11+CL of the effect).

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Counter Spell</span> - You roll a caster level check to prevent a spell from being cast. This must be done as a [[prepare]]d action in response to a spell being cast. The DC is (11+CL of the spell). If the spell the creature is casting has a higher caster level than your to dispel magic, you have [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on your caster level check.

You can use dispel magic on supernatural features, labeled ([[Su]]). The creature's CL is treated as (lvl+2).
Passive supernatural effects or [[prepared]] innate spells can be reactivated as a standard action.
