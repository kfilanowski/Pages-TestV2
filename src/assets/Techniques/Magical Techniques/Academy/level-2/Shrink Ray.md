This spell has a different effect depending on what you target.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Ally</span> - Creature shrinks down to the size of an action figure, only a few inches tall. It is unable to effectively damage creatures of small or larger size due to its diminutive nature. Their speed is reduced by 15. Any creature can say "big mode" to increase back to their normal size as a standard action.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Enemy</span> - Requires a [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]]. The target creature shrinks to small size [[on-hit]]. If already small or smaller, no effect.
- <span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Object</span> - Targeting an unattended object shrinks it down to 1/10th of its original size. Divide its weight by 100. The object can be no larger than 5x5x5 ft.

No matter the target, shrink ray can only affect 1 creature or object at a time. If you cast the spell again then all previous instances of it end.
