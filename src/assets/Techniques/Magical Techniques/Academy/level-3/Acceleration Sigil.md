Level 3 [[Academy]]
Tags: [[Sigil]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft                                                  |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 space that doesn't have a creature within 5 ft of it |
| Duration       | 10 minutes                                             |
| Save           | -                                                      |
Place a glowing bright sigil on the ground. When a creature steps within 5 ft of it, it activates, launching them 60 ft in the direction they were moving. The creature is sent slightly into the air to fly over minor obstacles and takes 20 [[Poise]] damage in addition to any [[Fall Damage]] or other hazardous terrain the creature is flung into.
