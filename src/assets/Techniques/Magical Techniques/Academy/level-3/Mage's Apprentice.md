Burn any amount of mana to summon a ghostly mage with (5*[[mana burn]]) HP. It follows your movement, including forced movement, to stay in your space at all times. The apprentice does not [[Threaten]]. It wields a singular staff. It is treated as a [[Constructs|construct]] that shares all of your ability scores, AC and saves. It has half your [[Poise]]. It shares your [[LvL]], but is always treated as a [[minion]].

During your turn, you can teach this apprentice one of the [[+Cantrips]] you know as a free action. It immediately casts this cantrip. It's [[Caster Level|CL]] is (your CL-10) minimum 1.
