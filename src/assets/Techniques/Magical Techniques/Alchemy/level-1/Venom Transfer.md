Level 1 [[Alchemy (T)]]
Tags: [[Poison]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | -          |
You jab your staff into a creature that is currently [[Venom|envenomed]] and borrow some of the venom for yourself. Using the venom as a catalyst, you can charge your next attack this round with an additional d8 (4) poison damage.

The envenomed creature you absorbed the venom from can immediately make a save to end the condition. If they fail, it does not advance the venom further and you cannot use this technique on them again to attempt to cure the venom.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Note: </span>
You can also use this technique against a defeated creature that is venomous. They get no benefit or drawback from it.
