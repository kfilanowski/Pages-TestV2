Level 1 [[Alchemy (T)]]
Tags: [[Upcast]], [[Glamer]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Metal      |
| Duration       | 10 minutes |
| Save           | -          |
You touch metal and give it a shiny new coat of paint, making it shimmer like gold. This changes nothing about the metals physical properties, only its appearance.

You can affect a 1 ft cube of metal.

A DC 10 [[+Nature]] check reveals the gold is fake.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- For every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, you can add 1 ft to the dimension of the cube you affect. (2 ft cube, 3 ft cube etc).
