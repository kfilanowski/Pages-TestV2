Level 2 [[Alchemy (T)]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Fort       |
You cause a creature to grow an additional oversized and unwieldy limb. The creature is [[clumsy]] 2 (fort negates). This clumsy cannot be removed while the limb persists.

The additional limb does not disappear over time and must be removed manually. 
- The limb can be safely removed with [[First Aid (P)|first aid]].
- A creature can also [[breakout]] as a swift action to remove the limb forcefully, inflicting [[Drained|drain]] 3.
