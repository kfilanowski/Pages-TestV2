Level 2 [[Alchemy (T)]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Persistent]], [[Air]]
Action: Standard
Focus: A smoke pipe

| Range          | -                                                                         |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius cube, at least one space of which must be adjacent to caster |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                                                  |
| Save           | Will                                                                      |
You take a deep hit from your pipe. When you exhale a cloud of colorful fragrant smoke fills the area. The smoke does not obscure vision significantly enough to give [[Concealment]].

Any creature within the area is [[fascinated]] by its incredible colors and tantalizing aroma while it persists (will negates).
