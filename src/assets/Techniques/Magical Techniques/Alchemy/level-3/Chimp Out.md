Transform a creature so they become a more ape-like version of themselves (fort negates). They gain a [[Climb]] speed of 30, have their [[Land Speed]] reduced by 15 and are effectively [[silenced]], only capable of making animal noises.

They gain the following special attack.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Special Attack: Poop Toss</span> [[Poison]] - Make a [[thrown]] [[Natural Attack]]. The creature takes 2d6 (7) poison damage and becomes [[sickened]] 2 [[On-hit]].

Can be removed by eating a banana or using poop toss.
