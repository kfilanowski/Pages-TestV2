## [[Crust]]

Level 3 [[Alchemy (T)]]
Tags: [[Poison]], [[Companion]], [[Recharge]], [[Mana Burn]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Fort       |
Summon an ooze inside of a creature. The ooze immediately tries to melt its way out. The affected creature takes 2d8+CL (9+CL) poison damage (fort halves).

The ooze then fights to aid you. It is [[Taunted]] by the creature it originally emerged from.

This spell can only be cast 1/minute.
