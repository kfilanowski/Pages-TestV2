Toss a sizzling sphere of toxic goo that explodes on impact. Make a [[Spell Attack]] against a creature. On impact the projectile explodes, dealing 4d8+CL (18+CL) poison damage (reflex halves dmg).

The original target has [[Advantage|Disadvantage]] on their save and is [[Venom|envenomed]] [[On-hit]].

On a miss, the projectile still explodes in the creatures space.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - For every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the range of this spell by 5 feet.
