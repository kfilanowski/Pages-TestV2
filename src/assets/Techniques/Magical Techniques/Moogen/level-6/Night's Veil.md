All enemies immediately become nearsighted by a shadowy mist that covers their eyes, reducing their vision range to 15 ft. All attacks made against a nearsighted creature from beyond this range are made as though the target was [[DD]]. This effect only inhibits sighted creatures.

You can choose to teleport just outside of a creature's vision range and [[hide]] as part of casting this spell.

The effect persists until the end of your next turn.
