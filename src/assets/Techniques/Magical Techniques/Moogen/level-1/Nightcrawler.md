Action: Swift

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 10 minutes |
| Save           | -          |
You gain [[nightvision]].

While in an area of [[Light Level|Dim Light]] or [[Light Level|Darkness]], you turn into a dark silhouette version of yourself, with only your eyes remaining the same. This is a [[Glamer]] effect. If you are exposed to bright light, your form slowly returns back to normal over 3 rounds (~20 seconds). Returning back to darkness turns you back into a silhouette. You can toggle this shadow form off/on if you want yourself to be seen.

While a silhouette, you get a +2 bonus on [[+Disguise]] and [[+Stealth]].
