Level 1 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Dark]], [[Concentration]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -                                               |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft line, 10 ft tall, originating from caster |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                        |
| Save           | Fort                                            |
You swipe at the air with your staff and a dark cloud washes over the area in front of you. The area is in total darkness while the spell persists. This also coats the casters space(s) in darkness.

You can increase the cast time of this spell to a standard action to have it deal 2d8+CL (9+CL) cold dmg when you initially create it (fort halves). This also gives the spell the [[Frost]] tag.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast</span> - Every 2 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the distance of the line by 5 feet.
