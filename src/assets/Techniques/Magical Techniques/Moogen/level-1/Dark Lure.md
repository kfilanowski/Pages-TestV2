Level 1 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Subtle]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 round    |
| Save           | Will       |
A nearly invisible whip of shadows emerges from your staff and wraps around a target. The target is pulled towards you in a subtle way that makes it feel like it is of their own volition. At the start of their next turn, they spend all of their movement to move closer to you and cannot move away, then they can take their turn as normal.

The DC increases by 4 if the target is [[DD]].

If the caster is [[defeated]] by the time the target's turn comes, the target is still lured.
