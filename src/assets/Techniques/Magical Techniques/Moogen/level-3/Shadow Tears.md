Level 3 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Dark]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
The target is [[blinded]] and leaves behind a trail of darkness wherever they walk (will reduces blinded to [[Blurred Vision]]). While impaired by this spell, the space the creature occupies and every space they move into is occupied by a cloud of [[Light Level|Darkness]] that extends 10 ft above the creature (aka increased based on size).

After the creature moves 60 ft, the spell ends.
