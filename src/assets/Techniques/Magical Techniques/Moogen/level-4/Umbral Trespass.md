Level 4 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Elusive]], [[Recharge]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | 1 rounds   |
| Save           | -          |
Fade away into shadowy mist and flow into the target of the spell. You disappear and enter [[stasis]] while you observe the world from the creature's PoV. On your next turn as a free action, you leave the creature's body in a violent slash, dealing 5d8+CL (22+CL) slash or pierce damage (fort halves) and dashing 15 ft away from the creature.

This spell cannot be cast again for 1 minute.
