Level 4 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Curse]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
A red mark appears along the creatures neck(s) resembling a bloody wound (will negates). They gain [[weakness]] 2 to all damage. As an immediate action over any distance, you can "pop" the mark, removing the curse and dealing 5d8+CL (23+CL) true damage.

This curse is removed by killing (not [[defeat]], killing) another creature of at least small size.
