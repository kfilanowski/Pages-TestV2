A shadowy copy of yourself splits from your body. It follows you around and attacks your enemies. If you are proficient in [[+Stealth]] then it shares your stealth bonus. An imaginary friend shares all of your ability scores, [[AC]], movement speed(s) and saves. It has half of your max hp and 20+CL [[Poise]]. It shares your [[LvL]], but is always treated as a [[minion]].

It's only form of attack is single swipe using whatever weapon you are currently wielding. It is not a real attack, so instead of rolling an [[Attack Roll]] it deals 2d8 (9) psychic damage (will halves). Every turn it exists in [[Light]] levels brighter than dim light, it takes 10 [[true damage]].

Doesn't [[Threaten]]. You can choose to burn any amount of mana, increasing the shadows damage by 2 for each mana. The burn from this spell is removed if it is [[Defeated]], but mana is not restored.
