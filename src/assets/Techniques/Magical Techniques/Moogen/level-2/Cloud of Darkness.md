Level 2 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Dark]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground |
| Duration       | 1 minute                                        |
| Save           | -                                               |
A cloud of black mist emerges from your fingertips. This mist absorbs all natural light and leaves the area where it covers in complete [[Light Level|Darkness]].

You can use the spell to [[Dispel]] spells with the [[light]] tag. If you succeed, the darkness spell consumes the light and takes hold.
