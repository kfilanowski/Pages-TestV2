Level 2 [[Moogen]]
Tags: [[Mind-Affecting]], [[Curse]], [[Glamer]], [[Psychic]], [[Upcast]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | Will       |
You alter a target's perception, causing it to perceive the world around it as though it were made entirely out of flesh, blood and viscera (will negates). It is [[shaken]] 2 while the curse persists.  If it interacts with the environment, such as opening a door or climbing a tree, the shaken penalty increases by 1.

If the creature is shaken 4 and has its shaken penalty increased through any means, it instead takes 2d8 (9) psychic damage.

To remove the curse you must hug a child.
