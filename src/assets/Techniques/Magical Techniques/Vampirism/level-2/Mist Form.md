You transform into an amorphous mist of an unusual color indefinitely. The cloud of mist is about a 5 ft cube in volume. Creatures must roll [[+Perception]] against your [[DC]] or [[+Stealth]] to realize this mist is potentially dangerous if it is clearly visible. Certain circumstances can cause the perception check to be waived, such as hiding on the surface of water, or other locations frequented by mist. Creatures automatically know something is up if they see you transform.

While in mist form you can perform no actions other than [[Fly|flying]] at a speed of 30 or dismissing mist form as a free action. You gain no defensive benefits while in mist form. If you take damage or fail a saving throw, you immediately leave mist form and are [[staggered]] for 1 round. 

As a vapor, you can travel through very thin spaces, such as air vents pipes and such. Particularly small holes such as keyholes are still possible, but require a [[FRA]]. You are also more susceptible to wind. Moving with a slight breeze is of no issue, but moving during a storm is impossible. You take a -4 penalty saves vs [[air]] effects.
