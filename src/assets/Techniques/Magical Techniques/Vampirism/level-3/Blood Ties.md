Level 3 [[Vampirism]]
Tags: [[Polymorph|poly, true]]
Action: Standard
Material: A vial of blood from a [[+Races|race]]

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 24 hours |
| Save           | -        |
You drink the vial of blood, transforming you into a copy of the creature you drink from. Your physical appearance changes to match theirs and you can swap between the creature’s voice and your own. You get a +4 bonus to [[+Disguise]] to appear as this creature. You can change your appearance between your own and the creatures as a swift action.
