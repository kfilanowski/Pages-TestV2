Level 3 [[Vampirism]]
Tags: [[Polymorph|poly, true]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | -      |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self   |
| Duration       | 1 turn |
| Save           | -      |
Immediately [[Disengage]]. For remainder of your turn, you transform into a [[Groups|swarm]] of ravenous beasts that can [[Fly]], like bats or crows. You can move directly through other creatures. When you do the creature is [[Drained]] for 3d6+CL (10+CL) hp and [[staggered]] for 1 round (fort changes the drain to [[True Damage]], halves it and negates the stagger).
