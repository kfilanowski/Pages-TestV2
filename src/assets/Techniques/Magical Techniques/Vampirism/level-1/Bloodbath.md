As part of casting the spell, you cut yourself with a held object taking d6 (3) [[Bleeding|bleed]] damage immediately and every turn after at the start of your turn. Whenever you take this damage, nearby enemies also start bleeding for the same amount.

You can choose to stop bleeding as an instant action, ending the spell.
