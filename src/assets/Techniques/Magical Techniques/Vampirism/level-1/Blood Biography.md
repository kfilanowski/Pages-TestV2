Level 1 [[Vampirism]]
Tags: -
Action: 1 minute

| Range          | Reach       |
| -------------- | ----------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Fresh Blood |
| Duration       | -           |
| Save           | -           |
You learn about a creature through bloodshed. The blood that is the target of the spell changes shape to form text in a language you understand. You can place the text on a surface of your choosing, such as a piece of paper in your position. If you don't choose then it forms on the surface the blood was on, or at your feet. The bloody message contains the following.

| Who are you?              | What the creature's name is                                                                           |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| What are you?             | Race/species, gender, profession etc.                                                                 |
| How was your blood shed?  | A brief summary of how the wound was inflicted, including who did it, provided the creature knows<br> |
| When was your blood shed? | Gives a date and time of day to the best of creatures knowledge<br>                                   |
