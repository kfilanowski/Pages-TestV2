Level 1 [[Vampirism]]
Tags: -
Action: Standard
Material: Fresh blood of 2 creatures

| Range          | -   |
| -------------- | --- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | -   |
| Duration       | -   |
| Save           | -   |
Take the blood of two creatures and mix them together. The blood then reacts depending on the relation between the two creatures.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Unrelated: </span>The 2 blood samples violently explode away from each other. This indicates that the two creatures have no relation to one another.
<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Related:</span> The 2 blood samples remain together. This indicates that the two creatures share a distant ancestry, at least 6 generations back.
<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Closely Related: </span>The 2 blood samples fuse and glow. This indicates the creatures share a close ancestry, or have some other blood ties, such as being converted by the same vampire family.
