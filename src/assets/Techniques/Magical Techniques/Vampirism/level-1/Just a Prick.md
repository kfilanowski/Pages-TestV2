A slashing or piercing weapon in your possession turns red. You can do one of the following.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Extract</span> [[Subtle]] - Extract a small vial of the creature's blood. The creature rolls a [[+Perception]] check, either opposed to your [[DC]] or [[+Sleight of Hand]] roll (your choice). If they fail their roll, the spell to goes unnoticed, even if other creatures are present.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Exsanguinate</span> - Make a [[Melee Attack]] that causes the target to [[Bleeding|bleed]] d6 damage [[On-hit]]
