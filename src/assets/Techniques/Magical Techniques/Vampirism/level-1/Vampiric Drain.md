Level 1 [[Vampirism]]
Tags: [[Concentration]], [[Healing]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
Launch a glowing red projectile at an enemy as a [[Melee Attack|melee]] or [[Ranged Attack|Ranged]] [[Spell Attack]]. The creature takes 2d8+CL (9+CL) [[True Damage]] and [[On-hit]] begins [[Bleeding]] for d6 damage. You also form a tether to this creature that prevents the bleed from ending. This tether is broken if the creature moves more than 30 ft away from you.

You heal equal to half the [[+HP]] damage you deal as part of this spell, including the bleed damage.

<span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Special:</span>
If you are a vampire, this has the [[Overheal]] tag.
