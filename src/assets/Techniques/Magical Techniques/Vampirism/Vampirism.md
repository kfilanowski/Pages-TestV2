Vampires are creatures that have cheated death through complex arcane rituals. Their price was in blood. In order to exist, they must drink the blood of intelligent creatures. Their transformation also comes with a large number of idiosyncrasies. It is through these unique circumstances that 3 of the original vampires made new magics to aid them in circumventing these new challenges. Each spell cast still has a piece of them.

Vampirism spells deal in unique effects that are traditionally ascribed to vampires. They also user clever ways to manipulate blood.

Each spell is attributed to a specific vampire, mostly for flavor reasons.

- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Senketula</span> - The eldest brother and the one most concerned with eternal life. His spells are largely focused on becoming a superior being through a multitude of various abilities.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Lovacious</span> - The partner of Senketula most concerned with power over others. Her spells are largely focused on manipulation.
- <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">Vrykul </span>- The youngest brother most concerned with raw power. His spells are largely focused on using blood as a weapon to be used against the living.
