Level 1 [[God Mode]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Upcast]], [[Teleportation]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 30 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
Fire a ray of volatile energy at a creature that deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) force damage. [[On-hit]] you can decide to either teleport them 5 feet in a specific direction, or double that distance in a [[random direction]].

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Upcast </span>- Every 4 [[Caster Level|CL]]s, increase the distance you teleport the creature 5 ft (which is then doubled if randomized).
