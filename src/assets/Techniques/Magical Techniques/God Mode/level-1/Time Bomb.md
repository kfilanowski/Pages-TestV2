Level 1 [[God Mode]]
Tags: [[Force]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft                                |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius, centered on a creature |
| Duration       | -                                    |
| Save           | Reflex                               |
You cause a rapidly ticking down unstable looking clock to appear above a creatures head. At the end of that creature's turn, it explodes, dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) force damage to all enemies within 10 ft (reflex halves dmg).

You can place this time bomb on an ally or an enemy. The time bomb never damages allies.
