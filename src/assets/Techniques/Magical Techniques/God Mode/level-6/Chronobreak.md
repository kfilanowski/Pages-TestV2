Level 6 [[God Mode]]
Tags: [[Force]], [[Teleportation]], [[Recharge]], [[Healing]], [[Contingent]]
Action: Instant

| Range          | -                                                         |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 10 ft radius burst, centered on an afterimage...see spell |
| Duration       | -                                                         |
| Save           | Reflex                                                    |
While this technique is prepared a ghostly afterimage of yourself mimics your actions and movement, but skipping a few seconds with a delay. It is [[Invisible]] unless you are in combat.

At any point you can take standard actions (ie it's your turn and you aren't suffering from [[CC]]), you can choose to revert back to the time your afterimage represents. You return back to the position you were in at the <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">beginning</span> of your <span style="color:rgb(192, 0, 0)">last</span> turn.

When you do, you burst in magical force, dealing 7d8+CL (31+CL) force damage to all enemies (reflex halves). You also restore 3d8+CL (13+CL) hp and cleanse all non-[[permanent status]] effects.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Pro Tip</span>
I recommend using a token to refer to where your afterimage is currently located. Because you can only use it during your turn, when you end your turn, you can safely update its position to where your turn started.
