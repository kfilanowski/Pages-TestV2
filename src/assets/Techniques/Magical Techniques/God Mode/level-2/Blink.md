Tags: [[Buff]], [[Teleportation]], [[Recharge]]
Action: Swift

| Range          | -        |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | Self     |
| Duration       | 1 minute |
| Save           | -        |
You sporadically blink out of the mortal plane into a private [[demiplane]]. At the end of your turn, roll a d6. On a 5 or 6, you blink out of existence, putting you in [[stasis]] until the start of your turn. When you reappear, the spell ends.

This spell cannot be used in consecutive turns.
