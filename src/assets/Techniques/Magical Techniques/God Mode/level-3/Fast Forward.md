Level 3 [[God Mode]]
Tags: [[Recharge]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | 60 ft      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 creature |
| Duration       | -          |
| Save           | -          |
The creature you target moves at incredible speed, allowing them to immediately take an additional turn after yours. This does not affect their position in initiative.

On the creatures next turn they are [[dazed]].

The recharge tag does not apply to the caster. A creature cannot be the target of this spell for 1 minute after being affected by it.

The magic of this spell is a bit unstable and can sometimes go overboard. After the daze ends, an affected creature must make a DC 8 [[luck check]] or age a number of years equal to (8-luck check result).
