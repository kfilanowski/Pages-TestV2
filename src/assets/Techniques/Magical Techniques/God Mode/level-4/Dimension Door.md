Level 4 [[God Mode]]
Tags: [[Teleportation]], [[Concentration]]
Action: Standard

| Range          | Reach, 120 ft               |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Target/[[AoE]] | 1 mostly solid flat surface |
| Duration       | 1 minute                    |
| Save           | -                           |
Touch a mostly flat solid surface and cause a incomplete portal to appear on it. So long as you continue to concentrate, at any point after as a standard action, you can touch another solid surface within 120 feet of the original and turn the portal on. For 1 minute, creatures can walk into one portal and appear out of the other. This does not require concentration and lasts the full minute. After this time passes, the portals close and you must place them again.

You can see through the portal as though it were a window leading to its destination.
