Demiplanes are a step above extradimensional spaces. They exist in the same ether between planes, but they are typically much larger and are permanent. They are typically created via magic like [[Fairy Ring Retreat]].

It is technically possible to reach a demiplane with the correct [[tuning fork]], but because demiplanes are typically private locations, it can be very difficult to obtain one, and the creators of the planes often have special abilities they can use to remove unwanted guests.

Demiplanes are shaped in accordance with the desires of their creators, and thus vary wildly in form and function. Some are secret laboratories used by alchemists to hide their secrets and others are lavish buildings used for exclusive parties.
