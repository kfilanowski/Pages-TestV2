Extradimensional spaces are locations that exist in an intermediary plane. They are small, self-contained bubbles in an unnavigable ether. These spaces are typically created by mages, such as through the [[Arcane Pocket]] technique.

There are no conventional means to access extradimensional spaces other than through the portal created by the effect that caused them to spawn into existence. Extradimensional spaces are often temporary and fleeting.
