The [[Psionics]] tree is something that has been developed piecemeal over long periods of time, culminating into its current form under the guidance of an entity by the name of Santa. Those who are more well informed may make the connection that Santa is actually an alias for an entity known as Glass Reflection: An [[Elf]] that was a world-renowned [[Spy]] once upon a time. Enough time passes and the mortals forget who you are.

Those who go to great lengths to find out more, trying to meet Santa or through dedication to the [[Chrimbus Spirit]], may even learn her true identity: Colene Larella. She goes to great lengths to keep her true identity hidden, hiding behind aliases and intermediaries for all but the most important of interactions.

Using a powerful device that amplifies her psychic powers, she scans the world for those who struggle, primarily children, and does her best to aid them in their trying times. She gives them gifts, sometimes in the form of presents, other times in companionship through a [[Poplin]] sibling.
