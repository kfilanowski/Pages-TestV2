Where to start? Nack is my version of earth.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Nack is filled with humanoids of varying sizes, shapes and lifespans.</span> It has plants and animals, some of which you will recognize, and many you won't. There are plenty of other types of creatures that break our IRL classifications entirely: Elementals that are composed of normally inanimate substances, aliens from other worlds looking to steal our cows and creatures raised from the dead.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">The various regions of the world all have a story to tell.</span> The Land of Storms is named such because there is a persistent drizzle over the continent, often increasing in intensity in storms. The Underworld is a place hidden away in the darkest reaches of the world, constantly shifting its position to prevent people from trying to get in to bring others back to life.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">The universe is suffused with magic!</span> Those with the talent to use mana can bend the world to their will. Wizards can use it to conjure fire from their fingertips, healers can use it to cure those burns and warriors can use it to cause the earth to quake beneath their feet. Everybody is born with access to mana, and history is made by civilizations that know how to use it wisely.

<span style="color:rgb(103, 254, 120)">Gods are tangible creatures that can be interacted with.</span> By acting in your gods name, you can receive insight into situations and special powers. Gods each have their own special interests in the world, such as maintaining the natural order, increasing the quantity of happiness in the world, or killing people just for the fun of it. Particularly dedicated followers may even get the chance to meet their god in person.

There are bits of lore scattered all around the website already. The dedicated lore section is more for the world itself, its culture, history, cosmology and noteworthy people.
Each race has its own lore section, gods have lore written in their sections, and eventually so will each bestiary entry.
